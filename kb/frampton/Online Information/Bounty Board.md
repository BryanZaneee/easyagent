# Bounty Board

**Bounty Board** is a page dedicated to Player Trade in **Dark Souls** and Dark Souls Remastered. The aim of this page is to assist players to find items they are missing by posting an asynchronous call and leaving means of contact. You may also use our Player Help & Trade Forums or come by our Discord.
