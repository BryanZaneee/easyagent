**Twinkling Titanite** is an Ore in **Dark Souls**. Ores function as upgrade components for Weapons and Armor. Some varieties may be given to Kingseeker Frampt, who converts them into lesser versions.

> This weapon-reinforcing titanite is imbued with a particularly powerful energy.
> Reinforces weapons that cannot be reinforced normally to +5.
> After this titanite was peeled from its Slab, it is said that it received a special power, but its specific nature is not clear.

### Usage

* Can be used to upgrade non-infusable weapons and rare armor (excluding Boss Soul and Dragon).
* See Demon Titanite and Dragon Scale for Boss Soul and Dragon weapon upgrades respectively.
* Examples of a non-infusable weapon: Black Knight Greatsword, Gold Tracer, Dragonslayer Greatbow.
* Examples of rare armor: Favor Set, Giant Armor Set, Chester's Set.

### Farming Tips

**Ash Lake**: Equip the Covetous Gold Serpent Ring and gather lots of Humanity if you wish. Rest at the bonfire near the beginning of Ash Lake. Upon defeating the first two giant clams you see, use Homeward Bone or the Homeward miracle to quickly respawn them. Repeat as much as you like. If the clams are too tough for you an Ultra Greatsword or similar weapon with high damage can infinitely stun the clams when timed correctly. These clams are very susceptible to Fire damage. If you have some high powered Pyromancies, two shots should take them down. If you simply cannot afford to purchase Homeward Bone in bulk, a trip to the Crystal Cave may be a better idea.

 **Crystal Cave**: After defeating Seath the Scaleless the bonfire in Crystal Cave will be unlocked. This allows for easy farming of the Man-Eater Shells for Twinkling Titanite. Higher level Pyromancy can take out these Shells in 2 hits. Longer range melee weapons, like Greatswords, Spears, etc imbued with Lightning can outrange enemy attacks and stun multiple enemies on hit, to facilitate safer and easier farming. You can warp to the bonfire in the Crystal Caves, making it much easier to leave and come back to this farming spot.
