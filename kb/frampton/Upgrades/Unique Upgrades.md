# Unique Upgrades

| Unique Upgrade in Dark Souls |
| --- |
| raw upgrade s |
| **Unique Upgrade** does not increase parameter bonus per upgrade level.  It has a maximum upgrade to +5 that increases **Base Damage** with superiority than that of the regular weapons in terms of the damage output. |

**Unique Upgrades** are a type of  Upgrade in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered.** They enable players to "Ascend" weapons and shields in a specific path.

### path and Requirements

**Unique Upgrades** for weapon are performed by  all blacksmiths, or with the Weapon Smithbox.

Note that each upgrade level requires  twinkling titanite s Twinkling Titanite or demon titanite s  Demon Titanite. or dragon scale s Dragon Scales  and a number of soul that varies depending on the weapon.

For Demon Upgrade:

* Upgrade from +0 to +5 requires 10  demon titanite s  Demon Titanite.

For Twinkling Titanite Upgrade:

* Upgrade from +0 to +5 requires 10 twinkling titanite s Twinkling Titanite.

For Dragon Scale Upgrade:

* Upgrade from +0 to +5 requires 10 dragon scale s Dragon Scales.

#### Demon Upgrade from 0 to +5

* Upgrade from 0 to +1 requires 1 demon titanite s  Demon Titanite.
* Upgrade from +1 to +2 requires 1 demon titanite s  Demon Titanite.
* Upgrade from +2 to +3 requires 2 demon titanite s  Demon Titanite.
* Upgrade from +3 to +4 requires 2 demon titanite s  Demon Titanite.
* Upgrade from +4 to +5 requires 4 demon titanite s  Demon Titanite.

#### Twinkling Titanite Upgrade From +0 to +5

* Upgrade from 0 to +1 requires 1 twinkling titanite s  Twinkling Titanite.
* Upgrade from +1 to +2 requires 1 twinkling titanite s  Twinkling Titanite.
* Upgrade from +2 to +3 requires 2 twinkling titanite s  Twinkling Titanite.
* Upgrade from +3 to +4 requires 2 twinkling titanite s  Twinkling Titanite.
* Upgrade from +4 to +5 requires 4 twinkling titanite s  Twinkling Titanite.

#### Dragon Scale Upgrade from +0 to +5

* Upgrade from 0 to +1 requires 1 dragon scale s Dragon Scale.
* Upgrade from +1 to +2 requires 1 dragon scale s Dragon Scale.
* Upgrade from +2 to +3 requires 2 dragon scale s Dragon Scales.
* Upgrade from +3 to +4 requires 2 dragon scale s Dragon Scales.
* Upgrade from +4 to +5 requires 4 dragon scale s Dragon Scales.
