**Large Magic Ember** is an Ember in **Dark Souls**. Embers are key items given to blacksmiths to unlock specific weapon upgrade paths. Once you provide the appropriate ember to a qualified blacksmith, that upgrade becomes permanently available for any suitable weapon, provided you have the necessary materials.

> Ember required for weapon ascension. Large magic ember, a form of sorcery. Handled only by the Vinheim blacksmiths.
> Ascends +5 magic weapons. Allows reinforcement to +10, the most powerful level for magic weapons

### Location

* The Duke's Archives. After defeating Seath the Scaleless, return to the archives where you first confronted Seath and open the chest in the middle of the room.

### Usage

* Give to Rickert of Vinheim to ascend +5 Magic weapons into +10 Magic.
