**Dung Pie** is a Projectiles in **Dark Souls**. Projectiles are offensive items designed to deal damage to Enemies and Bosses. These throwable objects can be hurled at hostile targets to inflict minor damage or trigger specific effects.

> Atrocious fecal waste material. Throw at enemy to build up toxins, but also builds up your toxicity.
> Although the stench makes it difficult to carry on one's person, turning an enemy toxic inflicts high damage over a period of time

### Usage

* Thrown item. Inflicts Toxic status on enemies, but also on yourself.
* Toxin build-up is 25 on you, and ?? on target.
* Toxic Power is 420 (7 HP/s) for 60 seconds.
* Trade one with Snuggly for a Demon Titanite.
* Can be "sold" to Frampt for 200 souls.

### Location

* Sold by Female Undead Merchant for 200 souls.
* Sold by Hawkeye Gough for 200 souls.
* Dropped by Dung Giants (all) in Blighttown.
* Drops from Infested Barbarian (50% Chance - Blighttown)
* Drops from Vagrants, specifically the White Good Vagrant. (Depths, Undead Burg, Undead Parish, Painted World of Ariamis, Darkroot Garden, Darkroot Basin, The Catacombs, Tomb of Giants, The Great Hollow, Ash Lake, Demon Ruins, Lost Izalith, Sen's Fortress, Blighttown, Anor Londo, New Londo Ruins Lower, New Londo Ruins Upper, The Duke's Archives, Crystal Cave, Northern Undead Asylum, Kiln of the First Flame  and Royal Wood)
