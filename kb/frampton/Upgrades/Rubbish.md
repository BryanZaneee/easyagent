**Rubbish** is a Tool in **Dark Souls**. Tools are unique, reusable equipment pieces designed to assist players in multiple gameplay situations, ranging simple interactions to helping during difficult boss fights.

> Rubbish with no value.
> Who in their right mind would bother carrying this around? Perhaps you need help.

### Usage

* Trade it with Snuggly for a Titanite Chunk.
* Sell it to Kingseeker Frampt for 1 soul.

### Location

* Treasure, found by dropping down to the lower area, right in front of the first fog door in Undead Burg.
* Possible reward from forest hunter covenant when a successful invasion has been completed.
* Possible starting item
* Video Location Timestamp 5:08
