**Enchanted Ember** is an Ember in **Dark Souls**. Embers are key items given to blacksmiths to unlock specific weapon upgrade paths. Once you provide the appropriate ember to a qualified blacksmith, that upgrade becomes permanently available for any suitable weapon, provided you have the necessary materials.

> Ember required for weapon ascension. This enchanted ember, a form of sorcery, is a vestige of the lost land of Oolacile. Ascends +5 magic wpn to enchanted weapon.
> (enchanted weapon can be magic reinforced to +5) The sorcerer's enchanted weapon that inflicts magic damage and is boosted by intelligence.

### Location

* Found in Darkroot Garden (second area; located pass Alvina) in a small pool guarded by Large Mushroom People.
* Video Location Timestamp 9:51

### usage

* Give it to Rickert of Vinheim to ascend +5 Magic weapons into Enchanted weapons.
