**Chaos Flame Ember** is an Ember in **Dark Souls**. Embers are key items given to blacksmiths to unlock specific weapon upgrade paths. Once you provide the appropriate ember to a qualified blacksmith, that upgrade becomes permanently available for any suitable weapon, provided you have the necessary materials.

> Ember required for weapon ascension. Chaos Flame Ember is an art of the lost city of Izalith. Handled only by blacksmiths knowledgeable in ancient methods.
>
> Ascends +5 flame weapon to chaos weapon. (chaos weapon can be reinforced to +5) Chaos weapons are demon weapons augmented by humanity which inflict fire damage.

### Location

* Found after beating Ceaseless Discharge, which is located right after the boss in Blighttown, Chaos Witch Quelaag.
* After you kill him, the lava will harden allowing you to walk to the area he was standing. The Ember is located in a pool of lava (not the item against the rock-wall), and is guarded by 7-8 or so Taurus Demons, so you better take them on one at a time.
* Use of the Flame Stoneplate Ring and the Flash Sweat spell is recomended, because you'll actually have to walk through the lava to get to the Ember. Also, the Gold-Hemmed Robe set provides great fire resistance, and can be looted from a corpse near Ceaseless Discharge.
* The Orange Charred Ring, dropped by the Centipede Demon, can be used to safely acquire the Ember.
* Video Location Timestamp 1:00

### Usage

* Give it to Blacksmith Vamos to modify +5 Fire weapons to the Chaos path.

### Notes & Tips

* Chaos weapons cannot be buffed.
* Chaos weapons will inflict damage depending on your Humanity level (max at 10 Humanity). The Humanity scaling decreases exponentially: the first humanity is worth more damage than the tenth. Bear this in mind.
* Chaos ascension also changes the fire damage to physical damage ratio; from 1:1 to approx 2:1.
* Exploiting the Dark Hand's R2 attack can give you a lot of soft Humanity, if you're worried about using up your reserves.
