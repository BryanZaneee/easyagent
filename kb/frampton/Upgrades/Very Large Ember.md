**Very Large Ember** is an Ember in **Dark Souls**. This ember can be used to modify standard weapons from +10 to +15 by giving it to Blacksmith Andre. However, you must first give him the Large Ember, or he will not request the Very Large Ember. You can find the Very Large Ember after lowering the water level in New Londo Ruins. Go up the staircase found in a corner of the room with all the wooden cubicles, and the ember is located in a chest on the third floor. Embers are key items given to blacksmiths to unlock specific weapon upgrade paths. Once you provide the appropriate ember to a qualified blacksmith, that upgrade becomes permanently available for any suitable weapon, provided you have the necessary materials.

### Location

* New Londo Ruins. After lowering the water level, go up the staircase found in a corner of the room with all the wooden cubicles. The ember is located in a chest on the third floor.
* Can be accessed relatively easily by taking the first elevator in the New Londo down, you shouldn't have to deal with too many enemies besides a few ghosts and the one Darkwraith that may chase you.
* The chest can also be reached by dropping down from the spot you encounter the first screaming female ghost (guarding some transient curse), after lowering the water level. This method allows you to bypass all the darkwraiths, since you'll land on a ledge on the second floor when dropping down.
* Video Location Timestamp 15:47

### Usage

* Give to Blacksmith Andre to modify +10 standard weapons up to +15.
* Must give the Large Ember to Andre first, or he will not ask for the Very Large Ember.
