# Int-Faith Catalyst Magic Adjustment Values

Every Catalyst in Dark Souls comes with a **Magic Adjustment** stat that dictates the potency of sorceries cast through it. This adjustment impacts the damage output of direct damage sorceries and boosts from weapon enhancement sorceries. Typically, a catalyst's magic adjustment scales with the user's intelligence. However, Oolacile Ivory Catalyst and the Oolacile Catalyst have fixed magic adjustments, while the Tin Darkmoon Catalyst's adjustment scales with faith instead of intelligence.

Certain Catalysts offer distinctive features. The Demon's Catalyst and the Tin Banishment Catalyst, though not as potent, can function as weapons due to their comparatively high physical damage potential. Meanwhile, the Manus Catalyst and Tin Crystallization Catalyst provide the highest magic adjustment but reduce the available casts per spell by half.
