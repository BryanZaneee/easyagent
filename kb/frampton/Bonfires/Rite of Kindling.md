**Rite of Kindling** is a Key Bonfire Item in **Dark Souls**. Key Bonfire Items are special items used only at Bonfires or at the Lordvessel within the Firelink Altar. They enable core progression functions, including upgrading equipment, managing inventory, and unlocking fast-travel capabilities to key Locations.

> This secret rite allows bonfires to be bolstered further with Kindling, so that even more Estus can be collected.
> Kindling was a sacred rite passed down among clerics, but all Undead can imitate the process, in the same manner that they restore their Hollowing with humanity.
> How peculiar that humans had found little use for humanity until they turned Undead.

### Usage

* Allows further kindling of Bonfires.
* Allows Bonfires to provide 15 to 20 Estus Flasks.

### Location

* The "Rite of Kindling" is learned by defeating Pinwheel in The Catacombs.
* Reah of Thorolund and her companions seemed to be searching for the Rite of Kindling.
