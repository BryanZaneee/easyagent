# Oolacile Sanctuary

**Oolacile Sanctuary** is a special DLC Location in **Dark Souls**, and included in the base game with **Dark Souls Remastered**. After defeating the Sanctuary Guardian, you'll make your way into here; the **Oolacile Sanctuary** is a simple location with only an NPC and item. This is a bonfire for the Artorias of the Abyss expansion - and an optional area in the game.

### Getting to know the locals

After defeating the Sanctuary Guardian, find the doorway and continue in to end up in this Graveyard-like area containing only one loot-able corpse with 1 Humanity. There is also a **bonfire** in the middle of the sanctuary. There won't be another bonfire for a while, after a field of relatively tough enemies and a difficult boss fight, so you may want to kindle this one.

os bonfire

Elizabeth, the only female mushroom-person and inhabitant of this area, can be seen from the **bonfire**. She sells the spells of Oolacile and other items, and will ask you to save Dusk once again. If you succeed in your quest, she will reward you (visit her page for more details).

To the right of Elizabeth is a path to the next area, the Royal Wood.
