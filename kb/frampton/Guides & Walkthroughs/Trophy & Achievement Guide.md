# Trophy & Achievement Guide

This page contains the list of trophies and achievements of the game, as well as guides to obtaining them. Spoilers ahead!

* **Online Trophies:** None, but online can be optionally used in order to attain trophies based off of weapons.
* **Minimum Playthroughs:** Three in order to attain the Knight’s Honor trophy. Planning on covenants and Boss Soul usage is important. Follow our Game Progress Route for an easier time.
* **Missable Trophies:** The multiple covenants found throughout the game can be missed on first playthrough. There are also two optional bosses found within the game that may also be missed.
* **Glitched Trophies:** None.

In order to achieve **all of the trophies** found within this **Dark Souls Trophy Guide** on one character you must collect all of the unique weapons found within the game along with all of the pyromancies and spells as well. For more information about this game you can visit the Dark Souls Wiki.

#### The Dark Soul Trophy The Dark Soulplatinum trophy

*Attain all other trophies found throughout the game.*

Self explanatory, but not exactly easy! The platinum trophy is acquired when all other trophies are unlocked.

#### Knights Honor Trophy Knight’s Honorsilver trophy

*Acquire all of the unique weapons found within the game.*

In order to acquire this trophy you must make every boss soul weapon in the game, with a few bosses having more than one unique boss soul weapon. Once these souls are collected you can have the Giant Blacksmith make your boss soul weapons. You must also acquire the weapons found after severing the tails of the Bridge Wyvern, Gaping Dragon, Seath the Scaleless, Priscilla, and the Everlasting Dragon. Other unique weapons that must be found are the weapons dropped by the silver knights, stone knights, and black knights. Certain NPC invaders will also drop weapons that you will need. You must also acquire the Giant’s Halberd, the crest shield, crescent axe, channeler’s trident and the demon’s spear. All other unique weapons can be found within chests and from joining different covenants. A full list is here

#### Wisdom of a Sage Trophy Wisdom of a Sagesilver trophy

*Acquire all of the sorceries found within the game.*

Many of the spells that you must have within this game must bought from the mages that you interact with, one of whom you must save and exhaust all dialogue options from. Additionally, two of these spells are acquired after the death of the two mages. There are only two spells that must be found within the game that are not sold one being *Strong Magic Shield* and the other **Remedy**. Also keep in mind that you may need a high intelligence in order to purchase these spells from the NPCs. A full list of Sorceries and where to find them can be found here.

#### Prayer of a Maiden Trophy Prayer of a Maidensilver trophy

*Acquire all of the miracles found within the game.* .

Much like ‘Wisdom of a Sage’ many of the miracles in the game you will have to purchase, however it is recommended that you buy these miracles as soon as you can. Additionally, there are seven miracles that you must collect from the game’s covenants: Gravelord Servant, Blade of the Dark Moon, and Warrior of Sunlight. One of these miracles will also require a Soul of Gwyn in order to receive it. There are also an additional five miracles that must be found throughout Lordran; two of them can be found by killing two of the Piascas found within the Duke’s Archives while the other three can be found in Ash Lake, the Catacombs, and the Painted World of Ariamis. A full list of the Miracles and where to find them can be found here.

#### Bond of a Pyromancer Trophy Bond of a Pyromancersilver trophy

*Acquire all of the pyromancies found within the game.*

Like the trophies above, many of the pyromancies found within this game must be bought by the many different pyromancers that you can find. You must also join the Chaos Servant covenant in order to acquire two pyromancies, however you can only acquire one after ranking up within the covenant. There are also four other pyromancies that you must find that are located in Lost Izalith, the Painted World of Ariamis, and Blighttown. A full list of Pyromancies and where to find them can be found here.

#### Way of White Trophy Covenant: Way of Whitebronze trophy

*Join the Way of White covenant.*

One of the easiest covenants to join, the quickest way to join is to talk to Petrus after arriving at Firelink Shrine. He will ask you to join the covenant, once he asks say ‘yes’ and you will be initiated into the covenant.

#### Princesss Guard Trophy Covenant: Princess’s Guardsilver trophy

*Join the Princess’s Guard covenant.*

In order to join this covenant, you must have acquired the Lordvessel from Gwynevere; after this talk with her again and she will offer you the ability to join her covenant, select ‘yes’ and you will be initiated into the covenant.

---

#### Blade of the Darkmoon Trophy Covenant: Blade of the Darkmoonsilver trophy

*Join the Blade of the Darkmoon covenant.*

In order to join this covenant you must have the Darkmoon Seance Ring that is found within the Catacombs. After this head down the lower bonfire of Anor Londo, the one that is in a lower room after you have lowered the rotating platform. If you have the ring the statue in front of the bonfire will disappear, head down towards the boss fog, here you will find the ability to kneel, do so, once you do you will be asked to join the covenant select ‘yes’ and you will be initiated into the Blade of the Darkmoon.

---

#### Sun Covenant: Warrior of Sunlight silver trophy

*Join the Warrior of Sunlight covenant.*

In order to join into this covenant a requirement of **25 faith** is needed, however this can be avoided if you help at least 4 people with the white soapstone. After you have met the requirements to join, you will have to cross the bridge the Hellkite Wyvern guards in the Undead Burg. Once you’ve arrived at the other side, you’ll see a doorway on the right hand side, go here and pray to the broken altar. Once you have prayed you will be asked to join the covenant. Select ‘yes’ and you’ll have joined.

#### Forest Hunter Trophy Covenant: Forest Hunter silver trophy

*Join the Forest Hunter covenant.*

There are two ways to join this covenant, one requiring the Crest of Artorias, that you purchase from *Blacksmith Andrei*. Once you have bought the crest, head down to the bonfire guarded by an illusory wall and use the crest to open the door. After the door has been unlocked you will have to fight NPCs guarding the forest: be mindful as they are quite powerful. Then head towards the stone structure to find a cat named Alvina. The second way is a little more difficult and is only recommended if you do not have the souls for the crest. Head down to Darkroot Basin, once there you will have to fight the Hydra and Crystal Golems. Upon the defeat of the enemies there head up the latter found on the left side of the basin. Once you have scaled this and another latter found on the other side of the river head across the walkway. You will eventually have to fight three large cats, after their defeat head through the forest, eventually you will find a stone structure and within it a cat named Alvina. Once you’ve reached Alvina she will ask you two questions **select ‘yes’ for both** and you will be initiated into the covenant.

#### Dark Wraith Trophy Covenant: Darkwraith silver trophy

*Join the Darkwraith covenant.*

**Missable**: In order to join this covenant, you will have to defeat the Four Kings that lurk within New Londo Ruins but you must still have the Lordvessel – **do not place it via Frampt before doing this section** (Depending on how far in the game you are you may also end up killing Ingward if you do not have the Lordvessel). After defeating the kings, a Primordial Serpent named Kaathe will appear. They will ask you a question once you talk to them select ‘**yes**‘ and you will be initiated into the covenant.

#### Gravelord Servant Trophy Covenant: Gravelord Servant silver trophy

*Join the Gravelord Servant covenant.*

Found within the catacombs, you will have to find an . #### Chaos Servant Trophy Covenant: Chaos Servant silver trophy

*Join the Chaos Servant covenant.*

After your battle with Quelaag head over to the bell, you will find a staircase leading down a level. Once you have descended you will find an illusory wall, speak to the creature here. You will be asked a question, once asked say ‘**yes**‘, once it has moved aside speak to the woman found at the side of the bonfire. Select ‘Enter Covenant’ and you’ll be initiated into the covenant.

#### Strongest Weapon Trophy Strongest Weapon bronze trophy

*Reinforce a weapon with a regular path using titanite shards, large titanite shards, titanite chunks, and a titanite slab.*

These resources can all be found off of enemy drops. These enemies being: Undead Soldiers, Red Cape Knights, Slime, Steel Knights, Bloated Heads (DLC), Crystal Lizards, Black Knights, Giants, and Darkwraiths. Some of them can also be bought from smiths throughout Lordran as well. During every NG sequence the player is given the chance to acquire 2-3 regular titanite slabs, depending on if they have DLC installed. One can be found as a drop after the defeat of the Stray Demon upon returning to the Northern Undead Aslyum, another can be received after completing the Siegmeyer’s storyline, and the last can be found behind a waterfall in Kalameet’s boss area. These slabs can also be farmed from the Darkwraiths found in New Londo ruins as well. These ascensions are all provided by Andrei of Astora in the Undead Parish, as long as the player has the Large Ember and the Very Large Ember. A full list of upgrade materials and embers can be found here.

#### Crossbreed Priscilla Trophy Defeat Crossbreed Priscilla silver trophy

*Defeat the Lifehunter, Crossbreed Priscilla.*

This optional boss can be found at the end of the Painted World of Ariamis. To gain access to this area you need the strange doll that can be found if you return to the Northern Undead Asylum after leaving it the first time. Once you have obtained it you must head towards the large painting in the room with the high beams in Anor Londo, this will grant you access into the Painted World.
