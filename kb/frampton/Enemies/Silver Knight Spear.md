**Silver Knight Spear**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Silver Knight Spear** can be driven forward using full body weight for precision thrusts or swung broadly for sweeping strikes, and can be obtained as a drop from Silver Knights patrolling Anor Londo. Spears are long-reaching thrusting weapons ideal for a defensive playstyle, allowing you to attack while keeping your guard up. Their relatively quick jabs are effective for interrupting enemy attacks. However, their predictable stabbing motion can be easy to block and is less effective against fast or small targets.

> The silver knights of Anor Londo guard the city using this beautifully slender weapon.
>  The spear can be wielded by both hands in a focused thrust that uses one's body weight, or swung in a large sweeping motion."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Drops from Silver Knight (2% Chance - Anor Londo)

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 1 soul.

An excellent choice for any dexterity-based characters looking for a divine weapon, being one of the only weapons with a divine effect and no faith requirement **/**scaling.
