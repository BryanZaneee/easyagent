**Ghost Blade** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered. Ghost Blade** is a cursed dagger carried by the spirits of New Londo, capable of harming ghosts thanks to its ethereal properties. Daggers are swift, precise weapons with a short reach, ideal for close-quarters combat. Their rapid attacks allow players to weave in successive strikes to stagger foes while maintaining the mobility to dodge. Typically featuring high critical damage and good Dexterity scaling, daggers are versatile enough to be wielded in the left hand, where they can also be used to Parry incoming attacks.

> The ghosts of New Londo clutch this wickedly sharp dagger with their bony hands and make mincemeat of the living."
>  "One of the cursed weapons which can inflict damage upon ghosts."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Drops from Banshees (1% chance - New Londo Ruins). It does NOT drop from other ghosts, they drop Jagged Ghost Blades instead.

### Hints and Tips

* Can be given to Kingseeker Frampt to receive 100 Souls.
* **Ghost Blade** can be upgraded by using Twinkling Titanite.
* R2 strong attack is changed to a double horizontal slash that can be followed up by a triple slash with an additional input.
* This weapon allows the player to damage Ghosts and Banshees in New Londo Ruins without the need for Transient Curse. However, only the physical weapon will do damage; magic will not affect ghosts, even if this weapon is equipped, and the player will not be able to block ghosts' attacks with a shield.
