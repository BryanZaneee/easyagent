**Divine Upgrades** are a type of Upgrade in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered.** They enable players to "Ascend" weapons and shields in a specific path.

### Divine Upgrade path and Requirements

**Divine Upgrades** for weapon and shield are performed by Andre of Astora at the Undead Parish.

Note that each upgrade level requires a number of soul that varies depending on the weapon.

* Ascension from Regular +5 to Divine +0 requires a Divine Ember and Green Titanite Shard to ascend.
* Divine upgrade from +0 to +5 requires 9 Green Titanite Shards.
* Divine upgrade from +5 to +6 requires a Large Divine Ember and White Titanite Chunk to ascend.
* Divine upgrade from +5 to +9 requires 7 White Titanite Chunks.
* Divine upgrade from +9 to +10 requires a White Titanite Slab.

#### From 0 to +5

* Divine upgrade from +0 to +1 requires 1 Green Titanite Shard.
* Divine upgrade from +1 to +2 requires 1 Green Titanite Shard.
* Divine upgrade from +2 to +3 requires 2 Green Titanite Shards.
* Divine upgrade from +3 to +4 requires 2 Green Titanite Shards.
* Divine upgrade from +4 to +5 requires 3 Green Titanite Shards.

#### From +5 to +9

* Divine upgrade from +5 to +6 requires 1 White Titanite Chunk and a Large Divine Ember.
* Divine upgrade from +6 to +7 requires 1 White Titanite Chunk
* Divine upgrade from +7 to +8 requires 2 White Titanite Chunks.
* Divine upgrade from +8 to +9 requires 3 White Titanite Chunks.

#### From +9 to +10

* Divine upgrade from +9 to +10 requires 1 White Titanite Slab.
