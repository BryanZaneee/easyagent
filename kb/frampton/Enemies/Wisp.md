**Wisp** is an Enemy in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Enemies are hostile creatures that respawn when players rest at a Bonfire or upon death. Special enemies that do not respawn are classified as Bosses, Mini Bosses or Invaders.

### Information

A floating disembodied head that faintly glows red. When they get close enough to you they'll violently explode.

### Attacks

* **Explosion** - AoE explosion in proximity to the player.

### Strategy

* They are attracted to noise, so they will approach when you are fighting.
* The Ring of Fog reduces their aggro range to almost zero.

Ensure you're not in any danger of falling off a ledge and try to get them to come to you. When they get close enough they'll start to shake and glow increasingly red. When you see this happening this is your cue to back off to a safe distance as quickly as possible, because they are about to explode.
