**Black Knight Sword** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls**. Not to be confused with the Black Knight Greatsword. The Black Knight Sword is a common drop from Black Knights, who can be found in areas such as the Undead Burg and the Undead Asylum. This weapon can be upgraded using Twinkling Titanite to further enhance its power. If desired, it can also be given to Kingseeker Frampt in exchange for 50 souls. Greatswords are heavy, powerful weapons with long reach and wide swings capable of hitting multiple enemies at once. This power comes at the cost of speed, as they are slow to swing and require high stats to wield effectively. Their immense force can easily stagger foes, often creating openings for follow-up attacks.

> *"Greatsword of the Black Knights who wander Lordran. Used to face chaos demons."*
>  *"The Large motion that puts the weight of the body into the attack reflects the great size of their adversaries long ago."*

### How to Get

* A chance to drop from the Black Knights who wield the same sword (20% chance). One or several may be found in Undead Burg, The Catacombs, Northern Undead Asylum (Revisit), Tomb of Giants and Kiln of the First Flame.

### Hints and Tips

* Can be given to Kingseeker Frampt to receive 50 Souls.
* **Black Knight Sword** can be upgraded by using Twinkling Titanite.
* Like every Black Knight weapon, this sword has a 20% damage modifier when used against Chaos Demons.
* An excellent mid-game sword with decent swing speed. At 40 Str and 18 Dex, Attack rating is at decent 500. Good for stun-locking weak armored and low poise enemies.
* Very effective while two-handed.
* The light attack is relatively fast and does high damage.
* The heavy attack is devastating when timed correctly: after a long windup, the player swings the blade vertically upward, causing enemies with low-mid poise to be flung in the air.
