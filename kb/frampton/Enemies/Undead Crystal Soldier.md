**Undead Crystal Soldier** is an Enemy in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Enemies are hostile creatures that respawn when players rest at a Bonfire or upon death. Special enemies that do not respawn are classified as Bosses, Mini Bosses or Invaders.

A weak crystal encrusted soldier with quite strong attacks.

### Drops

* Crystal Straight Sword (3% Swordsman)
* Caduceus Kite Shield (2% Swordsman)

### Attacks

* **Jump Attack**
* **Downward Strike**
* **Slash**
* **Shoulder Bash**

### Strategy

* Aggro them one at a time if possible; they become a pain in groups.
* Don't use magic weapons on them, as they are quite resistant to magic damage.
* If you have a strong enough weapon, they are easily staggered to death.
* They move quite slowly and it's simple to just backstab them after you dodge one of their slow moving attacks.
* Watch out for signs of them being buffed by a Channeler, because then they can deal serious damage.
* Don't forget about any Crystal Archers that are nearby; lure them away from the arrows to a safer battleground.
* They are weak to thrust and lightning attacks. This means weapons such as rapiers and spears would be ideal. Especially the lightning spear you may have recieved from Sens Fortress.
