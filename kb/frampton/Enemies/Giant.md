**Giant** is an Enemy in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Enemies are hostile creatures that respawn when players rest at a Bonfire or upon death. Special enemies that do not respawn are classified as Bosses, Mini Bosses or Invaders.

### Description

Huge warriors responsible for controlling the machinations and protection of **Sen's Fortress**. There are three of them across the fortress: the **Gate Giant**, the **Boulder Giant** and the **Firebomb Giant**. Apart from the **Boulder Giant**, they do not respawn.

### Drops

* Titanite Chunk (Guaranteed, except on Boulder Giant where it is an unknown percentage chance)
* 1000 souls

### Attacks

* **Stomp Attack -** can be blocked with high enough ST and a greatshield, but it's easier to simply back step it.
* **Ground Swipe** - can be blocked with high enough ST and a greatshield, but staying between his leg or behind him is another way of simply avoiding the attack
* **Charge Attack** - a combination of a stomp and swipe where he'll move forward at the same time and then remain idle while he catches his breath - this is a great time to land some hits.

### Strategy

Simply stay beneath them and hacking away at the ankles initially, but roll to avoid getting hit when they swipe the ground with their arms. They will start to stomp on you if you stay beneath them too long, so back off when you see them raise a leg to begin stomping. If you see them winding up for the charge attack then get behind them and wait until they finish, at which point they'll be wide open for a good few hits while they catch their breath.
