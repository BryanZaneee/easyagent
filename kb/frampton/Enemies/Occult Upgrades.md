**Occult Upgrades** are a type of Upgrade in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered.** They enable players to "Ascend" weapons and shields in a specific path.

### Occult Upgrade path and Requirements

**Occult Upgrades** for weapon are performed by Andre of Astora at the Undead Parish.

Note that each upgrade level requires a number of soul that varies depending on the weapon.

* Regular Upgrade from +5 to Divine +0 requires a Divine Ember and Green Titanite Shard to ascend.
* Divine upgrade from +0 to +5 requires 9 Green Titanite Shards.
* Divine upgrade from +5 to Occult +0 requires a Dark Ember and White Titanite Chunk to ascend.
* Occult upgrade from +0 to +4 requires 7 White Titanite Chunks.
* Occult upgrade from +4 to +5 requires 1 White Titanite Slab.

#### From 0 to +4

* Occult upgrade from +0 to +1 requires 1 White Titanite Chunk
* Occult upgrade from +1 to +2 requires 1 White Titanite Chunk
* Occult upgrade from +2 to +3 requires 2 White Titanite Chunks
* Occult upgrade from +3 to +4 requires 3 White Titanite Chunks

#### From +4 to +5

* Occult upgrade from +4 to +5 requires 1 White Titanite Slab.
