**Painting Guardian** is an Enemy in **Dark Souls** and Dark Souls Remastered. Enemies are hostile creatures that respawn when players rest at a Bonfire or upon death. Special enemies that do not respawn are classified as Bosses, Mini Bosses or Invaders.

### Description

Guardians of the massive painting that acts as the entrance to the Painted World of Ariamis, located at the bottom of the Chapel of Anor Londo. They are quick and agile enemies who wield two curved Painting Guardian Swords and Throwing Knives.

### Drops

* Throwing Knife x2 (10% Chance)
* Throwing Knife x5 (5% Chance)
* Painting Guardian Sword (2% Chance)

### Attacks

* **Quick One-Handed Swing Attack**
* **Heavy Two-Handed Swing Attack**
* **Quick Running Attack**

### Strategy

* They are big trouble in groups, its best to try to deal with a couple or so at a time. Do not let them get behind you as they will do a backstab attack. Wait for them to throw a dart or attack, and then sidestep and attack them. They are very fast, so attacks have to be quick. Do not let them heal.
* Try luring them out one at a time. Block the first swing then kill it.
   If fighting multiple Guardians, let both of them hit your shield at the same time, then kill them.
