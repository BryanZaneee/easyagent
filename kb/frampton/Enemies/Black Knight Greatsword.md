**Black Knight Greatsword**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls**. This greatsword is a common drop from the Black Knight found in the Undead Parish, specifically at the top of the tower where your character encounters undead rats. The weapon primarily scales with Strength, offering substantial damage output. Among Strength-scaling weapons, it is only surpassed by the Demon Great Machete in raw power, making it a formidable choice for Strength-focused builds. Ultra Greatswords are heavy weapons and require a considerable strength investment to wield. Their swings are slow but far-reaching, wide and extremely powerful, capable of stunning enemies caught in the hit. Ultra Greatsword users can therefore perform several stun-lock combos, missing an attack however leaves the wielder wide open for enemy retaliation. This weapon class has the highest physical damage reduction and stability when used for blocking incoming hits.

> *Greatsword of the black knights who wander Lordran. Used to face chaos demons."*
>  *"The large motion that puts the weight of the body into the attack reflects the great size of their adversaries long ago."*

### How to Get

* Drops from The Black Knight with Great Sword. (20% chance - Undead Parish and Kiln of the First Flame)
* Black Knight respawn in Kiln of the First Flame.

### Hints and Tips

* Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
* Rather effective as a shield breaker in pvp but if you miss you'll be left open to attacks.
* Two handed strong attack is similar to that of the Black Knight Greataxe, but faster.
* Special: One-handed R2 causes Knockback
* Special: Two-handed R2 causes Knock Up
* The Dragon Greatsword, Black Knight Greatsword and Demon Great Machete are slower than the Greatsword and the Zweihander, though they don't appear to be much slower, it is more difficult to stunlock enemies with the first three.
