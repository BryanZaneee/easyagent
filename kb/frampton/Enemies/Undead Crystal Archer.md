**Undead Crystal Archer** is an Enemy in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Enemies are hostile creatures that respawn when players rest at a Bonfire or upon death. Special enemies that do not respawn are classified as Bosses, Mini Bosses or Invaders.

### Information

A crystal encrusted undead armed with a bow and arrows. Their arrows seem to have a slight homing effect.

### Drops

* Longbow (2% Archer)

### Attacks

* **Arrow** - Fires an arrow from his bow. The arrows seem to home in on you some-what.

### Strategy

* Don't use magic weapons as they are ineffective on crystal enemies.
* When not actively trying to get them or when dealing with other enemies, stay in cover.
* Try to not have your back to them if you're out in the open.
* Rush them with your shield up and take them down.
* Archers are sometimes placed as bait for Crystal Soldier ambushes, so watch your back.
