# Divine

| Divine |
| --- |
| res divine |
| Deals bonus damage against Skeleton enemies, Crow Demon, and Mass of Souls. Also prevents Skeleton enemies from resurrecting. |

Divine is a gameplay mechanic in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. It is a **debuff status effect** and one of the **auxiliary special effects** and a **damage type**.

### General Information

* Divine is an auxiliary effect attained upgrading a weapon down the Divine Upgrade path (ascension by upgrading a Standard +5 weapon, with a requisite of Divine Ember and Large Divine Ember done by Andre of Astora), or finding an already imbued weapon with Divine.
* Effective against Skeleton enemies, Crow Demon, and Mass of Souls.
* Effect: Prevent Skeleton enemies from resurrecting in The Catacombs and Tomb of the Giants.

### Weapons with Auxiliary Effect

* Astora's Straight Sword
* Crescent Axe
* Grant
* Greatsword of Artorias
* Silver Knight Spear
