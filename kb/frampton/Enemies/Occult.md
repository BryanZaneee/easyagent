# Occult

| Occult |
| --- |
| res occult |
| Deal Extra damage to certain enemies and bosses |

**Occult** is a gameplay mechanic in **Dark Souls**. It is a **debuff status effect** and one of the **auxiliary special effect** and a **damage type**. Occult is an auxiliary effect attained by upgrading a weapon down the Occult upgrade path. This is done by first upgrading a weapon to Divine +5, then ascending it using the Dark Ember. Weapons imbued with the Occult effect deal extra damage to certain enemies and bosses, particularly those associated with divine beings or those weak to dark forces. This can be particularly useful in specific encounters where exploiting these weaknesses can significantly turn the tide of battle.

### General Information

* Occult is an auxiliary effect attained upgrading a weapon down the Occult upgrade path (Ascension by upgrading a  Divine +5 weapon with a requisite of Dark Ember) or finding an already imbued weapon with Occult.
* Effect: Deal Extra damage to certain enemies and bosses.
* Effective against Black Knight, Silver Knight, Royal Sentinel and Sentinel.

### Weapons with Auxiliary Effect

* Priscilla's Dagger
* Velka's Rapier
* Dark Hand

### Enemies, Bosses and Characters weak

* **Characters**
  + Paladin Leeroy
* **Enemies**
  + Black Knight
  + Silver Knight
  + Sentinel
  + Royal Sentinel
* **Bosses**
  + Ornstein and Smough
  + Gwyn Lord of Cinder
  + Dark Sun Gwyndolin

### Notes

* A Club already ascended to Occult can be found in Anor Londo together with Havel's Gear.
