**Greatsword of Artorias** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Greatsword of Artorias** is a refined blade similar to the Abyss Greatsword, scaling with Strength and Dexterity and remaining usable without full Faith/Intelligence requirements, albeit with hit deflection. Greatswords are heavy, powerful weapons with long reach and wide swings capable of hitting multiple enemies at once. This power comes at the cost of speed, as they are slow to swing and require high stats to wield effectively. Their immense force can easily stagger foes, often creating openings for follow-up attacks.

> *Sword born from the soul of the great grey wolf Sif, guardian of the grave of the Abysswalker Knight Artorias."
>  *"Sir Artorias hunted the Darkwraiths, and his sword strikes harder against dark servants."**

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Ascension with the Soul of (Great Wolf) Sif and either the **Broken Straight Sword** +10 or **Straight Sword Hilt** +10.
* In order to create the "Cursed" version of this sword (the variant that can damage Ghosts without applying Transient Curse), you can ascend the soul using any normal +10 upgraded Straight Sword. *See Greatsword of Artorias (Cursed)*.

### Hints and Tips

* Can be given to Kingseeker Frampt to receive 50 Souls.
* **Greatsword of Artorias** can be upgraded by using Demon Titanite.
* This weapon has split AR, which decreases the total damage significantly. As a trade off, it sports a 140 Divine damage modifier and a hidden passive modifier against Abyss enemies (Darkwraith Knights, The Four Kings), making it a potent choice for traversing lower New Londo Ruins and The Catacombs.
* Like the Abyss Greatsword, this sword requires only Strength and Dexterity for the Physical damage scaling. It is still usable even without meeting the Intelligence and Faith requirement, except it bounces back for every hit.

### Trivia

* In German the sword is called Finstertöter which translates to dark slayer which may be a callback to the counterpart of the moonlight greatsword in king's field The Dark Slayer. The cursed version is called Großschwert von Artorias In German.
