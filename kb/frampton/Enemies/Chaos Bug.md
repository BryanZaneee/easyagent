**Chaos Bug** is an Enemy in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Enemies are hostile creatures that respawn when players rest at a Bonfire or upon death. Special enemies that do not respawn are classified as Bosses, Mini Bosses or Invaders.

A small insect found in Lost Izalith and the Demon Ruins. Has no attacks and usually dies in one hit. There is one that will have glowing red eyes; this one does not respawn and drops the Sunlight Maggot headgear upon death.

### Drops

* Sunlight Maggot (Guaranteed) (only from the non-respawning one with glowing red eyes)
* Sunlight Medal (3% Chance)
* Red Titanite Chunk (0.5% Chance)
