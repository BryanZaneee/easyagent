**Black Knight Greataxe** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls**. This axe boasts one of the highest base damage stats in the game and comes with strong strength scaling from the start. The only drawback is that it cannot be enchanted, but it compensates for this with its powerful swings that can easily break through poise. You can acquire this weapon by defeating the Black Knight in The Catacombs or the Kiln of the First Flame.

> Greataxe of the Black Knights who wander Lordran. Used to face chaos demons."
>  "The large motion that puts the weight of the body into the attack reflects the great size of their adversaries long ago."

### Where to Find

* Black Knight (Axe) (The Catacombs) (does not respawn)
* Black Knight (Axe) (Kiln of The First Flame) (respawns).

### Tips and hints

* Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
* At 40 Strength and 18 Dex fully upgraded Black Knight Greataxe has 610 Attack rating.
* This weapon can be rather effective in pvp because of its high damage, decent swing speed and long range.
* The 1H strong attack results in a jump-slam attack.
* Special: The two-handed strong attack results in an attack that knocks the enemy up in the air
* Does 20% extra damage vs (chaos) demons.
