**Vile Maggot** is an Enemy in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Enemies are hostile creatures that respawn when players rest at a Bonfire or upon death. Special enemies that do not respawn are classified as Bosses, Mini Bosses or Invaders.

### Description

A clot of Vile Maggots will hatch once an Egg Carrier is killed. They are long and pale with sharp facial pincers, they resemble a real life "Grub".'

### Drops

* 18 Souls

### Attacks

* **Jumping** **Attack,** can be blocked.
* Running Attack**,** can be blocked.

### Strategy

These are creatures better just left alone as they are a hassle to deal with. They spit poisons at the player and lunge from a decent distance when gotten too close to. This can be lethal when there is more than one of them as the attack causes a approximate \*1 sec\* stunning before you can roll away. If you feel you MUST deal with them, it is recommended to be done at a distance or one at a time with melee.
