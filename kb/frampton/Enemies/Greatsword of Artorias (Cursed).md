**Greatsword of Artorias (Cursed)** is a Weapon in Dark Souls and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Greatsword of Artorias (Cursed)** is a soul-forged weapon born from Sif, guardian of Artorias’ grave, and can be upgraded with Demon Titanite. Greatswords are heavy, powerful weapons with long reach and wide swings capable of hitting multiple enemies at once. This power comes at the cost of speed, as they are slow to swing and require high stats to wield effectively. Their immense force can easily stagger foes, often creating openings for follow-up attacks.

> Sword born from the souls of the great grey wolf Sif, guardian of the grave of the Abysswalker Knight Artorias."
> "The sword can damage ghosts, as it was cursed when Artorias joined a covenant with the creatures of the Abyss."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Ascension with the Soul of (Great Wolf) Sif and any of the following normal +10 weapons: Dagger, Parrying Dagger, Bandit's Knife, Shortsword, Longsword, Broadsword, Balder Side Sword, Sunlight Straight Sword, Barbed Straight Sword, Darksword, Bastard Sword, Claymore, Man-serpent Greatsword, Flamberge, Zweihander, Greatsword, Demon Great Machete.
* In order to create the "Non-Cursed" version of this sword (the variant that has Divine and Abyss modifiers), you can ascend the soul using either the Broken Straight Sword +10 or Straight Sword Hilt +10. *See Greatsword of Artorias*.

### Hints and Tips

* Can be given to Kingseeker Frampt to receive 50 Souls.
* **Greatsword of Artorias (Cursed)** can be upgraded by using Demon Titanite.
* When ascending the weapon, it will only be displayed as "Greatsword of Artorias", it will not say "Cursed". To be certain of which sword you're creating, look at the stats, which are unique. This sword deals purely Physical damage, while the Non-Cursed version has Phy/Mag split damage.
* Once you make the Cursed sword variant and view its description, you will see that it is "Cursed".
* This is one of the Cursed weapons that can inflict damage against Ghosts without the need to consume Transient Curse.
* Like the Abyss Greatsword, this sword requires only Strength and Dexterity for the Physical damage scaling. It is still usable even without meeting the Intelligence and Faith requirement, except it bounces back for every hit.
* Unlike the game says, this weapon does NOT scale on Intelligence nor Faith at all.

### Lore

* Artorias and Sif are progenitors to The Abyss Watchers of DS3.
