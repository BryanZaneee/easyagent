**The Black Knights** are Mini Bosses in **Dark Souls****. Mini Bosses** are hostile creatures that do not respawn after being killed. It is said that these knights were burnt black during their battles with Chaos Demons, which granted them high Fire resistance. A few of them even accompanied Gwyn to the Kiln of the First Flame, where they were eventually burnt to ashes when Gwyn linked the First Flame. These Black Knights can be found throughout the game and do not respawn upon death, except for the ones at the Kiln of the First Flame.

### The Black Knights Information

The once proud Silver Knights of Gwyn, Lord of Cinder now roam around Lordran wielding powerful weapons, the Black Knight Shield and the Black Knight Set. It is said that these knights were burnt black in their fight with Chaos Demons, and this caused them to have high Fire resistance. Few even traveled with Gwyn to the Kiln of the First Flame, who were then burnt to ashes when he linked the First Flame, leaving behind just their spirits that roam the spectral staircase in between the Kiln and the Lordvessel.

### Strategy

Black Knights are formidable opponents with high poise and significant damage potential, making direct confrontations risky. To effectively deal with them, focus on Backstabs and Parries. Their recovery time after an attack is relatively slow, leaving them vulnerable to these tactics.

Their attack patterns are generally predictable, so timing your Parry as soon as you see their hands move will enable you to execute a Riposte. This method is both effective and satisfying, as you can interrupt their attacks and deal substantial damage. Note that while every attack except the Shield Bash can be parried, the shield bashes are particularly dangerous. They can drain a significant amount of your stamina and are often followed by a heavy attack or combo.

Additionally, Black Knights are most vulnerable right after completing a combo attack. Use this opportunity to sneak up behind them and perform a Backstab. By leveraging their attack patterns and recovery times, you can efficiently counter and defeat these powerful foes.

### Black Knights Tips

* It is often easier to run them off a ledge or bridge than to fight them, especially at lower levels. You still get the drops even if they die by falling off. So just let gravity do the work for you.
* Ranged attacks are usually the safest way to deal with these knights. Position yourself where they cannot reach you and then unload into them with your bows or firebombs.
* If you are rocking a heavy build, you should keep your distance as outmaneuvering the knights if not an option for you. Stay at the edge of the Knight's range and strike in between attacks, patiently taking away a bit of his health with each opportunity.
* Only the Black Knights in the Kiln of the First Flame respawn, the other ones do not respawn even if you rest at a bonfire
