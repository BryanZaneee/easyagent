**Skeleton Baby** is an Enemy in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Enemies are hostile creatures that respawn when players rest at a Bonfire or upon death. Special enemies that do not respawn are classified as Bosses, Mini Bosses or Invaders.

Skeletal babies, about knee height.

### Drops

* Humanity (2% Chance)

### Attacks

* Lunges forward a small amount. Inflicts toxin and relatively minor damage.

### Strategy

* Because they usually spawn in groups you should use low sweeping attacks. Some normal melee attacks wont hit them as they are shorter than your normal enemy. They can also inflict the toxic status. Come prepared.
* They are also completely infinite, endlessly spawning. A good place to farm souls and humanity with the Covetous Gold Serpent Ring and Covetous Silver Serpent Ring
