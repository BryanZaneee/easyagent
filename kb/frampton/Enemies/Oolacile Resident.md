**Oolacile Resident (Bloatheads)** is an Enemy in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Enemies are hostile creatures that respawn when players rest at a Bonfire or upon death. Special enemies that do not respawn are classified as Bosses, Mini Bosses or Invaders.

### (Bloatheads)Description

Humanoid creatures with long arms and a red, bloated head with numerous eyes.
 Note: Oolacile Residents drop a respectable amount of Large Titanite Shards making them prime for farming.

### Drops

* Large Titanite Shard (5% Chance)
* Large Titanite Shard x2 (1% Chance)
* Bloated Head (0.5% Chance)
* 400 Souls

### Attacks

* **Claw Attacks**
* **Combo Attack**
* **Running Attack**

### Strategy

Although they run quickly, they are quite clumsy and easy to evade. They have a running-claw attack that may catch you the first time, but afterwards, you should have no trouble blocking or evading. Their otherwise limited repertoire consists of close-range claw attacks that can be parried and riposted. At about Level 50, you should have no trouble with them. Also avoid attacking them in groups. Their attacks are powerful to anyone not using heavy armor. They also use a combo hit that can do devastating damage. Easily killed with Pyromancy. High Offense. Low Defense.
