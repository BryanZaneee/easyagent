**Jagged Ghost Blade** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Jagged Ghost Blade** allows attacks that can hit ethereal ghosts thanks to its cursed nature, granting rare devastating effects in New Londo. Curved Swords are agile, medium-range weapons that excel in Dexterity scaling and feature a fluid moveset. They primarily deal Slash and Thrust damage, making them ideal for confined spaces where their rapid, hard-to-evade attacks overwhelm foes. Their unique kick is replaced by an evasive slash that flips the wielder backward after striking.

> Jagged blade wielded by New Londo ghosts. Violent thrust attack."
>  "One of the cursed weapons. Inflicts damage to ghosts."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Dropped by dual blade ghost (1% chance - New Londo Ruins)

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
* Special: R2 attacks execute combo attacks which greatly build up Bleed.
* Special: Only the weapon itself deals damage to ghosts. Having it equipped will not allow magic or other weapons to deal damage to ghosts.
* Special: The heavy attack has a downward stabbing combo.
* Bleed build up is 30 per strike, and once inflicted will tear 30% of the opponents HP.
