**Flaming Attack Dog** is an Enemy in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered. Enemies are hostile creatures that respawn when players rest at a Bonfire or upon death. Special enemies that do not respawn are classified as Bosses, Mini Bosses or Invaders.

### Information

Fiery cousin of the Undead Attack Dogs. These red-colored fire-breathing demon wiener dogs are small and hard to hit.

### Attacks

* Bite - Fast attack, causes bleed and can't be parried.
* Jumping Bite - Fast slashing attack, causes bleed and can't be parried.
* Fire Breath - Slow ranged attack in which the dog breathes a stream of fire in a straight line. Has a long start-up time and deals fire damage.

### Strategy

Use a spear to skewer these guys. It aims better down low. Keep your shield up and circle as normal. Locking on and fighting them in corridors is advisable.The dogs will sometimes wait for 2-3 seconds then breathe fire at you. Attack after lunges or breath. They're actually pretty easy to kill if you take them slow.
