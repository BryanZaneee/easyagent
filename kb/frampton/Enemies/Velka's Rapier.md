**Velka's Rapier** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Velka’s Rapier** grants swift thrusting attacks with sin-steeped precision, though it cannot be buffed or coated with resins. It is a weapon used by pardoners serving Velka, Goddess of Sin. Piercing Swords are specialized thrusting weapons ideal for breaking an enemy's poise and exploiting openings for critical damage. They feature a fast, unique moveset of rapid consecutive attacks, including a far-reaching horizontal swipe that replaces the standard kick. Effective when wielded in either hand, they can also be used to parry.

> A symbolic, powerful thrusting sword used by the Pardoner serving Velka, Goddess of Sin."
>  "It is no mere symbol to be sure; the Pardoner is an inhuman swordsman, and wields this enchanted blade with special sword technique."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* In the Painted World of Ariamis, through the door requiring the Annex Key. After accessing the Annex using the Annex Key, continue upstairs. In the next section, there is a set of wooden planks on the right. Breaking them gives access to a corpse with the rapier on it.
* Video Location Timestamp 18:05

### Notes

* Cannot be coated with any resin, nor buffed via magic weapon or similar spells.
* Innately imbued with the Occult auxiliary effect (along with Priscilla's Dagger and Dark Hand).
* Like all piercing swords, replaces your kick with a back-stepping horizontal slash attack.

### Hints and Tips

* A good thrusting weapon for a high-INT build
* One-handed strong attack is replaced by a quick, two-hit reverse V-shaped swipe that does slash damage.
* Two-handed strong attack is replaced by a quick, two-hit V-shaped swipe that does slash damage.
* The follow-up to both strong attacks is a delayed thrust attack.
* Because of its innate magic attack, Velka's Rapier deals a significant amount of damage when performing critical strikes. Velka's Rapier will deal the highest crit damage of all piercing swords, but only with considerable stat investment.
* Give to Frampt to receive 5000 souls.
