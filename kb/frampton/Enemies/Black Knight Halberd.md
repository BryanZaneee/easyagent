**Black Knight Halberd**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Black Knight Halberd**, a halberd with sweeping body-weighted strikes once used by Black Knights and obtained rarely from their wielders. Halberds function like heavier, more powerful spears with versatile damage types. Their slower, sweeping attacks require precise timing to avoid lengthy recovery. This weapon class can be enhanced by items like the Leo Ring to significantly boost its damage output.

> Halberd of the black knights who wander Lordran. Used to face chaos demons. The large motion that puts the weight of the body into the attack reflects the great size
> of their adversaries long ago.

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Drops from The Black Knight, specifically the Halberd wielding Knight. (20% chance - Darkroot Basin, Tomb of Giants and Kiln of the First Flame).

### Hints and Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
* Uniquely, The Black Knight Halberd can out damage  the Black Knight Greataxe and the Black Knight Sword at 40 strength and 40 dexterity.
* As with all heavy weapons, a plunging attack with the Black Knight Halberd is an effective way to kill the Taurus Demon and Asylum Demon.
* The one-handed heavy attack seemingly strikes twice. The first "strike" however is not an attack and is a part of the wind-up leading to the actual strike. Uniquely, in Dark Souls 3 this was corrected and the first strike is considered a hit.
* The two-handed heavy attack has slightly shorter wind-up time to strike compared to the one-handed heavy attack.
