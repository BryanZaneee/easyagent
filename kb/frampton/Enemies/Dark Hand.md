**Dark Hand**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls**. When used as a shield, this weapon can reduce damage by 80% across all damage types and has the added benefit of being able to parry incoming attacks. As a weapon, its special right-handed heavy attack allows it to steal liquid humanity from NPCs or other players. Advancing through the Darkwraith covenant will enhance the amount of humanity you can steal with this attack. This item can be acquired by joining the Darkwraith covenant or by defeating Darkwraith Knights, who drop it in the New Londo Ruins. Fists are ultra-fast, low-stamina weapons with the shortest range, requiring players to engage at point-blank distance. Their rapid combos are excellent for breaking an enemy's guard or exhausting their stamina. Wielding them in the left hand allows for parrying, while using them in the right replaces the kick with a quick knee strike, adding versatile utility to their relentless offense.

> *The Darkwraiths, incited by Kaathe, use the power of the dark soul to absorb humanity, an art shared by this weapon, which also acts as a special shield.*
>
> *The ancients, particularly, could sap the humanity of even a replete saint in the blink of an eye."*

### Where to Find

* Joining the Darkwraith Covenant
* Drops from Darkwraith Knights (1% Chance - New Londo Ruins)

### Upgrade cost

* This weapon cannot be Upgraded.
* Leveling your Darkwraith rank will not make the Dark Hand more powerful, but cause it to steal more Humanity.

### Notes

In the right hand it is a powerful fist weapon with the ability to steal humanity, from players you have invaded or NPCs, while in the left hand it also becomes a shield.

* Give to Frampt to receive 1 soul.
* Can be used in left hand and Produces a good but low stability shield.
* Special: Pressing R2 while the Dark Hand is in your Right hand will initiate a grab attack, used to steal humanityfrom another player (PVP) or from some NPCs.
  + A normal Dark Hand will steal 1 humanity from players and NPCs with each grab, even if you are not a darkwraith member.
  + Ranking up as a Darkwraith will increase the amount of humanity drained with each grab.
    Darkwraith +1 = 3 humanity / +2 = 5 humanity / +3 = 10 humanity.
  + R2 bypasses any blocks and parries.
* This move can also be used multiple times to steal all humanity from PvP players and NPCs.
* Using R2 on an NPC should not cause them to become hostile, so you can use it freely, with the exclusion of the blacksmiths and Patches the Hyena, who will become hostile upon attack.
* Both you and your target must be at the same floor height or the R2 grab attack will only do damage instead of grabbing him.
* The R2 grab attack will have the same animation if they have no humanity left.
* Unlike in Dark Souls 3, the Dark Hand can parry while in the left hand, making it great for blocking elemental projectiles but still being able to parry melee attacks.
* Obtaining the Dark Hand through killing Dark Wraiths can easily be done through the use of high item drop rate on your character. (410 drop rate, for example).
* It appears to be possible to steal humanity with Dark Hand is equipped in your left hand by switching equips in the right, and then using L2. More specifically, switching from Dragonslayer Great Bow to Darkmoon Tin Catalyst in the right hand. (Unconfirmed).

See Humanity Farming for a list of NPCs, and how much humanity each one holds.
