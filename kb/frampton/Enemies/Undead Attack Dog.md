**Undead Attack Dog** is an Enemy in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Enemies are hostile creatures that respawn when players rest at a Bonfire or upon death. Special enemies that do not respawn are classified as Bosses, Mini Bosses or Invaders.

### Information

Undead dogs that prowl the streets in packs of two or more. They will attack and dodge suddenly.

### Attacks

* **Bite -** Fast attack, causes bleed and can't be parried.
* **Jumping Bite** - Fast slashing attack, causes bleed and can't be parried.

### Strategy

Use a spear to skewer these guys. It aims better down low. Keep your shield up and circle as normal. Locking on and fighting them in corridors is advisable.
