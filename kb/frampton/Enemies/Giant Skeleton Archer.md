**Giant Skeleton Archer** is an Enemy in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Enemies are hostile creatures that respawn when players rest at a Bonfire or upon death. Special enemies that do not respawn are classified as Bosses, Mini Bosses or Invaders.

### Information

 A bigger, more powerful, but also slower version of your average skeletal archer. Equipped with a gigantic version of the longbow with matching arrows. Has less health than the melee version.

### Attacks

**Fire Giant Arrow**

**Kick -** Moderate windup then kicks, still pushes a blocking player a short displacement.

### Strategy

Arrows are powerful, but the the skeleton aims slowly. Attack from the rear. Weak to divine weapons and strike damage.

* Longbow
