**Chained Prisoner** is an Enemy in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Enemies are hostile creatures that respawn when players rest at a Bonfire or upon death. Special enemies that do not respawn are classified as Bosses, Mini Bosses or Invaders.

### Description

The chained prisoner is an enemy similar to the Oolacile Residents, it's humanity has gone wild, mutating and corrupting his body and mind. The Chained Prisoner is in the Oolacile Township and guards the entrance to the Chasm of the Abyss.

### Drops

* Chain Helm (20% Chance)
* Chain Armor (20% Chance)
* Leather Gauntlets (20% Chance)
* Chain Leggings (20% Chance)

### General strategy

* the Chained prisoner will use the pole and chained balls that are attached to him to attack. Since parrying is impossible, stability is what you'll want for this opponent. Most weapons will be deflected by the chains around him, similar to the Fang Boar / Armored Tusk, Heavy Weapons like the Greatsword won't be deflected.
