**Darkmoon Soldiers** is an Enemy in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Enemies are hostile creatures that respawn when players rest at a Bonfire or upon death. Special enemies that do not respawn are classified as Bosses, Mini Bosses or Invaders.

### Darkmoon Soldiers Description

One named **Kei** is dressed in Balder Armour  with the matching Balder Shield; very agile in comparison to his partner **Juu**who also is wearing a Steel Set  with Tower Shield and Greatsword, this one is a tank enemy.

### Strategy

* Lead the Balder enemy out a ways from their friend. Should be a rather easy fight. Next, you can kite, block, or just bum-rush the Steel enemy. Both are relatively easy enemies. If you are a Pyromancer, a few Fire Orbs or Great Chaos Fireballs shall make fighting them a light task.
