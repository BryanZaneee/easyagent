**Mimic** is an Enemy in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Enemies are hostile creatures that respawn when players rest at a Bonfire or upon death. Special enemies that do not respawn are classified as Bosses, Mini Bosses or Invaders.

Mimics are enemies disguised as a treasure chest, hence their name. They are patiently waiting for a gullible adventurer to come walking straight into their greedy clutches. Even though they are creatures mimicking a chest, they still have unique treasures inside; which are most likely undigested possessions from their previous victim. Mimics will not respawn after they have been slain.

### General Info

They look very similar to normal chests, but there are a couple of ways to tell them apart.

* All chests have a chain attached to its right side. A Mimic's chain bends back and forth towards the front, while the chain of a normal chest curves in one large arc towards the back.
* **Mimics**appear to breathe. Look closely, or use the **binoculars** or a bow, the lid will open around every 10 seconds when it takes a breath.
* If you look closely, you'll notice that the colour of a mimic chest is paler than a regular chest.

### Interacting with an idle

* If you open a Mimic chest normally, it will grab you and attempt to eat you whole.
* If you attack the chest instead, it will rise up and reveal itself. Prepare to fight.
* If you throw a **Lloyd's Talisman** at the Mimic, it will open the lid without waking up so you can grab the goodies inside. This is very convenient when farming for the **Symbol of Avarice**, since they do not respawn.
* If you want to get a Symbol of Avarice it is recommended to punch the Mimic, then throw a Lloyd's Talisman
   at the mimic to stun it. Repeat this until you get the item.

### Attack Patterns

* Left or Right hand punch.
* Kick.
* Flying Kick.
* Grab. This attack will be lethal to most players since it's practically the same attack as the one where it grabs
   you if you try to open it while sleeping.

### Strategies

* A double-handed jump attack with a strong weapon will take off a chunk of it's health before it's even gotten up.
* Try to stay behind to avoid its attacks.
* It can also be killed with Poison Mist, without waking up. (toxic as well?)
* You may throw a Lloyd's Talisman at an enraged mimic to stun it, giving you time to land free hits on it.
