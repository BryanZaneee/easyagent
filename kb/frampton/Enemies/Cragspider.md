**Cragspider** is an Enemy in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Enemies are hostile creatures that respawn when players rest at a Bonfire or upon death. Special enemies that do not respawn are classified as Bosses, Mini Bosses or Invaders.

### Information

Fire-breathing insects with three pairs of legs and two pairs of what seem to be arms. Even though they also have wings, cragspiders don't seem to be able to fly properly. Instead, they use them to hover in the air for a short while before landing again.

### Attacks

* **Fiery Breath**Deals fire damage in a wide arc.
* **Poking Attack**
* **Back fire attack** (it summons a spherical flame after a cast time if you attach him from behind. It seems a rare attack, I've seen it only once)**.**

### Strategy

* Cragspiders are relatively easy to beat. Avoid their fiery breath and approach them to get some hits in while blocking their close range attacks.
* A good strategy is to two hand and just hit them until you run out of stamina then switch to one hand and attack as soon as you gain stamina which should be before they recover from last attack. No armor means curved swords deal excellent damage.

### Trivia

* There is a cut enemy from Lost Izalith, the Crag Hollow, which appears to be halfway between a hollowed human and a Cragspider. This suggests that Cragspiders are in fact humans corrupted by the Chaos Flame. This is further supported by the fact that they use a fire attack similar to the pyromancy Fire Whip.
