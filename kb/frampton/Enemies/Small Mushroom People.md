**Small Mushroom** is an Enemy in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Enemies are hostile creatures that respawn when players rest at a Bonfire or upon death. Special enemies that do not respawn are classified as Bosses, Mini Bosses or Invaders.

### Information

These enemies come in two varieties: small, largely-defenseless shrooms and enormous brutes. The little ones can attack, but will almost always fall flat on their faces instead, leaving them completely vulnerable. The large mushroom men deal tremendous damage with their slow wind-up punches, and can guard crush the player quite easily

### Drops

* 50 souls
* Gold Pine Resin (5% Chance)

### Attacks

* Mushroom Punch.
* Mushroom Faceplant.
* Escape!! Once attacked they will run, and potentially lure you, towards their 'parents'.

### Strategy

* Just whack away at the little ones while they're down, blocking successful swings if necessary. Alternately, just let them walk past you and backstab them.
