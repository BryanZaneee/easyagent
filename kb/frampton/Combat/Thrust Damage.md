# Thrust Damage

| Thrust Damage |

**Thrust Damage**is one of the Damage Types in Dark Souls. It is one of the seven main Damage Types and belongs to the Physical Damage category. Damage Types are a major part of the calculation that determines how much damage is dealt to enemies and are affected by many factors including your equipment, spells and temporary buffs.

### Items & Spells that increase Thrust Damage

* Leo Ring (applies only to counter attacks)
* Power Within (40% boost to all types of damage for 100 seconds, at the cost of draining health)
* Red Tearstone Ring (when health is under 20%)

### Equipment, Items & Spells that increase Thrust Resistance

### Notes and Trivia

* Thrust-damage weapons are very effective against dragons.
* Thrust-damage weapons are effective against enemies which can easily be staggered and to break charging attacks.
* Moonlight Arrow and Moonlight Butterfly Horn deal zero physical damage, and because of this, deal no Thrust Damage.
