**Toxic**is a gameplay mechanic in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. It is a **debuff status effect,** an **auxiliary special effect,** and a **damage type**.

### General Information

* Deals high Toxic damage in a period of time as long as 10 minutes, lasting until cured, expiration or death.
* The number in the weapon info means how much damage the poison will do in 1 minute,not the buildup.

#### Build up

When exposed to Toxic, a build-up bar will appear at the middle of the screen.

* The size of the bar is determined by the player's poison resistance.
* If the exposure stops, the bar will slowly decrease by 5 point per second (in 10 second you or enemies will restore 50 point).
* The ailment will take effect when the bar is full.

#### Effect

Once the toxic bar is full, your health bar will begin to decrease rapidly.

* Deals high Toxic damage in a period of time as long as 10 minutes, lasting until cured, expiration or death.
* Toxic give by Sniper Dart will last only 3 minutes but it is the most deadly.
* Toxic has similar visual and audio queues to poison, but is darker in color.
* Reduces stamina regeneration by 15 per second.

#### Cure

Using any of these methods or items will both cure and reduce any toxic buildup.

* Blooming Purple Moss Clump.
* The Remedy sorcery.
* Resting at a Bonfire.

#### Prevention

Wear equipment that boosts poison resistance.

* The Gold-Hemmed Black Set and the Painting Guardian Set have strong resistances against both poison and toxin.
