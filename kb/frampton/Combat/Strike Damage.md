# Strike Damage

| Strike Damage |
| --- |
| titanite slab |

**Strike Damage** is one of the Damage Types in Dark Souls. It is one of the seven main Damage Types and belongs to the Physical Damage category. Damage Types are a major part of the calculation that determines how much damage is dealt to enemies and are affected by many factors including your equipment, spells and temporary buffs.

### Weapons that cause

* Catalysts (except Demon's Catalyst, Tin Banishment Catalyst, and Tin Crystallization Catalyst)
* Fist Weapons (except Claws)
* Great Hammers (all)
* Hammers (except Pickaxe and Warpick)
* Shields (except Pierce Shield)
* Talismans (all)

### Items & Spells that increase

* Power Within (40% boost to all types of damage for 100 seconds, at the cost of draining health)
* Red Tearstone Ring (when health is under 20%)

### Equipment, Items & Spells that increase Strike Resistance

* Armor
* Blue Tearstone Ring (Boosts**physical**, magical, flame and lightning defenses by 50% when health is below 20%)
* Ring of Steel Protection
* Shields

### Notes and Trivia

* Strike damage is very effective against skeletons and fragile enemies.
* Strike damage is the most effective physical-type damage when facing enemies wearing heavy armor with some exceptions.
