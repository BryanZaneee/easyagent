# Slash damage

| Slash Damage |
| --- |
| titanite slab |

**Slash Damage** is one of the Damage Types in Dark Souls. It is one of the seven main Damage Types and belongs to the Physical Damage category. Damage Types are a major part of the calculation that determines how much damage is dealt to enemies and are affected by many factors including your equipment, spells and temporary buffs.

### Weapons that cause

* Black Knight Halberd
* Claws
* Curved Greatswords (all)
* Curved Swords (all)
* Daggers (all)
* Flamberge
* Great Scythe
* Katanas (all)
* Lifehunt Scythe
* Scythe

### Items & Spells that increase

* Power Within (40% boost to all types of damage for 100 seconds, at the cost of draining health)
* Red Tearstone Ring (when health is under 20%)

### Equipment, Items & Spells that increase Slash Resistance

* Armor
* Blue Tearstone Ring (Boosts**physical**, magical, flame and lightning defenses by 50% when health is below 20%)
* Ring of Steel Protection
* Shields

### Notes and Trivia

* Slash is very effective against enemies with no armor
* Slash is not useful against enemies wearing very heavy armor.
* Many Slash Damage weapons can also inflict bleed.
