**Standard Helm** is a Helm in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Warrior Set. A common yet robust helm made of iron, accompanied by armor crafted from thick leather. It provides reliable defense against physical attacks, making it a sensible choice for protection against arrows and other dangers. **Standard Helm**, part of the Warrior Set, protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped

> A sturdy helm made of iron with armor made of thick leather. Very common type of protective gear, it provides a sound level of defense. It is wise to wear a sturdy form of head protection against arrows and other physical threats.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Warrior Set. Pieces include Standard Helm, Hard Leather Armor, Hard Leather Gauntlets and Hard Leather Boots.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Warrior (Starting Class)
* Depths: in the room where you fight Gaping Dragon
