**Balder Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls. This helm is part of the Balder Set and worn by the knights of the ancient realm of Balder. Crafted from thick iron plates, it provides solid protection. **Balder Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Helm worn by the knights of the ancient kingdom of Balder. It is made from thick iron plates. Balder was the homeland of the Knight King Rendal, but it came to ruin after a great many Undead were spawned

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Balder Set, Pieces include Balder Helm, Balder Armor, Balder Gauntlets and Balder Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Crestfallen Merchant for 5,000 souls
* Random drop from Balder Knight
* Drops from random Balder Knight (1% chance - Sen's Fortress and Undead Parish)
