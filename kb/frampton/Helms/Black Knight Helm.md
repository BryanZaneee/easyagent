**Black Knight Helm** is a Helm in **Dark Souls**. A part of the Black Knight Set, this helm is worn by the Black Knights who roam Lordran. It decreases stamina recovery by 1 per second. **Black Knight Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Helm of the Black Knight who haunt Lordran. The knights followed Lord Gwyn when he departed to link the flame, but they were burned to ashes in newly kindled fire, wandering the world as disembodied spirits ever after.

### Information

* Reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second.
* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Black Knight Set. Pieces include Black Knight Helm, Black Knight Armor, Black Knight Gauntlets and Black Knight Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Kiln of the First Flame. Corpse on a ledge near the third black knight.
