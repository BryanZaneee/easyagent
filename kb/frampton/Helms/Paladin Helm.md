**Paladin Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Included in the Paladin Set, this helm is worn by Leeroy, the Paladin of the Catacombs. It reduces stamina recovery by 1 per second. The Way of the White's first Undead, Leeroy, was clad in golden armor. **Paladin Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Helm of Leeroy, Paladin of the Catacombs. Long ago, the Way of the White produced its first Undead, a paladin in golden armor. With the legendary treasures Grant and Sanctus, Leeroy set out for Lordran, Land of the Gods, in the first Undead mission of the Way of White.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
* Reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second.
* Part of the Paladin Set. Pieces include Paladin Helm, Paladin Armor, Paladin Gauntlets and Paladin Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Tomb of the Giants. Gravelord Nito's chamber after defeating black phantom Paladin Leeroy
