**Black Iron Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls. This helm, worn by Black Iron Tarkus, a knight celebrated for his immense strength, is made of a unique black iron that provides excellent defense, especially against fire. However, its weight renders it impractical for anyone other than Tarkus. It is part of the Black Iron Set. **Black Iron Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped

> *"Helm of Black Iron Tarkus, a knight known for his great strength. Built of a special black iron and providing strong defense, notably against fire, but so terrible heavy to be unwieldy to all but Tarkus himself."*

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Black Iron Set. Pieces include Black Iron Helm, Black Iron Armor, Black Iron Gauntlets and Black Iron Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Anor Londo. To the left of the giant painting leading to the Painted World of Ariamis
