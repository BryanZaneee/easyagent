**Pharis's Hat** is a Helm in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Hunter Set. This broad-brimmed hat is the signature headgear of the renowned archer hero Pharis.  Universally admired, Pharis's hat is especially popular among children. **Pharis's Hat** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped

> Broad-brimmed hat favored by the archer hero Pharis. Pharis was an accomplished archer, and though he was human, he ranked alongside Hawkeye Gough, one of the Four Knights of Lord Gwyn. His hat is universally popular among children.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Hunter Set. Pieces include Pharis's Hat, Leather Armor, Leather Gloves and Leather Boots.

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Darkroot Garden, behind the door closed with Crest of Artorias, drops from an NPC enemy with a bow.
* Drops from Clan of Forest Protectors (Guaranteed - Darkroot Garden)
