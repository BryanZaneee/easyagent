**Havel's Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Part of Havel's Set, this helm is worn by the warriors of Havel the Rock. Carved from solid stone, it offers immense weight and defense, embodying the unyielding nature of Havel's soldiers, who never faltered in battle. **Havel's Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Helm worn by Havel the Rock's warriors. Carved from solid rock, its tremendous weight is matched only by the defense it provides. Havel's warriors never flinched nor retreated from battle. Those unfortunate enough to face them were inevitably beaten to a pulp.

### Information

* Cannot be upgraded
* Part of the Havel's Set. Pieces include Havel's Helm, Havel's Armor, Havel's Gauntlets and Havel's Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Anor Londo. In a room with a fireplace. Hit the fireplace to reveal an illusory wall.
