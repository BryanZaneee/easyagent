**Black Sorcerer Hat** is a Helm in **Dark Souls**. A piece of the Black Sorcerer Set, this hat is worn by clandestine sorcerers of the Vinheim Dragon School. These sorcerers specialize in sound-based spells, maintaining their secrecy at all times. The hat aids in their discreet operations. **Black Sorcerer Hat** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped

> Hat worn by the secret sorcerers at Vinheim Dragon School. They secretly work with sound-based spells, and never reveal themselves.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Black Sorcerer Set. Pieces include **Black Sorcerer Hat**, Black Sorcerer Cloak, Black Sorcerer Gauntlets and Black Sorcerer Boots.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Can be found at the Sen's Fortress.
