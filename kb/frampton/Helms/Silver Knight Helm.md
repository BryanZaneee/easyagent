**Silver Knight Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls. This helm belongs to the Silver Knight Set and is worn by the Silver Knights who guard Anor Londo. **Silver Knight Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Helm of the Silver Knights who protect Anor Londo. When Lord Gwyn departed to link the Fire, his knights split into two groups. The Silver Knights remained in the forsaken capital in the service of their goddess."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Silver Knight Set. Pieces include Silver Knight Helm, Silver Knight Armor, Silver Knight Gauntlets and Silver Knight Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Anor Londo. In a chest guarded by two Silver Knights
