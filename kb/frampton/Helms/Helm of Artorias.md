**Helm of Artorias** is a Helm in Dark Souls. This helm belongs to Artorias the Abysswalker, one of Gwyn's four knights. The dark corruption of the Abyss and the weathered azure-blue tassel, once a symbol of honor, suggest the fate of its owner. Part of Artorias' Set and sold by Domhnall of Zena. **Helm of Artoria** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped

> "Helm of Artorias the Abysswalker, one of Gwyn's four knights. The death of the helm's owner can be surmised from the corrosive Dark of the Abyss, and the musty azure-blue tassel, once a symbol of pride and glory."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Artorias' Set. Pieces include Helm of Artorias, Armor of Artorias, Armor of Artorias and Leggings of Artorias.

### Where to Find \ Location Helm of Artoria

* Sold by Domhnall of Zena for 20,000 souls after defeating Artorias The Abysswalker
