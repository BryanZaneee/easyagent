**Fang Boar Helm** is a Helm in Dark Soulsand Dark Souls Remastered. This helmet decreases stamina recovery by 1 per second. It is the severed head of the fully-armored Fang Boar, taken by its slayer as proof of victory, reminiscent of how the Gods once claimed the head of an ancient dragon. Despite its origin, it serves as a surprisingly effective piece of protective gear. **Fang Boar Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Severed head of the fully-armored Fang Boar taken by the one who killed it as proof of his victory, just as the Gods once did with the head of the ancient dragon. Can be worn on the head as a surprisingly sound piece of protective gear."

### Information

* **Fang Boar Helm** can be upgraded by using Twinkling Titanite.
* Can be given to Kingseeker Frampt to receive 1000 Souls.
* This helmet reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Uncommon (20%) drop from Fang Boars.
