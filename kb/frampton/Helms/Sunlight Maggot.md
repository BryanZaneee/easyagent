**Sunlight Maggot** is a Helm in Dark Souls. One of three available light sources, alongside the Skull Lantern and the Cast Light spell. This is a revolting parasite that resides in Lost Izalith, completely immobile yet alive. When worn on the head, it emits a bright light, earning it the name Sunlight Maggot. **Sunlight Maggot** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> A loathsome parasite that inhabits Lost Izalith. It is completely immobile, yet still lives. When worn on the head, it emanates blinding light, which is why it is known as a Sunlight Maggot."

### Information

* **Sunlight Maggot** cannot be upgraded.
* One of three light sources available. The other two being the Skull Lantern and the Cast Light spell.
* Will work in the Chasm of the Abyss, can be useful to light up the path in the Tomb of Giants, or to reveal hidden doors in Oolacile Towship.
* Can be traded to Snuggly for the Old Witch's Ring.

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Dropped by the red-eyed Chaos Bug found in the secret passageway that connects Demon Ruins to Lost Izalith.
* Can be obtained either by using the shortcut from Demon Ruins to Lost Izalith (requires "Chaos Servant +2"), or through exploration behind the Titanite Demon in Lost Izalith (does NOT require "Chaos Servant +2").
* It can only be received once each play-through, since the red-eyed bug will not respawn.
