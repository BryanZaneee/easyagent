**Mask of the Sealer** is a Helm in Dark Souls. This mask is part of the Crimson Set, worn by the sorcerers who flooded New Londo to seal away the Darkwraiths and the Four Kings. It symbolizes the burden of their actions and their connection to the sealed city. **Mask of the Sealer** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Mask of the sorcerers who flooded New Londo to seal away the Darkwraiths, and the Four Kings who descended into Dark. It symbolizes their resolve to keep the seal shut forever and their atonement for all who were sacrificed, but two of the three forsook New Londo upon tiring of their duty.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Crimson Set. Pieces include Mask of the Sealer, Crimson Robe, Crimson Gloves and Crimson Waistcloth.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Found on a Sealer's corpse in Blighttown/Quelaag's Domain, above the elevator down to the swamp, across a tree root.
