**Mask of the Child** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Enhances stamina regeneration by 10 points per second, from a base of 45 points per second. This is one of the three masks of Pinwheel, the necromancer who stole the power of the Gravelord and governs the Catacombs. The mask, symbolizing the innocent child, improves stamina recovery speed. **Mask of the Child** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "One of the three masks of the Pinwheel, the necromancer who stole the power of the Gravelord, and reigns over the Catacombs. This mask, belonging to the naive child, slightly raises stamina recovery speed."

### Information

* Cannot be upgraded
* Increases stamina regeneration by 10 points per second, with a base of 45 points per second.
* Stacks with Cloranthy Ring, Grass Crest Shield and Green Blossom or Power Within.

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Dropped by Pinwheel in The Catacombs
* Dropped by Pinwheel servants right before Nito.
* Sold by Patches the Hyena in Firelink Shrine for 8,000 Souls.
* Drops from Pinwheel Servant (2% Chance - Tomb of Giants)
