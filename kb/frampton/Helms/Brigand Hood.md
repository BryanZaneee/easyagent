**Brigand Hood** is a Helm in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Bandit Set.

### Description

> Worn by the brigands who raid mountain hamlets and attack travelers. In addition to protecting against the blazing sun, dust, and sand, it helps them tell friend from foe in the heat of battle.

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Bandit (Starting Class)
* Valley of Drakes
