**Golem Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Sold by Domhnall of Zena after the Iron Golem is defeated, this helm is part of the Iron Golem Set. It is the head of the Iron Golem, the guardian of the ancient Sen's Fortress and vanquisher of heroes on their way to Anor Londo. Without its core, it is merely a heavy piece of iron that offers solid protection but significantly impairs stamina recovery. **Golem Helm**protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

>  "Head of the Iron Golem, guardian of the ancient Sen's Fortress, slayer of heroes who ventured forth to Anor Londo. Without its core, it is a mere hunk of iron, and can be equipped as solid protective gear, but its immense weight hinders stamina recovery."

### Information

* Cannot be upgraded
* Part of the Iron Golem Set. Pieces include Golem Helm, Golem Armor, Golem Gauntlets, and Golem Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Domhnall of Zena after defeating Iron Golem
