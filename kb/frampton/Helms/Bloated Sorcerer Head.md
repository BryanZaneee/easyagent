**Bloated Sorcerer Head** is a Helm in Dark Souls. The head of an Oolacile resident, transformed by the dark influence of Manus, the Father of the Abyss. Its surface is fissured and lined with numerous tiny red eyes, accented by protruding brain matter. It carries a faint enchantment, indicating it may have once belonged to a sorcerer. **Bloated Sorcerer Head** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "The head of an Oolacile resident whose humanity went wild after being devoured by the Dark of Manus, Father of the Abyss. The bloated head is fissured, the cracks lined with innumerable tiny red eyeballs and accented by protuding brain parts. It is lightly enchanted, suggesting that it may have belonged to a sorcerer."

### Information

* Cannot be upgraded
* Compared to the Bloated Head, the "light enchantment" cause this to have slightly lower physical and bleed resistances and slightly higher ones at every other resitance (except fire, which is the same).

### How to get / Where to Find

* Drops from Oolacile Sorceress (1% Chance - Oolacile Township)
