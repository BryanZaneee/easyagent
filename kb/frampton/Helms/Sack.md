**Sack** is a Helm in Dark Souls. A bloodstained patchwork sack worn by the Undead man-eating cook found in the Depths. Two eyeholes are roughly cut out, making it surprisingly soft and comfortable, though it provides almost no defense. It is dropped by the Butcher in the Depths. **Sack** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Bloodstained patchwork sack by the Undead man-eating cook lurking in the Depths. Two eyeholes have been haphazardly cut out. It is unexpectedly soft and comfortable to wear, but it is probably near meaningless in terms of defense."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Can be traded to Snuggly for a Demon's Great Hammer.
* Can be fed to Frampt for 100 souls (twice the Frampt sale price of a Demon's Great Hammer).

### Where to Find \ Location

* Dropped by the Butcher found in Depths
