**Wanderer Hood** is a Helm in Dark Souls. The hood of a roving traveler, crafted from sturdy leather. It offers good protection against wind and rain, making it a practical choice for journeys through harsh weather. It is part of Wanderer Set. **Wanderer Hood** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped

> Hood of an aimless traveler. Made from sturdy leather, it offers protection versus wind and rain.

### Information in Dark Souks

* Part of the Wanderer Set. Pieces include Wanderer Hood, Wanderer Coat, Wanderer Manchette and Wanderer Boots.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Wanderer (Starting Class)
* Blighttown

### Notes & Tips

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
