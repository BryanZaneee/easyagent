**Iron Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls. It is part of Iron Set. Part of the Iron Set, this helm was worn by Solaire of Astora, Knight of Sunlight. It is of high quality but lacks any particular powers, reflecting Solaire's unyielding spirit and dedication without reliance on supernatural enhancements. **Iron Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped

> Helm of Solaire of Astora, Knight of Sunlight. Of high quality, but lacking any particular powers. Solaire's incredible prowess must have come from rigorous training alone, for his equipment exhibits no special traits.

### Description

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Iron Set. Pieces include Iron Helm, Armor of the Sun, Iron Bracelet and Iron Leggings.

* Drops from Solaire of Astora
