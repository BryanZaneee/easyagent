**Giant Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Worn by the giant sentinels of Anor Londo, this helm is made from ancient brass and also offers protection against non-physical attacks. Due to its gigantic size, it is incredibly heavy for humans and hinders stamina recovery. It is part of the Giant Armor Set. **Giant Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Helm of the giant sentinels of Anor Londo. Made from ancient brass, it also offers protection versus non-physical attacks, but due to its giant size, it is extremely heavy for humans, and impedes stamina recovery."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Giant Armor Set. Pieces include Giant Helm, Giant Armor, Giant Gauntlets and Giant Leggings.
