**Gold-Hemmed Black Hood** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Part of the Gold-Hemmed Black Set, this hood was worn by the witch Quelana of Izalith, the mother of pyromancy and Daughter of Chaos. She donned this gold-hemmed black hood before even the Age of Fire, providing strong resistance against fire, poison, and other dangers. **Gold-Hemmed Black Hood** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Worn by the witch Quelana of Izalith, mother of pyromancy and Daughter of Chaos.
> She wore this gold-hemmed black hood before even the Age of Fire, and it offers strong resistance versus fire, poison, and other perils.

### Information

* Cannot be reinforced.
* Part of the Gold-Hemmed Black Set. Pieces include Gold-Hemmed Black Hood, Gold-Hemmed Black Cloak, Gold-Hemmed Black Gloves and Gold-Hemmed Black Skirt.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Found with the rest of the Gold-Hemmed Black Set in Ceaseless Discharge's room. Picking up this set will turn him hostile and trigger the boss fight.
