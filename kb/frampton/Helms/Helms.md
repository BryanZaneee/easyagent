# Helms

**Helms** are a type of Armor in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered** that protects the player's head. Helms add their defense values to a total that is then used to calculate how much damage is taken.

* Most **Helms** can be upgraded, and you can visit the Maximum upgraded versions page for further information.
