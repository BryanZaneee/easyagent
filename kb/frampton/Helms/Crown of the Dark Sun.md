**Crown of the Dark Sun** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Part of the Gwyndolin Moonlight Set, this crown belongs to Dark Sun Gwyndolin, the protector of Anor Londo. It requires immeasurable faith from its wearer and is imbued with Darkmoon power, enhancing all magic by approximately 10%. **Crown of the Dark Sun** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped

> Crown of the Dark Sun Gwyndolin, protector of the forsaken city of Anor Londo. This crown of the gods demands faith immeasurable of its wearer, but it is imbued with Darkmoon power that enhances all magic. The image of the sun manifests Gwyndolin's deep adoration of the sun."

### Information

* Grants approx. 10% bonus to magic.
* Most commonly associated with Faith based players.
* Stacks with Bellowing Dragoncrest Ring
* Less magic boost than the Crown of Dusk, but does not weaken magic defense.
* Does not affect the damage of Weapon Buffs.
* The Crown of the Dark Sun cannot be reinforced.
* Part of the Gwyndolin Moonlight Set. Pieces include Crown of the Dark Sun, Moonlight Robe, Moonlight Gloves and Moonlight Waistcloth

### Where to Find \ Location

* Purchased from Domhnall of Zena for 10,000 souls (Must kill Gwyndolin in order to have them appear in his shop).
