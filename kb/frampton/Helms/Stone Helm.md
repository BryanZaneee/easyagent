**Stone Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls. This mossy helm belongs to the Stone Knight, who protects the Darkroot Garden. Created by ancient magic, this helm is imbued with enchantments but is extremely heavy. It is part of Stone Knight Set. **Stone Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Moss-covered helm of the Stone Knight, defender of the Darkroot Garden. The Stone Knight is a product of ancient magic, and although this helm is also imbued with magic, it is incredibly heavy."

### Information

* Cannot be upgraded
* Part of the Stone Knight Set. Pieces include Stone Helm, Stone Armor, Stone Gauntlets and Stone Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Darkroot Garden, on the cliff behind a stone structure where Alvina is.
