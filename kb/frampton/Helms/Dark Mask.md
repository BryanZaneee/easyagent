**Dark Mask** is a Helm in Dark Souls.Part of the Dark Set, this mask is associated with the Darkwraiths, once knights of New Londo who turned to darkness. The skeletal visage is rumored to be partially fused with its wearer’s skin. **Dark Mask** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped

> Mask of the Darkwraiths, former knights of New Londo who descended into Dark. Some say the skeletal mask of an ancient Darkwraith is partially fused with the flesh of its face.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Some believe them to be revolutionaries, defenders of the human race, but they may rather represent evil Humanity.
* Part of the Dark Set. Pieces include Dark Mask, Dark Armor, Dark Gauntlets and Dark Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Obtained by getting Rank 2 in the Darkwraith Covenant
