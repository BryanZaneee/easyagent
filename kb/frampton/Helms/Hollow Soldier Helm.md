**Hollow Soldier Helm** is a Helm in **Dark Souls**. As part of the Hollow Soldier Set, this helm, though old and battered, remains quite sturdy due to its iron construction, offering reliable defense in combat. **Hollow Soldier Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped

> Helm worn by Hollow soldiers. It may be old and battered, but its iron construction makes it quite sturdy. It is wise to wear a sturdy form of head protection against arrows and other physical threats."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Hollow Soldier Set. Pieces include Hollow Soldier Helm, Hollow Soldier Armor and Hollow Soldier Waistcloth,

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Drops from Undead Soldier (1% Chance - Undead Burg, Undead Parish, Northern Undead Asylum, Firelink Shrine)
