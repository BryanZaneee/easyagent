**Guardian Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls. This helm reduces stamina recovery by 1 per second and is part of the Guardian Set. It is worn by the stone knights, who serve as guardians of the forest sanctuary. These stone knights are magical golems, and their enchanted helms are notably heavy. It is part of Guardian Set. **Guardian Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Helm of the stone knights, guardians of the forest sanctuary. The stone knights are golems animated by magic, and their enchanted helms are oppressively heavy."

### Information

* Cannot be upgraded
* Reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second.
* Part of the Guardian Set. Pieces include Guardian Helm, Guardian Armor, Guardian Gauntlets and Guardian Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Royal Wood. On one of the corpses on the ground. (AotA)
