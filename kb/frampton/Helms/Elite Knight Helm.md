**Elite Knight Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Included in the Elite Knight Set, this helm was once worn by an unnamed knight, possibly from Astora. Despite his dedication to the Undead mission, he became Hollow after perishing in the Undead Asylum. **Elite Knight Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Helm of a nameless knight, perhaps an elite knight of Astora. Although he was loath to give up on his Undead mission, he perished at the Undead Asylum, and went Hollow.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab

### Where to Find \ Location

* After a fog wall in Darkroot Garden. Guarded by two Stone Knights
