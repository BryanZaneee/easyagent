**Bloated Head** is a Helm in Dark Souls. This is the head of an Oolacile resident whose humanity was warped by the Dark of Manus, the Father of the Abyss. The head is swollen, with fissures lined by countless tiny red eyes, and a hard exterior with a mucous-filled interior. Wearing it would drive any sane person to madness. **Bloated Head** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "The head of an Oolacile resident whose humanity went wild after being devoured by the Dark of Manus, Father of the Abyss.
>
> The bloated head is fissured, the cracks lined with innumerable tiny red eyeballs with a hard outside and mucous-filled inside, no sane person could ever wear it."

### Information

* Cannot be upgraded.

### How to get / Where to Find

* Dropped by Oolacile Citizens in Oolacile Township.
* Drops from Oolacile Resident (0.5% Chance - Oolacile Township and Chasm of the Abyss)

### Notes & Tips

* Cannot be upgraded
* While wearing any of the bloated heads you will receive more damage from headshots
