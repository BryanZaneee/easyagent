**Big Hat** is a Helm in Dark Souls. A gigantic hat that is part of Big Hat's Set, worn by the great sorcerer Logan. It is both a distinctive and iconic piece of headgear, reflecting Logan's prowess in sorcery. **Big Hat** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped

> "Gigantic hat worn by the great sorcerer Logan. It completely hid his face, which led to his nickname "Big Hat." Famously antisocial, Logan used it to block out noise and people's stares so he could focus on his own thoughts, but it does not possess any special magic powers."

### Description

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Big Hat's Set. Pieces include Big Hat, Sage Robe, Travelling Gloves and Travelling Boots.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Dropped from Logan after he turnes hostile.
