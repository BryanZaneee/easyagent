**Shadow Mask** is a Helm in Dark Souls. This mask is a component of the Shadow Set, used by stealth operatives from the East. Its lightweight design prioritizes mobility over defense, making it ideal for practitioners of unique martial arts. The mask offers resistance to bleeding and poison, reflecting the hazardous nature of its wearers' duties. **Shadow Mask** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Black cloth mask worn by spooks from an Eastern land. Designed so as not to hinder their unique form of martial arts. While it sacrifices defense for the sake of greater mobility, it does offer resistance to bleeding and poison among other things, perhaps due to the nature of their duty.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Shadow Set. Pieces include Shadow Mask, Shadow Garb, Shadow Gauntlets and Shadow Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Found in Blighttown past first group of Infested Ghouls
