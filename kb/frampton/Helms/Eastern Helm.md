**Eastern Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Part of the Eastern Set, this distinctive helm is made in an Eastern land. It is exquisitely crafted and offers excellent defense, particularly against slash attacks, which are prevalent threats in Eastern battles. **Eastern Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> A distinctive helm made in an Eastern land. Exquisitely crafted, this helm offers excellent defense, particularly versus slash attacks, which are one of the main threats in battles in the East.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Eastern Set. Pieces include Eastern Helm, Eastern Armor, Eastern Gauntlets and Eastern Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Darkroot Garden, past the door locked with Crest of Artorias.
* Hug the wall on the right until you reach a cliff with armor set.
