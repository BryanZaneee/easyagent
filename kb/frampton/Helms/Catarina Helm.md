**Catarina Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Known for its distinct shape, this helm is worn by the Knights of Catarina. Although often mocked for resembling an onion outside of Catarina, the expertly crafted curved design makes it excellent for parrying. It is part of the Catarina Set. **Catarina Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Distinctively shaped helm worn by the Knights of Catarina. Outside Catarina, it is often ridiculed for their onion-like shape, infuriating the proud knights, but the masterfully forged curved design makes it very effective for parrying."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
* Reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second.
* Part of the Catarina Set. Pieces include Catarina Helm, Catarina Armor, Catarina Gauntlets and Catarina Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Crestfallen Merchant for 10,000 souls
