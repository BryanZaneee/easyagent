**Knight Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls. **Knight Helm,** part of the Knight Set, this helm is worn by lower-rank knights. Crafted from thin metal, its grooved texture provides enhanced protection despite the lightweight design. It strikes a balance between defense and mobility, suitable for those new to knighthood. **Knight Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped

> Helm equipment of a lower rank knight. Despite the thin metal used, the grooved texture gives them added protection.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Knight Set. Pieces include Knight Helm, Knight Armor, Knight Gauntlets and Knight Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Knight (Starting Class)
* Darkroot Basin: shallow water near the Hydra
