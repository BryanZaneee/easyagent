**Dingy Hood** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Worn by the Fire Keeper at Firelink Shrine and part of the Dingy Set, this hood is believed to have once been the white hood of a maiden. However, over time, it has darkened from smoke and ashes from bonfires. **Dingy Hood**protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped

> Worn by the Fire Keeper at Firelink Shrine. It is thought to have once been the white hood of a maiden, but smoke and ashes from bonfires darkened it over the years.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Dingy Set. Parts include Dingy Hood, Dingy Robe, Dingy Gloves and Blood-Stained Skirt.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Part of a Lautrec questline
