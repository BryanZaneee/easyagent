**Thief Mask** is a Helm in Dark Souls. It is part of Thief Set. Worn by those who dwell in shadows, this mask is favored by sinners seeking to hide their identities. While it helps conceal faces and muffle voices, it provides minimal physical defense. **Thief Mask** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Mask of sinners. They hide their faces, stifle their voices and hide in the shadows. It does not provide much in the way of defense.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Thief Set. Pieces include Thief Mask, Black Leather Armor, Black Leather Gloves and Black Leather Boots.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Thief (Starting Class)
* Lower Undead Burg: in a room of one of the second set of hollow thieves.
