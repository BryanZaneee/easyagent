**Mask of Velka** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Part of the Black Set, this mask is worn by pardoners serving Velka, the Goddess of Sin. The pardoners listen to confessions, urging reflection and salvation. Their masks symbolize separation from worldly desires, reflecting their duty and dedication. **Mask of Velka** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Mask worn by pardoners serving Velka, the Goddess of Sin. The pardoners listen to the confessions of
> sinners, urging reflection and salvation. Their masks symbolize separation from worldly desires.

### Dark Soyls Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Black Set. Mask of Velka, Black Cleric Robe, Black Manchette and Black Tights.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Painted World of Ariamis, behind the locked annex door.
