**Helm of Thorns** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Worn by Kirk, the Knight of Thorns, this helm is part of the Thorns Set. Its surface is covered with dense thorns, a distinctive trait of this notorious Darkwraith. **Helm of Thorns** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Helm of Kirk, knight of Thorns and notorious member of the Darkwraiths. A dense patch of thorns grows from its surface. It is a fitting item for the murderous Kirk, for by simply wearing it and rolling, one can damage enemies.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Thorns Set. Pieces include Helm of Thorns, Armor of Thorns, Gauntlets of Thorns and Leggings of Thorns.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Same place where The Fair Lady is. To spawn the armor set you need to defeat Kirk, Knight of Thorns in 3 places: Depths, Demon Ruins, Lost Izalith
