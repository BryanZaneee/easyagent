**Gargoyle Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Dropped by Bell Gargoyles in Undead Parish and Anor Londo, this helm belongs to the gargoyles that guarded the Bell of Awakening in the belfry of the Undead Church. Made of bronze and intended primarily for appearance, it provides little actual defense, as the gargoyles’ skin is naturally hard. **Gargoyle Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Helm of the gargoyle, an enchanted creature that guarded the Bell of Awakening in the belfry of the Undead Church. This bronze helm was for mere appearance's sake, as gargoyle skin is naturally hard. Little in the way of defense can be expected from it."

### Information

* **Gargoyle Helm** can be upgraded by using Twinkling Titanite.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Dropped by Bell Gargoyles in Undead Parish and Anor Londo.
* Guaranteed to drop from the last slain  Bell Gargoyles in Anor Londo if not obtained beforehand.
