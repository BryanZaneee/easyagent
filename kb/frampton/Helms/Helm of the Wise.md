**Helm of the Wise** is a Helm in Dark Souls. This peculiar helm hails from the ancient land of Zena, the homeland of the enigmatic merchant Domhnall. Its distinctive two horns are thought to symbolize wisdom. It is part of Adventurer's Set. **Helm of the Wise** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped

> A strange helm from the ancient land of Zena, birthplace of the curious dealer Domhnall. The two horns are believed to symbolize wisdom."

### Information

* Appearance based on the armet (head piece) of a suit of armor presented to Henry VIII by Holy Roman Emperor Maximilian I in 1514. (See Adventurer's Set.
* Parts include Helm of the Wise, Armor of the Glorious, Gauntlets of the Vanquisher and Boots of the Explorer.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Domhnall of Zena for 12,000 souls.
