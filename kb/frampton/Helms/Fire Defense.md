**Fire Defense** is a Stat in Dark Souls. **Fire Defense** shows your defense power and damage negation against fire attacks. Stats determine the strengths and weaknesses of your character. Defensive Stats are determined by your Attributes and Equipment.

### Information

* Defense against fire attacks.
* Also see Resistance and Fire Damage.

### Fire Defense Equipment

Defense against Fire damage attacks. The following are equipments, items, or spells that increase Fire Resistance.

### **Armors**

* **Starting Armors**
  + Tattered Cloth Set
* **Light Armors**
  + Gold-Hemmed Black Set
  + Great Lord Set
  + Tattered Cloth Set
* **Medium Armors**
  + Black Knight Set
  + Ornstein's Set
  + Artorias' Set
  + Gough's Set

### **Shields**

* Dragon Crest Shield (85% bonus resistance)
* Black Knight Shield (95% bonus resistance)

### **Rings & Spells**

* **Rings**
  + Flame Stoneplate Ring
  + Speckled Stoneplate Ring
  + Orange Charred Ring (Greatly reduces lava damage)
* **Spells**
  + Flash Sweat (Increases flame defense)
