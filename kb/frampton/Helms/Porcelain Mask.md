**Porcelain Mask** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Part of the Lord's Blade Set, this mask belonged to Lord's Blade Ciaran, one of Gwyn's Four Knights. The mask is lined with ivory locks of hair, adding a personal touch to the otherwise impersonal armor of this legendary warrior. **Porcelain Mask** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped

> Mask of the Lord's Blade Ciaran, one of Gwyn's Four Knights. The cyclops headpiece is common to all of the Lord's Blades, but Ciaran was determined to earn this soft porcelain mask as a unique decoration of honor. The mask is lined with ivory locks of hair.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Lord's Blade Set. Pieces include Porcelain Mask, Lord's Blade Robe, Lord's Blade Gloves and Lord's Blade Waistcloth.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Killing Lord's Blade Ciaran, she appears in the same area where you fight Artorias The Abysswalker
