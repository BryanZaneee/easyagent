**Witch Hat** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Available after defeating The Four Kings and part of the Witch Set, this hat belonged to the Rogue Witch Beatrice. Beatrice is one of the few magic users who did not study at the Dragon School, making her an exception among sorcerers. **Witch Hat** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Hat of the Rogue Witch Beatrice.
> Almost all magic users that employ catalysts studied at Dragon School, but Beatrice is one of the few exceptions. She braved the Abyss but did not live to tell of her ordeal.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Witch Set. Pieces include Witch Hat, Witch Cloak, Witch Gloves and Witch Skirt.

### Where to Find \ Location

* At Valley of Drakes area along with Beatrice's Catalyst. Go where the cave entrance to Blighttown is; the corpse is located at a ledge on at the opposite side of the cave entrance.
* Available after defeating The Four Kings
