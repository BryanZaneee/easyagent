**Crystalline Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Part of the Crystalline Set, this helm belonged to a Hollow knight partially turned to crystal. Although the crystal enhances defense, it is fragile and cannot be repaired. **Crystalline Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Helm worn by a Hollowed knight, who was partially crystallized. The crystal power provides powerful defense, but it cannot be used for long because it is easily broken and cannot be repaired."

### Information

* Cannot be upgraded
* Has the highest repair cost (100 souls per durability point)
* Part of the Crystalline Set. Pieces include Crystalline Helm, Crystalline Armor, Crystalline Gauntlets and Crystalline Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* The Duke's Archives. Outside in the area with Crystal Golems.
