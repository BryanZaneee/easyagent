**Smough's Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls. The helm of Smough, the Executioner, who guards the cathedral in the abandoned city of Anor Londo. It provides exceptionally high defense and can be worn by humans, though doing so is quite challenging. Part of Smough's Set. **Smough's Helm**protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Helm of Smough, the Executioner, protector of the cathedral at the forsaken city of Anor Londo. It offers extremely high defense and can be worn by humans, but not without great difficulty."

### Information

* Cannot be upgraded
* Part of the Smough's Set. Pieces include Smough's Helm, Smough's Armor, Smough's Gauntlets and Smough's Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Domhnall of Zena. You need to defeat Smough second in order to make him sell this armor.
