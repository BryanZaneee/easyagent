**Painting Guardian Hood** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Part of the Painting Guardian Set, this hood is worn by the alabaster-clad guardians tasked with protecting the paintings in Anor Londo. It offers substantial protection against magic, suitable for defending against magical threats. **Painting Guardian Hood**protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped

> Hood worn by the alabaster-clothed guardians of the paintings in Anor Londo. Offer substantial protection versus magic. They have guarded the Great Painting of Ariamis for ages, passing their duty down through generations, but the reason for doing so passed from all memory long ago.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Painting Guardians are connected to Filianore of DS3 and also to Dragons.
* Part of the Painting Guardian Set. Pieces include Painting Guardian Hood, Painting Guardian Robe, Painting Guardian Gloves and Painting Guardian Waistcloth.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Painted World of Ariamis
