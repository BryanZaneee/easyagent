**Brass Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls. This helm is part of the Brass Set and worn by the Darkmoon Knightess, the Fire Keeper of Anor Londo. **Brass Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Helm of the Darkmoon Knightess, Fire Keeper of Anor Londo. After becoming Undead, she visited the Dark Sun Gwyndolin at the Mausoleum of the Spiral Depths, became a Blade of Darkmoon, and assumed the flame-keeping duty. She received this helm, which hides her hideous form and helps her hunt the guilty."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Brass Set. Pieces include Brass Helm, Brass Armor, Brass Gauntlets and Brass Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Darkmoon Tomb, after defeating Dark Sun Gwyndolin
