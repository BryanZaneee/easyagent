**Cleric Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Part of the Cleric Set, this helm is sold by Patches the Hyena and worn by warrior clerics of the Way of White. Known for their resilience in battle, these clerics favor the helm's substantial size and weight, which enhances their imposing presence on the battlefield. **Cleric Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Helm worn by the warrior clerics of the Way of White, who are famous for being unyielding in battle. It is rather substantial in size and weight, making its wearer look even bigger than he actually is.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Cleric Set. Pieces include Cleric Helm, Cleric Armor, Cleric Gauntlets and Cleric Leggings.
