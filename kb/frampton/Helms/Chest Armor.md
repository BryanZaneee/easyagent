# Chest Armor

**Chest Armor** are a type of Armor in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered** that protects the player's chest. Chest Armors add their defense values to a total that is then used to calculate how much damage is taken.

* Most **Chest Armor** can be upgraded, and you can visit the Maximum upgraded versions page for further information.
