**Ornstein's Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls. This helm is part of Ornstein's Set, worn by the Dragonslayer Ornstein, guardian of the cathedral in Anor Londo. It decreases stamina recovery by 1 per second. **Ornstein's Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Helm of the dragonslayer Ornstein, who guards the cathedral in the forsaken city of Anor Londo. Ornstein is believed to be the captain of the Four Knights. His golden lion helm is imbued with the power of lightning and should provide good protection against it."

### Information

* Cannot be upgraded
* Can be fed to Frampt for 100 souls.
* Reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second.
* Part of the Ornstein's Set. Pieces include Ornstein's Helm, Ornstein's Armor, Ornstein's Gauntlets and Ornstein's Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Domhnall of Zena for 7,000 souls.
* You need to defeat Dragon Slayer Ornstein second.

### Notes & Tips

* Ornstein was named after the composer "Leo Ornstein".
* Ornstein reappeared as The Old Dragonslayer in DS2.
* The armor set of Ornstein was later rediscovered at Archdragon Peak (DS3).
