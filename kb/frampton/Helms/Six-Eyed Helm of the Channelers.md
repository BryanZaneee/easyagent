**Six-Eyed Helm of the Channelers** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Part of the Channeler Set, this helm is worn by the Channelers who serve Seath the Scaleless. The helm's six eyes, arranged in vertical columns, compensate for Seath's blindness and enhance the Channelers' perception. This helm is the heaviest sorcerer gear, imbued with magic for additional protection. **Six-Eyed Helm of the Channelers** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped

### Where to Find \ Location Six-Eyed Helm

* In a chest off in one of the side rooms of the Duke's Archives, near the stairs leading down to the courtyard that leads to the Crystal Cave.

### Six-Eyed Helm Notes & Tips

* Can't be upgraded
* Part of the Channeler Set. Pieces include Six-Eyed Helm of the Channelers, Robe of the Channelers, Gauntlets of the Channelers and Waistcloth of the Channelers.
