**Crown of the Great Lord** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Part of the Great Lord Set, this crown belonged to Gwyn, Lord of Cinder, who linked the First Flame. It cannot be upgraded. **Crown of the Great Lord** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Crown of Gwyn, Lord of Cinder, who linked the First Flame. Lord Gwyn, bearer of the ultimate soul, divided that power among his great clan before linking the flame. But he did keep his crown, perhaps to preserve a symbol of the monarch, for its actual power had fully subsided.

### Information

* Part of the Great Lord Set. Pieces include Crown of the Great Lord, Robe of the Great Lord, Bracelet of the Great Lord and Anklet of the Great Lord.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Domhnall of Zena for 20,000 souls after proceeding to NG+

### Notes & Tips

* Cannot be upgraded
