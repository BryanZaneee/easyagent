**Hollow Thief's Hood** is a Helm in **Dark Souls**.It is part of Hollow Thief Set. Part of the Hollow Thief Set, this hood is worn by Hollow thieves to conceal their guilty expressions. It serves as a symbol of their deceitful nature and criminal activities. **Hollow Thief's Hood** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Hood worn by Hollow thieves. Used to hide a guilty countenance.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab.
* Part of Hollow Thief Set.

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Drops from Undead Assassin also known as Hollow Thief (1% chance -  Lower Undead Burg)
