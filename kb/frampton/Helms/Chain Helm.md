**Chain Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls**.** This helm is part of the Chain Set, made of thin interlinking rings of steel. It is common throughout the human world due to its easy production, moderate weight, and good defensive properties. **Chain Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "A chainmail helm of thin interlinking rings of steel. It is common throughout the human world since it can be easily produced, it is not too heavy, and offers good defense. Knights may favor flashy armor, but for warriors on the battlefield, anything is fine as long as it keeps them alive."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Chain Set. Pieces include Chain Helm, Chain Armor, Leather Gauntlets, Chain Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location Xanthous Crown

* Sold by Undead Merchant (Male) for 500 souls
* Drops from Chained Prisoner (20% chance - Oolacile Township)

### Xanthous Crown Notes & Tips

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
