**Gough's Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Part of Gough's Set, this helm reduces stamina recovery by 1 per second. It is the helm of Hawkeye Gough, one of Gwyn's Four Knights, given as a token of knightly distinction. Crafted specifically for Gough, the eye holes were filled with tree resin by those who regarded him as a mere brute. **Gough's Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped

> "Helm of Hawkeye Gough, one of Gwyn's Four Knights. Received as a decoration of knightly honor. A helm crafted especially for the honorable Hawkeye Gough, only the eye holes were packed with tree resin by those who dismissed Gough as a brutish giant."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
* Reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second.
* Part of the Gough's Set. Pieces include Gough's Helm, Gough's Chest, Gough's Gloves and Gough's Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Dropped by Hawkeye Gough
