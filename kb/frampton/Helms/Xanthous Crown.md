**Xanthous Crown** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Part of the Xanthous Set, this mysterious item was once worn by the Xanthous King Jeremiah, the legendary exile. Its origins are unknown, adding to its enigmatic nature. **Xanthous Crown** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped

> *A mysterious item once worn by the Xanthous King Jeremiah, the legendary exile. No one knows where it came from. The crown bears high-quality cloth which is quite soft to the touch, but its bright yellow color stings the eyes, and it is clearly far too big."*

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite.
* Part of the Xanthous Set. Pieces include Xanthous Crown, Xanthous Overcoat, Xanthous Gloves and Xanthous Waistcloth.
* Can be traded to Snuggly for a Ring of Favor and Protection if not upgraded.

### Where to Find \ Location

* On a corpse behind Crossbreed Priscilla after defeating Dark Spirit Xanthous King, Jeremiah.

### Notes & Tips

* The crown is a reference to the Old Monk from Demon's Souls.
