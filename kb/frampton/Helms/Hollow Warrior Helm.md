**Hollow Warrior Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls. This helm is part of the Hollow Warrior Set, worn by Hollow warriors. Despite being made of iron and showing signs of age and wear, it still provides a wise level of protection against arrows and other physical threats. **Hollow Warrior Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Helm worn by Hollow warriors. Although it is made of iron, it is old, battered and worn out. It is wise to wear a sturdy form of head protection against arrows and other physical threats.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Hollow Warrior Set. Pieces include Hollow Warrior Helm, Hollow Warrior Armor and Hollow Warrior Waistcloth.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Dropped by Armored Zombies
