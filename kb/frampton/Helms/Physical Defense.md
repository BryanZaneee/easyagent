**Physical Defense** is a Stat in Dark Souls. **Physical Defense** shows your defense power and damage negation against standard physical attacks. Stats determine the strengths and weaknesses of your character. Defensive Stats are determined by Attributes and Equipment.

### Information

Defense that lowers the damage taken against physical attacks damage types.

### Strike Defense

Defense against strike damage such as weapons that has strike damage type. e.g hammers and shields. The following are equipments, items, or spells that increase Strike Resistance.
