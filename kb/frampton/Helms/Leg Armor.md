# Leg Armor

**Leggings** are a type of Armor in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered** that protects the player's feet and legs. Leggings add their defense values to a total that is then used to calculate how much damage is taken.

* Most **Leggings** can be upgraded, and you can visit the Maximum upgraded versions page for further information.
