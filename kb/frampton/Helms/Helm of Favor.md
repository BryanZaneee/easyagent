**Helm of Favor** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Part of the Favor Set, this helm is worn by Lautrec the Embraced and symbolizes the love of the goddess Fina. During his isolation, Lautrec abandoned all else, trusting in the goddess's affection for him. **Helm of Favor** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped

> "Helm of Lautrec the Embraced, representing the goddess Fina's love. During his solitude, he forsook everything, for he believed in the goddess's love for him."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Favor Set. Pieces include Helm of Favor, Embraced Armor of Favor, Gauntlets of Favor and Leggings of Favor.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Found in Anor Londo, on second floor of Ornstein and Smough boss area, after invading and defeating Lautrec.
