**Curse Resist** is a Stat in Dark Souls. **Curse Resistance** indicates your defensive strength and damage reduction against cursed attacks. Defensive Stats are determined by your Attributes and Equipment.

### Information in.

* Defense against physical attacks.
* Also see Resistance and Curse.

### Curse Defense Equipment

Defense against Curse. The following are equipments, items, or spells that increase Curse Resistance.

### **Armors**

* **Starter Armors:**Most starter armors offer very little curse resistance to none.
* **Light Armors:**Antiquated Set and Dingy Set
* **Medium Armors:**Paladin Set
* **Heavy armor sets:**Havel's Set

### **Shield**

* Bloodshield

### **Ring**

* Cursebite Ring
