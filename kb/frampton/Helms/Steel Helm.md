**Steel Helm** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Part of the Steel Set and dropped by The Berenike Knight, this helm belongs to the Knights of Berenike, famed for their heavy weaponry and armor. **Steel Helm** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Helm of the Knights of Berenike, known for their heavy armaments and armor. Countless Knights of Berenike, once extolled as the mightiest of mighty, became Undead and ventured to Lordran. But their journey was for naught, as they went Hollow, and became a threat to all Undead."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards (x6), Large Titanite Shards (x6), Titanite Chunks (x6) and Titanite Slab (x1)
* Part of the Steel Set. Pieces include Steel Helm, Steel Armor, Steel Gauntlets and Steel Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Dropped by The Berenike Knight
* Sold by Crestfallen Merchant for 5,000 souls
