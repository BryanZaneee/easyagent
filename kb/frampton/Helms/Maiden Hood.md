**Maiden Hood** is a Helm in Dark Souls. Part of the Maiden Set, this white hood is worn by traveling maidens as part of their formal attire, irrespective of their rank. Crafted to be soft and well-made, it offers minimal defense and is not suitable for battle. **Maiden Hood** protect the player's head by applying various defensive properties, it also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> White hood worn by traveling maidens. It is part of their formal attire, regardless of rank. It is soft and well-made, but does not offer much in the way of defense, making it ill-suited to use in battle.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Maiden Set. Pieces include Maiden Hood, Maiden Robe, Maiden Gloves and Maiden Skirt.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Duke's Archives. In one of the cells.
