### Build Strategy

Pros
- High vitality
- Above 53 poise
- High dexterity
- Can cause massive damage

Cons
- Very Risky in PvE
- Health drains

Always use power within with the base pyromancy flame or your health will drain faster as the ascended flame increases health drain with no damage increase. When your opponent runs away or dodges the whole fight just try to do the running attack or roll attack as it catches some off guard. When the red tearstone activates you should be able to kill anyone in 3 or less hits.
