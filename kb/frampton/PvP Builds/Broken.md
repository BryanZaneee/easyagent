### Build Strategy

Pros
- Quick and has good range
- Massive damage (OHKO backstab on most standard pvp builds)
- This build is pretty much unblockable (Can't block backstabs, or ripostes, or counters! Be sure to abuse all of them!)
Cons
- Requires good timing and reflexes (Susceptible to lag when playing online)
- Must use stamina wisely.
