**Pros**
- Good vitality
- Wide variety of miracle spells
- Can switch Ring of the Sun's Firstborn with a Hornet Ring for riposte and back back stabs (good damage with Sunlight Blade though you have to risk close range)
- Co-op Oriented play
**Cons**
- Low stamina and equipment burden due to low endurance
- Not so great defense and only has wolf ring poise due to cos-play armor Set
