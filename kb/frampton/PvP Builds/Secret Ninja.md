### Build Strategy

The Secret Ninja uses only true Ninja tactics like in Japan ( Nihon ). The Secret Ninja relies only on ripostes and back stabs, buts sometimes you can use the bleed of the Priscilla's Dagger or the Chaos Blade. P.S. The Priscilla's dagger bleed is recommended due to it's 500 Bleed Aux. Use the Dark wood Grain Ring to easily dodge attacks and in PvP, the Dark wood Grain Ring might glitch and cause some attacks to deal zero damage to you :D.
