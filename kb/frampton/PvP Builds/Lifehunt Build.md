### Build Strategy

Pros
-High vitality
-High endurance
-Great against turtles
-Great bleed resistance
-Above 53 poise
Cons
- If battle lasts too long could bleed out.

This build is all about bleeding out your opponent, so a good strategy is if your enemy is hiding behind his shield just hit him at least 3 to 4 times then go at him with the dagger for a large amount of bleed out damage. If you fight someone with the bloodshield use your pyromancy.
