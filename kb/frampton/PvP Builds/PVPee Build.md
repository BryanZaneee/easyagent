### Build Equipment

I am not going to list the equipment I used but just remember if going for light armor use Mask of the Father for the extra equip burden and also The wolf ring for the added poise (PVP) or The ring of steel protection (PVE). With Medium armor I use Havel Ring while with Heavy armor I use both Havel ring, Darkwood Grain Ring and Mask of the Father.
Normally I have a weapon of each type i.e a divine, occult, lightning, magic etc just for all situations. Please note that divine, occult, magic and enchanted weapons are more efficient in the 1st build. Always keep a mixture of weapons depending on who you are facing. If you are facing a fast hitting char do not use a heavy weapon for if you miss you are open to backstabs. Heavy weapons are efficient against light armored characters normally casters.

*Explanation*
Basically you can use all magic except Dragon Breadth and most miracles including Wrath of the God. 19 attunment allows 5 spell slots
Strength and Dexterity are enough to wield some powerful weapons including The Greatsword of Arthorias, Titanite Catchpole and Zwiehander
40 Endurance is a must. remaining points went into Vitality.
**2nd build:**
This build is designed to use all weapons available with pyromancy. Suggested class: Wanderer, Pyromancer or Sorcerer (only if going for the 15 in int), gift what ever you fancy
