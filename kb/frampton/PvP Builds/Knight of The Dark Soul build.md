### Build Strategy

Pros
-Decent Vitailty
-Great Endurance
-Great Strength
-Great Dexterity
- Ability to wear medium armor and carry heavy weapons
- Well Rounded in Dark Magic, Pyromancy, Miracles, and some Soceries
- Can do decent damage with Faith required weapons (ie Moonlight Sword, Artorias' reg. Greatsword)
- Great Poise and Endurance to tank major hits without staggering in medium armor

Cons
- Not the highest Vitality=Anyone with very high above 500 damage could really bring you down
-Might take time to make it right for you
- Adjust Accordingly, be mindful of what changes you make as leveling up gets harder
-Somewhat weak resistance to Poison, Toxic, Magic, and Lightning (Not counting Artorias' Greatshield)
-Really heavy and cumbersome if not using the right rings or items
