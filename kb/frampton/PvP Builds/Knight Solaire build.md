### Build Strategy

This is a role-playing build modeled after everyone's favorite sunbro, Knight Solaire. This build is intended for co-op and PvP, it also works great for PvE.

**Miracles (PvP):**

Wrath of the Gods x2, Great Magic Barrier, Sunlight Blade. Other options: Vow of Silence, Tranquil Walk of Peace.

**Miracles (co-op):**

Sunlight Spear, Great Lightning Spear, Heal. Spear miracles work great with Ring of the Sun's Firstborn and Crown of the Dark Sun in order to make short work of most bosses in the game.

**Covenant:**

Warrior of Sunlight (duh!)

**Strategy:**

This build is great for some jolly co-operation and PvP duels. For co-op make sure to use the spear miracles, they work great for taking down bosses and dangerous enemies. For PvP remember to cast Great Magic Barrier against casters, keep backstabbers away with Divine Claymore's R1 swings. When you get some separation, make use of your Divine Avelyn, it does great damage due to Faith scaling. If you're using Sunlight Straight Sword remember to utilize the Sunlight Blade buff. Most importantly, make good use of your Wrath of the Gods miracle, it does insane damage in conjunction with Ring of the Sun's Firstborn and Crown of the Dark Sun (make sure you're facing away from your opponent when you use it because it seems to go through shields). If you like, take 3 points off vitality and put it into attunement for another miracle slot.
