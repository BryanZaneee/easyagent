### Build Strategy

Pros
Carefully chosen equipment and spells lend a Dark Lord aesthetic.
Dark Bead can waylay unsuspecting opponents and threaten those who would otherwise attack.
Abyss Greatsword possesses encompassing reach.

Cons
Equipment is not absolutely top-tier nor optimized solely for winning.
Low poise is easily broken by greatswords and larger weapons.
A relatively inefficient parameter spread is required to use the Abyss Greatsword.
