### Build Strategy

This build utilizes the Chaos paired with the Red Tearstone ring, similar to the way Peeve Peeverson does it. The armor gives 44 poise (enough to avoid stunlocks from other Katanas) and decent defenses. With these stats you have an incredible number of weapons available, however I found the ones listed below to be the best.
Pros:
High Damage.
Best Katana in terms of bleed.
An incredible ability to turn fights around.
Cons:
Requires low health to reach maximum damage.
