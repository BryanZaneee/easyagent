### Build Strategy

This is my first build that utilizes the Abyss Greatsword. i based this build around the DLC weapons and armor, so having the Atorias of the Abyss dlc is NECESSARY!
The only reason that the Gold Tracer is used as a secondary is to level the playing field if you're fighting someone doing something OP, like spamming WoG, or TWOP.
Keep in mind that the Abyss Greatsword scales with humanity up to a total of 10, s IMG-20130816-00271.jpgo for the maximum damage have 10 humanity.

Pros
- High poise
- High defenses
- The Abyss Greatsword has a g reat moveset if used right
- Good Vitality

Cons
- Not as much damage compared to other greatswords
- Takes time to get accurate with 2-handed strong attack
- Takes time to get the equipment
- Lower Stamina
