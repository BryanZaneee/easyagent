### Build Strategy

You are basically equipped with some of the best armour and can at least do a medium roll.
You have the ability to string together large combos from your oversized sword which is either enhanced by lightning or your faith.
You can take large combos using your decent stamina pool or simply roll away
.You have the ability to cast a varied repertoire of miracles and pyromancy.
You can maneuver well enough to perform backstabs and ripostes.
Your natural poise will prevent you from being sent flying from every swing of large sword.Your Faith has enabled you with a innate ability to resist magic, this plus great magic barrier makes your class potentially a mage's deadliest bane. An Inquisitor.
.Your Vitality and armour are ideal, enabling you to take most blows without fearing death at least instantly.
As there will be times where you will want to use a ring other than Havel's, it would be best to have a lighter armour set, the paladin coupled with the golden hemmed set should work wonders.
Covenants should either be warrior of sunlight(co-op galore and sunlight spear), way of white( safe pve) or Darkmoon blade(to smite sinners, pvp). Inquisitors are lawful.
Your playstyle should be formed only after taking note of the above points, also this build can work wonders for pve as well.
