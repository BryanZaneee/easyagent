### **Pros:**

* Very high HP (1828 HP)
* High damage output with Pyromancy spam.
* Excellent stamina and stamina regeneration.
* Excellent defense: Good reach, very high defenses, resists, poise and ninja flipping.
* Highly versatile. Flexible movesets + reach + Heater Shield parrying + Composite Bow + 8 attunement slots + TWoP and Pyromancy allows you to handle a wide variety of threats.
* Great at crowd control.
* 45 Dex gives you the fastest cast time possible.

### **Cons:**

* Great Scythes/Fire Tempest require caution when in use due to high recovery or start up times.
* Damage output heavily diminished after Pyromancy is exhausted.

**Basic Playstyle:**

* Prebuff -> Cast Tranquil Walk of Peace -> Ambush/Engage Enemy -> Fire Tempest -> Finish with dead angled Great Combustion/Black Flame.
* High poise and HP allow you to poise through almost any hit to deliver a devastating Fire Tempest, so don't be afraid to bait a hit in order to get off a Tempest.
* Exploit high poise to backstab/Great Combustion/Great Fireball through enemy attacks.
* When spells are depleted, attack with Gold or Rotten Pine Resin buffed +15 weapons.
* Remember to prep/buff with Green Blossoms.
* Crown of Dusk/Dark Sun and/or Bellowing Dragoncrest Ring especially recommended when ambushing with Fire Tempest.
* If you're willing to prepare heal spells, chug estus, burn Humanity, or take a chance, prepare and use Power Within for devastating melee attacks/spells. **Remember to use an unupgraded Pyromancy Flame and Lingering Dragoncrest Ring to cast Power Within in order to minimize the health drain!**

**Note:** Sinking 2 points from Vitality and/or Endurance into Strength is recommended if you want to use the Avelyn crossbow.
