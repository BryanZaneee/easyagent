### Build Equipment

* **Right Hand**: Channeler's Trident, Velka's Rapier OR Moonlight Goodsword
* **Left Hand**: Tin Crystalization Catalyst, Grass Crest Shield OR Crest Shield OR Dragon Crest Shield
* **Head**: Crown of the Dark Sun
* **Chest**: Moonlight Robe
* **Hands**: Giant's Gauntlets +5
* **Legs**: Wasitcloth of the Channeler's
* **Rings**: Bellowing Dragoncrest Ring
* **Spells**: Crystal Soul Spear x2. Crystal Homing Soulmass x2. White Dragon Breath x2. Chameleon x1.

### Build Strategy

Pros
- Versatility
- Massive damage with spells; can also attack while blocking for decent damage.
- Very fun to play.
- Constant barrage of spells such as Crystal Soul Spear and Crystal Homing Soulmass combined with long-range attack-while-blocking weapons really keeps your opponents on their feet.
Cons
- Standard health; low armor; low poise.
