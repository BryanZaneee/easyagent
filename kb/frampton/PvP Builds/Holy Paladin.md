This is a player-created **Build** for Dark Souls. If you want help making your own build, you can consult this useful page on how to make a build by Blaine.

A melee focused Faith build that intends to mix in miracles to turn the fight in your favor. If you don't intend to use any heavy weapons feel free to bring the strength down to 30 and boost the faith to 50. Use any strength/dexterity weapon you would like. Try some divine weapons too.

* Build Name: Holy Paladin
* Build Level: 160
* Build Focus: PvP
* Build Main Stats:  Faith
* Starting Class: Bandit

### Build Strategy

Tips:
Don't be a\*\*\*\*\*with WOTG and TWOP. Try timing your attacks and using the balder swords spectacular 1/2 handed R2 attacks. Once you encounter the invader/host put on whatever miracles you feel you need to compete against the enemy. Throw in a WOTG when you think the enemy least expects it. IF you feel the need to rush the fight and be a little cheap, knock your opponent down with WOTG and backstab him when he stands again.
Note:
I used Dark Souls Mega Mule for this character. I have played through the story so many times it isn't even funny. DSMM is easy to install and very helpful. Look it up on youtube.
