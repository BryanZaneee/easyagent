### Build Strategy

* This build takes some time to get all of the right equipment. Specifically the Gargoyle's Halberd and Chaos Ember. Also, farming for the red titanite isn't fun but the build is worth it.
* Farming for the Gargoyle Halberd is best done by killing one of the bell gargoyle bosses, pick up the item he drops (if any) and pray. If it does not drop, quit the game via the start menu and reload until he drops it. Either gargoyle can drop it and neither of them have a better chance than the other to drop it. Also, you do not have to cut off the tail to receive the halberd.
* At some point, you must join the Daughter's of Chaos covenant to earn the better pyromancies and you will need to sacrifice 30 humanity to receive the Chaos Firestorm Pyro.
* It is easiest to farm the red titanite chunks once the shortcut to Lost Izalith has been opened and this is done by sacrificing the 30 humanity to the Daughter of Chaos.
* The pyromancies can be changed to fit the way you like to play. The Firestorm spell isn't as powerful as it was in Demon's Souls and therefore could be sacrificed to open up two more slots. The more Great Combustion you have, the better so load up on consecutive playthroughs.
* Acid Surge works miracles against Katana (Dex) builds however timing is crucial; you only get one shot.
* Using the Toxic Mist or Acid Surge is best done after a successful backstab. Using Toxic Mist immediately after the backstab almost guarantees they will be poisoned.
* If you are facing a dagger build, don't go easy on the Great Combustion when they get close. The halberd is decently slow to attack and opens you up for the backstab. Pyromancies, specifically Great Combustion, are the keys to beating them.
* Two handing the halberd gives you, with obvious attack bonus, a faster attack. With such high defense, a few hits here and there are not going to kill you.
  + In fact, if you prefer to two-hand the halberd, using the Grass Crest Shield in your off hand will up your stamina regeneration rate.
  + You could also put the Pyromancy Flame in the left hand allowing you to use either attack form, very quickly, back to back.
* For the pyromancies, feel free to change these up depending on how you play.
  + With 10 attunement slots, organization is the only problem. Being able to switch to the spell you need while fighting takes practice so sometimes doubling or tripling one or two of your spells can condense them and make them more manageable.
