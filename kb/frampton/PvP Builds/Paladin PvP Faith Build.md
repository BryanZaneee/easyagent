This is a player-created **Build** for Dark Souls. If you want help making your own build, you can consult this useful page on how to make a build by Blaine.

This build is aimed for PvP in either the Kiln of the First Flame or in Anor Londo. It is hugely versatile, and can use a wide range of weapons without sacraficing its effectivness. It uses IMO the best armor in the game, the Giant Set, yet can still ninja flip with the Dark Wood Grain Ring. It also uses the Avelyn to great effect, punishing casters and tanks while forcing more mobile enemies to close in on you, where you can use AoE attacks or your weapons.
