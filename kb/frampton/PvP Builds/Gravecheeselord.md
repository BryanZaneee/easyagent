### Build Strategy

Pros
- Moderate-high vitality and endurance
- Ability to use a huge array of spells, flexibility as to what spells you can attune
- High damaging spells
- Great cheese potential, kill the enemy while they spawn
- Mobile with 47 poise

Cons
- Min-maxed, so you won't be able to vary your armor too much and still fast roll
- No shield, you can trade a shield for the pyromancy flame, but you lose fast-roll
