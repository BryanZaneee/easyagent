### Build Strategy

Hey guys, I'd like to start off by saying I've played every imaginable build in this game, and this is by far the worst; the reason I've made this build is because this game simply isn't challenging enough as it is. Daggers are one of the worst weapons in the game, reason being they are outclasses by rapiers in every way - The Rapier +15 does more damage (in general as well as critical hits), has more range, and you can even attack while behind a shield! What was From thinking? I won't pretend to know, but what I do know is that the only reason you would choose a dagger in this game is either for critical hits or for weapon enchanting; ofcourse, if you're enchanting your weapon, why would you use an off-hand weapon? You wouldn't - since your enchanted main-hand weapon will do so much more damage than your off-hand, your simply giving up your ability to block for no benefit. That said, the only reason you would choose this build is because you're either terrible at this game, or you're godlike at this game - There is no in-between.
