**Thank You Carving** is a Carving in **Dark Souls**. Carvings are a useful tool to express yourself to other players, some including a vocal performance or making sound.

> Head carved of archtrees by gough in his imprisonment. Gough imparts an emotion to each and every completed carving, which helps him achieve personal enlightenment. When a head is disturbed, it speaks, reflecting the emotion conferred to it.
> This head says "Thank you". Have another look. Is this not the face of gratitude?

### Usage

* Says "Thank You" when it makes contact with a hard surface.

### Locations

* Buy from Hawkeye Gough for 5 souls
