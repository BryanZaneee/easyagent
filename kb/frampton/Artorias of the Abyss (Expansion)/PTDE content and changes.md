## Weapons

* Eagle Shield - physical damage reduction to 95%
* Elemental weapons have lower AR
* Greatswords and Curved Greatswords attack slower
* Blocking drains more stamina
* Less stability increase in greatshields when upgrading
* Quelaag's Furysword - decreased fire damage -Stats are still the same 90/255.
* Strength weapons receive some hyper armor. - Only Abyss Greatsword confirmed. [need more info]
* Some shield types have deflection when used in PvP [need more info]
* Stamina consumption increased for spear/rapier attacks from behind a shield.
* Black Knight Greataxe has reduced damage [needs confirmation]



## Equipment

* Dark Wood Grain Ring - flip now applies when equip load is at or below 25%
* Hornet Ring - nerfed to 30% critical bonus
* Symbol of Avarice - slower HP degeneration [needs confirmation] -Still depletes 5 hp per tick.
* Elite Knight set - total poise increased to 46
* Mask of the Father - nerfed to 5% equip load increase
* Mask of the Mother - nerfed to 10% HP increase
* Mask of the Child - decreased stamina regeneration [need more info]
* Gargoyle Helm - increased drop-rate from Bell Gargoyle

## Spells

* HCSM/HSM now do less damage [needs confirmation]
* Chaos Pyromancies buffed, humanity damage bonus is increased [needs confirmation] -Still doesn't scale with humanity.



## Gameplay

* Critical hits do less damage. This includes riposte and backstabs.
* Medium roll is faster/all rolls have been buffed [need more info]
* Gravelords receive humanity when an infected player dies to a BP mob [needs confirmation]
* Changes to poise [need info and confirmation] - No obvious changes to poise.
* New warpable bonfires in: Undead Parish (above Andre), The Depths, Painted World of Ariamis (note that you cannot warp from this bonfire), Tomb of the Giants, Duke's Archives, Crystal Cave [additional warps to new areas also]
* Crystal Golem AoE attack inflicts Curse status. [this is also present in the console versions, but the duration of the effect is so short that it's easily missed]
* Reah of Thorolund no longer can be drained 12 humanity (with Dark Hand), she now has 0 - Not true. She can still be drained for 12 humanity. (tested once she moved to the chapel)



## Glitches

* Bottomless Box glitch removed
* Dragon Headstone glitch removed
* Spell switch glitch removed
* Prism Stone item duplication glitch removed
* Bow backstab removed
* Dropping of undroppable items (Estus flask, Dark sign ect.) can no longer be dropped.



## Misc

* Dark Anor Londo NPCs no longer respawn
* Environmental lava damage increase
* Increased souls gained for killing certain enemies [need more info]
* In-game description of the Pendant fixed: "A simple pendant with no effect." "Even so, pleasant memories are crucial to survival on arduous journeys."

## NPCs

* **Elizabeth** - Merchant
* **Marvelous Chester** - Merchant - Black Phantom
* **Lord's Blade Ciaran** - Appears at the Artorias battle site after beating him. Trade Soul of Artorias for Dark Silver Tracer and Gold Tracer. Drops: Lord's Blade armor set.
* **Hawkeye Gough** - Must use Crest Key to unlock tower, from which he will shoot down the Black Dragon with his bow, allowing the player to fight it.

## Bosses



* **Sanctuary Guardian**
   Drops: Guardian Soul, Guardian Tail (cut off tail)
* **Artorias of the Abyss**
   Drops: Soul of Artorias, Armor set must be purchased from Domhnall of Zena.
* **Manus, Father of the Abyss**
   Drops: Soul of Manus
* **Black Dragon Kalameet** (optional)
   Drops: Calamity Ring, Obsidian Greatsword (cut off tail)

## Weapons



* **Obsidian Greatsword** - Obtained by cutting off the tail of Kalameet.
   Greatsword, Attack Type: Regular
   Two-handed R2 does a large AOE blast that inflicts DPS and uses 50 durability per use.
* **Guardian Tail** - Obtained by cutting off the tail of the Sanctuary Guardian.
   Whip, Attack Type: Regular
* **Dark Silver Tracer** - Gift/Drop from Ciaran.
   Dagger, Attack Type: slash/thrust
* **Gold Tracer** - Gift/Drop from Ciaran.
   Curved Sword, Attack Type: slash
   Cannot parry while in offhand but retains offensive moveset except for running attack.
* **Four-pronged Plow** - Rare drop from scarecrow gardeners in the Royal Wood.
   Spear, Attack Type: Thrust
* **Abyss Greatsword** - Ascend +10 Greatsword with Soul of Artorias.
* **Stone Greataxe** - Rare drop from Stone Knights in the Royal Wood.
* **Gough's Greatbow** - Gift form Hawkeye Gough after slaying Kalameet.
* **Gough's Great Arrow**
   Can only be used with a Greatbow.
* **Cleansing Greatshield** - Found after rescuing Sif in Chasm of the Abyss.
   Greatshield, Attack Type: Thrust
* **Manus Catalyst** - Ascend any catalyst with the Soul of Manus.
   Halves castings, high mag adjust (>logans, <cystal), heavy melee attack.
* **Oolacile Catalyst** - Dropped by Bloated Sorcerers.

## Items



* **Calamity Ring:** Causes the wearer to take double the damage from everything.
* **Broken Pendant:** Key to enter DLC.
* **Silver Pendant:** Blocks dark magic for a brief moment.
* **Crest Key:** Key to access Hawkeye Gough.
* **Elizabeth's Mushroom:** Healing item. Regenerates HP for 30 seconds.
* **Purple Coward's Crystal:** Multiplayer. Exit battle of stoicism.
* **Carvings:**Spoken Gestures:
  + Hello - I'm Sorry - Very Good! - Help Me! - Thank You.
* **Guardian Soul:** Use unknown/no use, Can be consumed for 12,000 souls.
* **Soul of Artorias:** Ascend a +10 Greatsword with the Soul of Artorias to create the Abyss Greatsword. Give soul to Ciaran to recieve the Dark Silver Tracer and Gold Tracer.
* **Soul of Manus:** Ascend a Catalyst with the Soul of Manus to create a Manus Catalyst. Trade soul with Snuggly for the Pursuers spell.

## Gameplay



* Optional New Sif intro. It only happens if you rescued him in the Chasm of the Abyss before fighting him in Lordran.
* A few new dialogue lines from Dusk of Oolacile.
* **Battle of Stoicism** - New PvP mode, that consists of an arena. To join, the player must first beat the boss Artorias the Abysswalker.
