**Very Good Carving** is a Carving in **Dark Souls**. Carvings are a useful tool to express yourself to other players, some including a vocal performance or making sound.

> Head carved of archtrees by gough in his imprisonment. Gough imparts an emotion to each and every completed carving, which helps him achieve personal enlightenment. When a head is disturbed, it speaks, reflecting the emotion conferred to it.
> This head says "Very Good!". Have another look. Does it not appear quite jovial?

### Usage

* Says "Very Good!" when it makes contact with a hard surface.
