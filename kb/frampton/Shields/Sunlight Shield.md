**Sunlight Shield** is a Standard Shield in Dark Souls. The shield of Solaire of Astora, Knight of Sunlight. It features a holy symbol, hand-painted by Solaire himself, though it lacks any divine powers. Standard Shields can be used not only to block an enemy but to parry their attack as well. This allows a riposte, a free attack by the player for a high amount of damage. Enemy attacks can be interrupted with a parry (pressing the Strong Attack button for the shield) and requires practice to learn the timing. Better at blocking than Small Shields.

> "Shield of Solaire of Astora, Knight of Sunlight. Decorated with a holy symbol, but Solaire illustrated it himself, and it has no divine powers of its own.
>  As it turns out, Solaire's incredible prowess is a product of his own training, and nothing else."

* This shield reappears in *Dark Souls 3*.
