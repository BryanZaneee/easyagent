**Red Titanite Chunk** is an Ore in **Dark Souls**. Ores function as upgrade components for Weapons and Armor. Some varieties may be given to Kingseeker Frampt, who converts them into lesser versions.

> Titanite chunk for weapon reinforcement. Red titanite has powerful fire energy.
> Reinforces fire weapons to +9, and chaos weapons to +4.
> With the discovery of chunks in Lordran, the race to locate the Legendary Slabs has begun. But could they be mere myth

### usage

* High level reinforcement material
* Reinforce Fire weapons to +9 and Chaos weapons to +4.

### location

* **Demon Ruins:**
  + Drops from Burrowing Rockworm (5% Chance), not farmable

* Between Demon Ruins and Lost Izalith:
  + Drops from Chaos Bug (0.5% Chance) (Note: Sunlight Medal is a much more likely drop, so this isn't very efficient).
* **Lost Izalith:**
  + One on a corpse in the pit with poison. Near a bunch of chaos eaters and another 2 corpses.
  + One on a corpse when you take stairs from the poison pit, near a Chaos Eater.
  + Drops from Chaos Bug (0.5% Chance).
  + Drops from Chaos Eater (10% Chance).
* **Kiln of the First Flame:**
  + Great-sword Black Knight (guaranteed drop, respawns).
* **The Great Hollow:**
  + One on a corpse near the top of the level.
  + Another on a corpse on a small branch just above the frogs.
  + Crystal Lizards also drop a red titanite chunk extremely rarely.
* **Northern Undead Asylum (when you come back):**
  + The two Black Knights drop a red titanite chunk each.
* **Snuggly the Crow:**
  + Trading an unupgraded Pyromancy Flame (+0) with him for a red titanite chunk.
* **Royal Wood**:
  + Drops from Crystal Lizard, jump down the waterfall right after the entrance of Kalameet's boss arena.

* **Oolacile Township:**
  + Above the entrance of the mansion with two sorcerers, using light to make the wall portion that looks like a door disappear.
* Drops from Vagrants, specifically the Red Good Vagrant (Depths, Undead Burg, Undead Parish, Painted World of Ariamis, Darkroot Garden, Darkroot Basin, The Catacombs, Tomb of Giants, The Great Hollow, Ash Lake, Demon Ruins, Lost Izalith, Sen's Fortress, Blighttown, Anor Londo, New Londo Ruins Lower, New Londo Ruins Upper, The Duke's Archives, Crystal Cave, Northern Undead Asylum, Kiln of the First Flame and Royal Wood).
