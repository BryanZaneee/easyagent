**Crest Shield** is a Standard Shield in Dark Souls. **Crest Shield,** belonging to an unnamed knight, likely a high-ranking knight of Astora, this shield significantly reduces magic damage. It is considered one of the most practical anti-magic shields due to its balanced combination of weight, defense, and stability. Standard Shields can be used not only to block an enemy but to parry their attack as well. This allows a riposte, a free attack by the player for a high amount of damage. Enemy attacks can be interrupted with a parry (pressing the Strong Attack button for the shield) and requires practice to learn the timing. Better at blocking than Small Shields.

### Acquired

* Drops from Oscar of Astora. (Guaranteed - Northern Undead Asylum Revisit)

### Hints and Tips

* A great kite shield with low requirements and very high magic resistance.
* By far the most practical anti-magic shield one can obtain especially considering it's weight, defenses, and stability combination.
* This shield reappears in *Dark Souls 3*.
