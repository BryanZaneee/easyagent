**Spider Shield** is a Standard Shield in Dark Souls. Wielded by the fierce mountain bandits, this shield is distinctively shaped with a large black spider design. It offers resistance to poison, making it an excellent choice for navigating Blighttown unscathed due to its effective poison defense. Standard Shields can be used not only to block an enemy but to parry their attack as well. This allows a riposte, a free attack by the player for a high amount of damage. Enemy attacks can be interrupted with a parry (pressing the Strong Attack button for the shield) and requires practice to learn the timing. Better at blocking than Small Shields.

> "Shield of the savage mountain bandits. Uniquely-shaped with a large black spider etched upon it."
>  "Has resistance to poison."

### Acquired

* **Starter Item**: Initial Equipment for Bandit.
* **Locations**: In the Valley of Drakes on the bridge to New Londo Ruins Lower on a corpse, and above the Giant Rat in the Depths by dropping down the hole behind the first Butcher. Video Location Timestamp 9:49

### Hints and Tips

* Special: Grants a good bonus to Poison resistance
* This is a great shield if you want to get through blighttown in one piece since it grants sweet poison resistance.
* This shield would reappear in *Dark Souls 3*.
