**Giant Shield** is a Greatshield in Dark Souls. **Giant Shield**, crafted from bronze and marcasite, this greatshield is employed by the giant sentinels of Anor Londo. Marcasite, also known as white iron, provides effective resistance to non-physical damage. However, designed for the giant sentinels, its weight is immense. Greatshields care extremely heavy shields that remove the option of parrying in favor of a shield bash, whilst also providing the best deflection capabilities of any category.

> Greatshield made of bronze and marcasite. Used by the giant sentinels of Anor Londo.
>  Marcasite, or white iron, is effective against non-physical damage, but as the shield is designed for the giant sentineles, its weight is unbearable."

### Acquired

* Buy from the Giant Blacksmith for 10,000 souls.
* Drops from Sentinel (1% chance - Anor Londo)
* Drops from Royal Sentinel (1% chance - Anor Londo)
