**Buckler** is a Small Shield in Dark Souls. **Buckler a** very small round metal shield with a large central protrusion for parrying. It features a long parry frame window, similar to the Target Shield. Small Shields can be used not only to block an enemy but to parry their attack as well. This allows a riposte, a free attack by the player for a high amount of damage. Enemy attacks can be interrupted with a parry (pressing the Strong Attack button for the shield) and requires practice to learn the timing.

> "Very small round metal shield. Parry attacks with large center protrusion.
>
>  Small shields are always less stable, but landing critical hits after parry is easier. This shield is specialized for parrying."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Purchase from the Undead Merchant for 800 souls.
* Drops from Balder Knight with Rapier (1% chance - Sen's Fortress and Undead Parish)

### Hints and Tips

* The Parry type is Special (Long)
* Special: Has the Long Parry frame window the same as the Target Shield.
* The Buckler also appears in *Dark Souls 2* and *Dark Souls 3*.
