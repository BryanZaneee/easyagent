**Bloodshield** is a Standard Shield in Dark Souls. **Bloodshield**, mentioned in lost legends, the Bloodshield is slightly enchanted with red blood, enhancing various resistances. It is particularly beneficial when using the Lifehunt Scythe, as it helps mitigate self-inflicted bleed damage. Standard Shields can be used not only to block an enemy but to parry their attack as well. This allows a riposte, a free attack by the player for a high amount of damage. Enemy attacks can be interrupted with a parry (pressing the Strong Attack button for the shield) and requires practice to learn the timing. Better at blocking than Small Shields.

> "The bloodshield spoken of in the lost legends. The red blood is slightly enchanted, and boost various resistances."

### Acquired

* **Location**: In the Painted World of Ariamis on the bridge near the Undead Dragon.
* Video Location Timestamp 5:53

### Hints and Tips

* Special: Raises Bleed/Poison/Curse resist by 50% of your base resistances (the values you have with no armor equipped).
* Predictive calculations are bugged, must be equipped to see properly modified stats.
* Incorrectly categorized in the Complete Strategy Guide.
* Useful when wielding the Lifehunt Scythe, which causes bleed damage on self.
* Also useful for fighting against Basilisk and Seath The Scaleless due to its ability to raise curse resistance.
