**Titanite Slab** is an Ore in **Dark Souls**. This ore is good for upgrading standard weapons from +14 to +15 and Lightning weapons from +4 to +5. It can be obtained as a drop from the Stray Demon in the Northern Undead Asylum, Darkwraiths, and Crystal Lizards. Additionally, it can be rewarded from completing the Siegmeyer questline and found inside a chest behind a waterfall in Royal Wood. Ores function as upgrade components for Weapons and Armor. Some varieties may be given to Kingseeker Frampt, who converts them into lesser versions.

### Usage

* Reinforce standard weapons to +15, Crystal and Lightning weapons to +5, reinforce standard armour to +10

### Location

* Drop: Stray Demon (Northern Undead Asylum) on return
* Rare drop: Darkwraiths (New Londo Ruins), Crystal Lizard (The Great Hollow)
* Siegmeyer quest line reward
* Royal Woods (AotA only), in a chest behind waterfall in Kalameet's area

### Notes

* Ascends Standard, Crystal, or Lightning weapons to their maximum potentials (+15, +5, and +5, respectively).
* One is dropped by killing the Stray Demon in the Northern Undead Asylum.
* One can be found in a chest near a waterfall in the area where you fight Black Dragon Kalameet. Defeating him is not necessary to obtain it. (AotA only)
* One is given to you if you successfully complete Siegmeyer of Catarina's storyline.
* They are extremely rarely dropped by Darkwraith Knights in the lower New Londo Ruins. This is the only place where they can be farmed. For more information, see Titanite Farming.
* Drop from Crystal Lizard in the The Great Hollow. (Confirmed on 11/30/12) Killing all 10 Lizards usually results in at least 1 Titanite Slab, sometimes as many as three.
