**Iron Round Shield** is a Standard Shield in Dark Souls. **Iron Round Shield**, a thick iron shield wielded by Shiva of the East. It is the heaviest among standard shields and can deflect enemy attacks effectively. This shield is capable of heavy deflection, a feature usually found in greatshields. Standard Shields can be used not only to block an enemy but to parry their attack as well. This allows a riposte, a free attack by the player for a high amount of damage. Enemy attacks can be interrupted with a parry (pressing the Strong Attack button for the shield) and requires practice to learn the timing. Better at blocking than Small Shields.

> "Thick iron shield of Shiva of the East. Heaviest of the standard shields. Deflects enemy attacks.
>  A famous specimen from Shiva's collection, but none who have faced it have lived to tell the tale."

### Acquired

* Drop from Shiva of the East.

### Hints and Tips

* It is capable of heavy deflection, an attribute usually reserved for greatshields.
* As mentioned above, this is great for those that desire an opening after stunning an enemy through Deflection. This is also great for those that utilize the Leo Ring for Thrust attacks.
* It appears in *Dark Souls 2* as the Iron Parma, and in *Dark Souls 3* as the Eastern Iron Shield.
