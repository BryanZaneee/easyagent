**Balder Shield** is a Standard Shield in Dark Souls. **Balder Shield**, a shield from the knights of the ancient kingdom of Balder, modified for increased stability. It offers above-average resistance to lightning and fire, but its magic resistance is notably low. Standard Shields can be used not only to block an enemy but to parry their attack as well. This allows a riposte, a free attack by the player for a high amount of damage. Enemy attacks can be interrupted with a parry (pressing the Strong Attack button for the shield) and requires practice to learn the timing. Better at blocking than Small Shields.

> "Shield of the knights of the ancient kingdom of Balder. Tweaked for improved stability.
>  Balder was the home of Knight King Rendal, but the kingdom was reduced to ruins after a widespread outbreak of Undead"

### Acquired

* Rare drop (1% chance) from Balder Knights who wield it in Undead Parish and Sen's Fortress (they also wield the Balder Side Sword).
* **Purchase:** Crestfallen Merchant for 4000 souls.

### Hints and Tips

* A heavier medium shield with stability that starts out very high.
* When fully upgraded, has the second highest stability of all medium shields
* Has above average lightning and fire resistance, but very low magic resistance
