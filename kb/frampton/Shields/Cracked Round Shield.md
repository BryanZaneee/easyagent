**Cracked Round Shield** is a Small Shield in Dark Souls. **Cracked Round Shield,** a round wooden shield that is cracked and nearly broken. It has stats similar to intact round shields but is inferior in every way. Suitable primarily for role-playing purposes. Small Shields can be used not only to block an enemy but to parry their attack as well. This allows a riposte, a free attack by the player for a high amount of damage. Enemy attacks can be interrupted with a parry (pressing the Strong Attack button for the shield) and requires practice to learn the timing.

> Round wooden shield. Cracked and nearly broken. Wood shields are lighter than metal shields, but this one has lower physical damage reduction stability, partly due to cracking."

### Acquired

* Initial equipment for Pyromancer.
* Dropped by Hollow Warriors (with shield) in the Firelink Shrine, Undead Burg, and Undead Parish.

### Hints and Tips

* Has very similar stats to the intact round shields, but worse in every meaningful way. Recommended for roleplaying only.
* Utilizes "Fast" type parrying although not recommended to use this shield unless cosplaying.
