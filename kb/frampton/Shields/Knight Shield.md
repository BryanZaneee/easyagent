**Knight Shield** is a Standard Shield in Dark Souls. **Knight Shield**, an elegantly carved and painted medium metal shield. Despite its fancy appearance, it's often considered a "Newbie trap" since the Hollow Soldier Shield and the Kite Shields sold by Blacksmith Andre offer superior weight, resistances, and stability. Standard Shields can be used not only to block an enemy but to parry their attack as well. This allows a riposte, a free attack by the player for a high amount of damage. Enemy attacks can be interrupted with a parry (pressing the Strong Attack button for the shield) and requires practice to learn the timing. Better at blocking than Small Shields.

> "This medium metal shield is elegantly carved and painted."
> "Although it is thought to have been used by ancient knights of nobility, its defense and capabilities are similar to other shields."

### Acquired

* On a corpse in the Undead Parish just past the Fog Gate. Guarded by an Balder Knight.
* Video Location Timestamp 4:33
* Video Location Timestamp 4:30
* Crystal Knight Shield can be found in one of the Mimics at the Duke's Archive, at the upper floor of the library.

### Hints and Tips

* It can be the first 100% damage reduction shield you'll run across in the open. The other option is the Dragon Crest Shield if one were go below the Firelink Shrine, to the New Londo Ruins area, and use the Master Key to open the gated elevator that leads up to the Valley of Drakes.
* Looks fancy, but is typically known as a "Newbie trap" because the Hollow Soldier Shield and Kite Shields sold by Blacksmith Andre are better in every way, including Weight, Resistances and Stability.
* It has higher AR (Attack Rating) than the Kite Shields.
