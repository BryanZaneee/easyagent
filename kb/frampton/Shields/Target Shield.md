**Target Shield** is a Small Shield in Dark Souls. **Target Shield**, a small, round metal shield with four protrusions designed for parrying. It is considered the best parrying shield because of its metal construction, which deflects some attacks, and the longest parry frames in the game, while still providing reliable blocking. Small Shields can be used not only to block an enemy but to parry their attack as well. This allows a riposte, a free attack by the player for a high amount of damage. Enemy attacks can be interrupted with a parry (pressing the Strong Attack button for the shield) and requires practice to learn the timing.

> Small round metal shield. Four protrusions used to parry attacks.
> Small shields are always less stable, but landing critical hits after parry is easier. This shield is specialized for parrying.

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Found on a body inside a small room in Lower Undead Burg just before the Capra Demon.
* Initial Equipment for Thief.
* Drops from Undead Assassin also known as Hollow Thief (1% chance -  Lower Undead Burg)
* Video Location: Found in this video at 3:04

### Hints and Tips

* The Parry type is Special (Long).
* Parry-wise, this is the best Shield due to it being metal (deflects some attacks) and having the longest parry frames in the game while being able to reliably block attacks.
* The Target Shield appears in both *Dark Souls 2* and *Dark Souls 3*.
