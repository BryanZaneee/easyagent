**White Titanite Chunk** is an Ore in **Dark Souls**. Ores function as upgrade components for Weapons and Armor. Some varieties may be given to Kingseeker Frampt, who converts them into lesser versions.

> Titanite chunk for weapon reinforcement. White titanite has powerful magic energy. Reinforces divine weapons to +9, and occult weapons to +4.
> With the discovery of chunks in Lordran, the race to locate the Legendary Slabs has begun. But could they be mere myth?

### Usage

* Reinforce Divine weapons to +9, Occult weapons to +4
