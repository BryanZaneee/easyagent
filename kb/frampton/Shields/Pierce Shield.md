**Pierce Shield** is a Standard Shield in Dark Souls. **Pierce Shield**, employed by certain knights of Catarina, this shield features a large protrusion in the center for attacking. It can be used as a thrust weapon, offering both offensive and defensive capabilities, which is uncommon for a light shield. Standard Shields can be used not only to block an enemy but to parry their attack as well. This allows a riposte, a free attack by the player for a high amount of damage. Enemy attacks can be interrupted with a parry (pressing the Strong Attack button for the shield) and requires practice to learn the timing. Better at blocking than Small Shields.

> "Shield of certain knights of Catarina, used in attacks with a large protrusion in the center."
>  "Can be used as a thrust weapon."

### Acquired

* Gift: Find Siegmeyer of Catarina in the Blighttown swamp near a group of slugs and give him 3 Purple Moss Clumps.

### Hints and Tips

* A light shield that can be used for both attack and defense, an attribute usually restricted to Greatshields.
* Upgrading the shield does not increase stability.
* Infusion does not decrease stability.
* This shield reappears in *Dark Souls 3*.
