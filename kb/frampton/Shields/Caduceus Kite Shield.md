**Caduceus Kite Shield** is a Standard Shield in Dark Souls. **Caduceus Kite Shield**, a medium metal shield featuring an ancient blue Caduceus. It shares the same stats as the Tower Kite Shield, with the only difference being its decorative design. It is carried by the quiet member of Reah's entourage. Standard Shields can be used not only to block an enemy but to parry their attack as well. This allows a riposte, a free attack by the player for a high amount of damage. Enemy attacks can be interrupted with a parry (pressing the Strong Attack button for the shield) and requires practice to learn the timing. Better at blocking than Small Shields.

> "Medium metal shield. Decorated by an ancient blue Caduceus. A standard, widely-used shield."

### Acquired

* Purchase: Andre of Astora for 1000 souls.
* Drops from Swordsman Undead Crystal Soldier (2% chance - The Duke's Archives)

### Hints and Tips

* Identical stats to the Tower Kite Shield, the only difference is the decoration. The quiet guy in Reah's entourage wields this.

### Lore

* The Caduceus Kite Shield is part of a long tradition of using caduceus patterns in Souls games.
