**Gargoyle's Shield** is a Standard Shield in Dark Souls. The bronze shield used by the gargoyles guarding the Bell of Awakening in the Undead Church. Despite being a metal shield, it has the weak physical resistance and stability of a +15 Leather Shield, while weighing as much as a Balder Shield. However, it offers good deflection against both fire and lightning, comparable to an Eagle Shield. Standard Shields can be used not only to block an enemy but to parry their attack as well. This allows a riposte, a free attack by the player for a high amount of damage. Enemy attacks can be interrupted with a parry (pressing the Strong Attack button for the shield) and requires practice to learn the timing. Better at blocking than Small Shields.

### Acquired

* Rare drop from any of the three Gargoyles with shields.
* Video Location Timestamp 9:24

### Hints and Tips

* Very strange for a metal shield. Has the flimsy physical resistance and stability of a +15 Leather Shield and weighs as much as a Balder Shield, but deflects both fire and lightning as well as an Eagle Shield.
* Upgrades very well. A +15 regular shield loses to an upgraded Silver Knight Shield by only a few stability points. +10 Fire enhancement brings the fire resist up to 90%, tying it for first place with the Black Knight Shield. Magic and lightning upgrades improve the defense only slightly.
