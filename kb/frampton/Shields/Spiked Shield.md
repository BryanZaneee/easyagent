**Spiked Shield** is a Standard Shield in Dark Souls. **Spiked Shield**, the shield of the notorious Darkwraith Kirk, Knight of Thorns, is covered with sharp spikes that induce heavy bleeding. Standard Shields can be used not only to block an enemy but to parry their attack as well. This allows a riposte, a free attack by the player for a high amount of damage. Enemy attacks can be interrupted with a parry (pressing the Strong Attack button for the shield) and requires practice to learn the timing. Better at blocking than Small Shields.

> "Shield of the infamous Darkwraith Kirk, Knight of Thorns. Covered with spikes."
>  "Can be used as a weapon. Sharp spikes cause heavy bleeding."

### Acquired

* Possible drop from any of the three encounters with Knight Kirk.

### Hints and Tips

* Parry is replaced by an overhead shield bash.
* Bleed build up is 33 per strike, and once inflicted will tear 30% of the opponents HP.
* This shield reappears in *Dark Souls 3*.
