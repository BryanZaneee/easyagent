**Small Leather Shield** is a Small Shield in Dark Souls. **Small Leather Shields** are compact, leather-covered round shields, reinforced with metal in critical areas. They allow for fast-rolling even with heavier gear, but their stability and damage resistance are limited. Small Shields can be used not only to block an enemy but to parry their attack as well. This allows a riposte, a free attack by the player for a high amount of damage. Enemy attacks can be interrupted with a parry (pressing the Strong Attack button for the shield) and requires practice to learn the timing.

> Small, leather-covered round shield, reinforced in critical spots with metal.
> Small shields are always less stable, but landing critical hits after parry is easier."

### Acquired

* Purchase from the Undead Merchant for 600 Souls.
* Initial equipment for Sorcerer.
* Video Location Timestamp 1:02

### Hints and Tips

* Weighing at only 0.5 units this is the lightest Shield in the game.
* Due to the above factor it can potentially be used to maintain fast-rolling with heavier equipment just be wary of it Stability and damage resistances.
* Considering its weight its stats are quite well off although lacking the 100% damage resist. It is more suited for a player wanting to maintain fast-rolling and parrying if possible, rather than defending.
* Has "Fast" type parrying.
* The Small Leather Shield appears in both *Dark Souls 2* and *Dark Souls 3*.
