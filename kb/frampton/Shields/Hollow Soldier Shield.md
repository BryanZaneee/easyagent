**Hollow Soldier Shield** is a Standard Shield in Dark Souls. **Hollow Soldier Shield**, a metal shield used by Hollow soldiers. Though heavily rusted, it remains sturdy and provides solid defense, making it a reliable choice for the early stages of the game.  Standard Shields can be used not only to block an enemy but to parry their attack as well. This allows a riposte, a free attack by the player for a high amount of damage. Enemy attacks can be interrupted with a parry (pressing the Strong Attack button for the shield) and requires practice to learn the timing. Better at blocking than Small Shields.

> "Metal shield wielded by Hollow soldiers. Heavily rusted but sturdy, providing a strong defense"

### Acquired

* Obtained as a rare drop from Undead Parish soldiers.
* Drops from Undead Soldiers wielding a shield (1% Chance - Undead Burg, Undead Parish, Northern Undead Asylum (Revisit), Firelink Shrine)

### Notes & Tips

* Once one of the best medium shields, but heavily nerfed in patch 1.04/1.05.
* Quite good for early areas of the game
