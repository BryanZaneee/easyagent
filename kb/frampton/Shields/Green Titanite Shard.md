# Green Titanite Shard

| Titanite shard for weapon reinforcement. Green titanite imbued with a special power. Reinforces magic, divine, fire weapons to +5.  Titanite shards are fragments of the Legendary Slabs. Titanite is etched into weapons to reinforce. |

**Green Titanite Shard** is an Ore in **Dark Souls**. Ores function as upgrade components for Weapons and Armor. Some varieties may be given to Kingseeker Frampt, who converts them into lesser versions.

> Titanite shard for weapon reinforcement. Green titanite imbued with a special power. Reinforces magic, divine, fire weapons to +5.Titanite shards are fragments of the Legendary Slabs.
> Titanite is etched into weapons to reinforce."

### Usage

* Reinforce Magic, Divine, and Fire weapons to +5

### Farming

* Farming method for early in the game: Save at first bonfire in the Depths. Run into the hallway, kill all Slimes, loot, return to Bonfire. Repeat.
* Farming method in Blighttown: Save at the bonfire in the swamp (or the great hollow entrance). Run to the far left side of the map until you run into the giant leeches and kill them. The leeches drop five green titanite shards (2.06% Chance).
* Drops from Slimes (2% Chance - Depths)
* Drops from Burrowing Rockworm (4.76% ~ 25.81% Chance - Demon Ruins)
* Drops from Giant Leech (2.06% Chance for 5 - Blighttown)
* Drops from Oolacile Sorceress (1% ~ 5% Chance - Oolacile Township and Chasm of the Abyss)
* Drops from Vagrants, specifically the White Good Vagrant. (Depths, Undead Burg, Undead Parish, Painted World of Ariamis, Darkroot Garden, Darkroot Basin, The Catacombs, Tomb of Giants, The Great Hollow, Ash Lake, Demon Ruins, Lost Izalith, Sen's Fortress, Blighttown, Anor Londo, New Londo Ruins Lower, New Londo Ruins Upper, The Duke's Archives, Crystal Cave, Northern Undead Asylum, Kiln of the First Flame  and Royal Wood)
