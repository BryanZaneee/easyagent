**Heater Shield** is a Standard Shield in Dark Souls. **Heater Shield**, a small metal shield that is standard and widely used. It is the lightest shield in the game that provides 100% physical damage resistance and is the only 100% shield with a fast parry speed. Standard Shields can be used not only to block an enemy but to parry their attack as well. This allows a riposte, a free attack by the player for a high amount of damage. Enemy attacks can be interrupted with a parry (pressing the Strong Attack button for the shield) and requires practice to learn the timing. Better at blocking than Small Shields.

> "Small metal shield. A standard, widely-used shield."
>  "This shield is easy to use, and is one of the smallest shields that offers 100% physical damage reduction."
