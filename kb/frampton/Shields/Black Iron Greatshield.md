**Black Iron Greatshield** is a Greatshield in Dark Souls. Forged from special black iron, this shield is even heavier than Knight Berenike’s Tower Shield. It excels in resisting fire attacks and is also effective for shield bashing. Greatshields are extremely heavy shields that remove the option of parrying in favor of a shield bash, whilst also providing the best deflection capabilities of any category.

> "Greatshield of the mighty knight Tarkus"
>  "Built of special black iron and even heavier than Knight Berenike's tower shield. Especially resistant to fire attacks and effective for shield bashing."

### Acquired

* Found on Iron Knight Tarkus' corpse in the grand painting hall of Anor Londo along with his matching armor set. He's on the ground floor, huddled in a corner to the left of the painting.

### Hints and Tips

* It's heavier (23 Str required for two-handed action), less durable and 1 point less stable than the Tower Shield, but it gains an extra +15% of fire resistance, making it the #2 fire protection shield in the game.
* Upgrades exactly the same way and same price as the Tower Shield.
* While listed above as a Greatshield, the in game UI actually lists it as a normal shield which is incorrect.
* The shield was designed as part of a contest FromSoftware had before Dark Souls was released. Adam Ledzion is the name of the creator and the original name for the shield was the “Blood and Souls Shield”. He was credited as a Shield Contest Design Winner in the end credits.
* This greatshield reappears in *Dark Souls 3*.
