**Caduceus Round Shield** is a Small Shield in Dark Souls. This round wooden shield from Lordran is adorned with an ancient blue Caduceus. It has the same stats as the Red & White Shield dropped by skeletons, with the only variation being its appearance.Small Shields can be used not only to block an enemy but to parry their attack as well. This allows a riposte, a free attack by the player for a high amount of damage. Enemy attacks can be interrupted with a parry (pressing the Strong Attack button for the shield) and requires practice to learn the timing

> Round wooden shield crafted in Lordran. Decorated by an ancient blue Caduceus.
>  The giant trees in Lordran are distant offspring of the great stone archtrees. This shield inherits their properites, and the wood greatly reduces magic damage."

### Acquired

* On a corpse in the graveyard next to Firelink Shrine.
* Video Location Timestamp 1:46

### Hints and Tips

* Identical stats to the Red & White Shield dropped by spooky skeletons, the only difference is in appearance.
* Has "Fast" type parrying.

### Lore

* The Caduceus Round Shield is part of a long tradition of using caduceus patterns in Souls games.
