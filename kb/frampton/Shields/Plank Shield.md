**Plank Shield** is a Small Shield in Dark Souls. **Plank Shield**, a makeshift shield constructed from wooden planks. It offers minimal protection and comes with the added disadvantage of moderate humiliation. Small Shields can be used not only to block an enemy but to parry their attack as well. This allows a riposte, a free attack by the player for a high amount of damage. Enemy attacks can be interrupted with a parry (pressing the Strong Attack button for the shield) and requires practice to learn the timing.

> "Makeshift shield built from wood planks. Provides minimal protection, but at a cost of moderate humiliation."

### Acquired

* Found on a corpse in Blighttown, just before the hidden entrance to The Great Hollow.
* Starter Item: Initial Equipment for Deprived.
* Video Location Timestamp 0:05

### Hints and Tips

* The Plank Shield also appears in *Dark Souls 3*.
