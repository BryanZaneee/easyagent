**Tower Shield** is a Greatshield in Dark Souls. This metal greatshield was wielded by Knight Berenike, famed for his formidable armor. Constructed from iron, it provides exceptional defense but is exceedingly heavy. Greatshields are extremely heavy shields that remove the option of parrying in favor of a shield bash, whilst also providing the best deflection capabilities of any category.

*"Metal Greatshield. Used by the Knight Berenike, known for his heavy armour.*
 *This plate of iron is heavily defensive but ultra heavy. Greatshields are very stable, and deflect attacks. However, one cannot parry, and instead bashes with the shield."*

### Acquired

* **Drop:** Tower Knights in Undead Parish, Sen's Fortress and the Painted World (respawns).
* **Purchase:** Crestfallen Merchant for 8000 souls.

### Hints and Tips

* Fabulously stable, but very heavy.
* Upgraded to Crystal +5 gives the shield 90 stability, making it the highest stability shield in the game, albeit with the downside of severely reduced and irreparable 30 durability.
* Inexpensive upgrades that mostly improve bashing damage.
