**East-West Shield** is a Standard Shield in Dark Souls. Standard Shields can be used not only to block an enemy but to parry their attack as well. This allows a riposte, a free attack by the player for a high amount of damage. Enemy attacks can be interrupted with a parry (pressing the Strong Attack button for the shield) and requires practice to learn the timing. Better at blocking than Small Shields.

> "A wooden kite shaped shield. It is decorated with the ancient symbol of a double-headed eagle, painted yellow."
>  "Wooden shields are lighter than metal shields, but with lower physical damage reduction, and reduced shield stability."

### Acquired

* **Starter Item:** Initial equipment for Cleric.
* **Purchase:** Undead Merchant (male) in Undead Burg for 400 souls.

### Hints and Tips

* Lightest Standard shield, with lower physical damage reduction (but decent lightning resistance), and reduced shield stability, as stated in its description.
* This shield reappears in *Dark Souls 3*.
