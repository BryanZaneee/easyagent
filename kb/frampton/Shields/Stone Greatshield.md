**Stone Greatshield** is a Greatshield in Dark Souls. Used by the Stone Knight, the guardian of the Darkroot Garden, this moss-covered, round greatshield offers substantial protection. Greatshields are extremely heavy shields that remove the option of parrying in favor of a shield bash, whilst also providing the best deflection capabilities of any category.

> Mossy round greatshield used by the guardian of the Darkroot garden, the Stone Knight."
>  "The Stone Knight is a creation of ancient magic, and this shield is imbued with the same power, but is also extremely heavy."

### Acquired

* Drops from the Great Stone Knight (2% chance - Darkroot Garden)

### Hints and Tips

* A very big and stable round shield, but heavier than some alternatives.
* Upgrades only increase the bashing damage by a smidge.
* The fantastic magic resistance (tied for 2nd place with the Crest Shield and the Cleansing Greatshield) makes it a boon for Str 26 characters shaping up to face the Moonlight Butterfly. Just weather its salvoes while two-handing the shield and switch to a proper weapon when it comes down to feed.
* This greatshield reappears in *Dark Souls 3*.
