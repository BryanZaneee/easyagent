**Rotten Pine Resin** is a Consumable in **Dark Souls**. Consumable Items grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> Rotten pine resin that has gained poison. Adds poison effect to right-hand weapon.
> Hits with affected weapon build up enemy poison level. If enemy is poisoned, damage will be inflicted for a period of time

### Usage

* Applies 180 poison element to right-hand weapon when consumed. (3 HP/s for 180 seconds)
* Duration: 60 seconds.
* May only be used on weapons that are on the standard +1...+15 weapon path and which are not inherently magical (see Weapon Buffs).

### Location

* Sold by Undead Merchant (Female) for 1,000 souls.
