**Purging Stone** is a Consumable in **Dark Souls**. Consumable Items grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> Ash-colored stone encasing a skull. Secret treasure of Arstor, the Earl of Carim. Reduces curse build-up and breaks curse.
> Humans are helpless against curses, and can only redirect their influence.
> The Purging Stone does not dispel curses, but receives them as a surrogate. The stone itself was once a person or some other being.

### Usage

* Reduces curse build-up and breaks curse.
* Does not remove the Transient Curse effect.

### Location

* Sold by Undead Merchant (Female) for 6,000 souls.
* Sold by Oswald of Carim for 3,000 souls. He only has 5.
* Dropped by Man-Eater Shells (10% Chance) in Ash Lake or the Crystal Cave.
* 2 can be traded with Snuggly for a Cracked Red Eye Orb.
* Drops from Vagrants, specifically the Red Good Vagrant. (Depths, Undead Burg, Undead Parish, Painted World of Ariamis, Darkroot Garden, Darkroot Basin, The Catacombs, Tomb of Giants, The Great Hollow, Ash Lake, Demon Ruins, Lost Izalith, Sen's Fortress, Blighttown, Anor Londo, New Londo Ruins Lower, New Londo Ruins Upper, The Duke's Archives, Crystal Cave, Northern Undead Asylum, Royal Wood and Kiln of the First Flame).
