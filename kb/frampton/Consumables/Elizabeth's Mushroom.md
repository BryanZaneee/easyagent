**Elizabeth's Mushroom** is a Consumable in **Dark Souls**. Consumable Items grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> Large medicinal mushroom of Elizabeth, keeper of the sanctuary.
> Eating this mushroom invigorates the flesh, and greatly restores HP for a limited duration.
> Its dramatic effect can make the difference between a warrior's life and death.

### Usage

* Regenerates health for 30 seconds when consumed.
* Regeneration rate is approximately 100 HP per second.
* Grants 1,000 souls when fed to Kingseeker Frampt.

### Location

* 1 Found on a corpse in the Royal Forest.
* Gift x3 from Elizabeth, after rescuing Dusk from Manus. Also drops from her if you kill Elizabeth.

### Notes & Tips

* Elizabeth's Mushroom is a Self Buff and interferes with other such buffs (i.e. Great Magic Barrier, Transient Curse, Power Within, etc.). As long as it's active it's not possible to cast other Self Buffs and vice versa.
