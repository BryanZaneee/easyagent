**Homeward Bone** is a Consumable in **Dark Souls**. Consumable Items grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> Bone fragment reduced to white ash. Return to last bonfire used for resting.
> Bonfires are fueled by bones of the Undead. In rare cases, the strong urge of their previous owner's to seek bonfires enchants their bones with a homeward instinct.

### Usage

* Transports player to the last visited Bonfire, player loses neither souls nor humanity.
* Same effect as the "Homeward" Miracle.

### Notes & Tips

* Bonfires were originally used to burn the bones of slaughtered cattle, hence the name; bone-fire.
* Homeward Bone is obviously a play on words with the expression "Homeward Bound".
