**Prism Stone** is a Consumable in **Dark Souls**. Consumable Items grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> Warm pebble emitting a beautiful phasing aura of seven colors, with a very rare eight.
> The rainbow stone does nothing special, but can serve as a path marker, and can be dropped off a cliff to judge height by the sound of descent.
> If a loud noise is heard upon its landing, then a fall off the ledge is surely lethal.

### Usage

* Used to make breadcrumb trails, or to mark areas already visited in confusing places.
* Used to determine the height of a fall. Listen to the sound of the stone hitting the ground. If it's loud, the fall will be fatal.
* Trade one to Snuggly for a Demon Titanite.

### Eighth color testing

Per the item description there are eight colors of prism stones. Testing from multiple persons does reveal eight colors. Testing from two sources yielded:

* Purple
* Yellow
* Green
* White
* Red/Orange
* Light Cyan
* Pink
* Blue
* Orange/Pink
* Red
* Slightly different green
