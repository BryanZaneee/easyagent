# Enemies

**Enemies** in **Dark Souls** and **Dark****Souls Remastered** are hostile NPCs and creatures that attack the player. This page gives an overview of all available enemies, and the linked pages provide information regarding enemy location, description, drops and strategy.‍‍‍‍‍‍‍‍‍ You can find a separate page with information on special enemies referred to as Bosses. Please note that enemy names may not be canon, as pages are created by the community long before official guides are available.

**Special Weaknesses** are a unique mechanic for certain enemies in Dark Souls. Certain Weapons possess unique modifiers to deal extra damage against certain types of enemies.

### Divine

Select Weapons will prevent Skeletons from resurrecting, and deal additional damage to Undead type enemies.

These weapons include:

* Astora's Straight Sword
* Crescent Axe
* Grant
* Greatsword of Artorias
* Silver Knight Spear
* Weapons with the Divine Upgrade Path

Enemies that count as undead include:

* Baby Skeleton
* Blacksmith Vamos
* Bone Tower
* Crow Demon
* Giant Skeleton
* Mass of Souls
* Skeletal Beast
* Skeleton
* Wheel Skeleton

### Occult

Select Weapons deal additional damage to Gods, their followers, and kin.

These weapons include:

* Dark Hand
* Priscilla's Dagger
* Velka's Rapier
* Weapons with the Occult Upgrade Path

Enemies and Bosses that count as gods or their followers include:

* Black Knight
* Dark Sun Gwyndolin
* Dragon Slayer Ornstein
* Gwyn, Lord of Cinder
* Paladin Leeroy
* Royal Sentinel
* Silver Knight

### Demon

Select Weapons deal +20% Damage to chaos demon type enemies.

These weapons include:

* Black Knight Greataxe
* Black Knight Greatsword
* Black Knight Halberd
* Black Knight Sword

Enemies and Bosses that count as chaos demons include:

* Asylum Demon
* Bat Wing Demon
* Capra Demon
* Centipede Demon
* Demon Firesage
* Taurus Demon
* Stray Demon

### Tail Cutting

While not an express weakness, the Enemies and Bosses that can have their tail or other body parts cut off include:

* Bell Gargoyle
* Black Dragon Kalameet
* Centipede Demon (arm, tail)
* Crossbleed Priscilla
* Everlasting Dragon
* Gaping Dragon
* Hellkite Drake
* Hydra (head only)
* Sanctuary Guardian
* Seath the Scaleless

### Special Weaknesses Notes

* The Ghosts in the New Londo Ruins can be attacked with cursed weaponry prior to using a Transient Curse or becoming cursed, which includes the Ghost Blade, Jagged Ghost Blade, and the Greatsword of Artorias (Cursed).
* The Lloyd's Talisman item will prevent enemy estus use and when used on a Mimic, it will open up and allow its item to be taken without becoming hostile.
* The Greatsword of Artorias and Greatsword of Artorias (Cursed) deal additional damage to Darkwraith Knights (including Knight Kirk) and the Four Kings.
