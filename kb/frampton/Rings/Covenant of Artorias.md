# Covenant of Artorias

| Covenant of Artorias |
| --- |
| 4002 |
| Used to enter the Abyss and fight the Four Kings Boss. |

**Covenant of Artorias** is a Ring in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered. This ring allows its wearer to traverse the Abyss, just as Artorias once did. It is awarded after defeating the Great Grey Wolf Sif. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> This ring symbolizes Knight Artorias's covenant with the beasts of the Abyss.
> Its wearer, like Artorias himself can traverse the Abyss.

### Effect

* Used to enter the Abyss and fight the Four Kings Boss.

### Acquired

* Awarded after killing the Great Grey Wolf Sif.

### Notes & Tips

* This ring is removed in New Game Plus. So you have to kill Sif in each playthrough to get this ring and be able to fight The Four Kings.
* If the ring is removed while in the Abyss, and the Four Kings are undefeated, the player will die with a unique animation.
* When summoned as a phantom to assist in The Four Kings fight, ***the summoned player also needs a ring to survive.***
* After defeating The Four Kings, the ring is no longer needed to survive in the abyss.
* If this item is dropped, it will reappear in the chest in Firelink Shrine.
* This item can not be traded, since it will not appear to the other player(s) if dropped.

### Application

* Ring provides no other bonuses.

### Lore

* Artorias and Sif are linked to The Abyss Watchers of DS3.
