# Bloodbite Ring

| Bloodbite Ring |
| --- |
| 4009 |
| Prevent Bleeding. |

**Bloodbite Ring** is a Ring in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered. **Bloodbite Ring**, despite the sinister rumors about its creation, this ring is a valuable asset for its ability to reduce bleeding. It's particularly useful when wielding the Lifehunt Scythe, as it inflicts bleed on the user with each successful hit. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> One of the infamous bite rings commissioned by Sir Arstor of Carim. Despite the dreadful rumors
> surrounding its creation, this ring is an unmistakable asset, in its ability to help prevent bleeding.

### Effects

* Increases base Bleed Resistance by 400%.

### Location

* Sold by Oswald of Carim in the Undead Parish for 10,000 souls.

### Application

* All of the bite rings are extremely effective in preventing their respective status condition, However, these ailments are rare in PvP and even more so in PvE, and are not recommended save for specific scenarios.
* Keep in mind bleeding deals burst damage when triggered, and this burst damage can occur again while you are still bleeding.
* Incredibly useful when wielding the Lifehunt Scythe, which causes bleed on self with every successful hit.
* Effects stacks with Bloodshield, Gargoyle's Halberd and Gargoyle Tail Axe.
