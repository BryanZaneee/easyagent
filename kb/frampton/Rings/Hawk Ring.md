# Hawk Ring

| Hawk Ring |
| --- |
| 4019 |
| Can be used to boosts bow range by 50%. |

**Hawk Ring** is a Ring in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered. **Hawk Ring**, owned by Hawkeye Gough, who led the Greatarchers, this ring increases the range of bows, making arrows fly as if shot from Gough's great bow, which brought down high-flying dragons. It also extends zooming capabilities, applicable to Binoculars as well. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> One of the special rings granted to the four knights of Gwyn. The Hawk Ring belonged to Hawkeye Gough, who led the Greatarchers.
>
> Boosts bow range, so that arrows fly like they were shot by Gough's great bow, which took down high-flying dragons.

### Effect

* Extends the range of all Bows by 50%, also increases damage because of how fall-off is calculated
* Zooming is extended as well, this also applies to the Binoculars

### Acquired

* Found in Anor Londo, in a chest behind the Giant Blacksmith. It is not necessary to kill the Giant Blacksmith, simply roll through the gap to his left.

### Notes

* This ring reappears in *Dark Souls 2* and *Dark Souls 3*.
