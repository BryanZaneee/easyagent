# Darkmoon Blade Covenant Ring

**Darkmoon Blade Covenant Ring** is a Ring in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered. **Darkmoon Blade Covenant Ring**, granted to members of the Darkmoon Blade covenant, this ring allows the wearer to answer Dark Sun Gwyndolin's summoning. It provides no bonuses and can be swapped for another ring after being summoned. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> Ring granted to those bound by the Darkmoon Blade covenant. Answer Dark Sun Gwyndolin's summoning.
> Gwyndolin, all too aware of his repulsive, frail appearance, created the illusion of a sister Gwynevere, who
> helps him guard over Anor Londo. An unmasking of these deities would be tantamount to blasphemy.

### Effect

* When wearing this ring you may be summoned into other players' dark Anor Londo as an invader, presumably as a result of the host killing Gwynevere.
* If the invader successfully kills the host, they will be rewarded with a Souvenir of Reprisal.
* If the Host manages to kill the invader, however, they will receive Souls as a reward.
* You must be part of the Darkmoon Blade covenant for the Rings effect to occur, but you do not need to be human.

### Acquired

* Granted upon joining the Darkmoon Blade Covenant

### Application

* Ring provides no bonuses, and can be unequipped in favor of a better ring after you are summoned.

### Trivia

* The Blades of The Dark Moon are the forerunners of the Blue Sentinels of DS2 and the Blue Sentinels of DS3.
