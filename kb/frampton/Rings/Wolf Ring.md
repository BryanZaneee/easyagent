# Wolf Ring

| Wolf Ring |
| --- |
| 4035 |
| Can be used to Increase Poise by 40. |

**Wolf Ring** is a Ring in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered. One of the four rings given to the knights of Gwyn, this ring belonged to Artorias the Abysswalker. It increase Poise by 40, reflecting Artorias' unyielding will and mastery with a greatsword.  Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> One of the special rings granted to the four knights of Gwyn. The Wolf Ring belongs to Artorias the Abysswalker. Artorias had an unbendable will of steel, and was unmatched with a greatsword.

### Effect

* Increase Poise by 40.

### Location

Accessed from the lower area of Darkroot Garden. Go down the path to the right of the large locked door, next to the hidden bonfire. Go past the area with 3 Ents in ambush, enter the fog and turn right at the clearing with the sleeping stone giants. Cut down the living tree that hides a trail.

Follow the trail and once past the second living tree stay to the right, there is a path right before the rubble which leads back upwards to the ring. A Great Stone Knight is close to the lower path entrance that will ambush you, if you don't kill him and walk up the path instead. Another Stone Knight is guarding the ring.
