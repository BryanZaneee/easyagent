# Calamity Ring

| Calamity Ring |
| --- |

**Calamity Ring** is a Ring in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered. This ring doubles the damage received by its wearer, offering no apparent benefit and serving as a challenge for those seeking a greater difficulty. It is obtained after defeating the Black Dragon Kalameet. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> A ring enchanted by the orange eye of Kalameet, the bringer of calamity. Doubles damage received by its wearer.
> A useless ring befitting of no finger. Best left unknown, or at least well hidden.

### Effects

* Wearing it will double the damage you sustain.

### Location

* Originally added with the Artorias of the Abyss DLC / bundle.
* Obtained after killing Black Dragon Kalameet.

### Application

* This ring may have been intended as an optional challenge.
* This ring can be handy for the first encounter against Seath the Scaleless where dying is mandatory to enter the prison cell area, as it increases the amount of damage taken and lessens the likelihood of being cursed by Seath's attack.
* his ring's effect can also be used for reaching 20% health faster in order to activate the effects of Red Tearstone Ring and Blue Tearstone Ring.

### Notes & Tips

* This ring reappears in *Dark Souls 3*.
