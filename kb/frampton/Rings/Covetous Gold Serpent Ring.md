# Covetous Gold Serpent Ring

| Covetous Gold Serpent Ring |
| --- |
| 4021 |
| Can be used to boosts Item Discovery. |

**Covetous Gold Serpent Ring** is a Ring in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered. **Covetous Gold Serpent Ring**, engraved with a serpent, this gold ring increases the wearer's item discovery rate, allowing for the amassing of more items from defeated foes. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> The serpent is an imperfect dragon and symbol of the Undead. Its habit of devouring prey
> even larger than itself has led to an association of gluttony. This gold ring, engraved with
> the serpent, boosts its wearer's item discovery, so that more items can be amassed.

### Effect

* Boosts item discovery by 200.

### Acquired

* Sen's Fortress. Get to the area with the boulder directing device and move the lever so that it goes west (same way as proper way to go to continue upstairs). Let the boulder roll 5-6 times so it fills up the hole then move the lever North (empty space). Now go into the West path and follow it through to the end. You will find a ring on a corpse in a window and a way into a room where the first **mimic** chest is.
  + Video Location Timestamp 9:22

### Application

* This ring makes finding/farming items much easier. It's effect stacks with the item discovery boost from Humanity, allowing a maximum of 410 item discovery.
* Wearing this ring when not farming is less recommended because the player is giving up the normal benefits of one of their ring slots for extra loot.

### Notes &

+ The ring's effect **does not stack** with the item discovery boost from the Symbol of Avarice.
+ You do not get any bonus effect from wearing both Serpent Rings at once, but their individual effects stack (+200 item discovery and +20% souls gained).
+ This ring reappears in *Dark Souls 2* and *Dark Souls 3*.
