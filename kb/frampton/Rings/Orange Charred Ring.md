# Orange Charred Ring

| Orange Charred Ring |
| --- |
| 4003 |
| Greatly reduces damage from lava. |

**Orange Charred Ring** is a Ring in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered. Enchanted by a witch, this orange ring greatly reduces damage from lava. The witch sisters gifted it to their sibling whose sores were inflamed by lava since birth. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> An orange ring enchanted by a witch. Reduces lava damage.
>
> Since his sores were inflamed by lava from birth, his witch sisters gave him this special ring.
> But fool that he is, he readily dropped it, and from that spot, a terrible centipede demon was born.

### Effect

* Greatly reduces lava damage.
* Adds fire damage element to kick motion.

### Acquired

* Dropped by the Centipede Demon.

### Notes & Tips

* Removed in New Game Plus.
* If this item is dropped, it will reappear in the chest in Firelink Shrine.
* This item can not be traded, since it will not appear to the other player(s) if dropped.
* Only Power Within will increase its fire damage. Nothing else will increase it.
