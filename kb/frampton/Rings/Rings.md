# Rings

**Rings** are accessories in Dark Souls**and Dark Souls Remastered** that provide various bonuses when equipped. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. It is possible to stack similar effects, such as combining Stamina regeneration between a ring and the Green Blossom consumable.

The **Dark Souls Remaster** version includes the DLC Artorias of the Abyss equipment as part of the base game.
