# Lingering Dragoncrest Ring

| Lingering Dragoncrest Ring |
| --- |
| 4039 |
| Boosts the length of the effects of sorceries. |

**Lingering Dragoncrest Ring** is a Ring in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered. **Lingering Dragoncrest Ring**, engraved with a lingering dragon, extends the duration of sorcery effects. It is particularly beneficial for characters not highly specialized in magic, as the time extensions are fixed. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> A special ring granted to only the most accomplished sorcerers at Vinheim Dragon School.
> The ring is engraved with a lingering dragon, and boosts the length of the effects of sorceries.

### Effect

* Increases the length of magic effects by 50%.
* Slows down the timer of effects while it is worn, meaning that 60 seconds of an effect only takes 40 seconds from the timer. As such, unequipping the ring after casting a spell will not keep the longer duration.
* Affects the following sorceries:
  + Hush - **Confirmed**
  + Fall Control - **Confirmed**
  + Cast Light - **Confirmed**
  + Hidden Body - **Confirmed**
  + Hidden weapon - **Confirmed**
  + Magic Shield - **Confirmed**
  + Strong Magic Shield - **Confirmed**
  + Magic Weapon - **Confirmed**
  + Great Magic Weapon - **Confirmed**
  + Crystal Magic Weapon - **Confirmed**
* Affects the following pyromancies:
  + Flash Sweat - **Confirmed**
  + Power Within - **Confirmed**
  + Iron Flesh - **Confirmed**
* Does not affect any miracles.
* Does not affect buffs from other sources, such as the Channeler's Trident or the Dragon Torso Stone.

### Location

* Can be bought from Griggs of Vinheim for 20,000 souls.

### Application

* This ring is more useful for characters not spec'd into high level magic, as these time increases are static.
* Great to use for increasing the duration of Magic Weapon, Great Magic Weapon and Crystal Magic Weapon, as unlike the Bellowing Dragoncrest Ring, it does affect Weapon Buffs (sorceries only).
* Also useful with Power Within since it increases the duration, but not the total HP loss.

### Notes & Tips

* This ring reappears in *Dark Souls 2* and *Dark Souls 3*.
