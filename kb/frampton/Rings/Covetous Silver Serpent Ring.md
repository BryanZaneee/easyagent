# Covetous Silver Serpent Ring

| Covetous Silver Serpent Ring |
| --- |
| 4022 |
| Rewards its wearer with additional souls for each kill. |

**Covetous Silver Serpent Ring** is a Ring in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered. This silver ring, engraved with a serpent, rewards the wearer with 20% more souls per kill. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> The serpent is an imperfect dragon and symbol of the Undead. Its habit of devouring prey
> even larger than itself has led to an association of gluttony. This silver ring, engraved with
> the serpent, rewards its wearer with additional souls for each kill.

### Effect

* Increases souls absorbed for each kill by 20%.
* Stacks with Symbol of Avarice: combined total bonus is +44%.
* Also applies to PVP Souls.

### Acquired

* Found in Tomb of the Giants, close to the second Bonfire.
* After encountering and killing the 2nd skeleton dog there's a skeleton archer trying to shoot at you. After killing the archer, drop down by the corpse behind him, and there's a hidden path to the ring (drop a Prism Stone off the edge to see the path).
* It's also possible to jump up to the ledge, where the ring is located, on the right side.
* **Note:** Be wary that after picking up the ring 5 skeleton towers will attack you.

### Application

* This is a fantastic ring that makes farming souls significantly easier. If you don't mind the occupied ring slot; it's viable to carry for the rest of the game.
* Offers no combat effects, its only purpose is to earn more souls from fallen enemies.
* After killing a boss, there is a slight pause before you earn the souls. Equip the ring before the souls arrive, and the bonus will still apply.

### Notes & Tips

* Stacks with the Symbol of Avarice and Overkill Bonus.
* This ring reappears in *Dark Souls 2* and *Dark Souls 3*.
* You do not get any bonus effect from wearing both Serpent Rings at once, but their individual effects stack (+200 item discovery and +20% souls gained)
