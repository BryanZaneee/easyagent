# Havel's Ring

| Havel's Ring |
| --- |
| 4000 |
| Boosts maximum Equip Load by 50%. |

**Havel's Ring** is a Ring in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Havel's Ring**, named after Havel the Rock, a battlefield companion of Lord Gwyn, this ring was worn by Havel's men to show faith in their leader and to carry heavier loads. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> This ring was named after Havel the Rock, Lord Gwyn's old battlefield compatriot.
> Havel's men wore the ring to express faith in their leader and to carry a heavier load.

### Effects

* Boosts maximum Equip Load by 50%.

### Location

* Obtained after defeating Havel The Rock in the shortcut between Undead Burg and Darkroot Basin, that can be accessed with either Master key or Watchtower Basement Key.

### Application

* This ring is a fantastic way to provide more defense for your character without the fear of a slower movement speed. Because its augmentation is percentage based this ring becomes more beneficial when the wearer has more stamina.
* Weapons in the ultra-large category usually weigh above 10 units, the heaviest weighing 28; so wearing this ring can help users of large weapons to be faster.
* Equipping Havel's ring changes max equipment load to 1.5 x base max equipment load. (+50%)
* Equipping Havel's ring and the Ring of Favor and Protection changes equipment load to 1.8 x base equipment load. (+80%)
* Equipping Havel's ring and the Ring of Favor and Protection and the Mask of the Father changes equipment load to 1.89 x base equipment load. (+89%)

### Notes & Tips

* Killing Havel at the same time he kills you will cause the ring to go away.
* Patch 1.06 nerfed Mask of the Father from 15% to 5%, which reduced the max equip increase from 2.07 to 1.89 x base equipment load. (from +107% to +89% after patch 1.06).
* This ring would reappear in *Dark Souls 3*.
