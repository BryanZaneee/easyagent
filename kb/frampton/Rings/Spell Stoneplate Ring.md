# Spell Stoneplate Ring

| Spell Stoneplate Ring |
| --- |
| 4007 |
| Boosts Defense Against Magic. |

**Spell Stoneplate Ring** is a Ring in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Spell Stoneplate Ring**, atoneplates symbolize true knighthood, granting strength to endure hardships. The blue stoneplate boosts defense against magic, symbolizing souls. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> Stoneplates, the symbol of a true knight, grant the strength to face various hardships.
> The blue stoneplate symbolizes souls, and boosts defense against magic.

### Effects

* Increases Magic Defense by 50 points.

### Location

* Sold by  Crestfallen Merchant in Sen's Fortress for 15,000 souls.

### Application

* This ring is a great way to improve your odds of surviving a magical attack, but this ring, as well as it's brethren, are enemy/area specific and generally not recommended to wear consistently.
