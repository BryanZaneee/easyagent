# Rusted Iron Ring

| Rusted Iron Ring |
| --- |
| 4025 |
| Can be used to remove the movement speed penalty. |

**Rusted Iron Ring** is a Ring in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered. This ring removes the movement speed penalty when traversing difficult terrains like deep water, swamps, and tar. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> This iron ring was used to shackle the guilty. It is terribly rusted, and faintly stained with blood.
> Those who find this strange ring to their liking will be pleased to find it easier to gain footing on poor ground such as swamps.

### Effect

* Removes the movement speed penalty while traveling in deep water, marsh, tar, etc.

### Acquired

* It is at the edge of the decrepit stairway behind the locked door on the third level's west wing, guarded by two Undead Soldiers, in the Northern Undead Asylum.

### Notes & Tips

* The ring's effect does **NOT** include slowing effects such as the Miracle spell Tranquil Walk of Peace.
* This ring is very helpful within Blighttown's swampy areas.
