# East Wood Grain Ring

| East Wood Grain Ring |
| --- |
| 4032 |
| Can be used to slow the loss of weapon durability. |

**East Wood Grain Ring** is a Ring in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered. **East Wood Grain Ring,**slows the loss of weapon durability, making it particularly useful for wielders of delicate swords from the East. However, it does not reduce durability loss from special weapon attacks. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> This special ring crafted in an Eastern land is made of gold, but with a wood grain crest on its surface. The ring slows the loss of weapon durability and is particularly useful to bearers of delicate swords crafted in the East.

### Effect

* Halves the rate at which weapons lose Durability.

### Acquired

* Purchase from Shiva of the East for 10,000 souls.

### Notes & Tips

* Especially effective for swords with low durability, Curved Swords and Crystal weapons in particular.
* The effect does not apply for special attacks from weapons, which lead to durability loss.
