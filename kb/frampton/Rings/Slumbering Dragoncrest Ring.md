# Slumbering Dragoncrest Ring

| Slumbering Dragoncrest Ring |
| --- |
| 4023 |
| Masks all sound emitting from its wearer. |

**Slumbering Dragoncrest Ring** is a Ring in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered. **Slumbering Dragoncrest Ring**, engraved with an everlasting dragon in silent slumber, this ring masks all sound emitted by its wearer. When paired with the Ring of Fog, it renders the user nearly undetectable. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> This ring was secretly worn by a surreptitious sorcerer at Vinheim Dragon School.
> The ring is engraved with an everlasting dragon in silent slumber, and masks all sound emitting from its wearer.

### Effect

* Wearer produces no sound whatsoever.

### Location

* Found in Sen's Fortress, on a rafter overlooking the first bridge with swinging blades. This area is accessed by dropping onto a walkway inhabited by a Snake-Headed Mage above a portion of the second ramp with rolling boulders that the player encounters.
  + After dropping onto this walkway and dealing with the Serpent Mage, go through a broken hole in the wall at one end of the platform, pass through a small jail cell, and drop down onto the rafters below the far wall of the jail cell. Turn left and follow the rafter to a small corner, where the ring is located on a corpse.
* Also dropped by Griggs of Vinheim

### Application

* This ring, coupled with the Ring of Fog, allows the user to be almost undetectable.
* Surprisingly enough, this ring really shines in PvE, where many enemies will not turn around as they usually would, securing the player a back-stab, or the ability to simply run past them.
* There is also a spell for this; Hush.

### Notes and tips

* This ring reappears in *Dark Souls 2* and *Dark Souls 3*.
