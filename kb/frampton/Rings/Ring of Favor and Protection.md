# Ring of Favor and Protection

| Ring of Favor and Protection |
| --- |
| 4029 |
| Can be used to boosts HP, Stamina, and Equip Load by 20% each |

**Ring of Favor and Protection** is a Ring in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered. This ring symbolizes the favor and protection of the goddess Fina, known for her "fateful beauty." It boosts HP, Stamina, and Equip Load by 20% each. However, it breaks permanently if removed. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> A ring symbolizing the favor and protection of the goddess Fina, known in legend to possess "fateful beauty."
>
> This ring boosts its wearer's HP, stamina, and max equipment load, but breaks if ever removed.

### Effect

* Boosts HP, Stamina and Equip Load by 20% each. **Permanently** breaks when removed.

### Location

* Dropped by Lautrec after you kill him.
* Also received from trading the Xanthous Crown to Snuggly
* Location Video Timestamp 28:32

### Application

* This ring will boost HP, Stamina and Equip Load by 20% each, but permanently breaks if it is ever removed.
* Utilizing this ring is also the only way to increase Stamina above 160 (Max of 192).
* Stacks with: Mask of the Mother, Tiny Being's Ring, Havel's Ring and Mask of the Father.

### Notes & Tips

* If you kill Lautrec before he gets back to the Firelink Shrine you won't be able to pick up his armor set later.
* If you kick him off the cliff, then reload the area. The ring will be where he was sitting. This is the easiest way to kill him.
* This ring reappears in *Dark Souls 3* as the Ring of Favor, and can be removed without breaking.
