# Old Witch's Ring

| Old Witch Ring |
| --- |
| 4037 |
| Can be used to talk to Quelaag's Sister. |

**Old Witch Ring** is a Ring in **Dark Souls**. This ring allows you to communicate with Quelaag's Sister, who would otherwise remain silent. You can obtain the ring by trading a Sunlight Maggot to Snuggly the Crow or by selecting it as a starting gift when creating your character. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> Old ring from an old witch. Engraved minutely with indecipherable script, but seemingly useless.

### Old Witch Ring Effect

* Allows the player to talk to Quelaag's Sister. Without the ring choosing to talk to her only prompts a "...?" response.

### Old Witch Ring Location

* Trading a Sunlight Maggot to Snuggly
* Can also be selected as a starting gift.

### Old Witch Ring Application

* Only known effect is the ability to talk to Quelaag's Sister.
* Does not halve the amount of humanity needed to level up in the Chaos Covenant. This is probably a misconception related to leaving and rejoining the covenant, which puts you back at half of your previous offerings.
