# Ring of the Sun Princess

**Ring of the Sun Princess** is a Ring in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered. **Ring of the Sun Princess**, enhances the synergy of miracles and is given by Gwynevere when joining the Princess Guard Covenant. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> This ring is granted to those who enter a Covenant with Gwynevere, daughter of Lord Gwyn and the Princess of Sunlight. This slightly warm ring boosts the synergy of miracles.
>
> The Princess of Sunlight left Anor Londo along with many other deities, and later became wife to Flame God Flann.

### Effect

* Boosts Miracle Synergy by 1 level. Only reliable way to utilize Miracle Synergy on PS3 and Remaster, also making it the only reliable way to improve healing miracles. Affects Emit Force and Wrath of the Gods as well.

### Location

* Gift from Gwynevere upon joining the Princess Guard Covenant.
* Video Location Timestamp 27:47

### Notes & Tips

* Can be traded to for Snuggly 2x Divine Blessing.
* This ring reappears, albeit with a slightly different appearance and effect, in *Dark Souls 3* as the Sun Princess Ring.
