# Ring of Steel Protection

| Ring of Steel Protection |
| --- |
| 4020 |
| Increases Physical Defense by 50 points. |

**Ring of Steel Protection** is a Ring in Dark Souls and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Ring of Steel Protection**, once belonging to Knight King Rendal, this ring boosts its wearer's defense against physical attacks, making it an excellent choice for any build or armor type due to its solid defense increase. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> This ring belonged to the Knight King Rendal. It grants its wearer protection by boosting defense against physical attacks.
>
> Of the many legends surrounding the Knight King Rendal, one of the more well-known speaks of his standing down a giant drake and slashing it to pieces.

### Effects

* Increases Physical Defense by 50 points.

### Location

* Sen's Fortress at the top of the first rolling boulder area. A Serpent Mage is standing in the entrance, and the ring is in a chest behind him.
* Video Location Timestamp 6:52

### Application

* Since the vast majority of enemies primarily perform physical attacks, this ring offers much more universal mitigation against enemy damage than similar rings that add defense against a single type of magical damage.
* A great Ring for all builds and armor varieties, due to it solidly increasing defense.

### Notes & Tips

* This ring reappears in *Dark Souls 2* and *Dark Souls 3*.
