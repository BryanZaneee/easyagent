**Skull Lantern** is a Tool in **Dark Souls**. Tools are unique, reusable equipment pieces designed to assist players in multiple gameplay situations, ranging simple interactions to helping during difficult boss fights.

> Skull Lantern of the Catacombs necromancer. Droops from his long beard locks.
>  This lantern alights the Tomb of the Giants, Nito's light-devouring domain of death.
>  Also serves as a fire damage strike weapon.

### **Usage in**

* A light that is carried in the dark, used like a shield.
* When used in the Tomb of Giants it will also reveal hidden path-markers in the walls.
* Also serves as a fire damage strike weapon.
* Can be traded to Snuggly for a Ring of Fog.

### Location

* Rare drop (2%) from necromancers killed in the Catacombs.
* Found as a treasure in the Tomb of Giants, in the pit close to the first bonfire.
  + This is the same pit that holds Patches' 'treasure.'
* Video Location Timestamp 9:16

### Notes & Tips

* One of three light sources available. The other two being the Sunlight Maggot and the Cast Light spell.
* It can be used to reveal hidden walls in Oolacile Township. (AotA only)
