# Red Tearstone Ring

| Red Tearstone Ring |
| --- |
| 4001 |
| Boosts the attack of its wearer when in danger. |

**Red Tearstone Ring** is a Ring in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered. **Red Tearstone Ring,** the rare tearstone gem is believed to sense imminent death. This red tearstone from Carim increases the attack power of its wearer when they are in danger. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> The rare gem called tearstone has the uncanny ability to sense imminent death.
> This red tearstone from Carim boosts the attack of its wearer when in danger.

### Effects

* Boosts attack stats by 50% when health is below 20%. This effect is visible in the status screen.
* Due to the way damage is calculated, the increase in damage will be larger in practice, often close to 100%.
* Also affects damage done by magic, pyromancy and miracles.
* Does **not** affect usable items such as firebombs or throwing knives,nor weapon buffs such as Gold Pine Resin or Magic Weapon.

### Location

* Valley of Drakes, above the giant gate.
* Video Location Timestamp 3:39

### Application

* A reincarnation of the Rat ring set, but the attack bonus this time is more potent (used to be 30% on the rat ring). This ring is recommended for anyone who wants to capitalize on a risk-reward method of play.
* This attack bonus applies to most forms of damage your character deals, other than usable items and weapon buffs.
* Since damage dealt decreases dramatically if your attack is lower than the enemy's corresponding defense stat, this ring - in conjunction with two-handing your weapon - can help with farming high defense enemies such as Drakes and Crystal Golems.

### Notes & Tips

* Boosts attack stats by 50% when health is below 20%.
* This ring reappears in *Dark Souls 2* and *Dark Souls 3*.
