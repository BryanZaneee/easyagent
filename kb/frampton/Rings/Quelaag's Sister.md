**Daughter of Chaos** is a Character in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**.

### Information

The Daughter of Chaos is one of the seven Daughters of Chaos, and sister to the boss Quelaag. She is a demon, changed through the Bed of Chaos, with the upper half of a woman attached to the body of a massive spider, and has contracted a sickness called 'blightpus', weakening her greatly. She is both the leader of the Chaos Servant Covenant and the Fire Keeper of the bonfire she sits beside.

* She is found behind a hidden wall in the chamber underneath the second bell tower in Quelaag's Domain.
  + Go down the stairs from the bell lever, cross directly over the circular platform from the bottom of the stairs and strike the wall on the other side of the room to reveal an additional chamber.
  + As you move down the subsequent passage, there will be an egg carrier blocking the way - Eingyi. He will ask you if you are a new servant, and express confusion at the lack of eggs on you. If you say 'YES', he will move out of the way and allow you to pass. A bonfire will be directly in front of you, and the Daughter of Chaos will be partly sunk into a wall on the right.
* She appears mute initially, but if you equip Old Witch's Ring (obtained either as a starting gift, or by trading a Sunlight Maggot to Snuggly in the Undead Asylum) you will be able to understand her speech.
  + Note: it is still possible to join the covenant even without the Old Witch's Ring.
* Her dialogue seems to indicate she is ill, to the point that she can't tell who you are, and assumes you are Quelaag.
* Striking her will make her recoil in fear, but she is unwilling (or unable) to fight back.

In Dark Souls 3 a humanoid spider corpse, much resembling the Fair Lady, can be found behind an illusory wall in Smouldering Lake with a Quelana Pyromancy Tome nearby.

### Actions

* **Upgrade Flasks** - The Daughter of Chaos is able to upgrade your Flasks using Fire Keeper Souls.
* **Join Covenant -** Will allow you to join the Chaos Servant Covenant.
* **(Upon having Joined Covenant) Leave Covenant -** Allows you to leave the Chaos Servant Covenant.

### Drops

* Upon killing her, she drops a Fire Keeper Soul.
  + Note that killing her will render the bonfire unusable until the next NG+.
