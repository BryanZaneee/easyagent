# Cloranthy Ring

| Cloranthy Ring |
| --- |
| 4004 |
| Increases Stamina regeneration by 20 points/second |

**Cloranthy Ring** is a Ring in **Dark Souls**. This ring boosts your Stamina regeneration by 20 points per second. It can be found in a hidden area near the center of the Great Hollow. To access it, you need to jump and land on the middle of the large tree within the Great Hollow. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> This ancient ring, engraved with a large green flower, is of unknown origin.
>
> Hastens the regeneration of stamina.

### Effects

* Increases Stamina regeneration by 20 points/second (base stamina regeneration is 45 points/second).

### Location

* Found in a secret area near the middle of The Great Hollow by jumping into the middle of the tree.
* Video Location Timestamp 9:42

### Notes

* Effect stacks with Grass Crest Shield, Mask of the Child and Green Blossom or Power Within.
* This ring would reappear as the Chloranthy Ring in *Dark Souls 2* and *Dark Souls 3*, further emphasizing that it was likely misspelled by accident.
