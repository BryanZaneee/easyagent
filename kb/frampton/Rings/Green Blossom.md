**Green Blossom** is a Consumable in **Dark Souls**. Consumable Items grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> Green weed, shaped like a flower. Temporary boost to stamina recovery speed.
> This uniquely bitter, biting herb is sometimes harvested in large quantities, but normally it is an annual plant found near water.

### Usage

* Increases Stamina recovery speed by 40 per second when consumed.
* Duration: 60 seconds.
* Stacks with: Cloranthy Ring, Grass Crest Shield and Mask of the Child.

### Location

* Sold by Crestfallen Merchant for 1,000 souls. (Unlimited amount.)
* Sold by Marvelous Chester for 1,200 souls **(AotA)** (Unlimited amount.)
* Drops from red Frog-Ray (15% or 25% Chance - Darkroot Garden)

### Notes & Tips

* Green Blossom is a Self Buff and interferes with other such buffs (i.e. Great Magic Barrier, Transient Curse, Power Within, etc.). As long as it's active it's not possible to cast other Self Buffs and vice versa.
