# Rare Ring of Sacrifice

**Rare Ring of Sacrifice** is a Ring in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered. **Rare Ring of Sacrifice**, this especially rare magenta-shaded ring prevents the loss of items upon death and removes any curses afflicting the wearer, though it breaks upon use. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> This mystical ring was created in a sacrificial rite of Velka, the Goddess of Sin.
> The magenta-shaded ring is especially rare. Its wearer will lose nothing upon death,
> and will be freed from any curse whatsoever, but the ring itself breaks.

### Effects

* Lose no souls or humanity upon death.
* No bloodstain will be left behind. Preserves any existing bloodstain for retrieval after revival.
* Prevents receiving the Cursed status after death but does not cure an existing curse status.
* Ring breaks on death (1 use).

### Locations

* Sen's Fortress, in the tower with Ricard the Archer.
* New Londo Ruins, on the roof near Ingward.
* Lost Izalith, on a branch right below a ledge.
* Trade one Twin Humanities with Snuggly to receive one Rare Ring of Sacrifice.
* Video Location Timestamp 24:04

### Application

* Keep in mind that wearing this ring basically wastes a slot, which puts you at a minor disadvantage already.
* Very useful in Seath's first encounter to preserve your souls and humanity.

### Notes & Tips

* The ring won't cure your status if you are already cursed.
* It will only cancel the curse effect if you are killed by curse while wearing it.
