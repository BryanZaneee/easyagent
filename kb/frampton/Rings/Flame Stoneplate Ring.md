# Flame Stoneplate Ring

| Flame Stoneplate Ring |
| --- |
| 4005 |
| Boosts Defence Against Flame. |

**Flame Stoneplate Ring** is a Ring in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Flame Stoneplate Ring**, boosts defense against fire, symbolizing flame. This ring improves survival odds against fire-based attacks, but like its counterparts, it is enemy-specific and generally not recommended for consistent wear. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> Stoneplates, the symbol of a true knight, grant the strength to face various hardships.
> The red stoneplate symbolizes fire, and boosts defence against flame.

### Effects

* Increases Fire Defense by 50.

### Location

* Sen's Fortress, after passing through the castle, you go outside and have to go across pillars, before those pillars are stairs going down. Two Balder Knights will be guarding a chest. The ring is there.
* Video Location Timestamp 19:32

### Application

* This ring is a great way to improve your odds of surviving a fire-based attack, but this ring, as well as it's brethren, are enemy specific and generally not recommended to wear consistently.
