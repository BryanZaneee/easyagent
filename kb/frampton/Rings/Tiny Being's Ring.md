# Tiny Being's Ring

---

| Tiny Being's Ring |
| --- |
| life ring |
| Increases maximum HP by 5%. |

**Tiny Being's Ring** is a Ring in Dark Souls and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Tiny Being's Ring, m**ade from an ancient, small red jewel, this ring grants a slight increase to HP. Rings can provide various powers, large and small, and their effective use can ease one's journey. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. The description of this item during character creation is incorrect when choosing it as a starting gift. The description claims that the ring regenerates lost HP over time. The actual item has no such effect. This error was fixed in Dark Souls Remastered.

> Ring made of an ancient tiny red jewel. Grants small increase to HP. Rings grant powers large and small.
> Their discovery, and effective use, can make one's journey easier.

### Effects

* Increases maximum HP by 5%.

### Locations

* Can be selected as a starting gift.
* Reward from Siegmeyer of Catarina after defeating nearby Silver Knights in Anor Londo.

### Application

* Stacks with Ring of Favor and Protection and Mask of the Mother.
* Mostly useful at higher HP values, and potentially in combination with the other HP items, because of the low percentage of increase. All items combined gives a ~45% increase to HP, and will push HP above 2000 at 43 Vitality.

### Notes and Tips

* This ring would reappear as the Life Ring in *Dark Souls 2* and *Dark Souls 3*.
