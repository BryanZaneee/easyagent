# Thunder Stoneplate Ring

| Thunder Stoneplate Ring |
| --- |

| Boosts Defence Against Lightning. |

**Thunder Stoneplate Ring** is a Ring in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered. **Thunder Stoneplate Ring**, stoneplates symbolize true knighthood, granting strength to endure various challenges. The yellow stoneplate enhances defense against lightning, improving survival odds against lightning-based attacks. However, like its counterparts, it is enemy-specific and generally not recommended for constant use. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> Stoneplates, the symbol of a true knight, grant the strength to face various hardships.
> The yellow stoneplate symbolizes thunder, and boosts defence against lightning.

### Effects

* Increases Lightning Defense by 50 points.

### Location

* Sold by Crestfallen Merchant in Sen's Fortress for 15,000 souls.

### Application

* This ring is a great way to improve your odds of surviving a lightning-based attack, but this ring, as well as it's brethren, are enemy specific and generally not recommended to wear consistently. Thunder is a very rare form of damage in PvE, so this ring is usually a wasted slot.
