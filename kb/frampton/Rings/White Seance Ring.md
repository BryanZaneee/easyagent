# White Seance Ring

| White Seance Ring |
| --- |
| 4014 |
| It grants additional attunement slots. |

**White Seance Ring** is a Ring in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered. **White Seance Ring,** grants extra attunement slots, which can be used for any type of spell, whether Sorcery, Miracle, or Pyromancy. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> A divine ring entrusted to the head bishop of the Way of White and apostle to Allfather Lloyd, uncle to Lord Gwyn.
> It grants additional attunement slots.
> The head bishop of the Way of White is the guardian of law and caste, and one of the great royals of Thorolund.

### Effect

* Grants 1 additional Attunement slot.

### Acquired

* Found in the The Duke's Archives. After being imprisoned, climb the ladder leading to the prison's exit. Before going through however, look left and you should see thin wooden planks with rods sticking out the sides. Drop down onto the second line of wooden planks and it will lead to a room containing a corpse. Looting this corpse will give you this ring and the Maiden Set.

### Application

* Can be used for any spell, be it Sorcery, Miracle or Pyromancy.
* The last attuned spell, when at capacity, will be disabled if the ring is removed.
* This ring also grants an attunement slot if you have none.
* Effect is identical to the Darkmoon Seance Ring, both can also be equipped simultaneously for 2 extra slots.
