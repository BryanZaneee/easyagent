# Regular Upgrades

| Regular Ascension in Dark Souls |

| Each upgrade level will increase the **base damage** of Regular Weapons, **damage scaling** (strengthStrength and/or dexterityDexterity), and **stability** of regular shields.  This is suitable for a long term use as it allows weapon to be repaired and thus the weapon will not break unlike crystal weapons. |

**Regular Upgrades** are a type of Upgrade in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered.** They enable players to "Ascend" weapons and shields in a specific path.

### Regular Upgrade path and Requirements

**Regular Upgrades** for weapon and shield are performed by Andre of Astora at the Undead Parish.

Note that each upgrade level requires a number of soul that varies depending on the weapon.

* Regular upgrade from 0 to +5 requires 9 Titanite Shards.
* Regular upgrade from +5 to +6 requires a Large Ember and Large Titanite Shard to ascend.
* Regular upgrade from +5 to +10 requires 9 Large Titanite Shards.
* Regular upgrade from +10 to +11 requires a Very Large Ember and Titanite Chunk to ascend.
* Regular upgrade from +10 to +14 requires 7 Titanite Chunks.
* Regular upgrade from +14 to +15 requires a Titanite Slab.

#### From 0 to +5

* Regular upgrade from 0 to +1 requires 1 Titanite Shard.
* Regular upgrade from +1 to +2 requires 1 Titanite Shard.
* Regular upgrade from +2 to +3 requires 2 Titanite Shard.
* Regular upgrade from +3 to +4 requires 2 Titanite Shard.
* Regular upgrade from +4 to +5 requires 3 Titanite Shard.

#### From +5 to +10

* Regular upgrade from +5 to +6 requires 1 Large Titanite Shard and a Large Ember.
* Regular upgrade from +6 to +7 requires 1 Large Titanite Shard.
* Regular upgrade from +7 to +8 requires 2 Large Titanite Shard.
* Regular upgrade from +8 to +9 requires 2 Large Titanite Shard.
* Regular upgrade from +9 to +10 requires 3 Large Titanite Shard.

#### From +10 to +14

* Regular upgrade from +10 to +11 requires 1 Titanite Chunk and a Very Large Ember.
* Regular upgrade from +11 to +12 requires 1 Titanite Chunk.
* Regular upgrade from +12 to +13 requires 2 Titanite Chunk.
* Regular upgrade from +13 to +14 requires 3 Titanite Chunk.

#### From +14 to +15

* Regular upgrade from +14 to +15 requires 1 Titanite Slab.
