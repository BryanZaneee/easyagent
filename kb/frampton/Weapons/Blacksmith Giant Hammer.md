**Blacksmith Giant Hammer** is a Weapon in Dark Souls and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Blacksmith Giant Hammer** allows heavy, high-impact swings crafted for lightning-tuned weapons by the Giant Blacksmith. Hammers deliver fast, powerful blunt-force strikes, excelling against armored enemies and skeletons. While they can break an opponent's guard and stagger them into a combo, they suffer from long recovery times. This makes each swing a committed risk, as a missed attack leaves the wielder vulnerable to immediate counterattacks.

> Wooden hammer of the giant blacksmith in Anor Londo who handles lightning weapons.
>  The giant blacksmith forges with this wooden hammer, as it would be hazardous to handle lightning weapons with metals.

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Dropped by the Giant Blacksmith in Anor Londo.

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
* A high damage weapon with a decent move set. While it requires killing a useful NPC, it might be worth it for the weapon's high damage output. If you want to try it out, consider killing the Giant Blacksmith before moving to NG+.
