**Blacksmith Hammer** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Blacksmith Hammer** grants durable and efficient strikes once held by Andre of Astora, reflecting a weapon built for service and craft. Hammers deliver fast, powerful blunt-force strikes, excelling against armored enemies and skeletons. While they can break an opponent's guard and stagger them into a combo, they suffer from long recovery times. This makes each swing a committed risk, as a missed attack leaves the wielder vulnerable to immediate counterattacks.

> Metal hammer of Andre of Astora, blacksmith at the Old Church.
>  Can be used as a strike weapon, but better left in the hands of its talented owner."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Murdering Blacksmith Andre of Astora

### Hints and Tips

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.

Chaos Upgrades are performed by Blacksmith Vamos in The Catacombs.
Chaos Upgrades require a Fire+5 weapon and to hand in the Chaos Flame Ember
Fire Reinforcement reduces Fire Damage but adds a bonus based on the Humanity you hold.
To reach max, you need: 1000 souls, 7 Red Titanite Chunks and 1 Red Titanite Slab
