**Parrying dagger** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered. Parrying Dagger** is a specialized off-hand blade designed for follow-up attacks after a successful parry. Daggers are swift, precise weapons with a short reach, ideal for close-quarters combat. Their rapid attacks allow players to weave in successive strikes to stagger foes while maintaining the mobility to dodge. Typically featuring high critical damage and good Dexterity scaling, daggers are versatile enough to be wielded in the left hand, where they can also be used to Parry incoming attacks.

> This dagger is useful after parrying and is normally equipped in the left hand."
> "A favorite of the knights of Carim, who are famous for fighting without a shield."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Can be found on a corpse in upper New Londo Ruins, in the same area as the first Banshee.
* Video Location Timestamp 2:38

### Hints and Tips

* Can be given to Kingseeker Frampt to receive 50 Souls.
* As a conventional weapon, it is an ordinary Dagger that deals a tiny amount less damage. The crit multiplier is the same, the stat scaling is nearly identical.
* The Parrying Dagger has the longest parry frame window in the game, comparable to that of Target Shield or Buckler.
* The parrying window has 8 frames, instead of 6 like every other (regular) Shield.

Chaos Upgrades require a Fire +5 weapon and to hand in the Chaos Flame Ember.
Fire Reinforcement reduces Fire Damage but adds a bonus based on the Humanity you hold.
To reach max, you need: 500 souls, 7 Red Titanite Chunks and 1 Red Titanite Slab.
