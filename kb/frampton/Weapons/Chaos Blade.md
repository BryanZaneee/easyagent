**Chaos Blade** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Chaos Blade** grants long-reaching vertical slashes ideal for tail cutting and high damage output, though it cannot be enchanted or buffed. Katanas are long-reach, fast-swinging Dexterity weapons that deal both Slash and Thrust damage and can inflict Bleed buildup. While their sharp blades are effective for parrying when wielded in the left hand, they tend to have low durability, requiring careful management.

> A curved sword born from the soul of Quelaag, daughter of the Witch of Izalith, who was transformed into a chaos demon.
>
> This blade inherits only the chaotic nature of Quelaag, and has a unique speckled design. Blade wielder erodes along with opponents."

### How to Get / Where to Find

Ascend a +10 katana with the Soul of Quelaag at the Giant Blacksmith in Anor Londo.

### Upgrade cost

* 5,000 souls (each upgrade)
* 1 x Demon Titanite (+1, +2)
* 2 x Demon Titanite (+3, +4)
* 4 x Demon Titanite (+5)

### Hints and Tips

The one-handed Strong Attack is replaced by a dashing horizontal slash, and it eats 20 HP with every successful blow.

* Give to Frampt to receive 1,000 souls.
* You will take no self-damage while performing a backstab or a riposte.
* Special: Damage scales with Humanity
* Bleed build up is 36 per strike, and once inflicted will tear 30% of the opponents HP.
* With 40 Dex and 10+ Humanity, Chaos Blade +5 has an attack value of 450.
* With 50 Dex and 10+ Humanity, Chaos Blade +5 has an attack value of 455, proving how little of a difference in damage there is after 40 Dex.
* Its length and vertical slashing motion make this weapon ideal for cutting off tails.
* This weapon cannot be enchanted with spells or items.
* If using this weapon Ring of the Evil Eye, Replenishment, or Bountiful Sunlight are ideal to counteract the damage you take while striking.
* Useful moveset (except strong attacks), fast swing speed and recovery with high attack rating and bleed make the chaos blade one of the best PvP weapons.
* This weapon would reappear in both *Dark Souls 2* and *Dark Souls 3*.
