**Longbow**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Longbow**, a larger bow for long-range engagements, useful for safely picking off strong foes from afar. Bows are dedicated long-range weapons that require arrows to fire. They are split into two distinct types: short bows and long bows, each with unique firing mechanics and no melee function.

> Large bow. Projectile weapon for experienced hunters.

### How to Get / Where to Find

* A ledge in Darkroot Garden, on the path leading down to Darkroot Basin. The corpse it is found on also contains the Hunter armor and some arrows.
* Drops from Undead Crystal Archer (2% chance - the Duke's Archives)

### Notes

Equip arrows to use. Hold bow and press L1 or LB to aim. Aim for heads of humanoid foes. Press L2/R2 or LT/RT to change type of arrows.

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* This bow is classified as a Long bow. It fires slower than short bows.
* The Long Bow is a larger version of the short bow, has slightly higher stat requirements to wield. Very useful for luring things out and killing strong foes from a safe distance.
* **Fun Fact:** when you draw the bow the string does not actually get pulled back (xbox 360 confirmed ) (the bow is fully drawn in PTDE)
* **Demon's souls version:** Different in apperance and has a range of 60 instead of 50.
