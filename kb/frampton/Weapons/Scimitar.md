**Scimitar** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Scimitar** allows rapid chained slashes that deal modest individual damage but become lethal in fluid combinations, though less effective against armored foes. Curved Swords are agile, medium-range weapons that excel in Dexterity scaling and feature a fluid moveset. They primarily deal Slash and Thrust damage, making them ideal for confined spaces where their rapid, hard-to-evade attacks overwhelm foes. Their unique kick is replaced by an evasive slash that flips the wielder backward after striking.

> Small, curved sword. Each hit inflicts little damage, but fluid chain attacks are deadly."
>  "The scimitar's sharp slashing attacks are effective against cloth and flesh, but not against metal armor or tough scales."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Starting weapon for Wanderer
* Sold by the Undead Merchant in the Undead Burg for 600 souls.
* Drops from skeleton with scimitar & shield (2% chance - Firelink Shrine Graveyard and The Catacombs)

### Hints and Tips

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* Compared to the Falchion, this weapon does very slightly less damage, but is also 1 weight point lighter.
* Kick motion is replaced with a backwards flip atta**ck**
