**Giant's Halberd**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Giant’s Halberd**, a lightning-tinged brass halberd of the giant sentinels, extremely heavy and difficult for non-giants to wield. Halberds function like heavier, more powerful spears with versatile damage types. Their slower, sweeping attacks require precise timing to avoid lengthy recovery. This weapon class can be enhanced by items like the Leo Ring to significantly boost its damage output.

> Large halberd made of old brass, used by the giant sentinels of Anor Londo."
>  "Brass contains traces of lightning, but as the halberd is designed for the giant sentinels, its weight is unbearable."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Drops from Sentinel (2% chance - Anor Londo)
* Drops from Royal Sentinel (2% chance - Anor Londo)
* Bought from Giant Blacksmith in Anor Londo.
* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
