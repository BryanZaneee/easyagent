**Large Club** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Large Club** allows solid, far-reaching strikes with useful roll attacks, offering slightly less power than the Great Club but better one-handed strong attacks and added poison buildup. Great Hammers are colossal, strength-demanding weapons typically wielded with both hands. Their immense weight translates into devastating swings that excel at staggering foes and shattering their guard.

> Large wooden club."
>  "The giant Undead of Blighttown wield these clubs, which became poisonous from the influence of their surroundings."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Drops from club wielder Infested Barbarian (2%  Chance - Blighttown).

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* Special: Has the Poison Auxiliary effect
* Special: Two-handed R2 attack causes Knockdown
* Buffing this weapon with Rotten Pine Resin will not double its poison power, since they don't stack, but it will double the build-up power. This means that only half the amount of attacks is needed to inflict the poison status, compared to a unbuffed version.
* Good damage, good reach and a good roll attacks. Slightly lower damage and shorter range than the Great Club but has better one-handed strong attack and poison auxiliary effect.
* Unlike the Great Club this weapon has a poison auxiliary effect.

Chaos Upgrades require a Fire+5 weapon and to hand in the Chaos Flame Ember
Fire Reinforcement reduces Fire Damage but adds a bonus based on the Humanity you hold.
To reach max, you need: 4,000 souls, 7 Red Titanite Chunks and 1 Red Titanite Slab
