**Canvas Talisman**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Canvas Talisman**, a pilgrim’s talisman favored by clerics, serving as the basic early-game miracle conduit. Talismans are wielded in order to cast Miracles.

> Medium for casting miracles of the Gods. Canvas Talisman is for clerics on a pilgrimage.
>  Equip talisman to cast miracles. Attune miracles from a scroll at a bonfire. Most miracles have limited number of uses."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Starting equipment for Cleric.
* Sold by Patches for 1,000 souls.

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
* Recommended for Faith levels: 27-39 (27-43 not counting the Darkmoon Talisman).
* For further info on Magic Adjustment values at every Faith level, see: Faith-Int Talisman Magic Adjustment Values.
* You can block with a talisman while two-handing it.
