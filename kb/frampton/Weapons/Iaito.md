**Iaito** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls**. This katana has higher Dexterity requirements than the Uchigatana and also offers superior Dexterity scaling, making it a good choice for Dexterity-based builds. It features the Bleed effect, with a buildup of 33 per attack, which can be particularly effective against enemies with high health pools. You can acquire this weapon by leaping onto a platform near the upper bonfire in Blighttown. Katanas are long-reach, fast-swinging Dexterity weapons that deal both Slash and Thrust damage and can inflict Bleed buildup. While their sharp blades are effective for parrying when wielded in the left hand, they tend to have low durability, requiring careful management.

> Katana forged in an Eastern land. The Katana is drawn from the scabbard at lightning speed, using an Iaijutsu technique. Iaijutsu allows the wielder to charge forward to swiftly cut down distant foes."

### Where to Find

* Found by leaping to platform, close to the upper bonfire in Blighttown.
* Video Location Timestamp 5:14

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* Strong attack is altered to forward dashing quickdraw.
* Bleed build up is 33 per strike, and once inflicted will tear 30% of the opponents HP.
