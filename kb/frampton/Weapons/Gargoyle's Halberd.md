**Gargoyle's Halberd**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls**. This weapon boasts higher base damage compared to the regular Halberd, but it has lower scaling. As a result, both weapons are somewhat balanced in overall effectiveness. You can obtain this weapon as a drop from either the Bell Gargoyles or the Anor Londo Gargoyles. Halberds function like heavier, more powerful spears with versatile damage types. Their slower, sweeping attacks require precise timing to avoid lengthy recovery. This weapon class can be enhanced by items like the Leo Ring to significantly boost its damage output.

> Halberd of the gargoyle guarding the Bell of Awakening in the Undead Church."
>  "Perfectly standard bronze halberd without any special power."

### Where to Find

* Random drop from either the Bell Gargoyles or the two Anor Londo Gargoyles. Can be farmed for by killing only one Bell Gargoyle and quitting the game/dying.
* A Gargoyle is located at Undead Parish. Anor Londo has two more Gargoyles.
* Video Location Timestamp 2:55

### Notes & Tips

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* Special: Increases Bleeding, Poison and Toxin resistance by 25%. (inventory screen = +60/+20, but equipped = 25%)
* Has a moveset similar to the Scythe, instead of the normal Halberd.
* For non-scaling infusions such as Lightning and Chaos, this is typically the strongest halberd option, as it has the highest base damage of the four infusable halberds, and the Halberd and Lucerne do not have their usual benefits of much stronger counter hits due to the split damage.

Chaos Upgrades are performed by Blacksmith Vamos in The Catacombs.
Chaos Upgrades require a Fire+5 weapon and to hand in the Chaos Flame Ember
Fire Reinforcement reduces Fire Damage but adds a bonus based on the Humanity you hold.
To reach max, you need: 1000 souls, 7 Red Titanite Chunks and 1 Red Titanite Slab
