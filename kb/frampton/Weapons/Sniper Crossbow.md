**Sniper Crossbow** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Sniper Crossbow**, a long-range crossbow of Carim marksmen that offers extreme distance but requires high Dexterity to use effectively. Crossbows are powerful ranged weapons that fire bolts, capable of high base damage and devastating headshots, though they lack the zoom function of bows. With no stat scaling, their effectiveness relies on elemental upgrades. They can also be fired instantly at close range for melee utility.

> Large crossbow with long distance used by Carim snipers. Often used with sniper bolts.
> But while crossbows are normally easy to use, the Sniper Crossbow requires dexterity."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Sen's Fortress. Close to bonfire, right below the broken bridge.
* Video Location Timestamp 22:25

### Notes

* Can not be ascended to Chaos or Raw Weapon
* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
