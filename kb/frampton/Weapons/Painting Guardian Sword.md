**Painting Guardian Sword** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Painting Guardian Sword** grants extremely fast, agile slashes with short reach, trading range for speed and precision. Curved Swords are agile, medium-range weapons that excel in Dexterity scaling and feature a fluid moveset. They primarily deal Slash and Thrust damage, making them ideal for confined spaces where their rapid, hard-to-evade attacks overwhelm foes. Their unique kick is replaced by an evasive slash that flips the wielder backward after striking.

> Curved sword of Anor Londo painting guards. Unique shape with flat tip."
> "The guardians, who strike down those who dare threaten the paintings, attack in a continuous, circular dancing motion, a technique passed down through generations."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Drops from Painting Guardians (2% Chance - Anor Londo).

### Notes & Tips

* Can be given to Kingseeker Frampt to receive 50 Souls.
* At 40 Dex, this weapon will surpass any pure physical damage curved sword in terms of AR, as well as being buffable and inflicting bleed.
* Aside from the S scaling for Int at Enchanted +5, this sword is better left at Regular upgrade and buffed with Miracles and Spells, considering it's attack speed and base AR.
* This weapon has very short Range, slightly longer than that of Daggers.
* R2 heavy attacks can be chained with each other into a combo.

### Lore

* Painting Guardians are connected to Filianore of DS3 and also to Dragons.

Chaos Upgrades are performed by Blacksmith Vamos in The Catacombs.
Chaos Upgrades require a Fire +5 weapon and to hand in the Chaos Flame Ember.
Fire Reinforcement reduces Fire Damage but adds a bonus based on the Humanity you hold.
To reach max, you need: 1,000 souls, 7 Red Titanite Chunks and 1 Red Titanite Slab.
