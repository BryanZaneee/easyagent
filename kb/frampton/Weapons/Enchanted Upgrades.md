**Enchanted Upgrades** are a type of Upgrade in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered.** They enable players to "Ascend" weapons and shields in a specific path.

### Enchanted Upgrade path and Requirements

**Enchanted Upgrades** for weapon and shield (with +5 Magic Upgrade) are performed by Rickert of Vinheim, found in New Londo Ruins, after giving him the Enchanted Ember.

Note that each upgrade level requires a number of soul that varies depending on the weapon.

* Regular upgrade from +5 to Magic +0 requires Green Titanite Shard to ascend.
* Magic upgrade from +0 to +5 requires 9 Green Titanite Shards.
* Magic upgrade from +5 to Enchanted +0 requires Enchanted Ember and Blue Titanite Chunk to ascend.
* Enchanted upgrade from +0 to +4 requires 8 Blue Titanite Chunks.
* Enchanted upgrade from +4 to +5 requires 1 Blue Titanite Slab.

#### From 0 to +4

* Enchanted upgrade from +0 to +1 requires 1 Blue Titanite Chunk.
* Enchanted upgrade from +1 to +2 requires 1 Blue Titanite Chunk.
* Enchanted upgrade from +2 to +3 requires 2 Blue Titanite Chunks.
* Enchanted upgrade from +3 to +4 requires 3 Blue Titanite Chunks.

#### From +4 to +5

* Enchanted upgrade from +4 to +5 requires 1 Blue Titanite Slab.
