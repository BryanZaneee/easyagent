**Spear**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Spear** offers long, precise thrusts usable while shielding, effective against armored foes but limited by narrow hitbox and shield vulnerability. Spears are long-reaching thrusting weapons ideal for a defensive playstyle, allowing you to attack while keeping your guard up. Their relatively quick jabs are effective for interrupting enemy attacks. However, their predictable stabbing motion can be easy to block and is less effective against fast or small targets.

> Standard spear used commonly by soldiers. Long reach, and can be used with shield up. Effective against hard exteriors, and hits for high damage at the right moment of an enemy's swing. But the hit radius is small, and it is easily blocked by shields."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Sold By: Male Undead Merchant (600 souls).
* Drops from Spear Wielder Infested Ghoul 2% chance - Blighttown)
* Dropped by Phalanx and Undead Soldier.
* Drops from Phalanx (1% Chance - Painted World of Ariamis)
* Drops from Undead Soldier wielding a spear and shield (2% Chance - Undead Burg, Undead Parish, Northern Undead Asylum (Revisit), Firelink Shrine)
* A Lightning version drops from a Mimic in the Sen's Fortress

### Notes & Tips

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.

To reach max, you need: 2000 souls, 10 Green Titanite Shards, 7 Red Titanite Chunks and 1 Red Titanite Slab

Chaos Upgrades require a Fire+5 weapon and to hand in the Chaos Flame Ember
Fire Reinforcement reduces Fire Damage but adds a bonus based on the Humanity you hold.
To reach max, you need: 500 souls, 7 Red Titanite Chunks and 1 Red Titanite Slab
