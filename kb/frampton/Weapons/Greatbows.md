# Greatbows

**Greatbows** are a Weapon Category in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Greatbows are huge long-ranged weapons that fire special great arrow ammunition. There are only 2 Great Bows: Dragonslayer Greatbow and Gough's Greatbow.  They are focused on delivering a **single-shot damage** to knock off opponents, but this also means they have a really poor firing rate and high stamina cost.

* Pros: Deals high damage with single-shot damage, longer distance than short and long bows and good for knocking off targets.
* Cons: Only useful for surprise attacks from a distance.
* Recommendation: Hawk Ring gives more range to complement the Great Bow one shot capability.
