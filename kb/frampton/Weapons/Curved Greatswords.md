# Curved Greatswords

**Curved Greatswords** are a Weapon Category in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Curved Greatswords are larger than Curved swords and are meant to be wielded two-handed due to their inherent weight and Strength requirements. Curved Greatswords generally have the highest attack rating among the Dexterity-based weapons and compliment it with excellent reach, good combo ability and heavy Poise damage. Their sweeping motions make them well-suited for attacking groups of enemies.

* Pros: Good Dexterity Scaling. Relatively good attack speed and higher attack power than Curved swords. Useful for engaging groups of enemies.
* Cons: Can be parried easily, heavy, limited selection.
