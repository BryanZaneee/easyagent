**Light Crossbow**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Light Crossbow**, a standard one-handed military crossbow that reloads slowly but frees the off-hand unlike bows. Crossbows are powerful ranged weapons that fire bolts, capable of high base damage and devastating headshots, though they lack the zoom function of bows. With no stat scaling, their effectiveness relies on elemental upgrades. They can also be fired instantly at close range for melee utility.

> Standard crossbow issued to soldiers. While bows require both hands, a crossbow is held in one, but arming each bolt takes time. "

### How to Get / Where to Find

* It can be found on a corpse in the Upper Undead Burg, along with 16 Standard Bolts. It is located on the balcony of one of the buildings. It can only be reached by jumping a gap from an adjacent building, most easily accessible from the roof, that is first entered via a fog door.
* Drops from Undead Knight Archer (2% - Sen's Fortress)
* Drops from Balder Knight with crossbow (2% chance - Sen's Fortress and Undead Parish)
* Drops from Undead Soldier wielding a crossbow (2% Chance - Undead Burg, Undead Parish, Northern Undead Asylum (Revisit), Firelink Shrine)
* Video Location Timestamp 5:15

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* Can **not** be modified towards **Raw** or **Chaos**
* Demon's souls version is lightly similar in appearance with the same range.
