**Man-Serpent Greatsword** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Man-Serpent Greatsword** is a brutal, single-edged bloodstained greatsword favored by the serpent men of Sen’s Fortress. Greatswords are heavy, powerful weapons with long reach and wide swings capable of hitting multiple enemies at once. This power comes at the cost of speed, as they are slow to swing and require high stats to wield effectively. Their immense force can easily stagger foes, often creating openings for follow-up attacks.

> "This single-bladed blood-stained greatsword is the choice weapon of the slithering serpent men of Sen's Fortress."
>  "An ordinary human will have great difficulty swinging this heavy beast effectively."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Rare drop from Man Serpent (2% Chance - Sen's Fortress or The Duke's Archives).

### Hints and Tips

* Can be given to Kingseeker Frampt to receive 50 Souls.
* Common choice for Strength builds in both PvE and PvP.
