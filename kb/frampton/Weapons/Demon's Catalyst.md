**Demon's Catalyst**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Demon’s Catalyst,** a molten rock catalyst from Izalith that can also function as a fire-based melee weapon. Catalysts are items equipped in a weapon slot that enable spellcasting. They allow you to cast sorceries, which can deal either Strike or Thrust damage depending on the spell. Sorceries are cast using the standard light attack buttons (R1/RB or L1/LB).

> Demon catalyst formed from Izalith molten rock. Can be used as fire weapon.
>
> The Demon Firesage was the first demon, and the last master of the original fire arts, before the Witch of Izalith was engulfed by Chaos, creating pyromancy."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Drops from Demon Firesage in Demon Ruins.

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* This catalyst's base Magic Adjust and Intelligence scaling is very low, and as such spells cast with it typically do less damage than spells cast with other catalysts, however due to its STR and DEX scaling and lower than average INT scaling, Dark Sorceries cast with this catalyst tend to do more physical damage and less magic damage than other catalysts, allowing you to deal more damage through an opponent's Great Magic Barrier.
* The fire damage from this catalyst's melee attack scales with intelligence, it is the only catalyst with Int scaling on its normal attack damage.
* For further info on Magic Adjusts values at every Int level, see: https://darksouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Magic+Adjustment
