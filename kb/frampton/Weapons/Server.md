**Server** is a Weapon in Dark Souls and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Server** grants lifesteal on hit through its occult energy, letting the wielder recover HP with each strike. Curved Greatswords are heavy, two-handed weapons that blend the sweeping arcs of curved blades with the raw power of greatswords. They offer the highest attack rating among Dexterity-focused arms, excelling at crowd control with their wide, group-clearing swings. These weapons also deliver substantial poise damage, allowing them to stagger opponents effectively while maintaining strong combo potential.

> A curved greatsword used in a mysterious ancient rite.
>  Imbued with a frightful occult energy which restores HP with each hit."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Poisoned swamp at the bottom of Blighttown, in the leeches nest.
  + Video Location Timestamp 19:52

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* Less of a "curved greatsword" and more of a huge billhook.
* Special: This sword restores 7 HP with each successful hit.
* Since the backstab utilizes two separate hits, backstabs will heal you 14 HP, equivalent to 2 normal swings.
* Attacking enemies during their death animation will grant you 30 HP bonus after their death. Upgrading to Chaos can reduce frequency of gaining additional HP for some reason. **Does not stack with the Ring of the Evil Eye!**
* Upgrading the Server to Fire or Chaos will also change its description:
  + *"A curved greatsword used in a mysterious ancient rite.*
  + *Imbued with a frightful heretical energy, this sword absorbs HP upon a foe's death."*
