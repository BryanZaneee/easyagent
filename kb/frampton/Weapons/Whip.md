**Whip**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Whip**,a leather whip ineffective against armor or scales but highly effective against exposed flesh. Whips are fast, long-range weapons with a wide attack radius that can stun agile enemies like skeletons. They offer a unique trade-off: while their attacks cannot be parried, the wielder cannot perform backstabs, ripostes, or plunging attacks. Whips can be used in the left hand to parry, and their rapid, chained swings compensate for a lack of traditional combos.

> Leather whip. Not intended for battle.
>  Virtually ineffective against armor and tough scales, but quite formidable against enemies with exposed skin."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Blighttown upper area. On a small platform, near the top of the sewer-shaft that ends up behind the lower bonfire.
* Video Location Timestamp 9:25

### and Remastered NOTE

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* No drop attack
* Cannot backstab
* Cannot Riposte after Parry
* Decent scaling and the ability to parry when equipped on the off-hand give this weapon some value when dualwielding. Unfortunately the dual-wield mechanic in Dark Souls is underwhelming, meaning the whip is mostly used as a roleplay/showy weapon.
