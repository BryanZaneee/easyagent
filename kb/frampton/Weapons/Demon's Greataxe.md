**Demon's Greataxe** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Demon’s Greataxe** enables overwhelming raw strength scaling, allowing its wielder to pulverize foes through sheer force alone. Great Axes are massive, strength-focused weapons with surprisingly short range and slow, vulnerable swings. Unlike most heavy weapons, they attack faster when wielded one-handed rather than two-handed, adding a unique tactical trade-off to their use.

> Carved from the bones of fellow demons. Wielded by the lesser Taurus demons."
>  "This axe is imbued with no special power, but can merrily beat foes to a pulp, providing you have the strength to wield it."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Dropped by Taurus Demons
* Sold by Shiva of the East for 10,000 Souls
* Drops from Minor Taurus Demon (5% chance - Demon Ruins)

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* At +15 with 35 Strength, this weapon has an AR of 661.
* At Crystal +5 with 66 Strength, this weapon has AR of 728 when one-handed and 767 when two-handed.
* A very solid pure Strength weapon, with S scaling for Strength.

Chaos Upgrades require a Fire+5 weapon and to hand in the Chaos Flame Ember
Fire Reinforcement reduces Fire Damage but adds a bonus based on the Humanity you hold.
To reach max, you need: 4,000 souls, 7 Red Titanite Chunks and 1 Red Titanite Slab
