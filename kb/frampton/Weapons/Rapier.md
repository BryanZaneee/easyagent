**Rapier** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Rapier** allows thrusting strikes that can be performed while guarding with a shield and excels at piercing resistant foes. Piercing Swords are specialized thrusting weapons ideal for breaking an enemy's poise and exploiting openings for critical damage. They feature a fast, unique moveset of rapid consecutive attacks, including a far-reaching horizontal swipe that replaces the standard kick. Effective when wielded in either hand, they can also be used to parry.

> Standard thrusting sword. Regular one-handed attack can be delivered with shield held up. Can parry with use of left hand. Thrusting attacks pierce and are effective against foes with hard exteriors, but the wielder is left open to damage after a swing."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Drops from Balder Knight with Rapier (2% chance - Sen's Fortress and Undead Parish)
* Sold by Undead Merchant

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* Like all piercing swords, replaces your kick with a back-stepping horizontal slash.
* Has a different parry animation while used in the left hand.
* Along with the dark silver tracer, rapier is one of the most damaging weapons for backstabs and ripostes compared to its weight and stat requirements. Rapier also has a useful moveset and it is commonly seen in PvP.
* **Demon's souls version:** Different in apperance but similar in move set.

To reach max, you need: 1,000 souls, 7 Red Titanite Chunks and 1 Red Titanite Slab
