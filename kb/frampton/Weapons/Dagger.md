**Dagger** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered. Dagger** is a standard small blade that delivers modest damage but excels at rapid strikes, especially effective for critical hits after a parry or a backstab**.** Daggers are swift, precise weapons with a short reach, ideal for close-quarters combat. Their rapid attacks allow players to weave in successive strikes to stagger foes while maintaining the mobility to dodge. Typically featuring high critical damage and good Dexterity scaling, daggers are versatile enough to be wielded in the left hand, where they can also be used to Parry incoming attacks.

> This standard small dagger has only a modest attack but can be jabbed in rapid succession, and is effective in critical hits such as after a parry or when stabbing in the back."
>  "With both slash and thrust attacks this dagger is useful in various situations."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Default equipment for the Sorcerer class.
* Purchased from Undead Merchant (male) (300 Souls).
* Video Location Timestamp 1:11

### Hints and Tips

* Can be given to Kingseeker Frampt to receive 50 Souls.
* While the Bandit's Knife has slightly longer range, better critical attacks, and a bleed effect, the basic Dagger possesses faster swing speed with some of the fastest normal attacks in the entire game, and a more versatile moveset. This fast swing speed leads to it being more efficient with buffs that add flat damage increase to weapons.
* Fantastic for producing sheer elemental DPS. The fast rate of attacks adds up to a lot of Fire/Lightning damage very quickly if you can achieve high uptime on the target. Ideal for bosses with timer-designed mechanics like The Four Kings.
* A very serviceable early weapon for Dex-oriented backstabbers and riposters who didn't start as Thieves.

Chaos Upgrades are performed by Blacksmith Vamos in The Catacombs.
Chaos Upgrades require a Fire +5 weapon and to hand in the Chaos Flame Ember.
Fire Reinforcement reduces Fire Damage but adds a bonus based on the Humanity you hold.
To reach max, you need: 500 souls, 7 Red Titanite Chunks and 1 Red Titanite Slab.
