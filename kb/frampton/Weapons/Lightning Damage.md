# Lightning Damage

**Lightning Damage** is one of the Damage Types in Dark Souls. It is one of the seven main Damage Types and belongs to the Elemental Damage category. Damage Types are a major part of the calculation that determines how much damage is dealt to enemies and are affected by many factors including your equipment, spells and temporary buffs.

### Equipment, Items & Spells that cause

* Blacksmith Giant Hammer
* Demon's Spear
* Dragonslayer Spear
* Giant's Halberd
* Gold Pine Resin
* Great Lightning Spear
* Lightning Bolt
* Lightning Spear
* Sunlight Blade
* Sunlight Spear
* Weapon Ascension to the Lightning Upgrades Path
  (A Spear already ascended to Lightning can be dropped by a Mimic in Sen's Fortress)

### Equipment, Items & Spells that increase Lightning Damage

* Crown of Dusk (20% damage boost to all spells, except for weapon augments, but lowers Magic defense by 30%)
* Crown of the Dark Sun (10% damage boost to all spells, including miracles (except for weapon augments)
* Power Within (40% boost to all types of damage for 100 seconds, at the cost of draining health)
* Red Tearstone Ring (Boosts damage by 50% when health is below 20%)
* Ring of the Sun's Firstborn (Miracle power: +20%)

### Equipment, Items & Spells that increase Lightning Resistance

* Blue Tearstone Ring (when health is below 20%)
* Dark Hand (as a shield gives 80% lightning resistance)
* Eagle Shield (75% lightning resistance + other damage types)
* Effigy Shield (90% lightning resistance)
* Ornstein's Set
* Silver Knight Set
* Smough's Set (Gives the highest lightning defense compared to all armor sets when fully upgraded)
* Speckled Stoneplate Ring (+25 lightning defense)
* Thunder Stoneplate Ring (+50 lightning defense)

### Enemies that deal

* Banshee
* Bat Wing Demon (Imps)
* Bell Gargoyle (Anor Londo)
* Drake
* Giant Blacksmith
* Ornstein and Smough
* Sanctuary Guardian (DLC)
* Solaire of Astora
* Titanite Demon

### Notes and Trivia

* Lightning is effective against enemies resistant to Physical-type damage and knights using heavy armor.
* Lightning is ineffective against enemies using lightning-based attacks, with an exception being the Titanite Demon.
* Sunlight Spear requires having the Soul of Gwyn, Lord of Cinder, essentially making it a miracle only for NG+.
