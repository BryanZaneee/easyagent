# Fire Upgrades

| Fire Ascension in Dark Souls |
| --- |
| fire upgrade s |
| **Fire ascension** adds **Fire Damage** to the weapon.  It increases **base damage** and **fire damage** that equals to each other per upgrade level and **Fire Defense** of the shields.  However, it removes all stat scaling bonuses. Recommended for low level players with low stat. |

**Fire Upgrades** are a type of Upgrade in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered.** They enable players to "Ascend" weapons and shields in a specific path.

### Fire Upgrade path and Requirements

**Fire Upgrades** for weapon and shield are performed by Blacksmith Vamos in The Catacombs.

Note that each upgrade level requires a number of soul that varies depending on the weapon.

* Regular upgrade from +5 to Fire +0 requires Green Titanite Shard to ascend.
* Fire upgrade from +0 to +5 requires 9 Green Titanite Shards.
* Fire upgrade from +5 to +6 requires a Large Flame Ember and Red Titanite Chunk to ascend.
* Fire upgrade from +5 to +9 requires 7 Red Titanite Chunks.
* Fire upgrade from +9 to +10 requires 1 Red Titanite Slab.

#### From 0 to +5

* Fire upgrade from 0 to +1 requires 1 Green Titanite Shard.
* Fire upgrade from +1 to +2 requires 1 Green Titanite Shard.
* Fire upgrade from +2 to +3 requires 2 Green Titanite Shards.
* Fire upgrade from +3 to +4 requires 2 Green Titanite Shards.
* Fire upgrade from +4 to +5 requires 3  Green Titanite Shards.

#### From +5 to +9

* Fire upgrade from +5 to +6 requires 1 Red Titanite Chunk and a Large Flame Ember.
* Fire upgrade from +6 to +7 requires 1 Red Titanite Chunk.
* Fire upgrade from +7 to +8 requires 2 Red Titanite Chunks.
* Fire upgrade from +8 to +9 requires 3 Red Titanite Chunks.

#### From +9 to +10

* Fire upgrade from +9 to +10 requires 1 Red Titanite Slab.
