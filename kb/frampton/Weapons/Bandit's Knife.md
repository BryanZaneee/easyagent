**Bandit's Knife** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered. Bandit’s Knife** is a wide, single-edged short blade favored by thieves, serving as a fast slicing weapon that shines at critical strikes from behind or after parrying. Daggers are swift, precise weapons with a short reach, ideal for close-quarters combat. Their rapid attacks allow players to weave in successive strikes to stagger foes while maintaining the mobility to dodge. Typically featuring high critical damage and good Dexterity scaling, daggers are versatile enough to be wielded in the left hand, where they can also be used to Parry incoming attacks.

> "This wide single-edged shortsword is the favorite of lowly thieves and bandits."
> "Primarily a slicing weapon, but highly effective when used for critical hits, such as after parrying or from behind."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Default equipment for the Thief class.
* Drops from Undead Assassin (2% chance - Lower Undead Burg).

### Hints and Tips

* Can be given to Kingseeker Frampt to receive 50 Souls.
* Very similar to the normal Dagger. It deals slightly better critical hits and inflicts Bleed, but can only perform slashing attacks.
* Powerful weapon for Dexterity builds, elemental or not. With the 147 critical modifier, backstabs and ripostes will deal extremely high damage.
* Bleed build up is 30 per strike, and once inflicted will tear 30% of the opponents HP.

Chaos Upgrades are performed by Blacksmith Vamos in The Catacombs.
Chaos Upgrades require a Fire +5 weapon and to hand in the Chaos Flame Ember.
Fire Reinforcement reduces Fire Damage but adds a bonus based on the Humanity you hold.
To reach max, you need: 500 souls, 7 Red Titanite Chunks and 1 Red Titanite Slab.
