**Sorcerer's Catalyst** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Sorcerer’s Catalyst, the standard Vinheim catalyst for casting sorceries, scaling well with Intelligence and intended for mid-level Int builds. Catalysts are items equipped in a weapon slot that enable spellcasting. They allow you to cast sorceries, which can deal either Strike or Thrust damage depending on the spell. Sorceries are cast using the standard light attack buttons (R1/RB or L1/LB).

> Sorcery catalyst used by sorcerers of Vinheim Dragon School. Equip catalyst to use sorceries. Attune sorceries from a scroll at a bonfire. Most sorceries have a limited number of uses."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Starting equipment for Sorcerer.
* Found on a corpse in the same room Griggs of Vinheim can be rescued in.
* Sold by Griggs of Vinheim after rescuing him from lower Undead Burg. Video Location: Found in this video at 2:21
* Sold by Blacksmith Rickert of Vinheim in New Londo Ruins.
* Video Location Timestamp 2:12

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* Recommended for Int levels: 27-40
