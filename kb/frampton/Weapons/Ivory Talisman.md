**Ivory Talisman** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Ivory Talisman**, a female cleric’s talisman whose miracle power scales strongly with the wielder’s Faith. Talismans are wielded in order to cast Miracles.

> Medium for casting miracles of the Gods.
>  Ivory Talismans are granted only to female clerics and their value is affected dramatically by their owner's faith."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Obtained by defeating Reah of Thorolund in The Duke's Archives after she hollows.
* Obtained by defeating Reah of Thorolund at Undead Parish church before she dies.
* Petrus will also drop the Ivory Talisman, but only after he has assassinated Reah of Thorolund.

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 500 souls.
* Recommended for Faith levels: 43-99 (not counting the darkmoon talisman)
* The Ivory Talisman is identical to the Sunlight Talisman, apart from the Faith requirements.
* You can block with a talisman while two-handing it.
