**Moonlight Butterfly Horn**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Moonlight Butterfly** Horn channels pure sorcerous energy derived from the Moonlight Butterfly, a magical creation of Seath, resulting in a horn weapon that deals predominantly magic-based damage. Spears are long-reaching thrusting weapons ideal for a defensive playstyle, allowing you to attack while keeping your guard up. Their relatively quick jabs are effective for interrupting enemy attacks. However, their predictable stabbing motion can be easy to block and is less effective against fast or small targets.

> Weapon born from the mystical creature of the Darkroot Garden, the Moonlight Butterfly. The horns of the butterfly, a being created by Seath, are imbued with a pure magic power."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Ascension: Ascend a +10 Spear or Rapier class weapon with the Soul of the Moonlight Butterfly, at the Giant Blacksmith in Anor Londo.

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 1000 souls.

### Lore

* The Moonlight Butterfly Horn is part of a long tradition of using caduceus patterns in Souls games.
