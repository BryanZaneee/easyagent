**Lifehunt Scythe**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Lifehunt Scythe**, a scythe born from Priscilla’s soul that inflicts devastating bleed equal to 50% of max HP. Halberds function like heavier, more powerful spears with versatile damage types. Their slower, sweeping attacks require precise timing to avoid lengthy recovery. This weapon class can be enhanced by items like the Leo Ring to significantly boost its damage output.

> Scythe born from the soul of Priscilla, the stark white crossbreed trapped inside the painted world of Ariamis.
>  Even the Gods feared Priscilla's lifehunt ability, and in the hands of a mortal, its power will turn upon its wielder."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Forge from a +10 Scythe, Whip,or Halberd and Soul of Priscilla (+5000 souls) at the Giant Blacksmith in Anor Londo.

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 14 souls.
* Bleed build up is 50 on the enemy and 40 on the player for each strike. Once triggered, bleed effect tears down 50% of enemy's (or player's) total health instantly.
* One of only two weapons in the game that causes 50% of maximum bleed damage, rather than the normal 30% from other Bleed effects, the other being Priscilla's dagger.
* Also resembles the scythe wielded by Rurufon in Shadow Tower Abyss, From Software's early title.

### Lore

* Priscilla - The Anthesis of Life - possessed a power that could kill even the very gods.
