# Physical Damage

| Physical Damage |
| --- |
| titanite slab |

**Physical Damage** or Regular Damage is a Damage Type in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. It is part of the seven main Damage Types. Damage Types are a major part of the calculation that determines how much damage is dealt to enemies and are affected by many factors including your equipment, spells and temporary buffs. Most Weapons are capable of dealing Physical Damage (excluding Moonlight Butterfly Horn, Moonlight Greatsword, and Pyromancy Flame), with the type of Physical Damage depending on their moveset.

Physical Damage outside of Regular Damage falls under Strike Damage, Slash Damage or Thrust Damage.

* Halberd
* Partizan

* Ultra Greatswords (all)
* Velka's Rapier
* Whips (all)

### Items & Spells that increase

* Power Within (40% boost to all types of damage for 100 seconds, at the cost of draining health)
* Red Tearstone Ring (when health is under 20%)

### Equipment, Items & Spells that increase Physical Resistance

* Armor
* Blue Tearstone Ring (Boosts**physical**, magical, flame and lightning defenses by 50% when health is below 20%)
* Ring of Steel Protection
* Shields (i.e. greatshields or standard shields)

### Notes and Trivia

* Physical is the most-common damage type in the *Dark Souls* series.
* "Regular" Physical Damage is absent from *Dark Souls 2*, leading to only three Physical Damage types. It would be reintroduced as Standard Damage in *Dark Souls 3*, and dealt by largely the same weapon classes.
