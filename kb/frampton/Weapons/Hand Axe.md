**Hand Axe** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Hand Axe** allows swift, lightweight strikes but demands careful spacing and timing to avoid leaving yourself exposed. Axes deliver heavy, high-damage swings with a wide arc, but their slow recovery makes missed strikes risky. Their broad swings can also be interrupted in tight spaces if they collide with walls.

> Small hand axe. Appears identical to a lumberjack's tool, but has an ideal weight and strength, and is easy to handle.
>  One wrong swing leaves the wielder wide open, so timing and proximity to the enemy must be judged carefully."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Undead Merchant (Male) in Undead Burg for 450 souls.
* Obtained as starting equipment for pyromancers.

### Hints and Tips

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* This is a surprisingly useful early-game weapon. Its low skill requirements make it available to all starting classes from the beginning. It has decent damage, good scaling with STR (C), a fast and useful moveset, and little stamina cost. Despite the in-game description, wrong swings do not leave the player any more open to punishment than, say, the Longsword. Hand Axe has a shorter reach than Straight Swords yet has a slightly faster moveset. It also has the ability to break the shield-guard of most enemies with one or two 2H R1 swings. This is especially useful during the early levels of the game, allowing the player to easily break through the shield of Undead Soldiers and even Balder Knights without having to kick them.
* Atacking shielded enemies while two-handing the Hand Axe does not result in knockback, allowing one to continuously strike at the shield until the enemy's stamina depletes, opening them to a critical attack.
* Two-handed R1 swing breaks the shield-guard of Undead Soldiers with a single strike. It takes two strikes to do the same to Balder Knights.

* For STR builds, Hand Axe +5 has a slightly bigger overall damage output than Gargoyle Tail Axe +5. This is because Hand Axe has C scaling with STR while Gargoyle Tail Axe scales with D. This makes Hand Axe the more preferable choice because, despite having a shorter reach, Hand Axe benefits from a similar yet faster moveset, as well as consuming less stamina.
