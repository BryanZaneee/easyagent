**Composite Bow**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Composite Bow**, a compact bow requiring higher Strength with the shortest range of all bows, unsuitable for long-distance sniping. Bows are dedicated long-range weapons that require arrows to fire. They are split into two distinct types: short bows and long bows, each with unique firing mechanics and no melee function.

> Composite bow emphasizing power. Requires more strength than standard bows.
>  However, its range is shorter, making it unfit for sniping.

### How to Get / Where to Find

* New Londo Ruins, on a ledge above the large gates overlooking Valley of Drakes.
* Video Location Timestamp 4:58

### and RemasteredComposite Bow Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* This bow is classified as a short bow. It fires faster than Long bows.
* This bow has the shortest range of all the other bows, with a total of 38 meters.
