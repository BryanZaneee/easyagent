**Raw Upgrades** are a type of  Upgrade in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered.** They enable players to "Ascend" weapons and shields in a specific path.

### Raw Upgrade path and Requirements

**Raw Upgrades** for weapon are performed by Andre of Astora at the Undead Parish.

Note that each upgrade level requires a number of soul that varies depending on the weapon.

* From Regular +5 to Raw +0 requires Large Ember and Large Titanite Shard.
* Raw upgrade from +0 to +5 requires 9 Large Titanite Shards to ascend.

#### From +0 to +5

* Raw upgrade from +0 to +1 requires 1 Large Titanite Shard
* Raw upgrade from +1 to +2 requires 1 Large Titanite Shard
* Raw upgrade from +2 to +3 requires 2 Large Titanite Shard
* Raw upgrade from +3 to +4 requires 2 Large Titanite Shard
* Raw upgrade from +4 to +5 requires 3 Large Titanite Shard
