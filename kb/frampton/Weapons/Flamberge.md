**Flamberge** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Flamberge** is a serrated greatsword designed to flay flesh and inflict severe bleeding. Greatswords are heavy, powerful weapons with long reach and wide swings capable of hitting multiple enemies at once. This power comes at the cost of speed, as they are slow to swing and require high stats to wield effectively. Their immense force can easily stagger foes, often creating openings for follow-up attacks.

> *This greatsword with an undulating blade is the choice weapon of the slithering serpent men of Sen's Fortress."
>  *"This weapon is designed to flay the skin and causes heavy bleeding."**

### How to Get the / Where to Find

* Purchased from Shiva of the East (10,000 Souls).
* Rare drop from Serpent Mages (2% Chance - Sen's Fortress or The Duke's Archives).

### Notes

* Can be given to Kingseeker Frampt to receive 50 Souls.
* Bleed build up is 36 per strike, and once inflicted will tear 30% of the opponents HP.
* Heavy R2 attacks are replaced with heavy slashes which inflict heavy knockback.

Chaos Upgrades are performed by Blacksmith Vamos in The Catacombs.
Chaos Upgrades require a Fire+5 weapon and to hand in the Chaos Flame Ember.
Fire Reinforcement reduces Fire Damage but adds a bonus based on the Humanity you hold.
To reach max, you need: 2,000 souls, 7 Red Titanite Chunks and 1 Red Titanite Slab.
