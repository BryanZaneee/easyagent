**Short Bow**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Short Bow, a fast-firing lightweight bow ideal for luring enemies and shooting more quickly than a longbow. Bows are dedicated long-range weapons that require arrows to fire. They are split into two distinct types: short bows and long bows, each with unique firing mechanics and no melee function.

> Small bow. Standard projectile weapon."
>  Equip arrows to use. Hold bow and press L1 or LB to aim. Aim. for heads of humanoid foes. Press L2/R2 or LT/R/T to change type of arrows.

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Initial equipment for Hunter.
* Undead Merchant (male) in Undead Burg sell the weapon for 1000 Souls.
* Drops from Hollows (2% chance - Undead Burg, Northern Undead Asylum, Depths, Northern Undead Asylum (upon returning) and Painted World of Ariamis)
* Drops from Torch Hollows (2% chance - Northern Undead Asylum, Lower Undead Burg, Depths and Painted World of Ariamis)
* Drops from skeleton (2% chance - Firelink Shrine Graveyard and The Catacombs)

### Notes

The Short Bow is the standard bow and very useful for luring things. This bow has both a lighter weight and a faster shooting speed than a longbow.

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* This bow is classified as a Short bow. It has faster fire rate than Long bows.
* Demon's souls version: Different in apperance but has the same range.
