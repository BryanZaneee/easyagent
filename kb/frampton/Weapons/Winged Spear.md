**Winged Spear** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Winged Spear** delivers barbed thrusts with excellent reach while guarding, performing well against tough targets but sharing the Spear’s small hit radius and susceptibility to shield blocks. Spears are long-reaching thrusting weapons ideal for a defensive playstyle, allowing you to attack while keeping your guard up. Their relatively quick jabs are effective for interrupting enemy attacks. However, their predictable stabbing motion can be easy to block and is less effective against fast or small targets.

> A long-hilted spear with a barbed point. Long reach, and can be used with shield up. Effective against hard exteriors, and hits for high damage at the right moment of an enemy's swing. But the hit radius is small, and it is easily blocked by shields."

### Hints and tips

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* A Spear, but better. Longer and deals more damage in every way, but requires better stats and benefits much less from Strength.
* Snatch it early from the graveyard to get a great starter weapon for a Dex class.
