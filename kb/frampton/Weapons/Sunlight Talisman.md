**Sunlight Talisman** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Sunlight Talisman**, solaire’s personal talisman symbolizing unwavering devotion, ideal for strong Faith builds. Talismans are wielded in order to cast Miracles.

> Medium for casting miracles of the Gods.
> The talisman of Solaire of Astora, the Knight of Sunlight, is decorated with a holy symbol, illustrated by Solaire himself.
> This talisman is a projection of Solaire's upstanding, unwavering faith."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Dropped by Solaire of Astora.
* Video Location Timestamp 13:35

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 1000 souls.
* Recommended for Faith levels: 35-99 (unless you have obtained the Canvas Talisman--which will be passed in Magic Adjustment by the Sunlight Talisman at 43 Faith-- or the Darkmoon Talisman.
  + At 35 Faith the Sunlight Talisman's Magic Adjustment (167) is equivalent to the Talisman's Magic Adjustment (167) and greater than the Thorolund Talisman's Magic Adjustment (165).
* The Sunlight Talisman is identical to the Ivory Talisman, apart from the Faith requirements.
* For further info on Magic Adjustment values at every Faith level, see: Faith-Int Talisman Magic Adjustment Values
* You can block with a talisman while two-handing it.
