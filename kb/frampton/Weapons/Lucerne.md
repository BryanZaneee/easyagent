**Lucerne**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Lucerne**, a beaked halberd whose every attack deals thrust damage by striking downward with its weighted head. Halberds function like heavier, more powerful spears with versatile damage types. Their slower, sweeping attacks require precise timing to avoid lengthy recovery. This weapon class can be enhanced by items like the Leo Ring to significantly boost its damage output.

> Halberd with a large, hard beak-like protrusion. Inflicts thrust damage.
> Instead of thrusting like a spear, the wielder of the Lucerne aims to club the head with its hard beak."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* After the first small bridge in the Catacombs. In a small room guarded by two skeletons.
* Video Location Timestamp 0:32

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* Damage wise is identical to the Halberd.
* All attacks deal thrust damage.
* Light attacks swing vertically. There is no follow-up 2nd attack however the repeated strikes land relatively quickly each attack.
* Two handed light attack has a unique vertical swing.
