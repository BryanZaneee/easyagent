**Tin Darkmoon Catalyst**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Tin Darkmoon Catalyst**, a catalyst created from Gwyndolin’s soul used for casting magic, though still requiring the minimum Intelligence to function. Catalysts are items equipped in a weapon slot that enable spellcasting. They allow you to cast sorceries, which can deal either Strike or Thrust damage depending on the spell. Sorceries are cast using the standard light attack buttons (R1/RB or L1/LB).

> Catalyst born from the soul of the Dark Sun Gwyndolin, Darkmoon deity who watches over the abandoned city of the Gods, Anor Londo. Gwyndolin is Gwyn's lastborn and a legitimate god, but he is also a Moon sorcerer, and this wand is boosted by faith, not intelligence."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Ascend from any other Catalyst except Demon's Catalyst, Izalith Catalyst, and Manus Catalyst for 5,000 Souls,
  along with Dark Sun Gwyndolin's soul at the Giant Blacksmith in Anor Londo.

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* This catalyst scales Mag Adj according to your Faith stat.
* To cast spells with it, you will still need the minimum Int requirement.
