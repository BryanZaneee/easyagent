**Notched Whip**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Notched Whip**, a barbed whip that causes significant bleeding and excels at striking unarmored targets. Whips are fast, long-range weapons with a wide attack radius that can stun agile enemies like skeletons. They offer a unique trade-off: while their attacks cannot be parried, the wielder cannot perform backstabs, ripostes, or plunging attacks. Whips can be used in the left hand to parry, and their rapid, chained swings compensate for a lack of traditional combos.

> Notched Whip with sharp spikes. Only slightly effective against armor and tough scales, but quite formidable against enemies with exposed skin. Also causes heavy bleeding.

### How to Get / Where to Find

Drop from killing NPC Phantom Xanthous King Jeremiah in the graveyard at Painted World of Ariamis.

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* Bleed build up is 33 per strike, and once inflicted will tear 30% of the opponents HP.
* Breaks 20 poise per swing.
