**Stone Greataxe** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Stone Greataxe** grants lethal crushing blows and benefits from buff effects that extend into its minor area-of-effect strike. Great Aaxes are massive, strength-focused weapons with surprisingly short range and slow, vulnerable swings. Unlike most heavy weapons, they attack faster when wielded one-handed rather than two-handed, adding a unique tactical trade-off to their use.

> Greataxe borne by the stone knights who guard the forest Sanctuary.
>  This greataxe, requiring inhuman strength to wield and more fitted to manual labor than battle, is nevertheless completely lethal."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Drops from Stone Guardian (2% chance - Royal Wood)

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
* Requires 32 Strength to be held in both hands
* 1-handed strong attack is a low horizontal swing. this has short range, but can be effective if used to stun an opponent who stays close to you.
* Special: 2-handed strong attack slams the greataxe into the ground. Doing a strong attack immediately afterwards causes the axe to be ripped out of the ground dealing strong attack damage in a small area. This can be quite useful in pvp.
* The Damage from buffs also apply to the small AoE attack
