**Reinforced Club** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Reinforced Club** grants strong early-game power through high damage and built-in bleeding, enabling it to tear down tougher foes with ease. Hammers deliver fast, powerful blunt-force strikes, excelling against armored enemies and skeletons. While they can break an opponent's guard and stagger them into a combo, they suffer from long recovery times. This makes each swing a committed risk, as a missed attack leaves the wielder vulnerable to immediate counterattacks.

> A club with leather nailed to the pommel. The burg Undead merchant proudly peddles this club, but its reinforcement is shoddy. Indeed, its attack is stronger, but the club is no longer dependably sturdy."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* **S**old by the Undead merchant (male).

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* One of the hardest hitting very low requirement weapons there is, but it's annoyingly slow to recover from a miss. Very good at crushing shield wielders and it's R2 attack is a leaping smash which is good for closing distances, but do not use when standing close to a non-giant enemy as you will most likely jump past them.
* One of the best early weapons because of its small requirements and strong attacks. Bleeding is very useful too, so you can easily beat stronger enemies.
* Bleed build up is 30 per strike, and once inflicted will tear 30% of the opponents HP.
