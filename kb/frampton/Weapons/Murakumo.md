**Murakumo** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls.**The **Murakumo** is considered the heaviest curved greatsword in the game and has the highest strength requirement within its weapon class. One of its standout features is its impressive damage output, making it the most powerful curved greatsword available. It is particularly effective at staggering high poise opponents, thanks to its heavy strikes. Despite its weight, the Murakumo is surprisingly quick for its size, allowing for swift and powerful attacks. This weapon can be obtained by killing Shiva of the East or as a rare drop from the Giant Skeletons found in the Firelink Shrine or Tomb of the Giants. Curved Greatswords are heavy, two-handed weapons that blend the sweeping arcs of curved blades with the raw power of greatswords. They offer the highest attack rating among Dexterity-focused arms, excelling at crowd control with their wide, group-clearing swings. These weapons also deliver substantial poise damage, allowing them to stagger opponents effectively while maintaining strong combo potential.

> Giant curved sword forged using special methods in an Eastern Land. This unparalleled weapon cuts like a Katana but is heavier than a Nata machete. Requires extreme strength, dexterity, and stamina to wield."

### Location

* Killing Shiva of the East.
* Rare drop from the Giant Skeletons in Firelink Shrine and the Tomb of the Giants.

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* The heaviest Curved Greatsword.
* Primarily scales with Dexterity.
* Due to its blade length and relatively fast speed, the offhand horizontal sweep is surprisingly handy for dual wielding builds.

To reach max, you need: 4,000 souls, 10 Green Titanite Shards, 7 Red Titanite Chunks and 1 Red Titanite Slab
