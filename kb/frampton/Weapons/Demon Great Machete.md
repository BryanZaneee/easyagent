**Demon Great Machete** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Demon Great Machete** is an iron-forged heavy blade used by lesser Capra Demons, enabling merciless smashing attacks and replacing strong attacks with a leaping downward strike. Ultra Greatswords demand high Strength to wield and are exceptionally slow, but their massive, sweeping attacks deal devastating damage and can stun-lock enemies into combos. Missing a swing leaves the user vulnerable, though these colossal blades offer the best physical damage reduction and stability when blocking attacks.

> The lesser Capra demons use these greatswords in pairs. The blade is cast iron and hooked."
>  "The sword is imbued with no particular magic, but for those who have the strength, its great weight will smash foes mercilessly."

### How to Get & Where to Find

* Rare drop from the Capra Demon boss.
* Drops from Minor Capra Demon (5% Chance - Demon Ruins)
* Purchased from Shiva of the East for 10,000 souls.

### Hints and Tips

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* One and two-handed strong attack is replaced with a jumping downward slash.
* Can be wielded two-handed at 27 Strength.
* The Dragon Greatsword, Black Knight Greatsword and Demon Great Machete are slower than the Greatsword and the Zweihander, though they don't appear to be much slower, it is more difficult to stunlock enemies with the first three.
