**Club** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Club** allows simple but effective blunt strikes that are easy to handle and can break enemy shield guards. Hammers deliver fast, powerful blunt-force strikes, excelling against armored enemies and skeletons. While they can break an opponent's guard and stagger them into a combo, they suffer from long recovery times. This makes each swing a committed risk, as a missed attack leaves the wielder vulnerable to immediate counterattacks.

> A simple wooden club. This simple bladeless strike weapon is effective against most foes, is easily handled, and can break the guard of a shield."
> "However, a single miss makes one wide open, so timing and proximities are crucial.

To reach max, you need: 1000 souls, 7 Red Titanite Chunks and 1 Red Titanite Slab
