**Warpick** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Warpick** grants thrust-based piercing damage and scales with both Strength and Dexterity, standing apart from typical blunt hammers. Hammers deliver fast, powerful blunt-force strikes, excelling against armored enemies and skeletons. While they can break an opponent's guard and stagger them into a combo, they suffer from long recovery times. This makes each swing a committed risk, as a missed attack leaves the wielder vulnerable to immediate counterattacks.

> Hammer with a large beak-shaped pommel. Originally a pickaxe; redesigned for battle."
> "This simple but deadly weapon hits heavily with thrust damage, such that it easily cracks metal armor."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Purchase from Andre of Astora in Undead Parish for 800 souls.

### Hints and Tips

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* Unlike most hammers that deal strike damage, the Warpick deals thrusting damage.
* Unlike most hammers that only scale with Strength, the Warpick also scales with Dexterity.
* Because of its thrusting property, this weapon is relatively bad at breaking the guard of shields.
* The Warpick, like the Pickaxe, breaks less poise than other hammer class weapons.
* The thrusting damage can be buffed on counter hits with the Leo Ring; making this weapon exceptionally powerful for its attack speed and its weight.
* Has a unique horizontal swinging attack for its R2.
* The Warpick has a slightly longer reach than any other hammer class weapon, except for the unmatched Blacksmith Giant Hammer.
* Demon's souls version: Has similar appearance but has a different move set.
