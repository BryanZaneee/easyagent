**Darkmoon Bow**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Darkmoon Bow**, a divine bow born from Gwyndolin’s soul that excels at firing Moonlight Arrows with powerful magic scaling. Bows are dedicated long-range weapons that require arrows to fire. They are split into two distinct types: short bows and long bows, each with unique firing mechanics and no melee function.

> Bow born from the soul of the Dark Sun Gwyndolin, Darkmoon deity who watches over the abandoned city of the Gods, Anor Londo.
>  This golden bow is imbued with powerful magic and is most impressive with Moonlight Arrows."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Upgrade a +10 bow with Dark Sun Gwyndolin's soul.

### Notes

* This bow is classified as a short bow. It fires faster than Long bows.
* Primarily scales with Dex and Faith.
* It's primarily used for firing Moonlight Arrows, a type of arrow that deals magic damage.
* Give to Frampt to receive 1000 souls.
