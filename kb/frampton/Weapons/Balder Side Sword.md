**Balder Side Sword** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls****.**This weapon can be obtained from Balder Knights in Undead Parish and Sen's Fortress, though its rarity makes it quite challenging to acquire. It features an enhanced version of the straight sword moveset, incorporating additional thrust attacks that deliver massive damage. This combination of movesets provides a versatile and powerful option for players looking to maximize their offensive capabilities.

> The knights of the ancient kingdom of Balder wielded these rock-solid, long swords which are excellent for thrust attacks."
>  "Balder was the home of Knight King Rendal, but the kingdom was reduced to ruins after a widespread outbreak of Undead."

### How to Get

* Rare drop (1% chance) from Balder Knights in Undead Parish and Sen's Fortress. Only knights wielding the Balder Shield have this sword.

### Hints and Tips

* Can be given to Kingseeker Frampt to receive 50 Souls.
* Appreciated by players for its adaptability and effectiveness due to its good reach, balanced moveset and low Stamina cost.
* The sword receives an A rating in Dexterity at +14/15 along the regular reinforcement path, making it excellent for Dexterity builds, and may also be buffed. The Enchanted elemental path grants an A scaling in Intelligence, making it a good option for Mages. Other elemental paths such as Lightning or Chaos are also effective for other builds, particularly when using the powerful R2 thrust attacks.
* Has a similar move set to Longsword or Shortsword, and the same base damage as Longsword, but generally greater range at similar Stamina cost.
* The one-handed strong attack (R2) is a thrust attack both for the first and second attacks in the combo, instead of a upswing on the second attack. The second R2 thrust is faster than the first, making it particularly dangerous if the target is staggered by the first thrust or otherwise using a slower weapon. It also tracks independently of the first thrust, enabling an experienced swordsman to punish moving targets with or without target lock.
* Thrust attacks do significantly more damage than the slashes, and can benefit from the Leo Ring.
* Greater range of the sword enables users to better distance themselves from targets, and reduces the likelihood of being parried provided one does not get too close. The powerful R2 thrust attacks are also very difficult to parry at range, similar to the thrust attacks of spears.
* Unless you desperately want this sword early, you're much better off farming it in Sen's Fortress than in Undead Parish. The two Balder Knights right near the bonfire can both drop the sword, and killing them is significantly faster than running from the blacksmith's bonfire in Undead Parish to the three Knights there who wield it (the one across the bridge, and two of the three on the first floor of the church). You'll also have access to the Covetous Gold Serpent Ring once in Sen's Fortress, dramatically increasing the sword's drop rate.
