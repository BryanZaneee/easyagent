**Pickaxe** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Pickaxe** allows thrust-focused attacks that suit Strength builds, turning a mining tool into a battlefield penetrator. Hammers deliver fast, powerful blunt-force strikes, excelling against armored enemies and skeletons. While they can break an opponent's guard and stagger them into a combo, they suffer from long recovery times. This makes each swing a committed risk, as a missed attack leaves the wielder vulnerable to immediate counterattacks.

> Traditionally a mining tool, but the hard cone on the pommel is effective in battle."
> "A downward swing of the Pickaxe can crush stone; a human head would be an afterthought"

### How to Get/ Where to Find

* Drops from Infested Barbarians (Boulder) in Blighttown (5% chance)

### Hints and Tips

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* One of the only weapons with thrust damage for STR builds

Chaos Upgrades are performed by Blacksmith Vamos in The Catacombs.
Chaos Upgrades require a Fire+5 weapon and to hand in the Chaos Flame Ember
Fire Reinforcement reduces Fire Damage but adds a bonus based on the Humanity you hold.
To reach max, you need: 1000 souls, 7 Red Titanite Chunks and 1 Red Titanite Slab
