**Oolacile Ivory Catalyst**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Oolacile Ivory Catalyst**, a white-barked catalyst from Oolacile that boosts sorcery adjustment but performs best with low-Int, gentle Oolacile sorceries unaffected by Intelligence. Catalysts are items equipped in a weapon slot that enable spellcasting. They allow you to cast sorceries, which can deal either Strike or Thrust damage depending on the spell. Sorceries are cast using the standard light attack buttons (R1/RB or L1/LB).

> Sorcery catalyst of lost land of Oolacile. Formed by enchanted whitebark branches. The whitebark boosts sorcery adjustment, but the sorceries of this lost land are gentle and not affected by intelligence."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Sold by Dusk of Oolacile in Darkroot Basin. After defeating the Hydra, find a cave at the back of the lake and kill the gold crystal golem there to free Dusk. Head back to the other side of the lake and summon her from her soul sign to buy from her.

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* Recommended for Int levels: 12-26
* While this catalyst is listed as having Intelligence scaling, it does not actually gain any magic adjust from your Intelligence stat.
* While this catalyst is listed as having Strength scaling, Dark Sorceries will not gain any extra damage from your Strength stat when cast with this catalyst.
* Dark Sorceries will do slightly more damage when cast with this catalyst than if cast with the Oolacile Catalyst.
* For further info on Magic Adjusts values at every Int level, see: https://darksouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Magic+Adjustment
