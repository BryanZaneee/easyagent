**Izalith Catalyst**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Izalith Catalyst**, the ancient catalyst of the Witch of Izalith boasting the highest durability of its class. Catalysts are items equipped in a weapon slot that enable spellcasting. They allow you to cast sorceries, which can deal either Strike or Thrust damage depending on the spell. Sorceries are cast using the standard light attack buttons (R1/RB or L1/LB).

> *Catalyst of the Witch of Izalith of long ago, when her daughters were still flame witches, before they were engulfed by the Chaos Flame. Before the birth of pyromancy, their wands were mediums for sorcery, but knowledge of this flame sorcery has long since vanished."*

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Drop from the Daughter of Chaos enemy in Lost Izalith, in front of the Bed of Chaos boss door.
* Video Location Timestamp 15:54

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 1000 souls.
* Stat is almost identical to Sorcerer's Catalyst, except it has higher durability and Int requirement.
* It has the highest durability among the catalysts.
* Recommended for Int levels: 27-40.
* For further info on Magic Adjusts values at every Int level, see: https://darksouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Magic+Adjustment.
