**Zweihander** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls**. This weapon can be found on a corpse in the Graveyard area behind Firelink Shrine. It is located in front of the large tombstone at the farthest end to the right of the stairs leading downwards, guarded by two Skeletons and a dormant Giant Skeleton. One of the standout features of this weapon is its overhead swing. While the overhead swing may not deal substantial damage, it compensates with significant poise damage, making it particularly effective for breaking through enemy defenses and interrupting larger foes. This makes it a strong choice for both defeating regular enemies and managing more formidable opponents. Ultra Greatswords demand high Strength to wield and are exceptionally slow, but their massive, sweeping attacks deal devastating damage and can stun-lock enemies into combos. Missing a swing leaves the user vulnerable, though these colossal blades offer the best physical damage reduction and stability when blocking attacks.

> One of the gigantic straight greatswords. As the name suggests, the Zweihander is held with two hands, but its wielder must still be inhumanly strong."
>  "It is this great weight that sends foes flying when hit solidly."

### How to Get

* Obtained from a corpse in the Graveyard area behind Firelink Shrine, in front of the large tombstone at the farthest end to the right of the stairs leading downwards, protected by two Skeletons and a Giant Skeleton, lying dormant.
* Video Location Timestamp 1:28

### Hint and Tips

* The light attacks are wide sweeps.
* The heavy attacks are overhead slams, which are capable of knocking down enemies.

### Notes

* High damage and high stagger weapon.
* Easily obtained very early on in a playthrough and suitable for endgame.
* The lightest of the ultra greatswords.
* Can be sold to Kingseeker Frampt for 50 souls.

Chaos Upgrades are performed by Blacksmith Vamos in The Catacombs.
Chaos Upgrades require a Fire+5 weapon and to hand in the Chaos Flame Ember
Chaos Reinforcement reduces Fire Damage but adds a bonus based on the Humanity you hold.
To reach max, you need: 2,000 souls, 7 Red Titanite Chunks and 1 Red Titanite Slab
