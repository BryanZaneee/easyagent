**Gargoyle Tail Axe** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Gargoyle Tail Axe** grants strong sweeping attacks and inherent resistance to poison and toxin, making it ideal for hazardous environments. Axes deliver heavy, high-damage swings with a wide arc, but their slow recovery makes missed strikes risky. Their broad swings can also be interrupted in tight spaces if they collide with walls.

> Sliced tail of the gargoyle guarding the Bell of Awakening in the Undead Church or patrolling in Anor Londo.
>  Can be used as a bronze battle axe. Bends dramatically during large attacks, owing to its nature as a tail."

### How to Get / Where to Find

Dropped by cutting off the tail of any gargoyle (Undead Parish, Anor Londo).

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* Special: This axe increases Bleeding, Poison and Toxin resistance x2. (inventory screen = x4, but equipped = x2) causing the illusion that you get poisoned and bled when you equip it.
* Its shaft also bends on heavy attacks.
* If your attack doesn't connect with anything, there will be a slight lag at the end of the attack, similar to halberd weapons.
* Upgrading the weapon **does not** increase its Bleed, Poison, and Toxin resistance. The reason the player sees the Bleed, Poison, and Toxin meters when upgrading is because you technically are unequipping and re-equipping the weapon each time you upgrade.
* Poison and Toxin resistance make this weapon a good choice in Blighttown.

To reach max, you need: 1,000 souls, 7 Red Titanite Chunks and 1 Red Titanite Slab
