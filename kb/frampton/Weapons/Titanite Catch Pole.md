**Titanite Catch Pole**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Titanite Catch Pole**, a polearm wielded by Titanite Demons with a unique leaping heavy attack copied from its monstrous users. Halberds function like heavier, more powerful spears with versatile damage types. Their slower, sweeping attacks require precise timing to avoid lengthy recovery. This weapon class can be enhanced by items like the Leo Ring to significantly boost its damage output.

> Weapon of the titanite demon, a faceless stone monster born from titanite slab.
> One of the enchanted weapons, perhaps from residual power of the titanite slab.
> Known for its leaping attack which comes smashing down on foes from above."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Rare (10%) drop from Titanite Demons.
* Video Location Timestamp 1:04

### Notes

* **Titanite Catch Pole** can be upgraded by using Twinkling Titanite.
* Can be given to Kingseeker Frampt to receive 100 Souls.
* This weapon's heavy attack is a unique jumping attack, being the same jumping strike the Titanite Demon uses.
* It is possible to acquire one right from the beginning in Undead Parish. If you do get it, it becomes a potent early game caster weapon due to its good weight, considerably low stat requirements (and no Intellect requirement), decent scaling on all attributes, and Physical/ Magic damage spread.

### Lore

* Titanite Demons may be part of an overarching theme that links Demons and Dragons to minerals.
