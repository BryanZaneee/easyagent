# Magic Damage

| Magic Damage |
| --- |
| blue titanite slab |

**Magic Damage** is one of the Damage Types in Dark Souls. It is one of the seven main Damage Types and belongs to the Elemental Damage category. Damage Types are a major part of the calculation that determines how much damage is dealt to enemies and are affected by many factors including your equipment, spells and temporary buffs.

### Equipment, Items & Spells that increase

* Bellowing Dragoncrest Ring (Magic damage: +20-26%)
* Crown of Dusk (20% damage boost to all spells, except for weapon augments, but lowers Magic defense by 30%)
* Crown of the Dark Sun (10% damage boost to all spells, including miracles (except for weapon augments)
* Red Tearstone Ring (Boosts damage by 50% when health is below 20%)
* Power Within (40% boost to all types of damage for 100 seconds, at the cost of draining health)

### Notes and Trivia

* Magic is effective against enemies resistant to Physical-type damage and knights using heavy armor.
* Magic is ineffective against enemies using magic or created by sorcery.
