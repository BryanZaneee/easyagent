**Dragon Bone Fist**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Dragon Bone** **Fist** allows fast, brutal hand-to-hand strikes created from the Iron Golem’s soul, and can be buffed or enchanted for additional damage like the Dragon Tooth and Dragon King Greataxe. Fists are ultra-fast, low-stamina weapons with the shortest range, requiring players to engage at point-blank distance. Their rapid combos are excellent for breaking an enemy's guard or exhausting their stamina. Wielding them in the left hand allows for parrying, while using them in the right replaces the kick with a quick knee strike, adding versatile utility to their relentless offense.

> A weapon from the soul of the Iron Golem, guardian of Sen's Fortress who repelled countless heroes who sought Anor Londo."
>  "The Gods fused the power of the soul with the great bones of the dragons, forming an appropriate core for the giant golem'"

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Ascending Caestus or Claws +10 with the Core of an Iron Golem at the Giant Blacksmith in Anor Londo.
* **Does not** ascend with Dark Hand.

### Hints and Tips

* Give to Frampt to receive 5000 souls.
* Special: Strong (R2) attack is a massive uppercut that shatters enemy defenses, and launches them high into the air.
* Special: Weapon grants a +15 to Magic DEF and +15 to Flame DEF.
* It can be enchanted / buffed for additional attack bonuses, like the Dragon Tooth and the Dragon King Greataxe.
* If the user has the same amount of STR and DEX, the Caestus will always have higher attack than the Dragon Bone Fist. This is because the Caestus scales with both STR and DEX, while the Dragon Bone Fist only scales with STR.

### Lore

* The Dragon Bone Fist is part of a theme that connects the Everlasting Dragons to stone.
