# Fire Damage

| Fire Damage |
| --- |
| red titanite slab |

**Fire Damage** is one of the Damage Types in Dark Souls. It is one of the seven main Damage Types and belongs to the Elemental Damage category. Damage Types are a major part of the calculation that determines how much damage is dealt to enemies and are affected by many factors including your equipment, spells and temporary buffs.

### Equipment, Items & Spells that cause Fire Damage

* Black Firebomb
* Charcoal Pine Resin
* Demon's Catalyst
* Dragon Head Stone
* Fire Arrow
* Firebomb
* Hammer of Vamos
* Obsidian Greatsword (Two handed strong attack)
* Orange Charred Ring (Adds fire damage element to kick motion)
* Pyromancies
* Pyromancy Flame
* Quelaag's Furysword
* Skull Lantern
* Weapon Ascension to the Fire Upgrades Path or Chaos upgrades path

### Equipment, Items & Spells that increase Fire Damage

* Red Tearstone Ring (Boosts damage by 50% when health is below 20%. Also affects damage done by magic, pyromancy and miracles)
* Bellowing Dragoncrest Ring (Pyromancy damage: +20-26%)
* Crown of Dusk (20% damage boost to all spells, except for weapon augments, but lowers Magic defense by 30%)
* Crown of the Dark Sun (10% damage boost to all spells (except for weapon augments)
* Red Tearstone Ring (Boosts damage by 50% when health is below 20%)
* Power Within (40% boost to all types of damage for 100 seconds, at the cost of draining health)

### Notes and Trivia

* Fire damage is very effective against hollows, which use light armor and beasts.
* Fire damage is ineffective against demons and dragon-type enemies.
* Notably Chaos Witch Quelaag, Centipede Demon, and Ceaseless Discharge are completely immune to Fire Damage.
