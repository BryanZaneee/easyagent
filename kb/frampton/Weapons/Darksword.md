**Darksword** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered. Darksword** is a thick-bladed straight sword wielded in an unconventional manner, obtained by reaching Rank 2 in the Darkwraith Covenant. Straight Swords offer a balance of power and reach, trading the raw speed of daggers for heavier, more impactful strikes. Their slower swing is compensated by a longer range and the ability to hit multiple opponents with a single, broad motion.

> The sword of the knights of the Four Kings of New Londo. Its blade is wide and thick and it is wielded in an unusual manner."
> "When the Four Kings were seduced by evil, their knights became Darkwraiths, servants of the Dark who wielded these darkswords."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Obtained by reaching Rank 2 in the Darkwraith Covenant.

### Hints and Tips

* Can be given to Kingseeker Frampt to receive 50 Souls.
* One handed R2 heavy attack is a upward slash followed by a downward slash that can be chained with one handed light attacks.

### Lore

* Some believe Darkwraiths to be revolutionaries, defenders of the human race, but they may rather represent evil Humanity.

Chaos Upgrades are performed by Blacksmith Vamos in The Catacombs.
Chaos Upgrades require a Fire +5 weapon and to hand in the Chaos Flame Ember.
Fire Reinforcement reduces Fire Damage but adds a bonus based on the Humanity you hold.
To reach max, you need: 1,000 souls, 7 Red Titanite Chunks and 1 Red Titanite Slab.
