**Demon's Great Hammer**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Demon's Great Hammer** allows brutally heavy strikes formed from the stone archtrees, lacking special effects but rewarding raw Strength with pure destructive output. Great Hammers are colossal, strength-demanding weapons typically wielded with both hands. Their immense weight translates into devastating swings that excel at staggering foes and shattering their guard.

> Demon weapon built from the stone archtrees. Used by lesser demons at North Undead Asylum.
>  This hammer is imbued with no special power, but will merrily beat foes to a pulp, provided you have the strength to wield it."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Beat the Asylum Demon during the first encounter.
  + Only drops it during the very first encounter, before going through the rest of the tutorial area and dropping back in after you pick up your weapons.
  + Easy strategy: Choose Black Firebombs as your gift - no need to be naked or only use your fists.
  + Alternatively: Wait until NG+ and beat him easily with your proper gear.
* Trade a Sack with Snuggly.

### Tips and hints

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* 31 strength is required to two-hand this hammer.
