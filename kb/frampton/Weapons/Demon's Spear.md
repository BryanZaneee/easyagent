**Demon's Spear**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Demon’s Spear** is carved from demon bone and used by slim Batwing demons, boasting the longest reach of any weapon in the game. Spears are long-reaching thrusting weapons ideal for a defensive playstyle, allowing you to attack while keeping your guard up. Their relatively quick jabs are effective for interrupting enemy attacks. However, their predictable stabbing motion can be easy to block and is less effective against fast or small targets.

> Carved from the bones of fellow demons. Wielded by the slim lesser Batwing demons. The weapon of these chaos demons wandering Anor Londo are different from those of other chaos demons, and are imbued with lightning."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Sold by Shiva of the East for 15,000 souls.
* Drops from Bat Wing Demon (2% Chance - Anor Londo)

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
* The Demon Spear is the longest reaching weapon in the game
