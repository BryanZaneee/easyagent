**Claymore** is a Weapon in Dark Souls and **Dark Souls Remastered**. This weapon can be acquired from a corpse in Undead Burg near the bridge guarded by the Hellkite Wyvern. It deals exceptionally high damage and is surprisingly fast for a greatsword. Its iconic one-handed thrust is quicker than most greatswords capable of this type of attack, making it a choice of a weapon. Greatswords are heavy, powerful weapons with long reach and wide swings capable of hitting multiple enemies at once. This power comes at the cost of speed, as they are slow to swing and require high stats to wield effectively. Their immense force can easily stagger foes, often creating openings for follow-up attacks.

> *This larger type of greatsword is normally wielded with two hands due to its weight."*
>  *"This highly flexible greatsword can be used to attack in swings or thrusts."*

### Location

* Looted from a body on the bridge, guarded by the Hellkite Wyvern, in upper Undead Burg.
* Video Location Timestamp 1:27

### Hints and Tips

* Can be given to Kingseeker Frampt to receive 50 Souls.
* While one-handed, first R2 attack is a heavy forward thrust, second R2 is a heavy upward slash. R1 into R2 is a true combo of slash and followup thrust.
* While two-handed, R1 and followup R1 are heavy vertical slashes. R2 and followup R2 are heavy horizontal slashes. R1, R2, R1 is a true three-hit slashing combo.
* Versatile moveset, high AR and moderate speed make this sword a very good and popular weapon for PvP.

Chaos Upgrades are performed by Blacksmith Vamos in The Catacombs.
Chaos Upgrades require a Fire +5 weapon and to hand in the Chaos Flame Ember.
Fire Reinforcement reduces Fire Damage but adds a bonus based on the Humanity you hold.
To reach max, you need: 2,000 souls, 7 Red Titanite Chunks and 1 Red Titanite Slab.
