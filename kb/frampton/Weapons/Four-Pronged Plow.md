**Four-Pronged Plow**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Four-Pronged Plow** provides stabbing and raking attacks with a distinctive farm-tool design dropped by Treant Gardeners in the Royal Wood. Spears are long-reaching thrusting weapons ideal for a defensive playstyle, allowing you to attack while keeping your guard up. Their relatively quick jabs are effective for interrupting enemy attacks. However, their predictable stabbing motion can be easy to block and is less effective against fast or small targets.

> Four-pronged Plow wielded by the wooden scarecrows, serfs of the forest Sanctuary.
>  The scarecrow serfs would not normally use these four-pronged plows as weapons, but their sharpness makes them very deadly."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Drops from the Treant Gardener in the Royal Wood.

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* The two-handed R2 is a very damaging lunge that causes the player to fall over if no enemy is hit. Costs a large amount of stamina.
