**Thorolund Talisman**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Thorolund Talisman**, a high-ranking clerical talisman with strong miracle adjustment not tied to Faith. Talismans are wielded in order to cast Miracles.

> Medium for casting miracles of the Gods. This talisman is only granted to high-ranking Thorolund clerics.
>  Has high miracle adjustment which, thanks to divine protection, is not dependent upon faith.

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Sold by Petrus of Thorolund for 5,000 souls.
* Sold by Patches the Hyena for 5,000 souls.

### Notes

* Recommended for Faith levels: 10-26.
* Although the interface says the Magic Adjustment scales with Faith, it doesn't scale at all. For higher Faith levels the simple Talisman is more potent.
* For further info on Magic Adjustment values at every Faith level, see: Faith-Int Talisman Magic Adjustment Values
* You can block with a talisman while two-handing it.
