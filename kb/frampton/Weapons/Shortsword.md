**Shortsword** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered. Shortsword** is a compact straight sword that delivers consistent and reliable damage, making it useful across a wide range of combat situations. Straight Swords offer a balance of power and reach, trading the raw speed of daggers for heavier, more impactful strikes. Their slower swing is compensated by a longer range and the ability to hit multiple opponents with a single, broad motion.

> This small straight sword is widely used, to an extent only matched by the longsword."
>  "An accessible sword which inflicts consistent regular damage and high slash damage, making it applicable to a variety of situations."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Default equipment for the Hunter class.
* Purchased from the Male Undead Merchant (600 Souls).
* Drops from sword-wielding Hollow Warriors.

### Hints and Tips

* Can be given to Kingseeker Frampt to receive 50 Souls.

Chaos Upgrades require a Fire +5 weapon and to hand in the Chaos Flame Ember.
Fire Reinforcement reduces Fire Damage but adds a bonus based on the Humanity you hold.
To reach max, you need: 1,000 souls, 7 Red Titanite Chunks and 1 Red Titanite Slab.
