**Talisman** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Talisman**, The standard miracle-casting talisman for common believers, found early in Firelink Shrine or purchased cheaply. Talismans are wielded in order to cast Miracles

> Medium for casting miracles of the Gods. Standard talisman issued to common believers.
>  Equip talisman to cast miracles. Attune miracles from a scroll at a bonfire. Most miracles have limited number of uses."

### How to / Where to Find

* Found in chest at Firelink Shrine, below the elevator to Undead Parish.
* Sold by Petrus of Thorolund for 1,000 souls.

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
* Recommended for Faith levels: There are better talismans available for any Faith level.
* For further info on Magic Adjustment values at every Faith level, see: Faith-Int Talisman Magic Adjustment Values
* You can block with a talisman while two-handing it.
