# Great Hammers

**Great Hammers** are a Weapon Category in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**.  Great Hammers are among the largest weapons in the game and require very high Strength, to the point they are mostly two-handed. Their heavy weight compounds to deliver large powerful swings, causing staggering and breaking enemy guard. The Large Club has an AOE lunging attack that covers a huge area.

* Pros: Deals high Strike Damage, excellent Poise-breaking ability and can stagger enemies/foes. Good Strength scaling.
* Cons: Very Heavy, with slow attacks and long recovery time, consumes a lot of stamina per hit.
* Recommendation: Couple with Iron Flesh or some heavy Armor.
