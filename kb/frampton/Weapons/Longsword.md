**Longsword** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered. Longsword** is a widely used straight sword that offers a balanced moveset, reasonable damage, low requirements, and efficient stamina usage. Straight Swords offer a balance of power and reach, trading the raw speed of daggers for heavier, more impactful strikes. Their slower swing is compensated by a longer range and the ability to hit multiple opponents with a single, broad motion.

> Widely-used standard straight sword, only matched in ubiquity by the shortsword."
>  "An accessible sword which inflicts consistent regular damage and high slash damage, making it applicable to a variety of situations."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Default equipment for the Warrior class.
* Purchased from Andre of Astora (1,000 Souls).
* Drops from Undead Soldiers with Sword and Shield (2% drop chance - Undead Burg, Undead Parish, Northern Undead Asylum (Revisit), Firelink Shrine).

### Hints and Tips

* Can be given to Kingseeker Frampt to receive 50 Souls.
* The Longsword sports a good combination of low weight, low stamina cost, very low stat requirements, and reasonable damage output to go along with its balanced moveset.
* As with many other straight swords, the Longsword's first strong (R2) attack is a forward thrust that deals good damage and can benefit from the Leo Ring. Longsword's second strong (R2) attack is an upward sweep action that runs from the end of the thrust attack animation.
* The speed of the second upwards thrust can make the longsword very dangerous in experienced hands: if the initial thrust connects, a second, equally damaging second attack can be landed almost immediately. This is particularly effective on targets with low Poise that can be staggered by the initial attack. Stamina cost and the possibility of targets evading the opening thrust makes this a high risk, high reward tactic.
* The second upwards thrust is not blocked by shields, so if you make the first thrust just out of range of the enemy, the second thrust will hit him through his shield. Excellent in PvP, but takes practice to be perfect. If executed improperly, can offer the enemy an opening to get behind you for a backstab.
