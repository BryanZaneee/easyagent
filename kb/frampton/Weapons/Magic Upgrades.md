**Magic Upgrades** are a type of Upgrade in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered.** They enable players to "Ascend" weapons and shields in a specific path.

### Magic Upgrade path and Requirements

**Magic Upgrades** for weapon and shield are performed by Rickert of Vinheim, found in New Londo Ruins. The Large Magic Ember is only required to get the weapon up to +10, since Rickert of Vinheim already has the ember required to upgrade to Magic +5.

Note that each upgrade level requires a number of souls that varies depending on the weapon.

* Regular upgrade from +5 to Magic +0 requires Green Titanite Shard to ascend.
* Magic upgrade from +0 to +5 requires 9 Green Titanite Shards.
* Magic upgrade from +5 to +6 requires a Large Magic Ember and Blue Titanite Chunk to ascend.
* Magic upgrade from +5 to +9 requires 7 Blue Titanite Chunks.
* Magic  upgrade from +9 to +10 requires a Blue Titanite Slab.

#### From 0 to +5

* Magic upgrade from 0 to +1 requires 1 Green Titanite Shard.
* Magic upgrade from +1 to +2 requires 1 Green Titanite Shard.
* Magic upgrade from +2 to +3 requires 2 Green Titanite Shards.
* Magic upgrade from +3 to +4 requires 2 Green Titanite Shards.
* Magic upgrade from +4 to +5 requires 3 Green Titanite Shards.

#### From +5 to +9

* Regular upgrade from +5 to +6 requires 1 Blue Titanite Chunk and a Large Magic Ember.
* Regular upgrade from +6 to +7 requires 1 Blue Titanite Chunk.
* Regular upgrade from +7 to +8 requires 2 Blue Titanite Chunks.
* Regular upgrade from +8 to +9 requires 3 Blue Titanite Chunks.
