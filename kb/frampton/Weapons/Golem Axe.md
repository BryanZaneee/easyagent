**Golem Axe** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Golem Axe** enables grounded fighters to counter airborne foes through its unique reach and impact. Axes deliver heavy, high-damage swings with a wide arc, but their slow recovery makes missed strikes risky. Their broad swings can also be interrupted in tight spaces if they collide with walls.

> An axe born from the soul of the Iron Golem, guardian of Sen's Fortress who repelled countless heroes who sought Anor Londo."
> "Its powerful one-handed attack imitates the Golem's blade of wind, but the heavy iron blade makes it quite unwieldy."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Ascending an axe weapon+10 with the Core of an Iron Golem At the Giant Blacksmith in Anor Londo.

### Hints and Tips

* Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
* Special: One handed strong (R2) attack produces an air slash projectile identical to the one used by Iron Golem. This attack consumes 30 weapon durability points and knocks down many targets.
* The axe still attacks physically when performing an air blast. At close range, both the melee hit and the blast will hit your target, causing high damage. This also means that it is very easy to parry.
* Can be a powerful tool to deal with airborne enemies for melee oriented characters capable of wielding it.
