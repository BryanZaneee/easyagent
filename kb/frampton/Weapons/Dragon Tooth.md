**Dragon Tooth**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Dragon Tooth** allows immense Strength-scaled impact forged from an eternal dragon fang, providing buffable damage worthy of Havel the Rock. Great Hammers are colossal, strength-demanding weapons typically wielded with both hands. Their immense weight translates into devastating swings that excel at staggering foes and shattering their guard.

> Created from an everlasting dragon tooth. Legendary great hammer of Havel the Rock."
>  "The dragon tooth will never break as it is harder than stone, and it grants its wielder resistance to magic and flame."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Chest behind a hidden wall in Anor Londo.
* Video Location Timestamp 15:37

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 5000 souls.
* Special: Adds 20 Magic and 20 Fire resistance. (+4 Dragon Tooth. inventory screen = +40/+40, but equipped = +20/+20)
* Special: Two-handed R2 attack causes Knockdown
* It is enchantable/buffable, like the Dragon Bone Fist and the Dragon King Greataxe.
* Scales with Strength.

### Lore

* The Dragon Tooth is part of a theme that ties the Everlasting Dragons to minerals.
