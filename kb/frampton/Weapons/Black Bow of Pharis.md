**Black Bow of Pharis** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Black Bow of Pharis**, a prized bow with superior range and refined handling, favored by the legendary archer Pharis. Bows are dedicated long-range weapons that require arrows to fire. They are split into two distinct types: short bows and long bows, each with unique firing mechanics and no melee function.

> The preferred black bow of the heroic archer Pharis.
>  Has a longer range than standard bows, but is more difficult to use. Without proper abilities, results will be underwhelming."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Darkroot Garden. Drops from Pharis, a faded, female hunter, who stands far to the right of the entrance of the area where Alvina sits, along the cliffside.

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* This bow is classified as a Long bow. It fires slower than short bows.
* This bow has the longest range out of all the bows, with a total of 65 meters.
