**Dark Silver Tracer** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered. Dark Silver Tracer** is a dark silver dagger wielded by Lord’s Blade Ciaran, offering exceptionally high critical damage and excelling in backstabs and ripostes for Dexterity-focused builds. Daggers are swift, precise weapons with a short reach, ideal for close-quarters combat. Their rapid attacks allow players to weave in successive strikes to stagger foes while maintaining the mobility to dodge. Typically featuring high critical damage and good Dexterity scaling, daggers are versatile enough to be wielded in the left hand, where they can also be used to Parry incoming attacks.

> A dark silver dagger used by the Lord's Blade Ciaran, of Gwyn's Four Knights.
>  The victim is first distracted by dazzling streaks of the Gold Tracer, then stung by the vicious poison of this dagger."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Acquired from Lord's Blade Ciaran alongside the Gold Tracer, in exchange for the Soul of Artorias, or by killing her (you can't acquire the daggers twice in one run). Ciaran can be found in the same place you encounter Knight Artorias, after you defeat him, and will ask for his Soul. Refusing will not make her hostile. Killing her causes her to drop both the tracers and her armor as loot if you have not traded the soul. (Causing her to appear may require you to reload or warp somewhere else after defeating Artorias).
* It is important to note that if the player speaks to Ciaran and refuses to give her the soul, she will disappear from your playthrough forever when next you reload the area.

### Hints and Tips

* Can be given to Kingseeker Frampt to receive 50 Souls.
* Dark Skilver Tracer can be upgraded by using Twinkling Titanite.
* A very serviceable late-game weapon for backstabs and ripostes, especially for Dexterity-oriented builds, offering the highest boost to critical damage of any weapon. This is further increased when paired with the Hornet Ring.
* Unique R2 attack inflicts Toxic buildup. Good for pairing with the Gold Tracer in the offhand, so that the user benefits both from the Gold Tracer's unique moveset in the left hand and the Silver Tracer's toxic inducing heavy attacks.
* Often used in conjunction with the Gravelord Sword for builds oriented around inflicting Toxic, either switching between the two for alternating attack speeds or, less commonly, by wielding the Gravelord Sword in the offhand (the weapon is less effective when used in this manner as it loses its distinctive thrust attack in exchange for rapidly switching between the two weapons attacks).
