**Ricard's Rapier** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Ricard’s Rapier** allows rapid multi-hit strong attacks that scale exceptionally well with buffs for massive damage output. Piercing Swords are specialized thrusting weapons ideal for breaking an enemy's poise and exploiting openings for critical damage. They feature a fast, unique moveset of rapid consecutive attacks, including a far-reaching horizontal swipe that replaces the standard kick. Effective when wielded in either hand, they can also be used to parry.

> A rapier with intricate decorations. Chosen weapon of the famous Undead Prince Ricard. Ricard's exploits are told in a monomyth."
> "He was born into royalty, but wandered the lands in a fateful ill-conceived journey. He became Undead and disappeared up North."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Ricard the Archer on top of Sen's Fortress.
* Video Location Timestamp 23:45

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* Special: If you press R2 (Heavy) once, you will do the standard double thrust BUT if you press R2 (Heavy) again right after the initial attack, you will do another four very fast thrusts. Although easily parried, this is one of the more lethal unique strong attacks in the game.
* Like all piercing swords, it replaces your kick with a back-stepping horizontal slash attack.

Often used with different buffs since its strong attack can deal incredibly high damage very fast. This makes it useful for killing bosses in PvE even in high NGs and also a deadly PvP weapon when used properly.
