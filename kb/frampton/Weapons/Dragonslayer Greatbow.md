**Dragonslayer Greatbow** is a Greatbow weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Dragonslayer Greatbow**, a massive ground-anchored greatbow of the Dragonslayers that fires specialized great arrows with immense force. Greatbows are massive ranged weapons that fire heavy great arrows. They specialize in delivering a single, high-impact shot capable of staggering or knocking down foes, but this comes at the cost of a very slow firing rate and high stamina consumption.

"Bow of the Dragonslayers, led by Hawkeye Gough, one of Gwyn's Four Knights.
This bow's unusual size requires that it be anchored to the ground when fired. Only uses specialized great arrows."

### Acquired

The Dragonslayer Greatbow is found in Anor Londo. To reach it, you must enter the church vestibule with the two giants facing each other that is located just before the boss fight with Ornstein and Smough. If you ascend either staircase in this room and reach the upper level, there is a broken stained glass window. Head through this window to land on a stair-like ledge. If you follow this ledge in a descending fashion, you will find the bow on a hollow corpse impaled on some metal spikes. To grab it, you have to roll or jump beyond the spikes to land on a flat area where the bow is accessible.

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
* Even though this weapon is two-handed, it will still require 20 strength to use.
* Can only be used with Dragonslayer Arrows and Gough's Great Arrows
