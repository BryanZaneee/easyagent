**Darkmoon talisman** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Darkmoon Talisman**, a covenant-based talisman optimized for extremely high Faith levels. Talismans are wielded in order to cast Miracles.

> Medium for casting miracles of the Gods.
> Granted to Darkmoon Blade knights, adherents to the Covenant of the Dark Sun Gwyndolin.
> This talisman demands dutiful faith from its owner, but has very high miracle adjustment".

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Obtained by giving 10 Souvenirs of Reprisal to Blade of the Dark Moon Covenant.

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 500 souls.
* Recommended for Faith levels: 40-99
* The best talisman for Faith levels 40 and above, but the price to get it can be high, especially for offline players.
* For further info on Magic Adjustment values at every Faith level, see: Faith-Int Talisman Magic Adjustment Values
* You can block with a talisman while two-handing it.
