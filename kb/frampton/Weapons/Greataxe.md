**Greataxe** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Greataxe** grants brutal impact that can hurl enemies with its sheer mass, though it requires tremendous handling strength. Great Axes are massive, strength-focused weapons with surprisingly short range and slow, vulnerable swings. Unlike most heavy weapons, they attack faster when wielded one-handed rather than two-handed, adding a unique tactical trade-off to their use.

> This greataxe is a veritable mass of iron. Its weight sends foes flying, but makes it difficult to handle without inhuman strength."
>  "Any miss makes one very vulnerable, as each swing requires the full force of the body."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Can be found in the Depths (in a box behind 5 rats near the bonfire).
* Sold by Crestfallen Merchant for 8000 souls.
* Rare drop from the Berenike Knights.
* Video Location Timestamp 5:36

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* The lightest of Great Axes.
* Greataxe +15 has a base physical damage of 350, which is the highest reachable through Regular Upgrades.
* Lightning Greataxe +5 has a staggering damage output of 700, bigger than Lightning Great Club +5 (674) and Lightning Zweihander +5 (650). However, its minimal reach and mediocre moveset make it not worth all the Titanite Chunks.
* Chaos Greataxe +5 with 10 Humanities has a devastating 729 Attack Rating, and the minimum strength to two-hand it is barely 22, making it fully capable of decimating most of the enemies in one to two hits.
* Special: Two-handed R2 causes knockdown
