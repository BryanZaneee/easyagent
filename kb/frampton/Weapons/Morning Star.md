**Morning Star** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Morning Star** allows brutal blunt strikes enhanced by its spiked pommel, making it a savage weapon of clerical warfare. Hammers deliver fast, powerful blunt-force strikes, excelling against armored enemies and skeletons. While they can break an opponent's guard and stagger them into a combo, they suffer from long recovery times. This makes each swing a committed risk, as a missed attack leaves the wielder vulnerable to immediate counterattacks.

> Hammer with a sharp spike on its pommel. One of the more barbaric cleric weapons.
>  Uniquely, this hammer inflicts thrust damage, and causes bleeding."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Firelink Shrine, in a chest found in the area you can reach by dropping from the ledge you find by following the stairs that is facing Petrus.
* Video Location Timestamp 0:56

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* The description lies. It doesn't inflict thrust damage at all.
* A godsend for repelling skeletons early on in the graveyard. The R2 attack scatters them all over the place, letting you stunlock stragglers.
* Bleed build up is 33 per strike, and once inflicted will tear 30% of the opponents HP.
* **Demon's souls version:** Has similar apperance but has a different move set.
