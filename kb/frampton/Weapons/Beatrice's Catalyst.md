**Beatrice's Catalyst**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Beatrice’s Catalyst**, an ancient catalyst of Witch Beatrice that channels old sorceries and becomes obtainable only after defeating the Four Kings. Catalysts are items equipped in a weapon slot that enable spellcasting. They allow you to cast sorceries, which can deal either Strike or Thrust damage depending on the spell. Sorceries are cast using the standard light attack buttons (R1/RB or L1/LB).

> Catalyst belonging to Beatrice, the rogue witch. Contrasts with Vinheim catalysts. This ancient catalyst shows signs of being used for age-old sorceries. It has passed the hands of many generations to get here."

### Location

* At Valley of Drakes area along with the Witch Armor Set. Basically go where the cave entrance to Blighttown is; the corpse is located at a ledge on at the opposite side of the cave entrance.
* Available only after you have defeated Four Kings boss.
* It is **not** necessary to have summoned Witch Beatrice.
* Video Location Timestamp 0:10

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 50 souls.
* Stat is identical to Sorcerer's Catalyst.
* Recommended for Int levels: 27-40
* For further info on Magic Adjusts values at every Int level, see: https://darksouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Magic+Adjustment
