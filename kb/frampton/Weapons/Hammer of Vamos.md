**Hammer of Vamos** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. **Hammer of Vamos** allows bone-deep crushing force wielded by the silent skeletal blacksmith of the Catacombs. Hammers deliver fast, powerful blunt-force strikes, excelling against armored enemies and skeletons. While they can break an opponent's guard and stagger them into a combo, they suffer from long recovery times. This makes each swing a committed risk, as a missed attack leaves the wielder vulnerable to immediate counterattacks.

> Metal hammer of Vamos, a skeleton blacksmith deep within the Catacombs who rarely speaks.
>  Can be used as a strike weapon, but better left in the hands of its talented owner.

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Murdering Blacksmith Vamos.

### Hints and Tips

* Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
