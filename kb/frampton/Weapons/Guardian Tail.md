**Guardian Tail**is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Guardian Tail, a flexible spiked tail-weapon with high poise-breaking ability and slow poison buildup unless augmented by Rotten Pine Resin. Whips are fast, long-range weapons with a wide attack radius that can stun agile enemies like skeletons. They offer a unique trade-off: while their attacks cannot be parried, the wielder cannot perform backstabs, ripostes, or plunging attacks. Whips can be used in the left hand to parry, and their rapid, chained swings compensate for a lack of traditional combos.

> Sliced tail of the Sanctuary Guardian. This flexible, spiked and highly poisonous tail would make a rather obnoxious weapon.

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Cutting off the Sanctuary Guardian's Tail (can also be obtained from one of the Lesser Sanctuary Guardians if missed)
* Best way to do so is to equip a weapon with a long reach and decent speed vertical slash, and bait the chimera to do its water splash attack by staying at medium range, GET HIT by it (it just staggers - no damage) prompting the boss to do his charge attack, roll to the right and cut once or twice.

### Notes

* Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
* Breaks 20 poise per swing.
* Do not expect to easily poison enemies with this,it takes a lot of strikes to do so. But if you have rotten pine resin you can enchant the Guardian Tail with it to quickly build up poison.
