**Wooden Arrow** is an Ammunition in **Dark Souls**. Ammunition is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as Bows and Crossbows and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as Enemies and Bosses.

> Standard arrow. These arrows do not fly particularly far, nor do they inflict a terrible amount of damage, but their cheap cost makes them useful when volume is required.

### Usage

* Ammo for Bows.
* Inflicts 35 Physical damage.

### Locations

**Wooden Arrow** can be purchased from

* Undead Merchant
* Undead Merchant (Female)

### Notes & Tips

* Cheapest arrows, low damage.
