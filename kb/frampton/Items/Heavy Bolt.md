**Heavy Bolt** is an Ammunition in **Dark Souls**. Ammunition is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as Bows and Crossbows and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as Enemies and Bosses.

> Large bolt. Used mainly with the Heavy Crossbow. These weighty bolts inflict high damage, but have reduced range.

### Usage

* Ammo for Crossbows.
* Inflicts 64 Physical damage.

### Locations

**Heavy Bolt** can be purchased from

* Undead Merchant (Male)
* Undead Merchant (Female)
* Crestfallen Merchant

### Notes & Tips

* High damage, shorter range.
