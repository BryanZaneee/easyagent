**Poison Arrow** is an Ammunition in **Dark Souls**. Ammunition is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as Bows and Crossbows and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as Enemies and Bosses.

> Arrow with point dipped in poison. Ensures the demise of prey in a hunt, or the target in an assassination. Once an enemy is poisoned, one need only wait.

### Usage

* Ammo for Bows.
* Inflicts 48 Physical damage and 180 Poison "damage" (poison buildup)

### Locations

* Can be purchased from Undead Merchant (female) for 100 souls per arrow.

### Notes & Tips

* Will not cause poison if stats are too low to wield the bow
* Headshots inflict more poison
