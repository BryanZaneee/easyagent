**Moonlight Arrow** is an Ammunition in **Dark Souls**. Ammunition is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as Bows and Crossbows and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as Enemies and Bosses.

> Arrow granted to those bound by Covenant to the Dark Sun Gwyndolin, Lord Gwyn's lastborn. Moonlight Arrows glow silver, and inflict magic damage.

### Usage

* Ammo for Bows.
* Inflicts 80 magic damage.

### Locations

* Can be purchased from Giant Blacksmith for 500 souls per arrow.

### Notes & Trivia

* Most suitable for Bows that scale with INT or FTH (Darkmoon Bow or any Bow with Magic, Enchanted, Divine or Occult upgrade), as INT/FTH scaling increases magic dmg.
