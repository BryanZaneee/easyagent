# Standard Arrow

**Standard Arrow** is an Ammunition in **Dark Souls**. Ammunition is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as Bows and Crossbows and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as Enemies and Bosses.

> Standard arrow. Arrows must be equipped to be fired.

### Usage

* Ammo for Bows.
* Inflicts 45 Physical damage.

### Locations

**Standard Arrow** can be purchased from:

* Undead Merchant
* Undead Merchant (Female)
* Crestfallen Merchant
* Blacksmith Vamos

### Notes & Tips

* Cheap everyday arrows.
