# Fire Arrow

| Fire Arrow |
| --- |

| Arrows fired with flaming tips.  Inflicts fire damage. |

**Fire Arrow** is an Ammunition in **Dark Souls**. Ammunition is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as Bows and Crossbows and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as Enemies and Bosses.

> Arrows fired with flaming tips. Inflicts fire damage.

### Usage

* Ammo for Bows.
* Inflicts 35 Physical damage and 50 Fire damage.

### Locations

**Fire Arrow** Can be purchased from:

* Undead Merchant (female).

### Notes & Tips

* Use a Fire or Chaos upgraded bow to increase the fire damage.
