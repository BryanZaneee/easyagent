**Wood Bolt** is an Ammunition in **Dark Souls**. Ammunition is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as Bows and Crossbows and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as Enemies and Bosses.

> Basic wood bolt. These bolts do not fly particularly far, nor do they inflict a terrible amount of damage, but their cheap cost makes them useful when volume is required.

### Usage

* Ammo for Crossbows.
* Inflicts 38 Physical damage.

### Locations

**Wood Bolt** can be purchased from

* Undead Merchant (Male)
* Undead Merchant (Female)

### Notes & Tips

* Cheapest bolts, low damage.
