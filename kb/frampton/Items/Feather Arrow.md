**Feather Arrow** is an Ammunition in **Dark Souls**. Ammunition is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as Bows and Crossbows and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as Enemies and Bosses.

> Red feather arrows of the Lord's hunters. Their long range makes them effective for sniping.

### Usage

* Ammo for Bows.
* Inflicts 50 Physical damage.

### Locations

**Feather Arrow** can be purchased from:

* Crestfallen Merchant
* Giant Blacksmith
* Marvelous Chester

### Notes & Tips

* Increased range for all bows.
* Flies at a faster velocity than other arrows.
