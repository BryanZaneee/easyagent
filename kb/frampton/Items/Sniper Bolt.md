# Sniper Bolt

**Sniper Bolt** is an Ammunition in **Dark Souls**. Ammunition is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as Bows and Crossbows and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as Enemies and Bosses.

> Red feather bolts used with the Sniper Crossbow. Their long range makes them effective for sniping.

### Usage

* Ammo for Crossbows.
* Inflicts 53 Physical damage.

### Locations

* Can be purchased from Crestfallen Merchant and Marvelous Chester.

### Notes & Tips

* Increased range.
