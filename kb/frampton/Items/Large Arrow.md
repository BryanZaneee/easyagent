**Large Arrow** is an Ammunition in **Dark Souls**. Ammunition is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as Bows and Crossbows and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as Enemies and Bosses.

> Large arrow. Heavier arrows cause more damage, but have a shorter range.

### Usage

* Ammo for Bows.
* Inflicts 60 Physical damage.

### Locations

**Large Arrow** can be purchased from

* Male Undead Merchant
* Undead Merchant (Female)
* Crestfallen Merchant
* Blacksmith Andre
* Blacksmith Vamos

### Notes & Tips

* High damage, but low range. Good for close quarter combat.
