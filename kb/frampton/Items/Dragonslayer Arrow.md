**Dragonslayer Arrow** is an Ammunition in **Dark Souls**. Ammunition is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as Bows and Crossbows and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as Enemies and Bosses.

> Giant dragon-hunting arrows, resembling iron spears. Used by Hawkeye Gough and his Dragonslayers, they puncture human flesh easily. Dragonslayer Arrows can only be used with the Dragonslayer Greatbow, and vice-versa.

### Usage

* Ammo for Greatbows.
* Inflicts 100 Physical damage.

### Locations

* Can be purchased from Giant Blacksmith
* Each arrow costs 500 souls.
* Drops from Silver Knight (3% ~ 7% Chance - Anor Londo)

### Notes & Tips

* Can only be used with the Greatbows.
