**Gough's Great Arrow** is an Ammunition in **Dark Souls**. Ammunition is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as Bows and Crossbows and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as Enemies and Bosses.

> Great arrow used by Hawkeye Gough, one of Lord Gwyn's Four Knights, for dragon slaying. Requires Greatbow. Gough personally crafts each stone tip, and wraps the shaft tightly with tree bark.

### Usage

* Ammo for Greatbows.
* Inflicts 105 Physical damage.

### Locations

* Can be purchased from Hawkeye Gough.
* Each arrow costs 650 souls

### Notes & Tips

* Can only be used with the Greatbows.
