# Standard Bolt

| Standard Bolt |
| --- |
| standard bolt |
| Standard crossbow bolt.  Used mainly with the Light Crossbow.  Equip crossbow with a bolt to fire. |

**Standard Bolt** is an Ammunition in **Dark Souls**. Ammunition is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as Bows and Crossbows and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as Enemies and Bosses.

> Standard crossbow bolt. Used mainly with the Light Crossbow. Equip crossbow with a bolt to fire

### Usage

* Ammo for Crossbows.
* Inflicts 48 Physical damage.

### Locations

**Standard Bolt** can be purchased from:

* Undead Merchant (Male)
* Undead Merchant (Female)
* Crestfallen Merchant

### Notes & Tips

* Cheap everyday bolts.
