# Faith-Int Talisman Magic Adjustment Values

Each Talisman in Dark Souls features a magic adjustment stat that influences the potency of the miracles cast with it. This adjustment affects the damage of direct damage miracles, the extra damage provided by weapon augmentation miracles, and the health restoration from healing miracles. For most Talismans, magic adjustment scales with the user's faith. However, the Thorolund Talisman has a fixed magic adjustment, and Velka's Talisman scales with intelligence instead of faith.

Talismans are equipped as Weapons and can be placed in either the right or left weapon slots. The weak attack or block button (depending on which hand the talisman is equipped in) will cast the selected miracle. Both the right-handed and left-handed strong attacks result in a swift yet weak strike. When used as melee weapons, all talismans deal Strike damage. Although talismans possess damage reduction values, they cannot be used to block attacks
