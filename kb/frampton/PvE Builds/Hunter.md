**Hunter** is a starting Class in Dark Souls. The Hunter is the sole class that begins with a bow, providing an early long-range attack option not available to other starting classes.  It starts with a Shortsword and a Short Bow as its starting weapons. The Starting Classes of Dark Souls will only give you a head start on certain stats. This is because you will be able to freely change your playstyle and build as you progress through out the game.

> Bow-wielding hunter. Can handle close range but vulnerable to magic.
