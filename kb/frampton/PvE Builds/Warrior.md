**Warrior** is a starting Class in Dark Souls**.**The **Warrior** is meant for players who prefer high damage and Slow paced classes. It starts with a Longsword and a Heater Shield (one of the better shields in the game) as its starting offensive and defensive equipments. The Starting Classes of Dark Souls will only give you a head start on certain stats. This is because you will be able to freely change your playstyle and build as you progress through out the game.

> Fearless warrior. Weapon expert. High strength, dexterity.

### Starting Equipment

This class starts with the following equipment:

Longswordheater shieldstandard helmhard leather armorhard leather gauntletshard leather boots

### Notes about This Class

* The Warrior starts with one of the better shields in the game, and high enough stats to use many decent weapons right away. It's a good class for people who want to jump right into the game with weapons and melee.
* This class is tied for the lowest combined casting stats among starting classes. This is ideal for a player that desires a full-on melee combat fighter due to it's stat distribution. On the flip side, it is a poor choice for those planning to utilize any form of magic.

### Builds That Use Class

* Purely speaking PvP-ONLY: The Warrior is one of two classes that can pull off the 50 Vitality, 40 Endurance, 40 Strength, 40 Dexterity build at Soul level 125 along with the Bandit.
