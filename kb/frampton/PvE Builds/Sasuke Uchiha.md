This is a player-created **Build** for Dark Souls. If you want help making your own build, you can consult this useful page on how to make a build by Blaine.

* Build Name: Sasuke Uchiha
* Soul Level: 27 min, as high as you want
* Starting Class: Cleric (to get started on faith) or any
* Gift: Master key or any
* Build Focus: PvE

### Build Equipment

* **Right Hand**: Uchigatana, Longbow
* **Left Hand**: Pyromancy Flame, Talisman of choice
* **Head**: --
* **Chest**: Dingy Robe
* **Hands**: --
* **Legs**: Brigand Trousers
* **Rings**: You can use any rings that you want. The Dark wood grain ring is preferred. The East wood grain ring is also useful so you don't have to repair your sword so much (since there's no shield it will take a beating).
* **Pyromancies:**
  Any of the basic offensive pyromancies
  Have Great fireball equipped when you get the chance
  No chaos, poison, or other pyromacies
* **Miracles:**
  Only lightning spear and great lightning spear

### Build Strategy

**Pros**
- Decent damage output
- Good offensive miracles
- Very agile

**Cons**
- No shield
- Low defense values

**Notes**
This build is just meant to resemble Sasuke; it's not meant to make any part of the game easier or anything. In fact, it will probably be harder for most because of the lack of shield, but if you play enough without one you kind of get used to it.

Make sure you change your eyes to red and have semi long black hair. Try to get the face as close to Sasuke as you can.

If you want you can keep the sword normal or raw and use gold pine resin, but no other resin or buff.

You can use pretty much any item; also, the Skull lantern is allowed.
