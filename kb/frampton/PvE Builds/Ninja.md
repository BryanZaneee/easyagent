### Build Strategy

The reason I choose to use the parrying dagger and the mailbreaker is because they look like Sai.

the Iaito has a more flexible moveset than the uchigitana. you could as easily use the chaos blade if you want.

Throwing knifes
Poison throwing knifes
black fire bombs
Lloyd's Talisman
the mailbreaker is the weapon with most riposte damage in the whole game. you will want to either make it chaos infused or normal +15 and use a resin. to maximice damage imput.

remember to put points into resistance since you will have no poise and almost no defense with the shadow set. the rest is up to you.
