### Details

You are a Goddess First Class, Belldandy! Release your holy power upon this world!
Status at level 80 (end of New Game): 30 vit - 19 atn - 11 end - 12 str - 8 dex - 11 res - 41 int - 30 fai - 10 hum
Status at level 145 (end of NG+, with **fast roll**): 41 vit - 28 atn - 32 end - 12 str - 8 dex - 11 res - 45 int - 50 fai - 10 hum

**Weapons:**

### Route

- Loot the Fire Link Shrine for some starting equipments, a shield will be useful to pair with your Mace (2).
- Collect some stuff in the beginning of the areas, Firekeeper Soul in New Londo Ruins, the spell Remedy (14) down some stairs in Blighttown (optional now), Grass Crest Shield after the Valley of Drakes, also level up Vitality to 16 and Faith to 20.
- Complete Undead Burg and cross the bridge with the dragon, to unlock the gate and join the Sunlight covenant (22)(16).
- Before the boss of Undead Parish, summon Solaire to help you with the battle, then buy some stuff from Oswald (4) and return to Fire Link (20).
- Complete Darkroot Basin for now, and if you want, buy the Crest from Andre for 20000 souls, to farm souls in the Garden.
- Go down The Depths and unlock Blighttown, but before going there, go back to Fire Link (12) to buy some spells (17) and be ready for a long trip.
- Cross Blighttown and Quelaag's Domain, to finally reach Demon Ruins and beat Ceaseless Discharge for the Chaos Flame Ember.
- Anastacia will be dead by now (8), but there is a bonfire nearby at Undead Parish to use, before you go back to the Azylum and loot that area (also trade some stuff with Snuggly).
- Complete the Garden now, defeating Sif, the Hydra and the golem, to save Dusk of Oolacile (15).
- Going down the Catacombs, defeat the boss and save Reah in the Tomb of Giants.
- Talk to Reah at Undead Parish (13), then prepare yourself for the Sen's Fortress (21). You should have the Chaos Mace by now, to make things easier.
- At Arno Londo (11), don't visit the Darkmoon Tomb yet, but enter and complete the Painted World of Ariamis right now.
- Return to Londo castle and pay a visit to Gwynevere, invade Lautrec world and check the giant blacksmith.
- Talk to Frampt to place the Lordvessel, proceed to complete the Tomb of Giants (5).
- The next stop is the Demon Ruins and then Lost Izalith, don't forget to farm Sunlight Medals from the Chaos Bugs in the area, so you can level up your covenant (22)(16).
- Join the Darkmoon covenant now (23), then you can farm Souvenir of Reprisal from the Crow Demons in the Painted World of Ariamis to incrase your rank (3). Also, farm Phalanx with your Wrath of the Gods miracle for easy souls, this is the best farm spot of the game. Change your covenant after (7)(24).
- Proceed to complete Duke's Archive (9)(18) and Crystal Cave (1).
- Now is the time to finish New Londo Ruins and defeat the Four Kings.
- Go back to the lake and enter the DLC, complete everything there (19) and return to the Fire Link.
- Now complete in any order, The Great Hollow (optional), Ash Lake (optional) and kill all the NPCs and Firekeepers for extra items and souls (optional);
- Enter the Kiln of the First Flame and finish the game (my recommended minimal level before a NG+ is 80).
- During the NG+ buy the Bracelet of the Great Lord (10) from Domhnall of Zena, to finally finish this build!
