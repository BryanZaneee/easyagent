### Build Equipment

Weapons:
Right Hand:
-Iaito +15 / Dragon bone fist +5(acting as Beowulf)
Left hand:
-Claymore+15 / Dragon bone fist +5(only obtainable in NG+)/Catalyst(any)
until you obtain the second Dragon bone fist +5 don't use the first one. So u'll be going "Force Edge/Yamato" or just "Yamato".("Beowulf" is just an optional style. but Vergil uses it as a main weapon.)

Armor:
-Helm: NONE!
-Body: wanderer coat +5 ( The most equal to the set of Vergil)
-Gloves: Witch Gloves+5
-Legs: Eastern Leggings+5

Rings:
- Dark Wood Grain Ring(try to kill only the Guardian) (PRIORITY!)
- Covetous Gold Serpent Ring (for Humanity/ Titanite Farming)
- Covetous Silver Serpent Ring (for leveling up)
- Ring of Steel Protection (for boss fights)
- Cloranthy Ring (for PVP)
- Hornet Ring (for Parry Love :D)

Sorceries:
(just plain and simple two Sorceries)
-Homing Soulmass
-Homing Crystal Soulmass
-Fall Control(if needed)

### Build Strategy

Note:
Starting out with Thief means you'll need to get Uchigatana, ASAP! So best if you buy everything you need from the Undead Merchant(Male) before killing him.
For gods SAKE USE WHITE HAIR and swepting back or something!
