### Build Strategy

Covenant: Blade of the Darkmoon
 As a Darkmoon Guerilla, it is your job to deal damage with deadly speed and efficiency. Recommend Shotel to break past shield turtles and with the high endurance, you can keep the combo going. If you use Ring of Fog with Hornet Ring, You can run to them from behind, backstab, and still get away if you want to. I just love the look of the armours but you can use gold-hemmed if you wish. Use the medium shields with 3lbs so you have 100 physical reduction with less weight. You don't have to upgrade your faith, but if you want heal and lightning go ahead. Or you can even use sorcery if you like. Remember to prioritize dodging become more skillful and less dependent on your shield .Try this Agile Warrior class out and message me if you like it.
