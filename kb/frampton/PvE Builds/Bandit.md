**Bandit** is a starting Class in Dark Souls.  The **Bandit** is meant for players who prefer a long-range style class that can also be decent in melee. It starts with a Battle Axe, and a Spider Shield as its starting weapons. The Starting Classes of Dark Souls will only give you a head start on certain stats. This is because you will be able to freely change your playstyle and build as you progress through out the game.

> Savage bandit. High strength. Wields heavy battleaxe.
