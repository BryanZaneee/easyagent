* Build Name: Darkmoon Gwyndolin Build
* Build Level:
* Build Focus: PvE
* Build Main Stats:
* Starting Class: Any (pyromancer and sorcerer work well)
* Starting gift: any (master key may make easier)
  ok this build may seem tricky at first for you have to level in these main stats: Attunement, Intelligence, Faith, Endurance, Vitality and either Dexterity or Strength if you want to go hybrid.

### Level Stats

Note: these stats will be based on the pyromancer's starting stats (pyromancer is the class i used to do this) boosting intelligence and attunement early on may make game easier but remember that you will need to branch off into vitality, endurance, and any other stat you choose
with this build you may or may not die a lot at first, it all depends on the player and how you start off, but this seems to be an excellent PvE build due to the fact that most of the enemies have hardly any magic resistances. I have also won quite a few PvP matches with this build due to most players also failing to have a good magic deffense, but with the spells, miracles, and pyromancies you should have equipped your low deffence wont matter too much in the end.

these stats are optional beginning you can choose whichever stats you want to start leveling at first, also humanity is not listed for it only increases item drops and deals with nonrelated skills
