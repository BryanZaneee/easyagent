### Build Strategy

Lore: Long ago in the time of the Ancients, When colorless Fog spread over the land of Boletaria so did Demons, There stood a Group or close Brothers who were known as Biorr and Vallarfax, Or The Twin Fangs, Before the Fog could consume the land Vallarfax, The youngest of the Brothers, Left Boletaria to tell of what happened to the once Powerful land, His stories brought Would-be Knights and Famed Adventures, Though... They all fell to the Colorless fog.., unable to find a worthy Knight Vallarfax made his way back to the Lands of Boletaria, He was unable, as like the rest of the Knights, To make his way through The Fog, Though Un-like the other Knights he was not slain nor did he live, Vallarfax had become Immortal, At a price, He would travel the the World through the Distortion of time searching for Humanity to keep himself sane, Finally after a Century he came across the land of Lodran, His thirst for Humanity and keeping himself Sane made him stay in the Hell-driven land, Much like his home of Boletaria, He may have been a Knight as strong as the Legends told, But he too Fell into a driven Madness unable to keep his Skills he was taken to the Asylum and left to die in his Warped state of mind. The voice of Old King Allant had spoken to him in this state, Or so he thought, And with new found Sanity he will make his way Through the Asylum. *This Is were your Lore Begins, And Mine Ends.*
