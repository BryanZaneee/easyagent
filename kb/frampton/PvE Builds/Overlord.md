Before you start to read any further, please note that this build is based primarily around player skill. If you don't have patience to survive repetition and the time to devote to obtaining the various skills necessary, do not bother reading any further. So far, I have obtained level 36 with this build and will continue to add onto this page as I advance further through the game. Also please note that if you haven't updated the game to 1.05 yet, do so now. You can't advance in this build unless you have the covenant Warrior of Sunlight and you can only get that through the patch which lowers the necessary Faith stat to join from 50 to 25.

### Build Strategy

This class is built around later dominating the environment through ranged spells, Riposte and brute force.

Advantages: A sense of pure victory when defeating a difficult boss

Disadvantages: The first twenty levels will make you hate life itself.

This is a build for those with the skill and drive to want a challenge. Thus, when looking over the build with an unbiased eye, you will find that it is a lot of challenge for simply egotistical reward. There are certainly better builds out there, so if it proves too much a challenge, find another.

Level Explanation

As you may notice, the stats don't raise in any sensible manner. Later, when reading the beginning guide for this build, it will start to make sense.

From the first opportunity to level, focus should be on the Faith stat and raising it to 25 for Heal, Lightning Spear and the Warrior of Sunlight Covenant. Once that is accomplished, focus turns to the Strength stat for use of this build's third primary weapon- The Claymore. From there, putting a point into Attunement allows for Heal and Lightning Spear to be your primary spells. Next, adding four solid points into Endurance allows for better stamina. Adding the next five points into Vitality makes sure you survive longer.


## Covenants

You begin with no covenant, no surprise there. Obtaining
Way of White to get Heal is your first goal.

Once you have reached level 20, getting
Warrior of Sunlight as your second Covenant is a must. It is the primary Covenant for this build.
