### Build Strategy

This build can get INSANE damage on bosses! Use Power Within and one of the Blade spells and watch the INSANITY! Despite being intended for co-op and pve, this build is perfectly viable for pvp due to fast damage output and access to spells like WOTG and DM/SL Blade.
This build is hard to get used to, due to no poise and no shield, but this adds challenge, and keeps you on your toes. Do try this build out, you'll not regret it!
