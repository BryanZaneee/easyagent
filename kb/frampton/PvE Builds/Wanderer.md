**Wanderer** is a starting Class in Dark Souls. The **Wanderer** is meant for players who prefer low damage but fast mobility. The extra points in Intelligence and Attunement means the Wanderer can also greatly be suited for combining sorcery with fast Dexterity weapons. The Starting Classes of Dark Souls will only give you a head start on certain stats. This is because you can freely change your playstyle and build as you progress throughout the game.

> Aimless wanderer. Wields scimitar. High dexterity.

### Class Notes & Tips

* While it may appear to be a Dexterity-focused class at first glance, the extra points in Intelligence and Attunement means the Wanderer can also greatly be suited for combining sorcery with fast Dexterity weapons. Spells like Great Magic Weapon or Crystal Magic Weapon are great candidates.
