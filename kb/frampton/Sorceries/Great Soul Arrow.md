**Great Soul Arrow** is a Sorcery in Dark Souls. **Great Soul Arrow**, an advanced sorcery more potent than the Soul Arrow, effective against enemies resistant to physical attacks, and its power is linked to the caster's intelligence. To cast a sorcery, you must use a Catalyst or Special Weapons that can cast Sorceries.

> *Sorcery which improves upon Soul Arrow. Fire strong soul arrow.
>  *At the Vinheim Dragon School, the acquisition of this spell marks an apprentice's coronation as a sorcerer*.*

### Use

* Ranged Attack
* Stronger soul arrow, same cast time as soul arrow, fewer uses.

### Acquired

* Griggs of Vinheim
* Big Hat Logan

### Notes and Tips

* Equip multiple to make up for reduced uses.
* This spell seems to deal damage equal to (Catalyst Mag. Adj. x 1.3). This was tested by using two catalysts at Int 50 against the first Undead in Firelink Shrine.
