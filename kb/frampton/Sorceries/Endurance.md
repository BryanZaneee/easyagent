**Endurance** is a Stat in Dark Souls. **Endurance** controls the player's Stamina, max Equip Load and resistance to Bleeding. Attributes can be increased by assigning points into them when Leveling Up.

### Information

Endurance controls the player's Stamina, max Equip Load and resistance to Bleeding.

* Stamina will not be increased after 40 Endurance, but Equip Load and Bleed Resistance will.
  + Actions reduces stamina such as dashing and attacking, stamina regenerates with a brief pause on actions. When stamina runs out, any actions cannot be carried out.
  + Stamina is capped at 160, which is when endurance reaches level 40. See the information box on the right side.
* The roll and run speed of the player will vary with the amount of equipped weight.
  + Equip weight above 100% of total Equip Load leaves the player unable to roll or run.
  + Equip weight between 50% to 100% of Equip Load gives slow movement, and causes the player to 'fat roll'.
  + Equip weight between 25% to 50% of Equip Load gives medium movement.
  + Equip weight below 25% of Equip Burden gives fast movement.
* Stamina regeneration is also reduced by higher equip burdens, at the same equip loads as roll and run speed reduction.
* Stamina regeneration is also increased passively by the following items/equipment.
  + Mask of the Child
  + Grass Crest Shield
  + Cloranthy Ring
  + Green Blossom (Consumable)
  + Note that these items/equipment can be stacked.
* Max equip burden can be increased further by wearing Havel's Ring, Ring of Favor and Protection, or Mask of the Father.
