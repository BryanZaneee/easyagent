**Resist Curse** is a Sorcery in Dark Souls. This sorcery removes curse build-up. Created by Ingward, a red-robed healer and guardian of the seal in New Londo, it provides protection against curses. To cast a sorcery, you must use a Catalyst or Special Weapons that can cast Sorceries.

> *Sorcery of the red-robed remedician Ingward, guardian of the seal in New Londo. Sacrifice humanity to undo curse.
> Abhorrent curses eat away at the core of one's very existence, and cleansing oneself of curses is no easy task indeed*

### Usage

* Support, Self
* Removes Curse build up.

### Acquired

* Ingward

### Notes and Tips

* If you're having trouble with curses and don't have the Cursebite Ring yet, this is a good alternative till you can get it but not very useful as curse areas are pretty limited.
* Contrary to the name and description, this sorcery does not increase curse resistance or remove curse using humanity.
* Its counterpart Remedy alleviates the other status ailments.
