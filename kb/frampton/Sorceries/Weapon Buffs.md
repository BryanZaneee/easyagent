# Weapon Buffs

| Weapon Buffs in Dark Souls 1 |
| --- |
| Sunlight Blade |
| Weapon Buffs enhance weapons using consumables or spells by infusing them with elemental damage or other useful effects for a limited duration. Only certain weapons are Enchantable and only one Weapon Buff can be active at a time. |

**Weapon Buffs**, also sometimes referred to as **Weapon Enchantments** or **Weapon Augmentations**, are a type of effect in **Dark Souls**. See also Self Buffs.

### Effects

* Weapon Buffs are placed by the player on their weapon to add elemental damage or other attributes for a limited duration.
* A Weapon Buff usually applies a unique visual effect to the weapon which can indicate what buff was applied.
* The strength of Weapon Buffs obtained by casting a spell scale with the Magic Adjustment stat of the Catalyst or Talisman used.
* Weapon Buffs are applied to the weapon equipped in the main (right) hand and not the off (left) hand (with the exception of Magic Shield and Strong Magic Shield).
* To apply Weapon Buffs via a spell, the catalyst or talisman must be in the left hand for the buff to be applied to the weapon in the right hand. For buffing shields or weapons with Magic Shield or Strong Magic Shield, the catalyst must be equipped in the right hand with the shield or weapon in the left hand.
* Weapon Buffs differ from Ring or Equipment Effects in that they are **mutually exclusive**:
  + There can only be one Weapon Buff active at any given time.
  + During the duration of a Weapon Buff **no other Weapon Buffs can be cast**.
* Weapon Buffs end either on their own after they expire or can be removed by the player by unequipping the buffed weapon, changing to the secondary weapon in the same slot or reloading the area. Reloading the area can be accomplished by:
  + Using a Homeward Bone or the miracle Homeward
  + Exiting the game and reentering

### Enchantable Weapons

* Weapon Buffs may only be used on weapons that are Enchantable. When an incompatible weapon is equipped, Weapon Buff spells or consumables will be greyed out and unusable. Compatible weapons include standard weapons ascended to the Regular, Raw, or Crystal upgrade paths excluding Bows, Crossbows, Catalysts and Talismans. Weapons ascended to the Lightning, Magic, Enchanted, Divine, Occult, Fire or Chaos paths cannot be buffed as they already possess permanent elemental augmentation.
* Boss soul weapons upgraded with Demon Titanite are not Enchantable.
* Additionally, some dragon weapons (upgraded with Dragon Scales) and most unique weapons (upgraded with Twinkling Titanite) are not Enchantable. There are exceptions to this rule; the following weapons ARE Enchantable:
  + Dragon Weapons:
    - Obsidian Greatsword
    - Dragon King Greataxe
    - Dragon Tooth
    - Dragon Bone Fist
  + Unique Weapons:
    - Silver Knight Straight Sword
    - Stone Greataxe

### Notes

* **\*Any** shield or weapon that can block in the left hand can be buffed with Magic Shield or Strong Magic Shield regardless of upgrade path.
* Use of the Crown of Dusk, Crown of the Dark Sun, Bellowing Dragoncrest Ring, or Ring of the Sun's Firstborn does NOT increase the damage of Weapon Buffs. The damage is solely dependent on the Magic Adjustment value of the catalyst or talisman casting the buff spell.
* The Lingering Dragoncrest Ring does increase the duration of Sorcery buffs only. It has no effect on Miracles.
