**Strong Magic Shield** is a Sorcery in Dark Souls. **Strong Magic Shield** grants the affected shield or left-hand weapon complete resistance to physical and all other damage types for the spell's duration. This makes even weapons with blocking capabilities function effectively as shields. This sorcery is an enhanced version of Magic Shield, offering high magical augmentation to the left-hand shield. To cast a sorcery, you must use a Catalyst or Special Weapons that can cast Sorceries.

> *Sorcery which improves upon Magic Shield. High magic augmentation: left shield.
>  *At Vinheim Dragon School, only magic swordsmen on special orders are allowed to learn this spell which grants temporary strength to greatshields*.*

### Usage

* Buff, Support, Shield
* **Duration: 15 seconds**
* Reinforces the equipped shield or weapon able to block in the left hand with a stronger magic effect. Works on all types of shields (even unique or upgraded to any path) and on all weapons able to block while on the left hand (even unique or upgraded to any path).
* Increases stability of the effected left hand item by 50% which reduces the stamina used to deflect a hit considerably (**only on shields**) and improves all the resistances of the **shield or weapon** enchanted to 100%.

### Notes and Tips

* Gives the affected shield or left hand weapon 100% physical resistance and 100% resistance to all damage types for the duration of the spell, making even weapons decent shields provided they have a blocking capability. This includes shield-like items such as the Skull Lantern, and the Dark Hand (the distinctive blue glow will not be visible on the Dark Hand's shield effect, but it is in there). Stamina cost for blocking is **not** reduced on weapons, and it does not increase the damage of left hand weapons.
* If the stability of the shield ≥100 after the spell is applied all attacks blocked will incur no stamina loss to the player, albeit until the spell runs out. This breakpoint occurs at 67 base stability of the shield.
* Extremely useful against any boss, but particularly those that have OHKO attacks or high burst damage, such as the 4 Kings, Kalameet, Manus, the Sanctuary Guardian, or the Hydras. Even a medium shield with Strong Magic Shield on it can soak all of Manus's dark magic sprays or one of his multi-hit attack combos with ease.
* Very useful in PvP as a counter against common spells/pyromancies such as Pursuers, CSS, Black Flame, or Wrath of the Gods. It can also be paired with a body buff (such as Hidden Body or Power Within) and various other active spell effects such as HCSM/Pursuers or Fall Control. Given the relatively short duration of this spell, it is best to cast it last.
* Can also cause deflection in PvP when blocking melee attacks by other human players.
* Unequipping the shield, or letting the spell run out, cancels this effect. As a consequence, it usually cannot be paired with a weapon buff, as using a focus to cast SMS will extinguish the weapon buff, and vice-versa.
* Does not raise damage when used on a weapon on the left hand.
* Duration increased by Lingering Dragoncrest Ring.
