**Remedy** is a Sorcery in Dark Souls. This spell reduces or eliminates build-up of Poison, Toxin, and Bleeding, and cures Poison and Toxin status. Developed by Yulva, a red-robed Sealer of New Londo, it is effective in mitigating various poisonous effects. To cast a sorcery, you must use a Catalyst or Special Weapons that can cast Sorceries.

> *Sorcery of the red-robed Yulva, one of the Sealers of New Londo. Reduce bleeding and poison build-up, and undo various poisonous effects.*
>  *One of the New Londo's unique healing sorceries. Perhaps she abandoned her Sealer duty to take her healing arts back to Blighttown*.

### Usage

* Support, Self
* Lessens or removes Poison, Toxic and Bleed build-up, also Cures Poison and Toxic status.

### Acquired

* Blighttown, in a chest next to the Crimson Set (accessed by crossing the branch at the top of the waterwheel).

### Notes and Tips

* Useful anywhere you encounter ailments; Blighttown, Lost Izalith, Painted World of Ariamis.
* Its counterpart spell Resist Curse alleviates Curse buildup.
* This spell would become Caressing Prayer in *Dark Souls 2*.
