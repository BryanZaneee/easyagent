**Magic Weapon** is a Sorcery in Dark Souls. **Magic Weapon** is a sorcery that infuses the weapon in your other hand with magic, adding magic damage to its current effects. This sorcery is ideal for spellcasters who use swords, providing magical enhancement to the right-hand weapon. To cast a sorcery, you must use a Catalyst or Special Weapons that can cast Sorceries.

> *Sorcery for casters who wield swords. Magic augmentation: right weapon.
> The power of the magic swordsmen of Vinheim is predicated upon this and Magic Shield. Many warriors learn sorcery just for this.*

### Usage

* Weapon Buff
* Duration: 60 seconds
* Enhances the right-hand weapon with Magic Damage.
* Weapon must be standard, crystal, or raw. Some Dragon and Unique weapons can be enchanted too (see Weapon Buffs).

### Acquired

* Griggs of Vinheim
* Big Hat Logan

### Notes and Tips

* Adds 0.8 x your Magic Adjustment stat (NOT Intelligence) magic damage to your weapon.
* This item cannot be used if any other Weapon Buffs are active, or vice versa.
