**White Dragon Breath** is a Sorcery in Dark Souls.**White Dragon Breath** is a sorcery crafted by Logan in his obsession with Seath the Scaleless. It releases Seath's crystal breath, causing a line of crystals to emerge from the ground and shoot directly at your target. To cast a sorcery, you must use a Catalyst or Special Weapons that can cast Sorceries.

> *Sorcery developed by Logan during his infatuation with Seath the Scaleless. Emit crystal breath of Seath the Scaleless.*
> *Although it no longer causes curses, what madness caused old Big Hat to appropriate this frightful power of the ancient dragons?*

### Usage

* Ranged Attack
* A straight line of crystals pop out of the ground in front of you, shooting towards your target.

### Acquired

* Dropped by Big Hat Logan after he goes insane at the end of his complete quest line.
* Alternatively, the spell can be obtained before completing the quest line by killing passive Big Hat Logan after buying all of his spells. This will prevent the player from obtaining Logan's Catalyst and the remainder of the Big Hat's Set as killing him at this point fails the questline.

### Notes and Tips

* Can only hit targets on the ground.
* Can hit multiple targets.
* The spell can climb up walls.
* A slow casting time comparable to heavy soul arrow.
* This spell seems to deal damage equal to (Catalyst Mag. Adj. x 2.5). This was tested by using two catalysts at Int 50 against the first Undead in Firelink Shrine.
