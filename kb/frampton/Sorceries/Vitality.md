**Vitality** is a Stat in Dark Souls. **Vitality** is a stat that modifies player's maximum Health points in Dark Souls. Attributes can be increased by assigning points into them when Leveling Up.

### Information

 Vitality governs the player's maximum Health points.

* This stat becomes drastically less useful after 30 vitality, and then again after 49 vitality. The amount of health points gained decreases from 34 at 30 vitality to 11 at 50 vitality.
* Health can be further influenced by items. See Health page for further information.
