**Dark Orb** is a Sorcery in Dark Souls. An abyssal sorcery uncovered by a sorcerer from Oolacile who was on the edge of madness. It launches a massive Dark Orb at the target, delivering a powerful, long-range dark attack. To cast a sorcery, you must use a Catalyst or Special Weapons that can cast Sorceries.

> *Abyss sorcery discovered by an Oolacile sorcerer on the brink of madness. Fires Giant Dark Orb.
> In contrast to standard soul sorceries, Abyss sorcery is weighty and inflict physical damage. Perhaps a human soul is closer to matter in its humanity.*

### Usage

* Ranged Attack
* Quickly fires a long-reaching Dark Mass at the target

### Acquired

* Found in Oolacile Township. *(AotA only)*
* Video Location Timestamp 6:52

### Notes and Tips

* Dark Sorceries deal a combination of physical and magic damage. The magic damage is determined by the INT scaling of your catalyst (as with normal sorceries), and the physical damage is determined by the STR and DEX scaling of your catalyst.
* This extra physical damage is not shown on your catalyst, and is a hidden stat.
* Two-handing the catalyst does not increase the physical damage of Dark Sorceries.
