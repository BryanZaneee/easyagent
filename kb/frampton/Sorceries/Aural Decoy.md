**Aural Decoy** is a Sorcery in Dark Souls. Similar to the Alluring Skull, this sorcery can be used to lure enemies away by creating a sound originating in the distance.  To cast a sorcery, you must use a Catalyst or Special Weapons that can cast Sorceries.

> *Sorcery used by playful sorcerers. Lure enemies away by creating a sound originating in the distance.
> This spell is not the first choice for serious sorcerers, but it has a surprising amount of applications, some of them extremely effective.*

### Usage

* Support, Ranged
* Lures enemies away by creating a sound originating in the distance.

### Acquired

* Griggs of Vinheim

### Notes and Tips

* The effect is similar to the Alluring Skull, but it affects a larger variety of enemy types.
* A laughing sound can be heard from the location of the impact, which will divert the targets attention and attacks toward it.
* When used against Shield and Spear Soldiers, they will often turn their backs against the caster, leaving them open to a backstab.
* Aural Decoy is helpful in the Tomb of the Giants when trying to obtain the Large Divine Ember from the room full of giant skeletons if using a bow/magic to take them out from safety.
* Aural Decoy is also very useful against the giant sentinels in Anor Londo, often disrupting their attack patterns and also causing them to turn around, which exposes them to easy attacks from behind.
