**Soul Arrow** is a Sorcery in Dark Souls. **Soul Arrow** is a basic sorcery that fires a small soul arrow forward. To cast a sorcery, you must use a Catalyst or Special Weapons that can cast Sorceries.

> *Elementary sorcery. Fire a soul arrow.*
>  *Soul arrows inflict magic damage, making them effective against iron armor, tough scales, and other physically resilient materials*

### Used

* Ranged Attack.
* Fires a magical projectile.

### Acquired

* Griggs of Vinheim
* Big Hat Logan
* Blacksmith Rickert of Vinheim

### Notes and Tips

* Starting spell for the Sorcerer class.
* This spell seems to deal damage equal to (Catalyst Mag. Adj. x 1.1). This was tested by using two catalysts at Int 50 against the first Undead in Firelink Shrine.
