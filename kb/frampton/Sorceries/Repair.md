**Repair** is a Sorcery in Dark Souls. Repair, an ancient sorcery lost land of Oolacil used to repair only equipped weapons and armor. To cast a sorcery, you must use a Catalyst or Special Weapons that can cast Sorceries.

> *Ancient sorcery of the lost land of Oolacile. Repair equipped weapons and armor.
> This sorcery was part of everyday life in Oolacile. Its effects resembles repair powder.*

### Usage

* Support
* Repairs only **equipped** weapons and armor.

### Acquired

+ Dusk of Oolacile

### Notes and Tips

* If you're not near a blacksmith or bonfire then it can be useful help.
* This sorcery can repair the crystalline armor set.
* Useful for some weapons with special attacks that quickly eat up durability, depending on exactly how much durability the ability costs.
* Most notably, can be cast repeatedly by resting over and over at a bonfire, allowing you to repair all your weapons and armor at no soul cost.
* It can not repair crystal weapons or shields.
* It repairs 150 durability points to your appointed weapon.
