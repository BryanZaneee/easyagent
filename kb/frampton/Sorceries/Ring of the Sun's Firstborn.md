# Ring of the Sun's Firstborn

| Ring of the Sun's Firstborn |
| --- |
| 4011 |
| Can be used to boosts the strength of offensive miracles by 20%. |

**Ring of the Sun's Firstborn** is a Ring in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered. **Ring of the Sun's Firstborn**, increases the strength of offensive miracles by 20%. This enhancement also applies to restorative miracles, boosting their healing power. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> Lord Gwyn's firstborn, who inherited the sunlight, once wore this ancient ring.
> Lord Gwyn's firstborn was a god of war, but his foolishness led to a loss of the annals, and rescinding of his deific status.
> Today, even his name is not known.

### Effect

* Boosts the strength of offensive miracles by 20%.

### Location

* Found in Anor Londo, at the bottom of the rotating bridge by the bonfire (Darkmoon Tomb).
* Video Location Timestamp 10:12

### Application

* A great way to increase the power of a player's miracles.
* Miracle potency affects the amount of health healed by restorative miracles as well.
* This does not increase the knock-back on miracles such as force.
* Does not boost the power of Weapon Buffs (i.e. Sunlight Blade or Darkmoon Blade).

### Notes and Tips

* Boosts the strength of offensive miracles by 20%.
* This ring would reappear in *Dark Souls 3* as the Ring of the Sun's First Born, with a different but more clearer description of "*The Sun's first born was once a god of war, until he was stripped of his stature as punishment for his foolishness. No wonder his very name has slipped from the annals of history*". It was not that his foolishness led to a loss of the annuals, but that his name was lost to the annuals of history due to his claimed foolishness.
