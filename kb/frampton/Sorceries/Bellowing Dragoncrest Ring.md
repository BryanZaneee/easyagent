# Bellowing Dragoncrest Ring

| Bellowing Dragoncrest Ring |
| --- |
| 4015 |
| Boosts the strength of sorceries. |

**Bellowing Dragoncrest Ring** is a Ring in Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered. **Bellowing Dragoncrest Ring**, engraved with an everlasting dragon, this ring is ideal for magic-focused players, as it boosts the power of sorceries. Players can equip up to 2 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible.

> A special ring granted to only the most accomplished sorcerers at Vinheim Dragon School.
> The ring is engraved with an everlasting dragon, and boosts the strength of sorceries.

### Effects

* Increases Sorcery, and Pyromancy damage before defense calculations by 20%, resulting in a total damage increase of generally 20-30%, more for less damaging spells.
* Does not increase damage of any Weapon Buff spells.
* Does not increase damage of miracles.

### Location

* Can be bought from Griggs of Vinheim for 20,000 souls.

### Application

* This is a fantastic ring for any player who is putting a focus on magic.
* Wearing this ring with the Oolacile Ivory Catalyst can allow characters with low intelligence to cast viable sorceries.
* Stacks with Crown of the Dark Sun
* Stacks with Crown of Dusk.

### Notes & Tips

* This ring reappears in *Dark Souls 3*, where it no longer affects pyromancies.
