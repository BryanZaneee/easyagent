**Cast Light** is a Sorcery in Dark Souls. An ancient sorcery that generates light to illuminate the surroundings. To cast a sorcery, you must use a Catalyst or Special Weapons that can cast Sorceries.

> *Ancient sorcery of the lost land of Oolacile. Cast a bright light upon surroundings. This light-producing sorcery is elementary but nonetheless demonstrates the achievements in mysticism of Oolacile. Such magic has not been developed even in Vinheim*

### Usage

* Support
* Duration: 5 minutes
* Cast light generates a bright light-orb, above your character's head, which illuminates the surroundings.

### Acquired

* Dusk of Oolacile

### Notes and Tips

+ You are only able to see your own light, meaning you cannot see the light of a co-op player, or vice versa.
+ This spell is especially useful in the Tomb of Giants.
+ Can be casted while any other buff is active, can even override itself.
+ Will continue it's duration while resting at a bonfire, so if you cast it near a bonfire and rest/leave, you basically get an additional free cast.
+ One of three light sources available. The other two being the Skull Lantern and the Sunlight Maggot.
