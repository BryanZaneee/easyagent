**Key to Depths** is a Key in **Dark Souls**and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Key Items are used to progress quests, unlock new areas, and expand merchant inventories. They often function as literal keys, granting passage through doors or access to previously unreachable Locations.

> Key opening the door from the lower Undead Burg to the Depths.
>
> Those banished from the Undead Burg eke out their existence in the Depths, a damp lair with no trace of sunlight. Nearly half of the Depths form a perilous flooded labyrinth.

### Usage

* Unlocks door to the Depths. Go down stairs by the Capra location, the door is at the bottom of the tower by two thiefs.

### Location

* Lower Undead Burg. Dropped by Capra Demon.
