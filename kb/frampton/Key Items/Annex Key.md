**Annex Key** is a Key in **Dark Souls**. Key Items are used to progress quests, unlock new areas, and expand merchant inventories. They often function as literal keys, granting passage through doors or access to previously unreachable Locations.

> Key to the annex in the Painted World of Ariamis.
> In the wintery painted world, there is a structure resembling an old cathedral; its annex serves as a type of storehouse

### Usage

* Opens Annex in Painted World of Ariamis. Access to Dark Ember.

### Location

* Found in the underground sewers section in Painted World of Ariamis, located in a dead end behind an illusory wall.
