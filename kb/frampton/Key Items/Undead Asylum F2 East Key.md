**Undead Asylum F2 East Key** is a Key in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered.**Key Items are used to progress quests, unlock new areas, and expand merchant inventories. They often function as literal keys, granting passage through doors or access to previously unreachable Locations.

> Key to the iron bars on the east side of the second floor of the North Undead Asylum.
>
> The Undead Asylum is a giant Undead prison, segmented by countless iron bars.
>
> Even if an Undead were to escape from a cell, passage to the outside world would not be gained easily

### Usage

* It unlocks the locked door on the third level's east wing, behind the boulder trap, in the Northern Undead Asylum.

### Location

* The Elite Knight in the Northern Undead Asylum will give it to you during his dying moments, along with five Estus Flasks.
* The Elite Knight/Oscar of Astora also drops this key if you choose to put him out of his misery instead of adhering to his final wishes.
* Drops from Oscar of Astora. (Guaranteed - Northern Undead Asylum and Northern Undead Asylum Revisit)
