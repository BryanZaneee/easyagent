**Crest of Artorias** is a Key in **Dark Souls**. Key Items are used to progress quests, unlock new areas, and expand merchant inventories. They often function as literal keys, granting passage through doors or access to previously unreachable Locations.

> This crest opens a door in the Darkroot Garden sealed by ancient magic. The door leads to the grave of Sir Artorias the Abysswalker.
> Many adventurers have left for the grave, but none have returned, for they make easy prey for local bandits. With such dangers, the crest can do more harm than good in the hands of the uninitiated

### Usage

* This crest unlocks the magically sealed door in Darkroot Garden.
* This area also has two other entrances, which makes the crest optional. Go past the Hydra and up the ladders for the second entrance, cross the stone bridge and find the third entrance through Alvina's tower.
* However, using the Crest may still be favored, due to the fact that a convenient Bonfire is beside the gate, making your involuntary return trips far easier.

### Location

* Sold by Blacksmith Andre for 20,000 souls.
* Dropped by Blacksmith Andre if killed.

### Notes & Trivia

* Artorias is related to The Abyss Watchers of DS3
