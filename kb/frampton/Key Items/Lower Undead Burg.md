# Lower Undead Burg

**Lower Undead Burg** is an area in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. The Undead Burg is divided in two sections. Section 1 is known as the "*Upper Undead Burg*" is a linear section leading to the **Undead Parish**. Section 2, "*Lower Undead Burg*" is accessible through a locked door on the Wyvern bridge that can be opened with the **Basement Key** found in **Undead Parish** and serves as the gateway to the **Depths**.

### Welcome to the!

Down Below - Earlier in the **Upper Undead Burg**on Hellkite bridge, after defeating the Taurus Demon and right before confronting the **Bridge Wyvern,**there was a locked door. Having found the **Basement Key** in the **Undead Parish**, this door is now accessible (**NOTE:** If you have not disposed of the Wyvern yet, you may need to travel to the **bonfire** in Undead Burg by using the shortcut under the bridge; don't worry, we'll be unlocking two new short-cuts promptly, so this longer route will be a one-time inconvenience). Entering the door will lead down some stairs and to a long ladder. Upon reaching the outdoors, take the upward stairs on your right to open a gate at the top. This a shortcut between Upper Undead Burg and Lower Undead Burg.

udb basementkeydoor

New Friends - Head back down to where you were and head down the stairs on your right beside the **big fire**. Here you will need to prepare yourself as you will have your first encounter with **Undead Attack Dogs**; they are very pacey and are venomous, however do not have a lot of health. Lure all three of them (one at a time) while remaining on the stairs, for the most effective method of combating them. Head all the way down until you are standing next to a well, facing a door. The first door on the right will hold someone asking for help; accessible with the **Residence Key**. Inside you will find **Griggs of Vinheim** (A sorcery trainer who will sell a variety of useful sorceries when he's back in **Firelink Shrine**). To his right is a corpse in a barrel, destroy the barrel and pillage the corpse to acquire the **Sorcerer Set** and **Sorcerer's Catalyst**. Head left, down the alley to find find a new enemy – or should I say a few - the Torch Hollow. There are a total of 7 (two on the left, 5 on the right); luring them one at a time, as for most enemies in this game, is the best strategy. Heading right will lead to a corpse with **Twin Humanities**, and a dead end.

udb griggs

Counter-Attack - Head back until you reach a **wagon** you should destroy, the extra space will be useful for what's to come. Past the wagon you will see **three closed doors**, one on either side of you and another ahead. As you continue forward, they'll open and three **Undead Assassins** will leap out for an **ambush**(**NOTE:**Be sure to watch out for their throwing knives, and countering ability). In one of the rooms on the right you will find a corpse holding a **Mail Breaker**. Head back out to the alleyway and follow it until you come across some grass but refrain from stepping on the grass just yet, as it will trigger three Undead Assassins to jump out the buildings ahead for another ambush. Instead, first deal with the two **Undead Attack Dogs** further down the alley that you can lure; if only one of engages you, just keep in mind that there is another roaming ahead.

On the left in a small arched corridor you will find a corpse in a barrel with a **Large Soul of a Lost Undead**. In one of the assassin's rooms on the right, you will find a corpse with the **Thief Set** and a **Target Shield**. Continue down the alley until you come to the **fog gate**. Behind it is the area's **boss**. However, we are taking a little detour.

Easy Access - Take a right at the **fog gate** and travel down the stairs. You will see an Undead Assassin waiting in the distance, before engaging him it's best to engage/lure the one around the corner on the left, hoping to catch you off-guard. After taking care of them, head to the very end where you will be met with a large gate and a**Large Soul of a Lost Undead** on a corpse. Behind you now will be a door on your left that will grant you access to the Depths, but it's currently locked. So we want to head up the stairs on the right, leading into a building. Climb the spiral staircase where you will encounter an **Undead Archer** about halfway up. Continue upwards until the staircase is blocked with debris, and make a right to find yourself back in the aqueduct. On your right is the Undead Merchant (Female) that sells a variety of items, including moss. Continue along the aqueduct and open the gate to unlock a **shortcut** to Firelink Shrine.

The Goatman - Head back to the **fog gate**we dodged earlier to continue on your quest. **BE WARNED**, as soon as you enter the fog gate you will be attacked by two Undead Attack Dogs and the big boss; be sure to dispose of the dogs early so you can focus your efforts on the Capra**...**

**udb capra**

**Boss fight**: Capra Demon

**NOTE**: When you return to Lower Undead Burg, be aware of the fact that there will now be four Undead Attack Dogs where the Fog Gate was. Once you dispose of the boss you will obtain the Key to the Depths, with which you will be able to continue your quest. Make your way toward the locked door we found earlier on near the Lower Undead Burg / Firelink Shrine short-cut to go to the Depths.

## Speed Run Walkthrough

A fast walkthrough of how to get to the end of the level the fastest, picking up only essential items goes here.

* Unlock the locked door at the beginning of the HellKite Bridge, using the Basement Key obtained in the Undead Parish.
* Unlock the shortcut between the upper and lower Undead Burg on the right staircase.
* Unlock one of the doors on the left in the alleyway to encounter Grigg of Vinheim.
* Head back and prepare from mass amounts of ambushes from Undead Attack Dogs and Undead Assassins.
* Continue straight into fog gate to engage the Capra Demon.
