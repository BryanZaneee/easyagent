**Blighttown Key** is a Key in **Dark Souls**and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Key Items are used to progress quests, unlock new areas, and expand merchant inventories. They often function as literal keys, granting passage through doors or access to previously unreachable Locations.

> Key to Blighttown from the Depths of the Undead Burg. Swallowed by the Gaping Dragon.
> As its name suggests, Blighttown is a place of great pestilence. Even the polluted inhabitants of the Depths are aware of its dangers, and built this mighty door in hopes that they could remain safely separated.

### Usage

* Unlocks Blighttown. Huge doors beside Domhnall of Zena (sewer merchant) in Depths.
* This door can only be opened from the Depths side.

### Location

* Depths. Dropped by Gaping Dragon.
