**Mystery Key** is a Key in **Dark Souls**and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Key Items are used to progress quests, unlock new areas, and expand merchant inventories. They often function as literal keys, granting passage through doors or access to previously unreachable Locations.

> The purpose of this key is unknown. It appears to be a basic prison cell key

### Usage

* Free Lautrec. Behind a breakable door, on the upper floor of the Undead Church, opposite end of boss entrance.
* If Lautrec is freed, he will be available as a summon for the Bell Gargoyle boss.
* He will also give a gift of one Sunlight Medal when he returns to Firelink Shrine.
* If Lautrec is not set free, he will still escape his imprisonment after the Gargoyles are slain, but gives no gift.

### Location

* Found behind the first ladder, after the armored boar, when going through the cellar in Undead Parish.
