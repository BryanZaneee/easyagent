**Archive Tower Giant Cell Key** is a Key in **Dark Souls**. Key Items are used to progress quests, unlock new areas, and expand merchant inventories. They often function as literal keys, granting passage through doors or access to previously unreachable Locations.

> Key to the giant cell below the Duke's Archives Tower. The giant cell once imprisoned countless maidens, but is now empty, save for a few key persons. They struggle to uphold their sanity, as the horde of "mistakes" writhe at a fearfully close proximity

### Usage

* The Duke's Archives Tower. Opens cell behind Pisacas. Free Big Hat Logan and a Fire Keeper Soul.
* Video Location Timestamp 31:16

### Location

* Found in chest at bottom of Archives, by the exit to the Crystal Cave.
