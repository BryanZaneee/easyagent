**Key to New Londo Ruins** is a Key in **Dark Souls**. Key Items are used to progress quests, unlock new areas, and expand merchant inventories. They often function as literal keys, granting passage through doors or access to previously unreachable Locations.

> Key to the iron bars separating the ruins of New Londo and Drake Valley.
> The ruins of New Londo were blocked off, for the cursed ghosts posed danger to life and spirit, and the legends speak of a terrible Dark which was sealed away

### Usage

* Unlocks barred shortcut between upper New Londo Ruins and Valley of Drakes.

### Location

* Blighttown. In a chest at the end of the cave, when entering from Valley of Drakes.
