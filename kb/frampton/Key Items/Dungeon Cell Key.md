**Dungeon Cell Key** is a Key in **Dark Souls**and**Dark Souls Remastered.**Key Items are used to progress quests, unlock new areas, and expand merchant inventories. They often function as literal keys, granting passage through doors or access to previously unreachable Locations.

> Key to the dungeon of the Undead Asylum to the North.
> A mysterious knight, without saying a word, shoved a corpse down into the cell, and on its person was this key. Who was this knight? And what was his purpose? There may be no answers, but one must still forge ahead.

### Usage

* Northern Undead Asylum. Unlock cell door.

### Location

* Northern Undead Asylum. On corpse dropped into cell.
