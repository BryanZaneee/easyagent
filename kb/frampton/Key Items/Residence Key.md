**Residence Key** is a Key in **Dark Souls**and **Dark Souls Remastered.**Key Items are used to progress quests, unlock new areas, and expand merchant inventories. They often function as literal keys, granting passage through doors or access to previously unreachable Locations.

> Key to a residence in the Undead Burg. Simple, sturdy design of the locks in the Burg keep out unwanted visitors.
> But this is a standard key in these parts, and will open several residences

### Usage

* Access 3x Gold Pine Resin in a residence in Undead Burg, close to the watchtower.
* Free Griggs of Vinheim in Lower Undead Burg.

### Location

* Undead Burg. Bought from Undead Merchant.
