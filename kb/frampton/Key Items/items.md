# items

**Items** in **Dark Souls** are found in game either on bodies, dropped from slain enemies, as rewards, sold by merchants, through trade with Snuggly, or from chests. Be careful when opening chests, as some may be Mimics

**Ammunition**, such as arrows and bolts, in **Dark Souls** are consumable items required to use ranged weapons like bows, greatbows, and crossbows. Players can equip two types simultaneously, and they are essential for dealing damage to Enemies and Bosses from a distance.
