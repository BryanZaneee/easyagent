**Sewer Chamber Key** is a Key in **Dark Souls**and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Key Items are used to progress quests, unlock new areas, and expand merchant inventories. They often function as literal keys, granting passage through doors or access to previously unreachable Locations.

> Key to the sewer chamber in the Depths. In any community, a few bad apples are sure to exhibit insatiable greed. If they were turned Undead, and banished to the Depths, would they reconsider their ways? Use this key to see for yourself

### Usage

* Unlocks Bonfire room in the first slime-hallway in the Depths, close to the kitchen.

### Location

* Depths. On a corpse in the room with the giant rat. The key is accessible from both sides of the iron bars.
