**Crest Key** is a Key in **Dark Souls**. Key Items are used to progress quests, unlock new areas, and expand merchant inventories. They often function as literal keys, granting passage through doors or access to previously unreachable Locations.

> The grooves of the crest are enchanted, the door sealed with a powerful spell.

### Usage

* Unlocks the door leading to Hawkeye Gough.

### Location

* Found in a mimic chest in the lower section of the Oolacile Township. When you reach a building with about 6 bloated residents and two casters, soon before you can unlock the shortcut, look for a staircase and follow it upwards. One the second level up you'll have a chance to look left and see an alcove with a burning fire. You can roll to access it, kill the mimic and obtain the key.
* Video Location Timestamp 17:44

### Notes & Tips

* The key is a manual pick-up.
