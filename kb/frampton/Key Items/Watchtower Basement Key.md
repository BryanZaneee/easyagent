# Watchtower Basement Key

**Watchtower Basement Key** is a Key in **Dark Souls**and **Dark Souls Remastered**.

### Usage

* Unlocks shortcut between Undead Burg and Darkroot Basin, guarded by Havel.

### Location

* Darkroot Garden. On top of the tower, after crossing the Moonlight Butterfly bridge.
