**Soul of a Hero** is a Soul in **Dark Souls**. Consuming a Soul item adds a specific number of souls to the player's stockpile. These souls can then be spent to make purchases, repair equipment, increase Soul Level, and upgrade Weapons and Armor.

> Soul of a hero of legend who has long ago gone Hollow. Use to acquire a large amount of souls.
> Souls are the source of all life, and whether Undead, or even Hollow, one continues to seek them.

### Usage

* Gives 10,000 souls when consumed.

### Location

* Sen's Fortress: Inside a hanging cage beside Big Hat Logan.
* Anor Londo: Guarded by the second of the infamous Silver Knight archers.
* Anor Londo: Balcony above the Titanite Demon, guarded by a Silver Knight.
* Crystal Cave: In front of the Moonlight Butterfly right after crossing the last invisible bridge.
* Tomb of Giants: There's two in the last area before the entrance to Nito.
* Lost Izalith: On a ledge above the underpass, close to the Rare Ring of Sacrifice.
