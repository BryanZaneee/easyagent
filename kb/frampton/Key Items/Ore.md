# Ore

**Ores** in **Dark Souls** are are Items used in the upgrading of weapons and armor. Some ores can be fed to Kingseeker Frampt, and then broken down into smaller variants.
 Please visit the Titanite Farming page for ideas on where to find and farm Titanite ores.
