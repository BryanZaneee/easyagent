**Undead Asylum F2 West Key** is a Key in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered.**Key Items are used to progress quests, unlock new areas, and expand merchant inventories. They often function as literal keys, granting passage through doors or access to previously unreachable Locations.

> Key to the iron bars on the west side of the second floor of the Northern Undead Asylum.
>
> The Undead Asylum is a giant Undead prison, segmented by countless iron bars.
>
> But even if a hero found a key in Lordran to liberate this prison, would he have the means, or the heart, to ever come back?

### Usage

* It unlocks the locked door on the third level's west wing, guarded by two Undead Soldiers, in the Northern Undead Asylum.

### Location

* On the roof of the building which houses Petrus of Thorolund in the Firelink Shrine.
* It is accessible after unlocking the elevator short-cut back to the Firelink Shrine from the Undead Parish.
