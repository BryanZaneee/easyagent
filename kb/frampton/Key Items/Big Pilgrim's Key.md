**Big Pilgrim's Key** is a Key in **Dark Souls**and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Key Items are used to progress quests, unlock new areas, and expand merchant inventories. They often function as literal keys, granting passage through doors or access to previously unreachable Locations.

> Key to the inner door of the Undead Asylum main hall. Big key belonging to a chosen Undead pilgrim.
>
> But this Chosen Undead knows not what this pilgrimage has in store

### Usage

* It it used to open the locked door guarded by the Asylum Demon, which lets you exit the Northern Undead Asylum.

### Location

* It is dropped by the Asylum Demon if you killed him when you are supposed to, during the second encounter.
* However, if the Asylum Demon is killed during the first encounter then this key will be dropped by the Elite Knight/Oscar of Astora when you either speak to him or kill him.
* Drops from Oscar of Astora. (Guaranteed - Northern Undead Asylum and Northern Undead Asylum Revisit)
