**Basement Key** is a Key in **Dark Souls**and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Key Items are used to progress quests, unlock new areas, and expand merchant inventories. They often function as literal keys, granting passage through doors or access to previously unreachable Locations.

> Opens the narrow passage leading below at the far face of the great bridge in the Undead Burg.
> The lower Undead Burg is a treacherous place. Do not turn your back on the wily thieves, or the wild dogs who serve the Capra Demon

### Usage

* Undead Burg. Unlocks the small door at the start of the Hellkite bridge.

### Location

* Found on steps to the Undead Parish, right behind the big gate.
