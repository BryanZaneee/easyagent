# Multiplayer Items

**Multiplayer Items** in **Dark Souls** are Consumables, tools, and unequippable objects used for online play and covenant interactions. Most require Online Mode to function and allow players to engage in cooperative or PvP activities, including leaving messages, summoning allies, or being summoned into other worlds.
