**Archive Tower Giant Door Key** is a Key in **Dark Souls**. Key Items are used to progress quests, unlock new areas, and expand merchant inventories. They often function as literal keys, granting passage through doors or access to previously unreachable Locations.

> Key to the giant door leading out from the uppermost floor of Archive Tower, which now serves as a prison.
> The key's design resembles not the key of a prison; indeed, the tower was once no prison at all, but a trove of precious tomes

### Usage

* The Duke's Archives. Exit Archive Tower.
* Unlocks the large doors at the ledge above the ladder, at the top of the stairs.

### Location

* Archive Tower. Found in chest behind gramophone.
