# Silver Coin

| --- |

**Silver Coin** is a Tool in **Dark Souls**. Carvings are a useful tool to express yourself to other players, some including a vocal performance or making sound.

> Coin made of silver, with the portrait of the legendary Knight King Rendal on its face.
> Even coins of great value in the world of men have little value in Lordran, where the accepted currency is souls.
> Those who dream of returning to the outside world are fond of carrying these around

### Usage

* Can be sold to Kingseeker Frampt for 3.000 souls.
* Video Location Timestamp 17:16

### Location

* Drops 5 pieces from Mimic. (100% Chance - Sen's Fortress, Anor Londo, The Duke's Archives and Oolacile Township)
* Drops from Vagrants, specifically the Red Good Vagrant. (Depths, Undead Burg, Undead Parish, Painted World of Ariamis, Darkroot Garden, Darkroot Basin, The Catacombs, Tomb of Giants, The Great Hollow, Ash Lake, Demon Ruins, Lost Izalith, Sen's Fortress, Blighttown, Anor Londo, New Londo Ruins Lower, New Londo Ruins Upper, The Duke's Archives, Crystal Cave, Northern Undead Asylum, Kiln of the First Flame  and Royal Wood)
