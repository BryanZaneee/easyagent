**Gold Coin** is a Tool in **Dark Souls**. Carvings are a useful tool to express yourself to other players, some including a vocal performance or making sound.

> Coin made of gold, with Allfather Lloyd and his white halo shown on its face.
> Even coins of great value in the world of men have little value in Lordran, where the accepted currency is souls.
> Those who dream of returning to the outside world are fond of carrying these around.

### Usage

* Can be sold to Kingseeker Frampt for 5.000 souls.

### Location

* Drops from Mimic. (100% Chance - Sen's Fortress, Anor Londo, The Duke's Archives and Oolacile Township)
* Can be found in the Painted World of Ariamis hidden under some barrels near a ladder when coming up a building full of albino rats.
* Found on a corpse in Royal Wood, near three unaggressive Treant Gardeners (they will become hostile if you will loot the corpse).
* Drops from Vagrants, specifically the Red Good Vagrant. (Depths, Undead Burg, Undead Parish, Painted World of Ariamis, Darkroot Garden, Darkroot Basin, The Catacombs, Tomb of Giants, The Great Hollow, Ash Lake, Demon Ruins, Lost Izalith, Sen's Fortress, Blighttown, Anor Londo, New Londo Ruins Lower, New Londo Ruins Upper, The Duke's Archives, Crystal Cave, Northern Undead Asylum, Kiln of the First Flame and Royal Wood)
