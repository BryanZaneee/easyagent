**Lord's Blade Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered (AotA only)**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Robe of the Lord's Blade Ciaran, one of Gwyn's Four Knights.
> A robe common to all of the Lord's Blades. These deadly women shift nimbly between layers of darkness, etching streaks of gold into the night air, and planting visions of terror into the minds of their targets.
> The cyclops headpiece is common to all of the Lord's Blades, but Ciaran was determined to adorn this soft porcelain mask as a unique decoration of honor. The mask is lined with ivory locks of hair.

### Information

* **Lord's Blade Set** can be upgraded by using Twinkling Titanite.

### Location

* Dropped by Lord's Blade Ciaran who spawns after you defeated Artorias the Abysswalker and reloaded the zone.
* **Note**: consuming Soul of Artorias before reloading the zone will prevent Ciaran from spawning in.
