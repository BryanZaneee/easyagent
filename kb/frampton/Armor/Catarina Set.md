**Catarina Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Outside Catarina, they are often ridiculed for their onion-like shape, infuriating the proud knights, but the masterfully forged curved design makes it very effective for parrying."

### Information

* **Catarina Set** can be upgraded by using Twinkling Titanite.
* Each armor piece of this set reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second (4 for full set).
* Despite its description, this set **does not** provide any bonus to in-game parry. It is simply implied that the armor itself has better damage deflection due to its curved design.

### Location

* Purchased from Crestfallen Merchant (34,000 Souls total).
