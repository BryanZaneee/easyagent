**Pyromancer Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Hood/Robe/Manchette- Worn by the pyromancers of the Great Swamp. Though it appears tattered, it is actually quite strong. Their attire offers substantial protection against poison, fire, and other forces of nature out in the hinterlands where they were driven. Boots made of thick leather. The boots of the pyromancers are incredibly tough, on account of the rugged grasslands and treacherous swamps they must traverse. Their soles are nearly impenetrable.

### Information

* **Pyromancer Set** can be upgraded by using Titanite Shard (to +3), Large Titanite Shard (to +6), Titanite Chunk (to +9) and Titanite Slab (to +10).
* This set is useful in Blighttown due to its considerably high Poison resistance. With enough caution and patience, you can run from dry land to dry land through most of the bottom section and save yourself some Purple Moss Clumps.
* The look of this set differs slightly depending on the gender of the character.

### Location

* Default equipment for the Pyromancer class.
* Found in Blighttown: From the bonfire in Blighttown run straight past the first flying bug slightly to your left out in the distance. Circle around to the left of the pillar then look right; the armor and the Posion Mist spell are on the body in the corner.
* Video Location Timestamp 18:25

### Notes & Tips

* This set would reappear in *Dark Souls 2*.
