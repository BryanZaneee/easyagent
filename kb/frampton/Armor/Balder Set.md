**Balder Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Helm/Armor/Gauntlets/Leggings-Apparel worn by the knights of the ancient kingdom of Balder. It is made from thick iron plates.
>  Balder was the homeland of the Knight King Rendal, but it came to ruin after a great many Undead were spawned.

### Information

* **Balder Set** can be upgraded by using Titanite Shard (to +3), Large Titanite Shard (to +6), Titanite Chunk (to +9) and Titanite Slab (to +10).

### Location

* Rare drop from Balder Knights.
* Purchased from Crestfallen Merchant (23,000 Souls total).
