**Hunter Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Broad-brimmed hat favored by the archer hero Pharis. Pharis was an accomplished archer, and though he was human, he ranked alongside Hawkeye Gough, one of the Four Knights of Lord Gwyn. His hat is universally popular among children.
> Armor/Gloves/Boots- Made of soft leather. Very common type of protective gear.

### Information

* **Hunter Set** can be upgraded using Titanite Shard (to +3), Large Titanite Shard (to +6), Titanite Chunk (to +9) and Titanite Slab (to +10).

### Location

* Default equipment for the Hunter class.
* Found in Darkroot Garden/Basin, on a platform near the small serpentine path between Garden and Basin.
* Pharis's Hat: Drop from a Forest Protector.
