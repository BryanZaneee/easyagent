**Ornstein's Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Armor of the dragonslayer Ornstein, who guards the cathedral in the forsaken city of Anor Londo. Ornstein is believed to be the captain of the Four Knights.
>  His golden lion helm is imbued with the power of lightning and should provide good protection against it.

### Information

* **Ornstein's Set** cannot be upgraded.
* Ornstein's Helm, Ornstein's Armor (chestpiece) and Ornstein's Gauntlets each reduce Stamina regeneration by 1 per second (3 for full set).

### Location

* Purchased from Domhnall of Zena (27,000 Souls total) after defeating Ornstein and Smough. During the boss fight, defeat **Smough first**, then defeat empowered Ornstein. If you kill Ornstein first, Domhnall will sell Smough's armor instead.

### Notes & Tips

* Ornstein was named after the composer Leo Ornstein.
* Ornstein reappeared as The Old Dragonslayer in DS2.
* The armor set of Ornstein was later rediscovered at Archdragon Peak in DS3.
