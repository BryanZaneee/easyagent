**Cleric Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Armor set worn by the warrior clerics of the Way of White, who are famous for being unyielding in battle.
> Among such armors, those that were black belonged to warrior clerics sent on missions, many of whom were high level priests.

### Information

* **Cleric Set** can be upgraded using Titanite Shard (to +3), Large Titanite Shard (to +6), Titanite Chunk (to +9) and Titanite Slab (to +10).
* Each armor piece of this set reduces Stamina recovery by 1 per second (chest by 2) for a total of 5 for the full set.

### Location

* Purchased from Patches the Hyena (22,000 Souls total).
