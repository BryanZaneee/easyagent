**Crimson Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Armor of the sorcerers who flooded New Londo to seal away the Darkwraiths, and the Four Kings who descended into Dark.
> Mask- It symbolizes their resolve to keep the seal shut forever and their atonement for all who were sacrificed, but two of the three forsook New Londo upon tiring of their duty. The Sealers were once known as healers, and the bright crimson was a symbol of that.

### Information

* Crimson Set can be upgraded by using Twinkling Titanite.
* Favored by many players early game for it's light weight and easy accessibility via the shortcut in New Londo.

### Location

* Found on a Sealer's corpse in Blighttown, next to a chest containing Remedy.
* After riding the waterwheel, go to where the one Cragspider can be found and go across the tree branch.
