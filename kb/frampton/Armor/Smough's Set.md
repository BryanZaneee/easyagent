**Smough's Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Worn by Smough, the Executioner, protector of the cathedral at the forsaken city of Anor Londo. It offers extremely high defense and can be worn by humans, but not without great difficulty.

### Information

* **Smough's Set** cannot be upgraded.
* Each armor piece of this set reduces Stamina recovery by 1 per second (chest by 2) for a total of 5 for the full set.

### Location

* Purchased from Domhnall of Zena (27,000 Souls total) after defeating Ornstein and Smough. During the boss fight, defeat **Ornstein first**, then defeat empowered Smough. If you kill Smough first, Domhnall will sell Ornstein's armor instead.
