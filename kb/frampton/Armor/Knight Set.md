**Knight Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Helm/Armor/Gauntlets/Leggings- Equipment of a lower a rank knight. Despite the thin metal used, the grooved texture gives them added protection.

### Information

* **Knight Set** can be upgraded using Titanite Shard (to +3), Large Titanite Shard (to +6), Titanite Chunk (to +9) and Titanite Slab (to +10).

### Location

* Default equipment for the Knight class.
* Found in Darkroot Garden/Basin, in the shallow water near the Hydra.
