**Gwyndolin Moonlight Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Garb of the Dark Sun Gwyndolin, protector of the forsaken city of Anor Londo.
> The crown of the gods demands faith immeasurable of its wearer, but it is imbued with Darkmoon power that enhances all magic. The image of the sun manifests Gwyndolin's deep adoration of the sun.
> The power of the moon was strong in Gwyndolin, and thus he was raised as a daughter. His magic garb is silk-thin, and hardly provides any physical defense.

### Information

* Headpiece grants approx. 10% bonus to magic.
* Cannot be upgraded

### Location

* In order to obtain this armor, you will have to 'break' the Blade of Darkmoon covenant by killing its leader, Dark Sun Gwyndolin. After doing so, Domhnall of Zena will sell each armor piece for 10,000 souls each.
