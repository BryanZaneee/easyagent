**Giant Armor Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Armor of the giant sentinels of Anor Londo.Made from ancient brass, it also offers protection versus non-physical attacks, but due to its giant size, it is extremely heavy for humans, and impedes stamina recovery.

### Information

* **Giant Armor Set** can be upgraded by using Twinkling Titanite.
* The entire armor set upgraded to +5 has the highest total Physical Defense in the game.
* Each armor piece of this set reduces Stamina recovery by 1 per second (chest by 2) for a total of 5 for the full set.

### Location

* Purchased from the Giant Blacksmith in Anor Londo (26,000 Souls total).
