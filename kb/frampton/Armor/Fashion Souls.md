# Fashion Souls

**Fashion Souls** in **Dark Souls** and Dark Souls Remastered is a term used to describe aesthetically pleasing and cosplay outfits that show player creativity for Dark Souls Builds. This page is dedicated to showcasing such creations. You can add your own fashion by replicating the formatting below.

* In order to edit the section, click the pencil button on the top right where it states "edit".
* You need a Fextralife Account to upload images
* Images should be reduced to a maximum width of 250px for the thumbnail
* Please name images in all lowercase, without spaces: name\_of\_your\_fashion.jpg (for example)
