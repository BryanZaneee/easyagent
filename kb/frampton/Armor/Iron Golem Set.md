**Iron Golem Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Armor of the Iron Golem, guardian of the ancient Sen's Fortress, slayer of heroes who ventured forth to Anor Londo.
> Without it's core, it is a mere hunk of iron, and can be equipped as solid protective gear, but its immense weight hinders stamina recovery.

### Information

* **Iron Golem Set** cannot be upgraded.
* Each armor piece of this set reduces Stamina recovery by 1 per second (chest by 2) for a total of 5 for the full set.

### Location

* Purchased from Domhnall of Zena (22,000 Souls total) in his second location after defeating the Iron Golem in Sen's Fortress.
