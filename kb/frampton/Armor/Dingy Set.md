**Dingy Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered.**Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Worn by the Fire Keeper at Firelink Shrine.
> It is thought to have once been the white armor set of a maiden,
> but smoke and ashes from bonfires darkened it over the years.

### Information

* **Dingy Set** can be upgraded by using Titanite Shard (to +3), Large Titanite Shard (to +6), Titanite Chunk (to +9) and Titanite Slab (to +10).
* Great for dealing with Basilisks (curse frogs) since it has a high curse resistance compared to other sets in the early game.

### Location

* Dropped by Anastacia of Astora as part of the Firelink Shrine event that takes place after you rang both Bells of Awakening or picked up the two Fire Keeper Souls located in the Undead Parish and Blighttown.
