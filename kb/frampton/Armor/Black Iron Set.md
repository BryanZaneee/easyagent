**Black Iron Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Armor set of Black Iron Tarkus, a knight known for his great strength.
>  Built of a special black iron and providing strong defense, notably against fire, but so terribly heavy to be unwieldy to all but Tarkus himself.

### Information

* **Black Iron Set** can be upgraded by using Twinkling Titanite.
* One of the highest physical defense armor sets in the game. Only slightly worse than Giant's in that regard, but weighs slightly less and has (higher) Curse resistance.
* This set has the highest Fire resistance of any armor set in the game (at +5 and as a full set).
* Each armor piece of this set reduces Stamina recovery by 1 per second (chest by 2) for a total of 5 for the full set.
* It is believed that Iron Knight Tarkus was the hero who broke the window near Ariamis painting, allowing himself and others to progress deeper into Anor Londo.

### Location

* Found in Anor Londo, on the Iron Knight Tarkus corpse to the left of World of Ariamis painting.
