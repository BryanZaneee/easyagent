**Havel's Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Apparel worn by Havel the Rock's warriors. Carved from solid rock, its tremendous weight is matched only by the defense it provides.
> Havel's Warriors never flinched nor retreated from battle. Those unfortunate enough to face them were inevitably beaten to a pulp.

### Information

* **Havel's Set** cannot be upgraded.
* This set has the highest Poise of any armor set in the game.
* While its high poise and exceptional defenses make it appealing, its extreme weight means it'll likely bring a player's equip weight down to heavy unless they have extremely high Endurance.
  + A common strategy is to equip individual pieces of the set (i.e. gauntlets or helmet) on top of lighter armor in order to boost one's Poise.
* Each armor piece of this set reduces Stamina recovery by 1 per second (chest by 2) for a total of 5 for the full set.

### Location

* Found in Anor Londo.
* This set is located in the basement of the main building leading to Gwynevere. On the base floor, there is an illusory wall in a fire place that may be struck and opened, leading to the basement. You'll find several chests in the basement, one containing Havel's full set of armor, and one being a Mimic chest.
