**Gough's Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**(AotA only). Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Armor of Hawkeye Gough, one of Gwyn's Four Knights.
> Armor donned by Gough since his days as a dragonslayer. The medallion, bequeathed by the Lord Himself, and the dragon bone pauldrons are symbols of the highest honors.

### Information

* **Gough's Set** can be upgraded by using Twinkling Titanite.
* Each armor piece of this set reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second (4 for full set).

### Location

* Obtained by killing Hawkeye Gough.
