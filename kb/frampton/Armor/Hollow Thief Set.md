**Hollow Thief Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Leather apparel worn by Hollow thieves. It stifles noise and helps its wearer hide in the shadows.

### Information

* **Sorcerer Set** can be upgraded by using Titanite Shard (to +3), Large Titanite Shard (to +6), Titanite Chunk (to +9) and Titanite Slab (to +10).
* This armor set lacks Gloves.

### Location

* Dropped by Undead Assassins.
