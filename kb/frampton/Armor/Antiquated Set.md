**Antiquated Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Special magic crown bestowed upon Dusk, Princess of Oolacile, upon her birth. Its wearer is blessed by all manner of magic. This raises the power and effect of the wearer's magic, but damage suffered by magic attacks also rises.
>  From the ancient fallen land of Oolacile. Its ivory-colored silk features elaborate embroidery and is imbued with ancient magic power.
>  One cannot expect any physical protection from this garment, as it was not meant to be worn in battle.

### Information

* **Antiquated Set** can be upgraded by using Twinkling Titanite (except for the Crown of Dusk that cannot be upgraded).
* The Crown of Dusk boosts power of Sorceries, Miracles and Pyromancies by **20%** (stacks with Bellowing Dragoncrest Ring/ Ring of the Sun's Firstborn). However it also reduces the wearer's Magic defense by **30%**.

### Location

* Found in Darkroot Basin, after saving Dusk of Oolacile. Kill the Golden Crystal Golem in the back of the Darkroot Basin cave (far behind the Hydra). Then go to the rock on the shore line where you fought the Hydra, and summon Dusk (you DO NOT have to be in human form). Reload, then return to where you found the golem. You will find the corpse with the set there.
* See Dusk of Oolacile page for further details about her spawn and despawn requirements.
