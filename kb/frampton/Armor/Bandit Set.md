**Bandit Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> "Worn by the brigands who raid mountain hamlets and attack travelers"
> Hood- In addition to protecting against the blazing sun, dust, and sand, it helps them tell friend from foe in the heat of battle.
> Armor/Gauntlets/Trousers- The addition of metal over a base of cloth and leather offers good mobility and defense.

### Information

* **Bandit Set** can be upgraded using Titanite Shard (to +3), Large Titanite Shard (to +6), Titanite Chunk (to +9) and Titanite Slab (to +10).
* The look of this set differs slightly depending on the gender of the character.

### Location

* Default equipment for the Bandit class.
* Found in the Valley of Drakes.
