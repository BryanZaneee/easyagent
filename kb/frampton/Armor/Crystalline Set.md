**Crystalline Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Helm / Armor / Gauntlets / Leggings- worn by a Hollowed knight, who was partially crystalized.
> The crystal provides strong defense, but they cannot be used for long because they are easily broken and cannot be repaired.

### Information

* **Crystalline Set** cannot be upgraded.
* This armor set can be repaired, despite its deceiving description to the contrary. However, this repair is very expensive (100 Souls per durability point).

### Location

* Found in The Duke's Archives/Crystal Cave, in the middle of the forest with the Crystal Golems.
* Video Location Timestamp 33:21
