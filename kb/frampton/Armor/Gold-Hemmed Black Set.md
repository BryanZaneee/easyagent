**Gold-Hemmed Black Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Worn by the witch Quelana of Izalith, mother of pyromancy and Daughter of Chaos.
> She wore this gold-hemmed black set before even the Age of Fire,
> and it offer strong resistance versus fire, poison, and other perils.

### Information

* This set cannot be upgraded.
* Has the second highest poison resistance in the game (with Painting Guardian Set being the first).

### Gold-Hemmed Black Location

* Obtained in Demon Ruins, within the Ceaseless Discharge boss room at the farthest end. You will find a grave with a corpse lying on top containing it. Picking up the armor set will activate the boss fight, if not activated already.
