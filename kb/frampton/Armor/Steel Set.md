**Steel Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Armor set of the Knights of Berenike, known for their heavy armaments and armor.
> Countless Knights of Berenike, once extolled as the mighiest of mighty, became Undead and ventured to Lordran. But their journey was for naught, as they went Hollow, and became a threat to all Undead.

### Information

* **Steel Set** can be upgraded by using Titanite Shard (to +3), Large Titanite Shard (to +6), Titanite Chunk (to +9) and Titanite Slab (to +10).
* As a set and at +10, provides the highest Physical damage protection of any Medium armor set, greater than several Heavy Armor sets.
* Each armor piece of this set reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second (4 for full set).

### Location

* Purchased from Crestfallen Merchant (23,000 Souls total).
* Rare drop from Berenike Knights.
