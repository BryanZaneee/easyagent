**Witch Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Armor set of the rogue witch Beatrice. Almost all magic users that employ a wand studied at Dragon School, but Beatrice is one of the few exceptions. She braved the Abyss but did not live to tell of her ordeal.

### Information

* **Witch Set** can be upgraded by using Twinkling Titanite.

### Location

* Found in Valley of Drakes area along with Beatrice's Catalyst. Proceed to the cave entrance to Blighttown; the corpse is located at a ledge on at the opposite side of the cave entrance.
* Available only after you have defeated the Four Kings boss.
* It is **not** necessary to have summoned Witch Beatrice.
