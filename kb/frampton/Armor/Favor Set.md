**Favor Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Apparel of Lautrec the Embraced, representing the goddess Fina's love.
> During his solitude, he forsook everything, for he believed in the goddess's love for him.
> The goddess's arms wrap around it, as if to embrace the wearer.

### Information

* **Favor Set** can be upgraded by using Twinkling Titanite.

### Location

* Anor Londo: You will get this as a result of killing Knight Lautrec at the end of his sidequest (see his NPC page for further details).  Can be found in Ornstein/Smough's boss room after the end of the fight. Stand at the bonfire on the 2nd floor and run to the left. There should be a lootable corpse you can grab the armor from.
