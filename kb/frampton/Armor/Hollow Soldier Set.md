**Hollow Soldier Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Helm/Armor- Apparel worn by Hollow soldiers. It may be old and battered, but its iron construction makes it quite sturdy.
> It is wise to wear a sturdy form of head protection against arrows and other physical threats.
> Waistcloth worn by Hollow soldiers. It is so tattered that it barely serves its purpose anymore.

### Hollow Soldier Set Information

* Hollow Soldier Set can be upgraded by using Titanie Shard (to +3), Large Titanite Shard (to +6), Titanite Chunk (to +9) and Titanite Slab (to +10).

### Location

* Uncommon drop from Undead Soldiers.
