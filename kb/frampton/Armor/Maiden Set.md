**Maiden Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Apparel worn by traveling maidens. It is part of their formal attire, regardless of rank.
> It is soft and well-made, but does not offer much in the way of defense, making it ill-suited to use in battle.

### Information

* **Maiden Set** can be upgraded by using Titanite Shard (to +3), Large Titanite Shard (to +6), Titanite Chunk (to +9) and Titanite Slab (to +10).

### Location

* Found on a corpse in the Duke's Archives.
