**Thief Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Mask of sinners. They hide their faces, stifle their voices and hide in the shadows. It does not provide much in the way of defense.
> This armor is made of smooth black leather is extremely quiet, a good thing for those who hide in the shadows. The leather itself is thick, offering a decent level of protection.

### Information

* **Thief Set** can be upgraded by using Titanite Shard (to +3), Large Titanite Shard (to +6), Titanite Chunk (to +9) and Titanite Slab (to +10).
* The look of this set differs slightly depending on the gender of the character.

### Location

* Default equipment for the Thief class.
* Lower Undead Burg: found in a room with the second set of Hollow thieves.
* Video Location: Found in this video at 3:04
