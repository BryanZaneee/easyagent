**Guardian Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**(AotA only). Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Armor of the stone knights, guardians of the forest sanctuary.
> The stone knights are golems animated by magic, and their enchanted helms/amor/gauntlets/leggings are oppressively heavy.

### Information

* **Guardian Set** cannot be upgraded.
* Each armor piece of this set reduces Stamina recovery by 1 per second (chest by 2) for a total of 5 for the full set.

### Location

* Armor pieces can be found on several corpses lying in the Royal Wood.
