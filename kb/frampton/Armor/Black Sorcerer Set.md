**Black Sorcerer Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Outfit worn by the secret sorcerers at Vinheim Dragon School.
> They secretly work with sound-based spells, and never reveal themselves.

* **Black Sorcerer Set** can be upgraded by using Titanite Shard (to +3), Large Titanite Shard (to +6), Titanite Chunk (to +9) and Titanite Slab (to +10).

### Location

* Found on a corpse in Sen's Fortress.
