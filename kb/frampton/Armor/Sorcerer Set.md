**Sorcerer Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Hat/Cloak/Gauntlets/Boots- Worn by proper sorcerers who studied at Vinheim Dragon School.
> "The majority take pride in having studied at the academy and look down on breaking the formal dress code established for sorcerers.

### Information

* **Sorcerer Set** can be upgraded by using Titanite Shard (to +3), Large Titanite Shard (to +6), Titanite Chunk (to +9) and Titanite Slab (to +10).

### Location

* Default equipment for the Sorcerer class.
* Lower Undead Burg, by destroying the barrel with the corpse in the same room Griggs is trapped in.
* Video Location: Found in this video at 2:21
