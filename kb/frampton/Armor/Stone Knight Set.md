**Stone Knight Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Moss-covered armor of the Stone Knight, defender of the Darkroot Garden.
> The Stone Knight is a product of ancient magic, and although this armor is also imbued with magic, it is incredibly heavy.

### Information

* **Stone Knight Set** cannot be upgraded.
* This set is popular for fighting the Four Kings boss, due to it's high Poise and Physical damage resistance. It is however relatively weak to magic, which the Four Kings also use.
* Each armor piece of this set reduces Stamina recovery by 1 per second (chest by 2) for a total of 5 for the full set.

### Location

* Found in Darkroot Garden, past the locked magical door.
* Stay to the left side once you enter after using the Crest of Artorias. Keep following the cliffside on the left till you come across a broken stone outpost. Go through the stone outpost past the Giant Cat and once on the other side turn to the left until you find a chest with the Stone Knight armor inside. The chest will be next to the underside of the bridge on the left side.
