**Silver Knight Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Apparel of the Silver Knights who protect Anor Londo.
> When Lord Gwyn departed to link the Fire, his knights split into two groups. The Silver Knights remained in the forsaken capital in service of their goddess.

### Information

* **Silver Knight Set** can be upgraded by using Twinkling Titanite.
* Unlike many Medium/Heavy armor sets, this set does not lower Stamina regeneration.

### Location

* Anor Londo, in a treasure chest guarded by a spear-wielding Silver Knight.
