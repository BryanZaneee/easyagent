**Shadow Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Black cloth clothing worn by spooks from an Eastern land.
> Designed so as not to hinder their unique form of martial arts.
> While they sacrifice defense for the sake of greater mobility,
> they do offer resistance to bleeding and poison among other things, perhaps due to the nature of espionage.

### Information

* **Shadow Set** can be upgraded by using Twinkling Titanite.
* The look of this set differs slightly depending on the gender of the character.

### Location

* Found on a corpse in Blighttown.
* Once you have entered the area through sewers, progress through the upper levels of the zone, past the blight brutes and across the shaky bridge to your eventual right. Hug the right dirt wall and go through the opening between the wall and the wooden building. Proceed further along the dirt path, until you see a corpse with an item near a torch, right beneath the dirt pathway. Drop down. The full armor set will be on that corpse.
