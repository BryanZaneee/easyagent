**Adventurer's Set** is an Armor Set in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered**. Armor Sets do not provide specific set bonuses, so players are free to mix and match pieces to suit their build. The effectiveness of any combination is determined by its total resistances and weight, which directly impact your performance in combat.

> Helm/Armor/Gauntlets/Boots- Strange equipment from the ancient land of Zena, birthplace of the curious dealer Domhnall.
> Helm- The two horns are believed to symbolize wisdom.
> Armor-The many medals are believed to symbolize glory.
> Gloves-The gold bracelets symbolize a vanquisher.
> Boots-The inlaid silver rings symbolize an explorer.

### Information

* **Adventurer's Set** cannot be upgraded.
* Armor of the Glorious (chestpiece) and Gauntlets of the Vanquisher (arms) each reduce Stamina regeneration by 1 per second (2 for total set).

### Location

* Sold by Domhnall of Zena (both in the Depths and then under the aqueduct that leads to the Undead Burg).
