**Fire Surge** is a Pyromancy in Dark Souls. A pyromancy unfamiliar to the Great Swamp that emits a continuous stream of fire from the palm, allowing for a sustained surge of flames. To cast a Pyromancy, you must use a Flame or Special Weapons that can cast Pyromancies.

> *Pyromancy foreign to the Great Swamp. Create a surge of flame from palm of hand.
> Not all pyromancy originates in the Great Swamp. One hears rumors of unknown pyromancers inhabiting various lands, and this spell is the work of one such outlander.*

### Usage

* Short Ranged Attack
* Shoots a continous stream of fire from your palm.

### Acquired

* Located in Painted World of Ariamis. Dropped from the Bloated Dredge which can be found by either dropping through the well in the main courtyard or going through the invisible walls via the basement where the Annex Key is also found.
* Video Location Timestamp 25:27

### Notes and Tips

* You can lock onto enemies and hold the spell button down to fire the spell. This will use the spell up until depleted or you let go of the button.
* The Bloated Dredge that drops the spell won't attack you unless you aggravate it first.
* If you miss out on the spell the first time you kill the Bloated Dredge, you can go back to do it again until you pick the spell up.
* A current game bug means that this spell can slow down and even freeze your consoles when using it against certain enemies. It can also cause other players to drop out when doing PvP.
