**Combustion** is a Pyromancy in Dark Souls. A fundamental spell for novice pyromancers that ignites a flame in front of the caster. Despite its simplicity, the flame produced is intense, making it a staple in elementary pyromancy. To cast a pyromancy, you must use a Flame or Special Weapons that can cast Pyromancies.

> *Elementary pyromancy. Create powerful flame in hand.
> One of the most basic pyromancies. For this very reason, the flame thus created is fierce.*

### Usage

* Melee ranged attack
* Fast, melee ranged attack.

### Acquired

* Laurentius at the Firelink Shrine after saving him in The Depths
* Quelana (The Swamp in Blighttown)

### Notes and Tips

* Can equip multiple for increased uses, receives the biggest boost from the Bellowing Dragoncrest Ring.
