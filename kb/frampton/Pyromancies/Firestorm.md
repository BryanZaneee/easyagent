**Firestorm** is a Pyromancy in Dark Souls. An ancient pyromancy that generates pillars of flame around the caster, dealing fire damage in a wide area with added knockback effects. To cast a pyromancy, you must use a Flame or Special Weapons that can cast Pyromancies.

> *Primal pyromancy taught by Quelana of Izalith. Erect fire pillars in vicinity.
> The storm of flame is indiscriminate, and incinerates all nearby life.*

### Usage

* Area of Effect
* Fire damage area of effect spell with knockback.

### Acquired

* Quelana (The Swamp in Blighttown)

### Notes and Tips

* Equip multiple for increased uses.
