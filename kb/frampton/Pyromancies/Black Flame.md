**Black Flame** is a Pyromancy in Dark Souls. Discovered from the Dark Abyss by a wandering pyromancer in Oolacile, this spell conjures a great flame in one's palm, akin to Great Combustion. It is a quick, powerful close-range attack designed to break guards and consumes a high amount of stamina when blocked. To cast a pyromancy, you must use a Flame or Special Weapons that can cast Pyromancies.

> *A Pyromancy discovered from the Dark Abyss by a pyromancer who wandered into Oolacile. Conjure a great flame into one's palm.
> Black Flames are weighty and cause Physical damage. Enough to smack away the mightiest of shields.*

### Usage

* Melee-range spell
* Similar to Great Combustion. A quick and powerful close-range spell designed to break guards. Consumes a very high amount of stamina when blocked.

### Acquired

* Found on a corpse in the Chasm of the Abyss. (PTDE and Remastered only)

### Notes and Tips

* Does a combination of physical and fire damage.
* This Pyromancy is not required for the trophy post-patch.
* This spell would reappear in *Dark Souls 3*.
