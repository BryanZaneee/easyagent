**Chaos Fire Whip** is a Pyromancy in Dark Souls. A short-range fire spell that leaves behind lava residue. It operates similarly to Fire Whip and is an art of the Flame of Chaos, which consumed the Witch of Izalith and her daughters. To cast a pyromancy, you must use a Flame or Special Weapons that can cast Pyromancies.

> *Art of the Flame of Chaos, which engulfed the Witch of Izalith and her daughters.
>  *Sweep foes with chaos fire whip which changes strength depending on humanity.*
>  *The fire whip was wielded by the eldest of the Daughters of Chaos*.*

### Usage

* Short Ranged Attack
* Short ranged fire spell with lava residue. Follows same mechanics as Fire Whip.

### Acquired

* Chest in Lost Izalith, guarded by a Chaos Eater, by the entrance to Bed of Chaos.
* Video Location Timestamp 16:30

### Notes and Tips

* Damage is not dependant on Humanity, like the description implies.
* Leaves lava behind for a bit. Follows same mechanics as Fire Whip.
