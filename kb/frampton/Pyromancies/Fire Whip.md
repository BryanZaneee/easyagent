**Fire Whip** is a Pyromancy in Dark Souls. An ancient pyromancy that sweeps enemies with a whip-like flame. While it shares its roots with Combustion, **Fire Whip** is notably more challenging to control. To cast a pyromancy, you must use a Flame or Special Weapons that can cast Pyromancies.

> *Primal pyromancy taught by Quelana of Izalith. Sweep foes with fire whip.
> The root pyromancy of Combustion, but much more difficult to wield.*

### Usage

* Short Range
* Sweep foes with fire whip.

### Acquired

* Quelana (The Swamp in Blighttown)

### Notes and Tips

* Hits twice/cast
