**Poison Mist** is a Pyromancy in Dark Souls. A unique pyromancy crafted by Eingyi that conjures a cloud of poisonous mist, enveloping the area and inflicting poison status. To cast a pyromancy, you must use a Flame or Special Weapons that can cast Pyromancies.

> *Unique pyromancy crafted by Eingyi, considered a heretic even at the Great Swamp. Create poison mist.
> Eingyi became an egg-bearing Undead who serves a deformed young girl who speaks an untelligible tongue.*

### Usage

* Cloud/Area of Effect
* Breathes out a cone of poison mist that fills the surrounding area causing poison status.

### Acquired

* Swamp in Blighttown
* Eingyi, the Egg-burdened npc near the Daughter of Chaos. You must speak to Eingyi while having an Egg for a head at least once in order for this pyromancy to be available for purchase.
* Video Location Timestamp 18:25

### Notes and Tips

* Poison Mist has a status value of about 225, and will drain 3.75 HP/s for 180 seconds.
* The upgrade level of the pyromancy glove will not affect the power and duration.
* Does not affect caster and allies.
* Best used on groups of enemies.
