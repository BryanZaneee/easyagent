**Great Fireball** is a Pyromancy in Dark Souls. The most powerful variant of Fireball, conjuring a massive fireball thrown at enemies. Named after Salaman the Master Pyromancer, who believed pyromancy originated from a reverence for fire, this spell deals substantial damage upon impact. To cast a pyromancy, you must use a Flame or Special Weapons that can cast Pyromancies.

> *Ultimate fireball pyromancy. Hurl a giant fireball.
> Salaman the Master Pyromancer, also called the Great Fireball after this spell, believed pyromancy was rooted in an adoration of fire. Those who acquire this spell usually agree.*

### Usage

* Short to medium range attack
* Thrown giant fireball, large AoE..

### Acquired

* Quelana (The Swamp in Blighttown)

### Notes and Tips

* A more powerful version of the Fireball and Fire Orb with a greater area of effect too. The spell can only be purchased from Quelana.
