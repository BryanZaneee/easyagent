**Acid Surge** is a Pyromancy in Dark Souls. A pyromancy from outside the Great Swamp that emits a corrosive acid, deteriorating the durability of weapons and armor by shooting a cloud from the caster's palm. To cast a pyromancy, you must use a Flame or Special Weapons that can cast Pyromancies.

> *Pyromancy foreign to the Great Swamp. Emit acid which corrodes weapons and armor.
>  *Not all pyromancy originates in the Great Swamp. One hears rumors of unknown pyromancers inhabiting various lands, and this spell is the work of one such outlander*.*

### Acid Surg Usage

* Cloud/Area of Effect
* **Duration: 8 Seconds**
* Shoots a cloud of corrosive acid from your palm. Deteriorates equipped weapons and armor (damages durability).

### Acid Surg Acquired

* Near the edge of the cliff in the Painted World of Ariamis. In the graveyard area, to the right side of the stairs, as you enter.
* Video Location Timestamp 23:46

### Acid Surg Notes and Tips

* Will not corrode your own equipment.
* Removes around 1 point of durability per second.
* Only effective against opponents in PvP. Does not work on regular enemies or NPCs.
* Equip multiple for added uses.
