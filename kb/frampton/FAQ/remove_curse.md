# remove curse

Here is a guide on how to remove the status effect curse.

### Guide

You need a Purging Stone to remove a curse. These can be obtained in a variety of ways:

* Trade Cracked Red Eye Orbs with **Snuggly the Crow**. You get 2 Purging Stone for every 1 orb given.
* Purchase from the Undead Merchant female for 6000 souls in the Firelink Shrine aqueduct
* Purchase from Black robed man for 4000 souls after the Belfrey Gargoyles in the Undead Burg
* Speak to Ingward in New Londo Ruins. He cures curse, and gives you the key to the lower section of the ruins if you have the Lordvessel.
* Purging Stones can be farmed from Man-Eater Shell's in the Crystal Cave. This is much easier after defeating Seath the Scaleless in turn unlocking the bonfire making for quick farming runs.
* Use the Purging Stone to remove the curse effect.

Alternatively, There is an NPC called Ingward in New Londo Ruins who will cure curse in exchange for 1 humanity, however it requires a tricky run through the ghosts
