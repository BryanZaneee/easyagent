**Replenishment** is a Miracle in Dark Souls. A common miracle among cleric knights that gradually restores HP over a short duration. To cast a miracle, you must use a Talismans or Special Weapons that can cast **Miracles**.

> *Common miracle amongst cleric knights. Gradual HP restoration.
> Beware of the cleric knight blessed by Replenishment, for he shall not fall easily.*

### Usage

* Self
* **Duration**: 60 seconds
* Gradually restores HP over time for a short duration.
* Heals 10HP/s for 60 seconds, which gives a total of 600HP.

### Acquired

* Received from Reah of Thorolund after rescuing her from the two hollows in the Tomb of the Giants

### Notes and Tips

* The Ring of the Sun Princess will increase the healing to 12HP/s (duration unchanged), for a total of 720HP.
* Replenishment is a Self Buff and interferes with other such buffs (i.e. Great Magic Barrier, Green Blossom, Power Within, etc.). As long as it's active it's not possible to cast other Self Buffs and vice versa.
