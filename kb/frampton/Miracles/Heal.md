**Heal** is a Miracle in Dark Soulsand **Dark Souls Remastered**. An elementary miracle cast by clerics that restores a moderate amount of the caster's HP, with effectiveness determined by Magic Adjustment. To cast a miracle, you must use a Talisman or Special Weapons that can cast **Miracles**.

> *Elementary miracle cast by clerics. Restores HP.
>  *To cast a miracle, the caster learns a tale of the Gods, and says a prayer to be blessed by its revelations.*
>  *Heal is the shortest of such miraculous tales*.*

### Usage

* Can only be used to heal yourself, has no effect on allies.
* Heals a moderate amount of the casters HP, depending on Magic Adjustment.
  + The amount of HP healed = Mag Adjust x 3

### Acquired

* Sold by Petrus of Thorolund for 4,000 Souls.
* Sold by Reah of Thorolund for 1,000 Souls.
* Sold by Patches the Hyena for 5,000 Souls.

### Notes and Tips

* Default equipment for the Cleric class.
* The Ring of the Sun Princess will slightly increase the healing amount.
