**Gravelord Sword Dance** is a Miracle in Dark Souls. A miracle exclusive to the servants of Gravelord Nito, the first of the dead. Giant Gravelord swords emerge in the vicinity, dealing magic damage at 2.5 times the equipped Talisman's magic adjustment. This multiplier increases by 0.1 for each rank in the Gravelord Servant covenant and decreases by 0.1 if not in the covenant. To cast a miracle, you must use a Talismans or Special Weapons that can cast **Miracles**.

> *Miracle known only by the servants of the first dead, Gravelord Nito. Giant Gravelord swords jut out in vicinity.
> Nito sleeps deep within the Giant Catacombs, quietly overseeing all death, and waiting for his servants to usher in the Eye of Death.*

### Usage

* Area of Effect
* Deals magic damage for 2.5x the equipped talisman's magic adjust; this multiplier is increased by 0.1 for each rank-up of the Gravelord Servant covenant, and reduced by 0.1 while not in the Gravelord Servant covenant.
* AoE miracle similar to Gravelord Nito's "scream sword" attack.
  + Several swords will pierce the ground impaling enemies in the area. Miracle is very effective when used in closed quarters such as hallways or bridges as several swords may impale the same enemy. Note that the swords pierce out on grounds that the player has line of sight to, meaning it can not be used to hit enemies behind walls or around corners.

### Acquired

* Recieved upon joining the Gravelord Servant Covenant. As for covenant item, you can have it only once per character.
* Video Location Timestamp 3:47

### Notes and Tips

* Each Cast cost 20 uses, so it can be used twice to full effect.
* This miracle has no faith requirements, and can be used to great effect by a faithless sorcerer with Velka's Talisman.
* Members of the Gravelord Servant Covenant will receive a damage bonus of 10% per Covenant Rank, up to a maximum of 30% at Gravelord Servant +3. Nonmembers may still use the miracle, but will suffer a -10% damage penalty.
