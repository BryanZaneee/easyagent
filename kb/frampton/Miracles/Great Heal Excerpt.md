**Great Heal Excerpt** is a Miracle in Dark Soulsand **Dark Souls Remastered**. A great miracle for advanced clerics that restores a significant amount of HP. It's a cut-down version of Great Heal with the same effect but lower requirements and only one cast. To cast a miracle, you must use a Talisman or Special Weapons that can cast **Miracles**.

> *Great miracle cast by advanced clerics. Restores high HP.
> Great Heal Excerpt borrows from only several verses of Great Heal. As a result, it can only be cast a stark few times.*

### Usage

* Can only be used to heal yourself, has no effect on allies.
* Heals large amount of HP to caster.
* Cut down version of Great Heal. Same effect with lower requirements, but only one cast.
  + The amount of HP healed = Mag Adjust x 8

### Acquired

* Sold by Petrus or Thorolund for 10,000 Souls.
* Sold by Patches the Hyena for 10,000 Souls.
* Sold by Reah of Thorolund for 2,000 Souls.

### Notes and Tips

* This spell does not affect allies as the erroneous load screen info would have you believe.
