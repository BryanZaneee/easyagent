**Soothing Sunlight** is a Miracle in Dark Souls. A special miracle bestowed upon the maidens of Gwynevere, Princess of the Sun, which restores a large amount of HP for the caster and nearby allies. To cast a miracle, you must use a Talismans or Special Weapons that can cast **Miracles**.

> Special miracle granted to the maidens of Gwynevere, Princess of the Sun. Restores high HP for self and vicinity.
> The miracles of Gwynevere, the princess cherished by all, grant their blessing to a great many warriors.

### Usage

Heals large amount of HP to caster and nearby allies.

* Self and Allies
* Scales with Talisman/Faith.
* The amount of HP healed = Mag Adjust x 6.

### Acquired

* Drops from Pisaca (Guaranteed - The Duke's Archives)
* Video Location Timestamp 13:33

### Notes and Tips

* Must be part of the Princess Guard Covenant to use.
* The Ring of the Sun Princess will give 25% of your Mag Adjust value as additional HP.
