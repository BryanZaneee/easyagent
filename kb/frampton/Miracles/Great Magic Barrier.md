**Great Magic Barrier** is a Miracle in Dark Souls. An enhanced version of Bishop Havel's miracle, offering a more powerful defensive magic coating to reduce incoming magic damage briefly. To cast a miracle, you must use a Talismans or Special Weapons that can cast **Miracles**.

> *Miracle of Bishop Havel the Rock. Cover body in powerful def. magic coating.
> Havel the Rock, an old battlefield compatriot of Lord Gwyn, was the sworn enemy of Seath the Scaleless. He despised magic, and made certain to devise means of counteraction.*

### Usage

* Support: Reduces incoming magic damage for a short time.
* Duration: 40 seconds.

### Acquired

* Ash Lake: in the tree with the Basilisks, accessed from a hollow branch on the outside.
* Video Location Timestamp 4:39

### Notes and Tips

* Stronger version of Magic Barrier.
* Good for the Four Kings battle.

* Extremely effective against Seath the Scaleless' breath attacks.
* Also extremely effective against Dark Sun Gwyndolin's magic strikes, small and large alike.
* Great Magic Barrier is a Self Buff and interferes with other such buffs (i.e. , Green Blossom, Power Within, etc.). As long as it's active it's not possible to cast other Self Buffs and vice versa.
