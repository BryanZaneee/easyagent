**Great Lightning Spear is a Miracle** in Dark Souls. An advanced miracle of the Warrior of Sunlight covenant, enabling the casting of a giant lightning spear. A great Lightning Spear is drawn from the hand with the Talisman and hurled at the target. To cast a miracle, you must use a Talismans or Special Weapons that can cast **Miracles**.

> *Miracle passed down by those bound by the Warrior of Sunlight covenant. Hurl giant lightning spear.
> The weapon of the God of War, who inherited the sunlight of Lord Gwyn, but had respect only for arms, and nothing else.*

### Usage

* Attack, Ranged
* Draws a great Lightning Spear, from the hand that holds the Talisman, which is hurled towards the target.

### Acquired

* Gift upon reaching rank 1 in the Warrior of Sunlight Covenant. As a covenant item, you can have it only once per character.
* Covenant Related. Offer 10 Sunlight Medals.

### Notes and Tips

The great lightning spear is more powerful and larger in size compared to the regular lightning spear.

 At 50 faith, using sunlight talisman and ring of the sun's firstborn, the three lightning spear miracles does the following damage on a random undead in the undead burg:

* Lightning Spear - 458
* Great Lightning Spear (this miracle) - 644
* Sunlight Spear - 861
