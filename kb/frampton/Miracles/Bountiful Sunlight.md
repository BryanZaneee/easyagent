**Bountiful Sunlight** is a Miracle in Dark Souls. A special miracle granted to the maidens of Gwynevere, Princess of the Sun. It gradually restores HP over time for the caster and nearby allies. To cast a miracle, you must use a Talismans or Special Weapons that can cast **Miracles**.

> *Special miracle granted to the maidens of Gwynevere, Princess of the Sun. Gradual HP restoration for self and vicinity.
> The miracles of Gwynevere, the princess cherished by all, grant their blessing to a great many warriors.*

### Usage

* Self, Nearby Allies
* **Duration**: 60 seconds
* Gradually restores HP over time for a short duration to self and nearby allies.
* Heals 10HP/s for 60 seconds, which gives a total of 600HP.

### Acquired

* Drops from Pisaca (Guaranteed - The Duke's Archives)
* Video Location Timestamp 13:36

### Notes and Tips

* Must be part of the Princess Guard Covenant to use.
* The Ring of the Sun Princess will increase the healing to 12HP/s (duration unchanged), for a total of 720HP.
* The healing effect does not stack if a nearby ally also happens to be using either Bountiful Sunlight or Replenishment.
* Bountiful Sunlight is a Self Buff and interferes with other such buffs (i.e. Great Magic Barrier, Green Blossom, Power Within, etc.). As long as it's active it's not possible to cast other Self Buffs and vice versa.
