**Lightning Spear** is a Miracle in Dark Souls. A miracle passed down to members of the Warrior of Sunlight covenant, allowing them to hurl a lightning spear. A Lightning Spear is drawn from the hand holding the Talisman and thrown at the target. To cast a miracle, you must use a Talismans or Special Weapons that can cast **Miracles**.

> *Miracle passed down to those bound by the Warrior of Sunlight covenant. Hurl lightning spear.
> Lightning spears inflict rare lightning damage, and are very effective against magic, fire, and most of all, dragons.*

### Usage

* Attack, Ranged
* Draws a Lightning Spear, from the hand that holds the Talisman, which is hurled towards the target.

### Acquired

* Gift upon joining the Warrior of Sunlight Covenant. As for covenant item, you can have it only once per character
* Video Location Timestamp 10:01

### Notes and Tips

This spell does damage roughly 2.4 x magic adjust (tested on undead at firelink).

At 50 faith, using sunlight talisman and ring of the sun's firstborn, the three lightning spear miracles does the following damage on a random undead in the undead burg:

* Lightning Spear (this miracle) - 458
* Great Lightning Spear - 644
* Sunlight Spear - 861

This should not be confused with the weapon *Lightning Spear*, that can e.g. be found in Sen's Fortress. This weapon is a normal spear that has been lightning-enchanted.
