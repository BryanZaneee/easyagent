**Vow of Silence** is a Miracle in Dark Souls. A secret rite of Velka that prevents magic casting within its area of effect. It creates an AoE field around you that stops all spell-casting, including your own, affecting Magic, Miracles, and Pyromancy. To cast a miracle, you must use a Talismans or Special Weapons that can cast **Miracles**.

> *Secret rite of the black-haired witch Velka. Prevents casting of magic within effect area.
> Velka, the Goddess of Sin, is a rogue deity, but she is versed in arts both new and old, and is considered to have a great range of influence even as gods are concerned.*

### Usage

* Self, Area of Effect
* Duration: 30 seconds
* Creates an AoE field around you that stops all spell-casting within its area of effect, including your own.
  + Affects Magic, Miracles and Pyromancy.
  + Magic effects from weapons, like the Stone Greatsword and the Moonlight Greatsword, can still be used.

### Acquired

* Painted World of Ariamis. On top of the square tower above the church, accessed by the Annex Key.
* Video Location Timestamp 16:50

### Notes and Tips

* The miracle has no effect on some Bosses. (Notably, Four Kings, Moonlight Butterfly, Gwyndolin)
* Confirmed for Moonlight Butterfly: although Vow of Silence symbol appears around her, there is no silencing effect to her magic, only to the caster's own.
* It is very effective at revealing the location of Crossbreed Priscilla when she is invisible, as the ward still appears around her.
* This spell would reappear in *Dark Souls 3*.
