**Emit Force** is a Miracle in Dark Souls. An outland miracle, foreign to the Way of White, that emits a shockwave. It shoots a large ball of force energy, exploding on impact and damaging the target and nearby enemies, with the ability to deflect sorceries. To cast a miracle, you must use a Talismans or Special Weapons that can cast **Miracles**.

> *Outland miracle, foreign to the Way of White. Emit Shockwave.
> Considered an alternative branching of Force. Emits an expanding shockwave orb.*

### Usage

* Ranged, AoE
* Shoots a large ball of force energy to explode on the target causing damage to the target and close surrounding enemies.
   Can deflect sorceries..

### Acquired

* Gift from Siegmeyer of Catarina in Firelink Shrine, for helping him in Anor Londo.

### Notes and Tips

* Summons a big magic orb which is hurled towards the target. Explodes upon impact.
* The radius is slightly smaller than that of Force and deals more or less equal damage to all enemies in the radius.
* The base damage and radius scale with miracle resonance, from 240 base damage and 1.5m radius at level 0, to 280 damage and 2.5m radius at level 4
* Its AoE is useful for dealing with groups of shielding enemies. Aiming such that the projectile lands behind the target will damage them normally even if their shield is up
