**Magic Barrier** is a Miracle in Dark Souls. A miracle of Bishop Havel the Rock that covers the body in a defensive magic coating, reducing incoming magic damage for a short duration. To cast a miracle, you must use a Talismans or Special Weapons that can cast **Miracles**.

> *Miracle of Bishop Havel the Rock. Cover body in defensive magic coating.
> This coating greatly boosts magic defense, assisting warriors who must face the magic which Bishop Havel countered so proficiently.*

### Usage

* Support: Reduces incoming magic damage for a short time.
* **Duration**: 40 seconds.

### Acquired

* Sold by Reah of Thorolund for 6,000 souls.

### Notes and Tips

* Magic Barrier is a Self Buff and interferes with other such buffs (i.e. Great Magic Barrier, Green Blossom, Power Within, etc.). As long as it's active it's not possible to cast other Self Buffs and vice versa.
