# Recommended EndGame Weapons

**Recommended Endgame Weapons** is a **Dark Souls** guide created by used **Aierce**. Please be mindful that the recommendations on this page are not "official" and they sum up to one player's opinion on the best approach. Therefore, please do your own research before committing to any one build.

* Guides like this one but updated to **Dark Souls Remastered** will likely be added under the Guides & Walkthroughs page so keep an eye out.

**Author's note:**

I have seen countless "Best weapon" threads around the internet, and all of them were pretty inconclusive, so I decided to take it upon myself to find out what the REAL best weapon is. After HOURS of calculations, testing, and speed runs, I understood why these treads were inconclusive; the weapons are way too different from one another to even compare them. But, I still didnt give up. I divided the weapons in different categories according to their speed and damage. and managed to find a group of "Best" weapons for each. If anything, use this little "guide" to know what weapons to look out for as you play.

 BEFORE YOU READ: Be aware that these calculations were made using a stat calculator as well as personally testing them with my own test character. In both of these cases, damage calculations were done using max stats (All stats 99). This means that if your character has all stats more or so well rounded (Str, Dex, Int, and Faith are all around 30), then these numbers SHOULD apply. In other cases I WILL post other runner up weapons at the bottom that are worth considering. If you are reading this BEFORE building your character then take into consideration that after 30 each stat gives horrible returns per level. After 40 any increase to damage is almost nonexistent unless you buy like 20 levels at a time. So I wouldn't consider raising any of these stats beyond 40 unless you're going for a max level character. Throughout this block of text you will realize that I mention some weapons before showing their stats. this is because before calculating all this I did a rough overlook to define the best weapons in each category. For example for the piercing weapons, the Estoc owns all the other upgradable piercing weapons by a long shot, So I didn't even consider the others.
 Damages are shown in the following format: 720(378/342/0/0) This means that this weapon does 378 Physical Dmg, 342 Magic Dmg, 0 Fire Dmg and 0 Lighning Dmg, for a grand total of 720 Dmg.

 Final Note: Notice that I did not take into consideration enemy defenses in this guide, which is something you should do. If you are using a weapon with 2 types of damage (refer to example above). Then the ACTUAL damage would have to be calculated against the defense of BOTH types of the enemy, this usually lowers the actual damage you deal. This is SPECIALLY true in PVP. Dual types deal roughly 100 LESS damage in PVP due to enemy defenses.

The only difference is that the Bandit's knife has a higher Critical Stat (147 vs 131). ### CONCLUSIONS

There is no absolute best weapon, since it's mostly a matter of opinion. If you go for damage, then go for the Great Club or Great axe. If you are more of an STR/DEX build, then the crystal or Normal Demon's greataxe. For faster weapons go for the crystal or enchanted Battle Axe or DarkSword. Consider the Crescent Axe since it is also quite strong. In my opinion the best weapon for my build which is quite balanced. Is either the Murakumo or the Greatsword of Astorias. They both have decent speed and enough damage to defend myself. If you find yourself taking a lot of damage, then hide behind a good shield and use a Pike or the Dragonslayer Spear. I would also take into consideration the Black Knight Weapons... They are a LITTLE (barely noticable) faster than their counterparts, and look a LOT better. I would especially reccomend the Black Knight Greataxe, which has an incredible range.
