**Pyromancer** is a starting Class in Dark Souls Wiki.The **Pyromancer** is one of the stronger starting classes, thanks to its early access to pyromancy and well-balanced stat distribution. It also has the lowest soul requirements for leveling up. The Starting Classes of Dark Souls will only give you a head start on certain stats. This is because you will be able to freely change your playstyle and build as you progress through out the game.

> Great Swamp pyromancer. Casts fire spells. Wields a hand axe.

### Notes & Tips

* Beginning at level 1, this class is among the stronger starting options in the game due to its early access to pyromancy and good general stat distribution as well as having the lowest soul requirements to level up.
* Keep in mind that even though the Pyromancy Flame may say it scales with Intelligence, pyromancy spell damage does not actually scale with Intelligence, or any stat at all for that matter. It's only for its melee punch attack. The best one can do to improve Pyromancy stat-wise is raising Dexterity to increase casting speed
