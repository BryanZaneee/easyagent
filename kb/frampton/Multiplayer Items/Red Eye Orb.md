**Red Eye Orb** is a Multiplayer Item in **Dark Souls**. Multiplayer Items are consumables and tools used exclusively for online interactions. Most require an active online connection and enable players to engage in cooperative or PvP activities, such as leaving messages, summoning allies, or being summoned into other worlds.

> Online play item. Invade another world. Defeat the master of the world you have invaded to acquire humanity.
> The Darkwraiths of Kaathe use this orb to seek humanity and plunge further into dark. Perhaps they are more human than we?

### Usage

* Unlike its cracked brethren, the item is not consumed upon use, and can be used infinitely
* You can use the orb in any multiplayer-able area. Note that it may be greyed out if too close to a bonfire or a crossroad to multiple areas
* Can invade only players who have not killed the area boss yet.
* Can invade anyone with higher level than you or 10% lower level than you.
* Humanity is gained when you defeat a 'master of world'

### Location

* You receive this item after ranking up once with the Darkwraith covenant.
* Level one can be reached by offering 10 humanity  to Darkstalker Kaathe.
* You may only use the Red Eye Orb as long as you are part of the Darkwraith covenant.

### Notes & Tips

* The color of this eye orb is based on the Red Eye Stone, obtained by killing The Maiden in Black in Demon's Souls.
* Some believe Darkwraiths to be revolutionaries, defenders of the human race, but they might just be evil Humanity.
