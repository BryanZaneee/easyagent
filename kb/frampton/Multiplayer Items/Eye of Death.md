**Eye Of Death** is a Multiplayer Item & Consumables in **Dark Souls**.

### Usage

* The Eyes of Death are used to level up in the Gravelord Servant Covenant.
* They are also used to put signs on the ground, which will send Black Phantoms into 3 random players worlds. The players can then search for the sign and invade the gravelord. If the invaders are defeated, the gravelord gains additional Eyes of Death. Level range is +/- 10% of the host's level + 10 levels.
* A black aura appears around the player, indicating the Eye of Death is active.
* **You can't fight any bosses while the Eye of Death is active**, but you can walk between areas freely.
* The effect is removed when you die, or quit and reload the game as well as being summoned, invading via cracked orbs or using homebound or homeward bones.
* The player can only visit Nito if they have one or more Eye(s) of Death in their inventory.
* For further info about the requirements and effects of the Eye of Death, see the Gravelord Servant page.

### Location

* 3 are obtainable in The Catacombs, behind a Titanite Demon.
* 3 are in the Tomb of Giants. Behind the 1st bonfire, it's up a path to the left of the ladder that goes to the Large Divine Ember.
* Patches the Hyena sells three of them for 3,000 Souls each.
* Drops from Basilisk (6% Chance - Depths, The Great Hollow and Ash Lake)
