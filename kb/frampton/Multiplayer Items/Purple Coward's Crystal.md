**Purple Coward's Crystal** is a Multiplayer Item in **Dark Souls**. Multiplayer Items are consumables and tools used exclusively for online interactions. Most require an active online connection and enable players to engage in cooperative or PvP activities, such as leaving messages, summoning allies, or being summoned into other worlds.

> Online play item.
> Quit battle of Stoicism.
> (Session ends if host leaves)
> Victory in the battle once led to ancient Anor Londo, but even in the absence of its overseer, capitulation is a disgrace. In the name of a warrior's honor, do not quickly resort to use of this crystal.

### Usage

**PTDE only**

* Multiplayer item. Exit Battle of Stoicism.

### Location

* After defeating Artorias and exiting the arena, take the right path and go forward.
