**Dragon Scale** is an Ore in **Dark Souls**. Ores function as upgrade components for Weapons and Armor. Some varieties may be given to Kingseeker Frampt, who converts them into lesser versions.

> Dragon scale for reinforcing dragon weapons. Peeled from an ancient dragon.
> A dragon is inseparable from its scales, and the transcendent apostles, who seek the perpetuity of the ancient dragons, have crossed the very end of the earth to seek this invaluable treasure.

### Usage

* Can be used to upgrade Dragon weapons.
* Can be used to increase player's rank in the Path of the Dragon covenant.

### Location

* Rare (5%) drop from Drakes in the Valley of Drakes.
* Trade with Snuggly: 1x for Egg Vermifuge.
* 1x from the Undead Dragon in the Valley of Drakes.
* 1x from the Undead Dragon in the Painted World of Ariamis.
* 1x from the Hydra in Darkroot Basin.
* 2x from the Black Hydra in Ash Lake.
* 1x in a chest close to the second bonfire in Blighttown.
* 1x on a corpse just to the left of the Black Hydra in Ash Lake.
* 1x on a floating corpse past the Man-Eater Shells in Ash Lake.
* 1x inside the hollow tree on the beach in Ash Lake.

* Drops from Vagrants, specifically the Red Good Vagrant.
