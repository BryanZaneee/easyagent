**Blue Eye Orb** is a Multiplayer Item in **Dark Souls**. Multiplayer Items are consumables and tools used exclusively for online interactions. Most require an active online connection and enable players to engage in cooperative or PvP activities, such as leaving messages, summoning allies, or being summoned into other worlds.

> Online play item. Invade world of player in Book of the Guilty. Subdue player to acquire Souvenir of Reprisal. (Only Covenanter can use the item)
>
> These mystical orbs are granted to Blades of the Darkmoon, knights who serve the Dark Sun Gwyndolin, so that they may serve the Gods in meting out vengeance.

### Use

* Use this item to invade the world of a player who has 'sin', and more likely someone who is listed in the Book of the Guilty.
* Like the Red Eye Orb, this item has unlimited uses.
* Can invade BELOW the users' own level, to what limit is not confirmed, can invade above own level also.
  + This has been changed in the Remastered version, so that the Blue Eye Orb in that version has the same summon range as the Red Eye Orb.
* Can be used while either human or hollow, and does not drop a bloodstain.
* Note that it may be greyed out if too close to a bonfire or crossroad to multiple areas.

### Location

* Given to the player upon joining Blade of the Dark Moon covenant.

### Bugs

* Once in a blue moon you may come across with a bug that makes you invade as a red phantom instead of a blue spirit of vengeance. Killing the target in this form does not grant a souvenir of reprisal but instead gives humanity. Receiving 'sin' after a kill is unknown. **You receive sin when the user you "invaded" gets killed by you.**
