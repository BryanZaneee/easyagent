**Dried Finger** is a Multiplayer Item in **Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered**. Multiplayer Items are consumables and tools used exclusively for online interactions. Most require an active online connection and enable players to engage in cooperative or PvP activities, such as leaving messages, summoning allies, or being summoned into other worlds.

> Dried finger with multiple knuckles.
> Shriveled but still warm. With this many knuckles, surely it cannot belong to anything human.

### Usage

* Resets the internal invasion timer. This way you can be invaded with greater frequency.
* This internal timer is a fail safe so that people aren't constantly invaded.
* Can be used multiple times without consumption.
* **Dark Souls Remastered only**: Unlocks summoning of up to 3 co-op partners, and allows invasions from up to 2 players at the same time. (Max online 6 players in one session)

### Location

* Painted World of Ariamis. Drop down onto the balcony, from first left turret when facing the castle from the bonfire.
* **In Dark Souls Remastered**, the dried finger is now sold by the Undead Merchant in the Undead Burg, and the item from its previous location is a Twin Humanities.
* **Note:** Only one of this item can be held, leaving the player unable to pick it up in the next game cycle.
