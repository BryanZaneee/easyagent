**Black Eye Orb** is a Tool in **Dark Souls**. This item is used to invade Lautrec's world in Anor Londo. During the battle against Dragon Slayer Ornstein and Executioner Smough, you will receive a notification indicating that the Black Eye Orb is quivering. This signal means that you can now invade Lautrec's world. It can be found on a corpse located beneath the Bonfire in Firelink Shrine. Tools are unique, reusable equipment pieces designed to assist players in multiple gameplay situations, ranging simple interactions to helping during difficult boss fights.

### Usage

* Used to invade Lautrec's world in Anor Londo, right before the Ornstein and Smough entrance.

### Location

* Found on corpse beneath the bonfire in Firelink Shrine.
