**Sunlight Medal** is a Multiplayer Item in **Dark Souls**.

### Usage

* Sunlight Medals are items used to increase your rank in the Warrior of Sunlight covenant.
* Can also be traded with Snuggly for a White Titanite Chunk.

### How to obtain

* Talk to Lautrec after you save him, and you will receive 1 medal.
* Invite Solaire to defeat a boss with you and make sure he lives until the end. (you must be in human form to summon phantoms)
* Summon a member of the Warrior of Sunlight covenant and beat the boss with that player. (you must be in human form to do this)
* Be a member of the Warrior of Sunlight and beat a boss with the host in co-op.
* Loot 3 Sunlight Medals from a chest guarded by a spear-wielding Silver Knight in the room opposite the first bonfire in the castle of Anor Londo.
* Rare drops from Chaos Bug (3% Chance - Demon Ruins or Lost Izalith)
