**Griggs of Vinheim** is a Character in **Dark Souls**. You first encounter Griggs of Vinheim trapped in a room in the Lower Undead Burg, where he is searching for his master, Big Hat Logan. After you free him, he relocates to Firelink Shrine, where he will sell you useful sorceries. If you purchase all of his items, he will stay in Firelink Shrine. However, if you do not buy everything he has to offer, he will eventually leave to reunite with Big Hat Logan. Depending on your choices and progression, Griggs can either be found hollowed in Sen's Fortress or remain at Firelink Shrine. He sells various items such as weapons and rings to players.

### Encounter

Griggs of Vinheim is found in the Lower Undead Burg, locked in a room that can only be opened with the Residence Key, which can be purchased from the Undead Merchant. The Master Key will not work on this door. To reach Griggs, you'll first need to obtain the Basement Key from the Undead Parish; it's located after the portcullis gate that closes behind you after defeating the Fang Boar. Once you have the Basement Key, head to the bridge guarded by the Hellkite Dragon. There's a locked door across from the exit of the Taurus Demon's boss arena. Unlock this door, slide down the ladder, and descend the stairs. At the bottom of the second set of stairs, turn right where there's a large fire. Go into the alley where you'll encounter a torch-wielding Undead. Griggs is in the second room on the left. After speaking with Griggs, carefully destroy the barrels in the room (make sure to roll instead of attacking to avoid hitting Griggs) to obtain the Sorcerer Set and a Sorcerer's Catalyst.

* After you free Griggs, he appears in the Firelink Shrine, to the right of the archway that is straight ahead from the bottom of the stairs leading down from the Undead Burg. He sells various spells and items.
* After you have bought all his spells and items, and Big Hat Logan has left for The Duke's Archives, Griggs goes to look for his master. He can be found in Sen's Fortress, hollowed, next to the Black Sorcerer Set and Hush sorcery. He will attack, so be nice and end his misery.

Speculation: According to his clothing and equipment, Griggs may be a spy that the Vinheim Dragon School sent to observe Big Hat Logan.

### Drops

* 1000 souls
* Humanity
* Sorcery: Hush
* Slumbering Dragoncrest Ring
* **He will still drop all of these items if you kill him when he first arrives in Firelink Shrine whether or not Logan is there yet or has already left.**

### Notes

* You must have an intelligence of at least 10 before you can buy from him.
* With Dark Hand's R2 move you can steal 3 humanity before he dies.
* You can find both Hush and the Slumbering Dragoncrest Ring in Sen's Fortress as treasure, so killing him isn't necessary to acquire them.
