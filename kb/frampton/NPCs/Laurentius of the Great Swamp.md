**Laurentius of the Great Swamp** is a Character in **Dark Souls.**Your first encounter with Laurentius occurs after defeating the Butcher in the Depths. He is trapped inside a barrel, placed there by the Butcher. After you free him, Laurentius will relocate to Firelink Shrine. If you have acquired pyromancies from The Fair Lady, Laurentius will become curious and ask where you obtained such power. If you answer "yes," he will leave for Blighttown and eventually become hollow. If you answer "no," he will remain at Firelink Shrine as a merchant, offering his pyromancy services.

### Information

He is a Pyromancy trainer rescued from The Depths. Was apparently being held captive by an undead chef.

* To find him, enter the Depths through the iron door unlocked with the Key to Depths  right after Capra Demon. Go down the stairs until you reach the area with the hollows and tables, and drop down through the archways, then follow the hallways until you reach the area with the Butcher who is waiting on an elevated platform; (if you reach a closed wooden door, go the other way). Defeat the Butcher, and move into the room full of barrels. Laurentius is in a barrel in the corner when you find him. It is recommend to roll through the barrels to free him rather than attacking.
* After being rescued Laurentius can be found by taking the first stairway up from Firelink Shrine and turning left, he will be sitting on the ground.
* If you hadn't chosen Pyromancer at the start, he will give you a Pyromancy Flame and offer to teach you pyromancies. He can also upgrade the flame using souls.

### Laurentius Encounters

* Encountered in The Depths shortly after the first butcher mini-boss and guarded by another butcher. Found stuck in a barrel.
* After talking to him in The Depths, he will return to Firelink Shrine.
* If you speak to him after meeting the Daughter of Chaos in Quelaag's nest or speaking with the witch in Blighttown or possessing one of the Chaos pyromancies, he will ask you where you found such awesome pyromancy. If you agree to reveal to him what you know, the next time you come back to Firelink Shrine, he will be gone.
* After he leaves Firelink Shrine, you may encounter Laurentius in the swamp at Blighttown where he will start attacking you whether you attacked him or not. (Laurentius is likely Hollow during this encounter).

### Laurentius Drops

* 1000 Souls

### Laurentius Note

* You can also drain him using the Dark Hand for 5 humanity. One at a time with normal dark hand. All 5 in one hit while member of Dark Wraith covenant at +3. Doing so will not make him hostile.
