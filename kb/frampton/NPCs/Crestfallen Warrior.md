### Information

The Crestfallen Warrior is a Character in Dark Souls. He is the first NPC you will meet at Firelink Shrine. He has grown weary of the world and fears his hollowing is inevitable. When engaging in conversation with him, he will reveal useful information about the world and your objectives.

* Be careful about engaging him in combat. He can be quite troublesome at early levels.
* Bait him to follow you on the bridge where a Ring of Sacrifice can be found, for an easy 1000 souls.
* Once hollowed he will be a hostile NPC located at the bridge entrance to the New Londo Ruins.
* Depending on how the Player personally views him, he can be considered a good friend or ally as he keeps a keen eye on all events, major or minor, that goes on and freely has conversation with the player's character about them leading one the option to investigate.

### Drops

* No items

### Trivia

* The Crestfallen Warrior is part of a recurring character archetype of characters in recent Fromsoftware titles. These include a character of the same name in *Demon's Souls*, Crestfallen Saulden in *Dark Souls 2*, Hawkwood the Deserter in *Dark Souls 3*, and Roderika in *Elden Ring*.
* In his final statements he hints at wanting to "do something about" the creature of the foul stench and abhorrent noise (Kingseeker Frampt), it is a possibility this encounter is what caused him to go Hollow.
