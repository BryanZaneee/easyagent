### Information

**Sieglinde of Catarina** is a Character in Dark Souls. She is the daughter of Siegmeyer of Catarina. She is first found in the yard between the Duke's Archives and the Crystal Cave, and is essential to Siegmeier's storyline. She, like her father, is easily identifiable by her set of Catarina Armor.

### Catarina Storyline

Note: the article for Siegmeyer has a more detailed explanation of how to progress through this storyline.

 After you help Siegmeyer in Blighttown, by giving him three pieces of purple moss, the Golden Crystal Golem will appear in the small forest outside the Duke's Archives. Defeat the golem to free her, and to advanced their story-line. *(It is possible that she will "bug" and you will not be able to speak with her. If this happens, log out and back in. She will now be in a different position and you should be able to speak with her.)*
 When you talk to her she thanks you and asks about her father. Speak with her again here or at Firelink Shrine and she will ask if you have seen her father. You can answer "Yes" or "No", but if you answer "No" her path will end. Answer "Yes", and she will leave Firelink. Once you have saved Seigmeyer from Lost Izalith come back to Firelink to find her there again. Once you speak with her she will head off to Ash Lake, where she can be found at the first bonfire (Nearest to the Great Hollow). Here you can see the final ending for her and her father, where she will give you a **Titanite Slab.**
