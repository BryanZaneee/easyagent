**Nico of Thorolund** is a Character in Dark Souls. He is the friend of Vince of Thorolund and Reah of Thorolund.

### Encounters

* Appears at Firelink Shrine, along with Vince of Thorolund and Reah of Thorolund, after beating the Capra Demon. They are found near Petrus of Thorolund.
* Appears to be quite simple, will only hum when talked to.
* Will disappear from Firelink Shrine after all original speech with Reah, Vince, Petrus, and Nico is exhausted.
* Later appears as a hollow in the Giant's Tomb. Reah will ask you to kill hollow Nico and Vince when you encounter her in the Giant's Tomb.

### Drops

* 1000 souls, both as human and hollow

### Note

* If attacked at Firelink Shrine, he will become hostile along with Reah, Vince, and Petrus.

### Character Info

* Armor: Heavy Cleric set (no helm)
* Left Hand: Caduceus Kite Shield
* Right Hand: Crescent Axe and Thorolund Talisman
* Rings: none
* Spells: Heal
* Special: none
