**Witch Beatrice** is an **NPC Phantom** in **Dark Souls****. Witch Beatrice** is a rogue witch who casts spells that players can summon, she did not attend the Vinheim Dragon School but instead learned her craft from experienced elders, adhering to traditional witch practices. Her catalyst, passed down through generations, bears the marks of ancient sorceries.

### Information

* Beatrice can be summoned for the Moonlight Butterfly Boss fight and the Four Kings Boss fight.
  + If the Moonlight Butterfly is defeated without her help, she will not be available for the Four Kings.
* Players can pick up her Armor Set and her Catalyst  upon defeating the Four Kings Boss in the Valley of Darkness

### Equipment

**Weapons Witch Beatrice Carries:**

* Beatrice's Catalyst
* Cracked Round Shield

**Armor Witch Beatrice Wears:**

* Witch Hat
* Witch Cloak
* Witch Gloves
* Witch Skirt

**Spells Witch Beatrice Casts:**

* Homing Soulmass
* Great Heavy Soul Arrow

### Notes & Tips

* She, like the chosen undead, wanted to fight the Four Kings and did so before the time of New Londo's flooding. This makes her ancient in the storyline.
* She was also one of the three people able to traverse the abyss. The Chosen Undead (player character), and Artorias being the other two.
