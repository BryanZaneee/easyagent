**Knight Kirk** is an NPC Phantom in **Dark Souls.**Knight Kirk is an NPC invader designed to give players a taste of PvP combat in Dark Souls. While he doesn't contribute to the game's story, his invasions provide a challenging experience for players. Kirk can invade multiple times, and his known invasion locations include the Depths, the Demon Ruins, and Lost Izalith. Each encounter with Kirk offers a chance to test your PvP skills, and defeating him in all three locations will eventually reward you with his Thorned armor set.

### Information

Kirk, also known as the Knight of Thorns, is an infamous knight who is often associated with the Darkwraith faction, although it was revealed in an interview that he is actually a Chaos Servant. This connection becomes evident after defeating him three times, as his body can be found in the same room as the Fair Lady. Kirk is notorious for his ruthless nature, with his primary goal being to steal Humanity and make his prey bleed through the brutal cuts inflicted by his spiked weapons and armor. His gear is designed to deal damage even when he dodges or rolls into opponents, making him a formidable foe. When facing Kirk, beware of his dodge/roll attacks, as they can inflict significant damage. Once you defeat him and obtain his armor, you can also utilize this unique mechanic to your advantage.

### Encounters

| **Locations** | **Events** |
| --- | --- |
| The Depths | Invades you if you are in human form, only if you have not defeated Gaping Dragon. He appears near the Basilisk area. |
| Demon Ruins | Invades you if you are in human form, only if you have not defeated the Demon Firesage, you also need to place the Lordvessel for him to appear. He appears right after the first Capra Demon on the cliff path. |
| Lost Izalith | Invades you if you are in human form, only if you have not defeated The Bed of Chaos. He appears near the fog wall leading to The Bed of Chaos. |
