**Large Ember** is an Ember in **Dark Souls**. Embers are key items given to blacksmiths to unlock specific weapon upgrade paths. Once you provide the appropriate ember to a qualified blacksmith, that upgrade becomes permanently available for any suitable weapon, provided you have the necessary materials.

> Ember required for weapon ascension. A large, high-quality ember. Handled by the blacksmith of Astora.
>
> Ascension for +5 standard weapon. Allows reinforcement to +10.
>
> Also, can ascend +5 std wpn to raw wpn. (raw weapon can be reinforced to +5)

### Location

* Located in the Depths, continue down the path until you reach the kitchen. There you will find the chest which is located along the back wall behind the butcher's table.
* Video Location Timestamp 1:36

### Usage

* Given to Blacksmith Andre to allow him to modify +5 normal weapons to +10 normal, and +5 raw.
