**Demon Titanite** is an Ore in **Dark Souls**. Ores function as upgrade components for Weapons and Armor. Some varieties may be given to Kingseeker Frampt, who converts them into lesser versions.

> Special titanite stolen from a faceless stone beast known as a Titanite Demon.
> When the nameless blacksmith deity passed, from several Slabs, great beasts arose.
> The power of titanite remains within them, and they still roam Lordran today.

### Usage

* Can be used to upgrade weapons forged from Boss Souls.

### Locations

* Obtained from Titanite Demons (2x from the respawning Demon in Lost Izalith and the non-respawning Demon in Anor Londo, but 1x from the rest).
* Trade with Snuggly: 1x for Dung Pie, 1x for Prism Stone.
* 1x dropped from the Titanite Demon near Andre in Undead Parish.
* 1x found in the first chest guarded by two Sentinels in Anor Londo.
* 1x found in the chest hidden down behind the first elevator tower in Anor Londo.
* 2x found in the chest inside the bedroom guarded by three Silver Knights in Anor Londo.
* Drops from Vagrants, specifically the Red Good Vagrant. (Depths, Undead Burg, Undead Parish, Painted World of Ariamis, Darkroot Garden, Darkroot Basin, The Catacombs, Tomb of Giants, The Great Hollow, Ash Lake, Demon Ruins, Lost Izalith, Sen's Fortress, Blighttown, Anor Londo, New Londo Ruins Lower, New Londo Ruins Upper, The Duke's Archives, Crystal Cave, Northern Undead Asylum, Kiln of the First Flame, and Royal Wood).
