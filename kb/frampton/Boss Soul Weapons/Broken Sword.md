**Broken Straight Sword** is a Weapon in **Dark Souls** and **Dark Souls Remastered. Broken Straight Sword** is a weak unupgraded blade ideal for practicing parries without risking accidental kills. Straight Swords offer a balance of power and reach, trading the raw speed of daggers for heavier, more impactful strikes. Their slower swing is compensated by a longer range and the ability to hit multiple opponents with a single, broad motion.

> *Half of the blade of this straight sword is broken off.
> Only the completely Hollowed would even consider using this as a weapon**."*

### How to Get / Where to Find the Broken Straight Sword

* Drops from Hollows (2% chance - Undead Burg, Northern Undead Asylum, Undead Parish, Depths, Northern Undead Asylum and Painted World of Ariamis).
* Drops from Torch Hollows (2% chance - Northern Undead Asylum, Lower Undead Burg, Depths and Painted World of Ariamis).
* Drops from sword-wielding Infested Ghouls (2% - Blighttown).

### Hints and Tips

* Can be give to Kingseeker Frampt to receive 50 Souls.
* Can be upgraded to +10 and later ascended with the Soul of Sif to make the uncursed version of Greatsword of Artorias.
* An excellent weapon to use unupgraded to help practice parrying against various foes without risking to kill them.
