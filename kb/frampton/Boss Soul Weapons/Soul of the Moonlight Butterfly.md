**Soul of the Moonlight Butterfly** is a Soul in **Dark Souls**. Consuming a Soul item adds a specific number of souls to the player's stockpile. These souls can then be spent to make purchases, repair equipment, increase Soul Level, and upgrade Weapons and Armor.

> Soul of the mystical Moonlight Butterfly, which flitters in the Darkroot Garden. Special beings have special souls. The butterfly's soul is a creation of Seath the Scaleless. Use to acquire a huge amount of souls, or to create a unique weapon.

### Usage

* Moonlight Butterfly Horn (ascended from a +10 spear or rapier)
* Crystal Ring Shield (ascended from a +10 shield)
* Souls gained: 1,200 from using. 8,000 from feeding to Frampt.

### Location

* Obtained by killing the Moonlight Butterfly. (Guaranteed - Darkroot Garden)
* Note: the Crystal Butterflies in Crystal Cave do not drop boss souls
