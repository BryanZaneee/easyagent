**Titanite Shard** is an Ore in **Dark Souls**. Ores function as upgrade components for Weapons and Armor. Some varieties may be given to Kingseeker Frampt, who converts them into lesser versions. Upgrade Materials, meanwhile, are employed to boost a piece of equipment's stats and improve its overall performance.

> Titanite shard for weapon reinforcement. Most common titanite material. Reinforces standard weapons to +5.Titanite shards are fragments of the Legendary Slabs. Titanite is etched into weapons to reinforce

### Usage

* Reinforce standard weapons and shields to +5 (9 required to upgrade from 0 to +5)
* Reinforce standard armors to +3 (4 required to upgrade from 0 to +3)

### Location

* Sold by Blacksmith Andre (800 souls)
* Sold by Blacksmith Vamos (800 souls)
* Sold by Giant Blacksmith (800 souls)
* Sold by Crestfallen Merchant (1,000 souls)
* Sold by Hawkeye Gough (800 souls)
* Drops from Balder Knights (8% chance for 1 in Undead Parish, 21.6% chance for 1, and 1.8% chance for 2 in Sen's Fortress)
* Drops from Undead Soldier (1.6% chance for 1 in Undead Burg, Undead Parish, Northern Undead Asylum (Revisit), and Firelink Shrine)
* Drops from Undead Soldier (0.4% chance for 1 in Northern Undead Asylum)
* One drops from the non-respawning Berenike Knight in Undead Parish
