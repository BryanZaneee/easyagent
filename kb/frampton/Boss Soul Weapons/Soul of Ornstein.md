**Soul of Ornstein** is a Soul in **Dark Souls**. Consuming a Soul item adds a specific number of souls to the player's stockpile. These souls can then be spent to make purchases, repair equipment, increase Soul Level, and upgrade Weapons and Armor.

> Soul of Ornstein, Dragonslayer Knight who guards the cathedral in the forsaken city of Anor Londo.
> Special beings have special souls. Lord Gywn granted this soul to his four most trusted knights. Use to acquire a huge amount of souls, or to create a unique weapon.

### Usage

* Dragonslayer Spear (Ascended from +10 spear weapons)
* Gives 12,000 souls when consumed. 8,000 souls from feeding to Frampt.

### Location

* Obtained by killing Dragon Slayer Ornstein last.
