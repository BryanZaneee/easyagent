**Crystal Ring Shield** is a Small Shield in Dark Souls. This weapon, originating from the mystical Moonlight Butterfly of the Darkroot Garden, is imbued with potent crystal power, akin to the creation of Seath the Scaleless. It has the ability to emit rings of crystal light. Small Shields can be used not only to block an enemy but to parry their attack as well. This allows a riposte, a free attack by the player for a high amount of damage. Enemy attacks can be interrupted with a parry (pressing the Strong Attack button for the shield) and requires practice to learn the timing.

> Weapon born from the mystical creature of the Darkroot Garden, the Moonlight Butterfly. Imbued with a powerful crystal power, like the butterfly created by Seath the Scaleless. Can emit crystal light rings

### How to Get / Where to Find

* **Ascend:** From the Soul of the Moonlight Butterfly and a +10 shield by the Giant Blacksmith.

### Notes and Tips

* Has the highest magic resistance in the game, tied with Havel's Greatshield.
* Special: L2 or R2 Attack (depending on which hand holds the shield) sends out a vertical magic disc that strikes enemies at range.
* The special attack uses 4 durability points and ignores the East Wood Grain Ring.
* This shield **can** be repaired. Do not mistake this for crystal equipment. It has crystal in its name but it is NOT crystal equipment.
