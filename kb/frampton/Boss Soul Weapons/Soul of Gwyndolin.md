**Soul of Gwyndolin** is a Soul in **Dark Souls**. Consuming a Soul item adds a specific number of souls to the player's stockpile. These souls can then be spent to make purchases, repair equipment, increase Soul Level, and upgrade Weapons and Armor.

> Soul of Darkmoon Gywndolin, god of the Dark Sun and guardian of deserted Anor Londo.
>  Special beings have special souls. Use the soul of Gywn's lastborn to acquire a huge amount of souls, or to create a unique weapon.

### Usage

* Darkmoon Bow (Ascended from +10 bow weapons)
* Tin Darkmoon Catalyst (Ascended from cane catalyst)
* Gives 16,000 souls when consumed. Can also be sold to Kingseeker Frampt for 20,000 souls.

### Location

* Obtained by killing Dark Sun Gwyndolin.
