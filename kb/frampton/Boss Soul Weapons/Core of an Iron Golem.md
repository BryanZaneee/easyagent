**Core of an Iron Golem** is a Soul in **Dark Souls**. Consuming a Soul item adds a specific number of souls to the player's stockpile. These souls can then be spent to make purchases, repair equipment, increase Soul Level, and upgrade Weapons and Armor.

> Soul serving as the core of the Iron Golem, guardian of Sen's Fortress, and slayer of countless heroes seeking Anor Londo. Originally a bone of an everlasting dragon. Use to acquire a huge amount of souls, or to create a unique weapon.

### Usage

* Dragon Bone Fist (Ascended from +10 fist weapons)
* Golem Axe (Ascended from +10 axe weapons)
* Gives 12,000 souls when consumed. 8,000 souls from feeding to Frampt.

### Location

* Obtained by killing  Iron Golem
