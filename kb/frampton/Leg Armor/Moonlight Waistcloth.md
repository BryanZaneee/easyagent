**Moonlight Waistcloth** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Gwyndolin Moonlight Set.

### Description

*"Waistcloth of the Dark Sun Gwyndolin, protector of the forsaken city of Anor Londo. The power of the moon was strong in Gwyndolin, and thus he was raised as a daughter. His magic garb is silk-thin, and hardly provides any physical defense."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Domhnall of Zena after killing Dark Sun Gwyndolin

### Notes

* Cannot be upgraded
* Part of the Gwyndolin Moonlight Set. Pieces include Crown of the Dark Sun, Moonlight Robe, Moonlight Gloves and Moonlight Waistcloth.
