**Holy Trousers** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Holy Set.

### Description

"Trousers donned by men of the cloth. Though plain to the eye, its hardy fabric repels rain and keeps the body warm. Those dissatisfied with church teachings must test their faith by going on a spiritual journey. Such is the ritual self-purification of the Way of White."

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Cleric (Starting Class)
* The Catacombs: on a cliff in the bonewheel skeleton room. To access the cliff, jump down from the second rotating bridge above.
