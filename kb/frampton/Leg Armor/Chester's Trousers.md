**Chester's Trousers** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**.

### Description

*"Black leather trousers worn by Marvellous Chester, a man of mystery lost in the past. These exquisitely sewn trousers are decorated with silver trinkets of varying sizes, and have straps on the inner legs for carrying crossbow bolts."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Killing Marvellous Chester in human form

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Chester is suspected to hail from the Bloodborne universe, because of his clothing and mentions of time travel.
* Part of the Chester's Set. Pieces include Snickering Top Hat, Chester's Long Coat, Chester's Gloves and Chester's Trousers.
