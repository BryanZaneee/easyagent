**Boots of the Explorer** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Adventurer's Set.

### Description

*"Strange boots from the ancient land of Zena, birthplace of the curious dealer Domhnall. The inlaid silver rings symbolize an explorer."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Domhnall of Zena for 10,000 souls

### Notes

* Cannot be upgraded
* Part of the Adventurer's Set. Parts include Helm of the Wise, Armor of the Glorious, Gauntlets of the Vanquisher and Boots of the Explorer.
