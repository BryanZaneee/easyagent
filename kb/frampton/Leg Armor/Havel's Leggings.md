**Havel's Leggings** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Havel's Set.

### Description

*"Leggings worn by Havel the Rock's warriors. Carved from solid rock, their tremendous weight is matched only by the defense it provides. Havel's warriors never flinched nor retreated from battle. Those unfortunate enough to face them were inevitably beaten to a pulp."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Anor Londo. In a room with a fireplace. Hit the fireplace to reveal an illusory wall.

### Notes

* Cannot be upgraded
* Part of the Havel's Set. Pieces include Havel's Helm, Havel's Armor, Havel's Gauntlets and Havel's Leggings.
