**Balder Leggings** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Balder Set.

### Description

*"Leggings worn by the knights of the ancient kingdom of Balder. It is made from thick iron plates. Balder was the homeland of the Knight King Rendal, but it came to ruin after a great many Undead were spawned ."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Crestfallen Merchant for 5,000 souls
* Drops from random Balder Knight (1% chance - Sen's Fortress and Undead Parish)

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Balder Set, Pieces include Balder Helm, Balder Armor, Balder Gauntlets and Balder Leggings.
