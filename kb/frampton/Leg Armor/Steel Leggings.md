**Steel Leggings** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Steel Set.

### Description

*"Leggings of the Knights of Berenike, known for their heavy armaments and armor Countless Knights of Berenike, once extolled as the mightiest of mighty, became Undead and ventured to Lordran. But their journey was for naught, as they went Hollow, and became a threat to all Undead."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Dropped by The Berenike Knights
* Sold by Crestfallen Merchant for 5,000 souls

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards (x6), Large Titanite Shards (x6), Titanite Chunks (x6) and Titanite Slab (x1)
* Part of the Steel Set. Pieces include Steel Helm, Steel Armor, Steel Gauntlets and Steel Leggings.
