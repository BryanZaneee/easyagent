**Crystalline Leggings** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Crystalline Set.

### Description

*"Leggings worn by a Hollowed knight, who was partially crystallized. The crystal power provides good defense, but they cannot be used for long because they are easily broken and cannot be repaired."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* The Duke's Archives. Outside in the area with Crystal Golems.

### Notes

* Cannot be upgraded
* Has the highest repair cost (100 souls per durability point)
