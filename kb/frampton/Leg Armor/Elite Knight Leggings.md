**Elite Knight Leggings** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Elite Knight Set.

### Description

*"Leggings of a nameless knight, perhaps an elite knight of Astora. Although he was loath to give up on his Undead mission, he perished at the Undead Asylum, and went Hollow."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* After a fog wall in Darkroot Garden. Guarded by two Stone Knights

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
