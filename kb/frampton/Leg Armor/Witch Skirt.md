**Witch Skirt** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Witch Set.

### Description

*"Skirt of the rogue witch Beatrice. Almost all magic users that employ catalysts studied at Dragon School, but Beatrice is one of the few exceptions. She braved the Abyss but did not live to tell of her ordeal."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* At Valley of Drakes area along with Beatrice's Catalyst. Go where the cave entrance to Blighttown is; the corpse is located at a ledge on at the opposite side of the cave entrance.
* Available after defeating The Four Kings

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Witch Set. Pieces include Witch Hat, Witch Cloak, Witch Gloves and Witch Skirt.
