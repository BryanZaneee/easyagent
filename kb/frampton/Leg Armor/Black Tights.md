**Black Tights** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Black Set.

### Description

*"Tights worn by pardoners serving Velka, the Goddess of Sin. The pardoners' attire is uniformly black in color and said to be imbued with Velka's mystical power, which provides resistance against all manner of magic."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Painted World of Ariamis, behind the locked annex door.

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Black Set. Mask of Velka, Black Cleric Robe, Black Manchette and Black Tights.
