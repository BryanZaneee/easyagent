**Shadow Leggings** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Shadow Set.

### Description

*"Black cloth leggings worn by spooks from an Eastern land. Designed so as not to hinder their unique form of martial arts. White they sacrifice defense for the sake of greater mobility, they do offer resistance to bleeding and poison among other things, perhaps due to the nature of espionage."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Found in Blighttown past first group of Infested Ghouls

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Shadow Set. Pieces include Shadow Mask, Shadow Garb, Shadow Gauntlets and Shadow Leggings.
