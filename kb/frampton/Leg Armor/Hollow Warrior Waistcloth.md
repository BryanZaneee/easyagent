**Hollow Warrior Waistcloth** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**.It is part of Hollow Warrior Set.

### Description

*"Waistcloth worn by Hollow Warriors. It is so tattered that it barely serves its purpose anymore."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Dropped by Hollow Warrior.

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Hollow Warrior Set. Pieces include Hollow Warrior Helm, Hollow Warrior Armor and Hollow Warrior Waistcloth.
