**Leggings of Thorns** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Thorns Set.

### Description

*"Leggings of Kirk, knight of Thorns and notorious member of the Darkwraiths. A dense patch of thorns grows from their surface. They are a fitting item for the murderous Kirk, for by simply wearing them and rolling, one can damage enemies."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Same place where The Fair Lady is. To spawn the armor set you need to defeat Kirk, Knight of Thorns in 3 places: Depths, Demon Ruins, Lost Izalith

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Thorns Set. Pieces include Helm of Thorns, Armor of Thorns, Gauntlets of Thorns and Leggings of Thorns.
* While wearings this leggins will make your kicking do damage which also stacks with the Orange Charred Ring
