**Dark Leggings** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**.It is part of Dark Set.

### Description

*"Leggings of the Darkwraiths, former knights of New Londo who descended into Dark. Their leggings transformed, and remain a symbol of the Dark servants and their diabolical art of Lifedrain."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Obtained by getting Rank 2 in the Darkwraith Covenant

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Some believe them to be revolutionaries, defenders of the human race, but they may rather represent evil Humanity.
* Part of the Dark Set. Pieces include Dark Mask, Dark Armor, Dark Gauntlets and Dark Leggings.
