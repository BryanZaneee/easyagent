**Hard Leather Boots** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Warrior Set.

### Description

*"Boots made of thick leather. Very common type of protective gear, they provide a sound level of defense."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Warrior (Starting Class)
* Depths: in the room where you fight Gaping Dragon
