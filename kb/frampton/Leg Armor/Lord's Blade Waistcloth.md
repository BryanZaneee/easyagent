**Lord's Blade Waistcloth** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Lord's Blade Set.

### Description

*"Waistcloth worn by the Lord's Blade Ciaran, one of Gwyn's four knights. Waistcloth common to all of the Lord's Blades. These deadly women shift nimbly between layers of darkness, etching streaks of gold into the night air, and planting visions of terror into the minds of their targets."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* By killing Ciaran in the same place you fought Artorias.

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Lord's Blade Set. Pieces include Porcelain Mask, Lord's Blade Robe, Lord's Blade Gloves and Lord's Blade Waistcloth.
