**Hollow Thief's Tights** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Hollow Thief Set.

### Description

*"Tights worn by Hollow thieves. They stifle noise and help their wearer hide in the shadows."*

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Drops from Undead Assassin also known as Hollow Thief (1% chance -  Lower Undead Burg)

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
