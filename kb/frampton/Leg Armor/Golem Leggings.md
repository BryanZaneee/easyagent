**Golem Leggings** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Iron Golem Set.

### Description

*"Legs of the Iron Golem, guardian of the ancient Sen's Fortress, slayer of heroes who ventured forth to Anor Londo. Without its core, they are mere hunks of iron, and can be equipped as solid protective gear, but their immense weight hinders stamina recovery."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Domhnall of Zena after defeating Iron Golem

### Notes

* Cannot be upgraded
* Part of the Iron Golem Set. Pieces include Golem Helm, Golem Armor, Golem Gauntlets, and Golem Leggings.
