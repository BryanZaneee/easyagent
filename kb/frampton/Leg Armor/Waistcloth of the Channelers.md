**Waistcloth of the Channelers** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Channeler Set.

### Description

*"Waistcloth of the Channelers, sorcerers that serve Seath the Scaleless. Even after the onset of Seath's madness, the "snatchers" as they were often called, ventured to far lands to find suitable human specimens. The heaviest of protective gear for sorcerers, and imbued with magic."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* In a chest off in one of the side rooms of the Duke's Archives, near the stairs leading down to the courtyard that leads to the Crystal Cave.

### Notes

* Cannot be upgraded
* Part of the Channeler Set. Pieces include Six-Eyed Helm of the Channelers, Robe of the Channelers, Gauntlets of the Channelers and Waistcloth of the Channelers.
