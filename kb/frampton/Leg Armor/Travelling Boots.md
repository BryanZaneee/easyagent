**Travelling Boots** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**.It is part of Big Hat's Set.

### Description

*"Boots worn by Big Hat Logan. They are indistinguishable from the ordinary kind worn by travelers."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* In a chest in Duke's Archives after Logan turnes hostile.

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Big Hat's Set. Pieces include Big Hat, Sage Robe, Travelling Gloves and Travelling Boots.
