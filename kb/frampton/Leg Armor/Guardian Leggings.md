**Guardian Leggings** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Guardian Set.

### Description

*"Leggings of the stone knights, guardians of the forest sanctuary. The stone knights are golems animated by magic, and their enchanted leggings are oppressively heavy."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Royal Wood. On one of the corpses on the ground. (AotA)
  + Video Location Timestamp 5:25

### Notes

* Cannot be upgraded
* Reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second.Part of the  Guardian Set. Pieces include Guardian Helm, Guardian Armor, Guardian Gauntlets and Guardian Leggings.
