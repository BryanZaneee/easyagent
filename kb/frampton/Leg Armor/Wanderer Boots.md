**Wanderer Boots** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Wanderer Set.

### Description

"*Boots of an aimless traveler.**Made from sturdy leather. A must-have for long journeys*."

### Where to Find \ Location

* Wanderer (Starting Class)
* Blighttown

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Wanderer Set. Pieces include Wanderer Hood, Wanderer Coat, Wanderer Manchette and Wanderer Boots.
