**Maiden Skirt** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Maiden Set.

### Description

*"White skirt worn by traveling maidens. It is part of their formal attire, regardless of rank. It is soft and well-made, but does not offer much in the way of defense, making it ill- suited to use in battle."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Duke's Archives. In one of the cells.

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Maiden Set. Pieces include Maiden Hood, Maiden Robe, Maiden Gloves and Maiden Skirt.
