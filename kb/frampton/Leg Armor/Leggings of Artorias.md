**Leggings of Artorias** is a Leg Armor in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Artorias' Set.

### Description

*"Leggings of Artorias the Abysswalker, one of Gwyn's four knights. The death of the their owner can be surmised from the corrosive Dark of the Abyss, which has compromised their protective utility."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Domhnall of Zena for 20,000 souls after defeating Artorias The Abysswalker

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite

* Artorias is related to The Abyss Watchers of DS3.
* Part of the Artorias' Set. Pieces include Helm of Artorias, Armor of Artorias, Armor of Artorias and Leggings of Artorias.
