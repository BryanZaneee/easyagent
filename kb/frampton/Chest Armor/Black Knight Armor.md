**Black Knight Armor** is a Chest Armor in **Dark Souls****.** This armor is part of the Black Knight Set, which can be found on a corpse near a ledge in the Kiln of the First Flame. The set is known for its high Fire Resistance, Physical Defense, and Poise, but it falls short in resisting magic and lightning damage. **Black Knight Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Armor of the Black Knight who haunt Lordran. The knights followed Lord Gwyn when he departed to link the flame, but they were burned to ashes in newly kindled fire, wandering the world as disembodied spirits ever after.

### Information

* Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
* Reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second.
* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Black Knight Set. Pieces include Black Knight Helm, Black Knight Armor, Black Knight Gauntlets and Black Knight Leggings.

### Location

* Kiln of the First Flame. Corpse on a ledge near the third black knight.
