**Elite Knight Armor** is a Chest Armor in **Dark Souls.** Part of the Elite Knight Set, this armor was worn by a nameless knight, possibly an elite knight of Astora, as suggested by the fire-warding heraldic symbol on its blue surcoat. Despite his dedication to his Undead mission, he perished in the Undead Asylum and eventually went Hollow. **Elite Knight Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Armor of a nameless knight, perhaps an elite knight of Astora, based on the fire-warding heraldic symbol on its blue surcoat. Although he was loath to give up on his Undead mission, he perished at the Undead Asylum, and went Hollow."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab

### Where to Find \ Location

* After a fog wall in Darkroot Garden. Guarded by two Stone Knights.
* Part of the Elite Knight Set. Pieces include Elite Knight Helm, Elite Knight Gauntlets and Elite Knight Leggings.
