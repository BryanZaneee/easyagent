**Moonlight Robe** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** The robe of Dark Sun Gwyndolin, the protector of Anor Londo. Made of silk, it offers little in terms of physical defense. It is part of the Gwyndolin Moonlight Set. **Moonlight Robe** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Robe of the Dark Sun Gwyndolin, protector of the forsaken city of Anor Londo. The power of the moon was strong in Gwyndolin, and thus he was raised as a daughter. His magic garb is silk-thin and hardly provides any physical defense."

### Information

* Cannot be upgraded
* Part of the Gwyndolin Moonlight Set. Pieces include Crown of the Dark Sun, Moonlight Robe, Moonlight Gloves and Moonlight Waistcloth.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Domhnall of Zena after killing Dark Sun Gwyndolin
