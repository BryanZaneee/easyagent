**Havel's Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.**Part of Havel's Set. This armor, worn by Havel the Rock's warriors, is carved from solid rock. Its tremendous weight is only matched by the unparalleled defense it provides. **Havel's Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Armor worn by Havel the Rock's warriors. Carved from solid rock, its tremendous weight is matched only by the defense it provides. Havel's warriors never flinched nor retreated from battle. Those unfortunate enough to face them were inevitably beaten to a pulp.

### Description

* Cannot be upgraded
* Part of the Havel's Set. Pieces include Havel's Helm, Havel's Armor, Havel's Gauntlets and Havel's Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Anor Londo. In a room with a fireplace. Hit the fireplace to reveal an illusory wall.
