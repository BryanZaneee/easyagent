**Antiquated Dress** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.**A dress from the ancient, fallen land of Oolacile, made of ivory-colored silk with intricate embroidery and imbued with ancient magical power. This garment was not designed for battle, so it offers no physical protection. It is part of Antiquated Set. **Antiquated Dress** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "A dress from the ancient fallen land of Oolacile. Its ivory-colored silk features elaborate embroidery and is imbued with ancient magic power. One cannot expect any physical protection from this garment, as it was not meant to be worn in battle."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Antiquated Set. Pieces include Crown of Dusk, Antiquated Dress, Antiquated Gloves and Antiquated Skirt.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Found in Darkroot Basin, after saving Dusk of Oolacile. Kill the Golden Crystal Golem in the back of the **Darkroot Basin** cave (far behind the Hydra), go back to where the corpse was in front of the Hydra, and summon her (you DO NOT have to be in human form), then return to where you found the golem. You will find the set there.
