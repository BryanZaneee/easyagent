**Steel Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** It is part of Steel Set. Armor of the Knights of Berenike, renowned for their heavy armaments. These knights, once celebrated as the mightiest, ventured into Lordran after becoming Undead. Unfortunately, their journey ended in despair as they succumbed to Hollowing and became a threat to all Undead. It is part of the Steel Set. **Steel Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Armor of the Knights of Berenike, known for their heavy armaments and armor. Countless Knights of Berenike, once extolled as the mightiest of mighty, became Undead and ventured to Lordran. But their journey was for naught, as they went Hollow, and became a threat to all Undead."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards (x6), Large Titanite Shards (x6), Titanite Chunks (x6) and Titanite Slab (x1)
* Part of the Steel Set. Pieces include Steel Helm, Steel Armor, Steel Gauntlets and Steel Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Dropped by The Berenike Knights
* Sold by Crestfallen Merchant for 8,000 souls
