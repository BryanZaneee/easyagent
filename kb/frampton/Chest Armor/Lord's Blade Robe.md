**Lord's Blade Robe** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Part of the Lord's Blade Set, this is the robe worn by Lord's Blade Ciaran, one of Gwyn's Four Knights. It is a garment shared by all of the Lord's Blades. **Lord's Blade Robe** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Robe of the Lord's Blade Ciaran, one of Gwyn's Four Knights. A robe common to all of the Lord's Blades, These deadly women shift nimbly between layers of darkness, etching streaks of gold into the night air, and planting visions of terror into the minds of their targets."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Lord's Blade Set. Pieces include Porcelain Mask, Lord's Blade Robe, Lord's Blade Gloves and Lord's Blade Waistcloth.

### Where to Find \ Location

* By killing Ciaran in the same place you fought Artorias.
