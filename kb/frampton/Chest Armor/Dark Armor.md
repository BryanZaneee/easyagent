**Dark Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Part of the Dark Set, this armor belongs to the Darkwraiths, former knights of New Londo who fell into darkness. Their armor, now twisted by the Dark, symbolizes the Dark servants and their malevolent Lifedrain technique. **Dark Armor**is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Armor of the Darkwraiths, former knights of New Londo who descended into Dark. Their armor transformed, and remains a symbol of the Dark servants and their diabolical art of Lifedrain.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Some believe them to be revolutionaries, defenders of the human race, but they may rather represent evil Humanity.
* Part of the Dark Set. Pieces include Dark Mask, Dark Armor, Dark Gauntlets and Dark Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Obtained by getting Rank 2 in the Darkwraith Covenant
