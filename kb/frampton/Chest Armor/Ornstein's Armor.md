**Ornstein's Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Part of Ornstein's Set, this armor reduces stamina regeneration by 1 per second. It was worn by the dragonslayer Ornstein, who guards the cathedral in the abandoned city of Anor Londo. The golden lion armor is infused with the power of lightning, offering strong protection against it. **Ornstein's Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Armor of the dragonslayer Ornstein, who guards the cathedral in the forsaken city of Anor Londo. Ornstein is believed to be the captain of the Four Knights. His golden lion armor is imbued with the power of lightning and should provide good protection against it."

### Information

* Cannot be upgraded
* Can be fed to Frampt for 100 souls.
* Reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second.
* Part of the Ornstein's Set. Pieces include Ornstein's Helm, Ornstein's Armor, Ornstein's Gauntlets and Ornstein's Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Domhnall of Zena for 8,000 souls.
* You need to defeat Dragon Slayer Ornstein second.

### Notes & Tips

* Ornstein was named after the composer "Leo Ornstein".
* Ornstein reappeared as The Old Dragonslayer in DS2.
* The armor set of Ornstein was later rediscovered at Archdragon Peak (DS3).
