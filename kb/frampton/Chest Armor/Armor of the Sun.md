**Armor of the Sun** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls. Part of the Iron Set, this chestpiece was worn by Solaire of Astora, Knight of Sunlight. It is of high quality but lacks any particular powers, reflecting Solaire's unyielding spirit and dedication without reliance on supernatural enhancements. **Armor of the Sun** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Armor of Solaire of Astora, Knight of Sunlight. The large holy symbol of the Sun, while powerless, was painted by Solaire himself. Solaire's incredible prowess must have come from rigorous training alone, for his equipment exhibits no special traits."

### Description

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Iron Set. Pieces include Iron Helm, Armor of the Sun, Iron Bracelet and Iron Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Dropped by Solaire of Astora
