**Crystalline Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Part of the Crystalline Set, this armor was worn by a Hollowed knight who had become partially crystallized. While the crystal grants significant defense, it is fragile and cannot be repaired, making it a temporary shield. **Crystalline Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Armor worn by a Hollowed knight, who was partially crystallized. The crystal provides powerful defense, but it cannot be used for long because it is easily broken and cannot be repaired."

### Information

* Cannot be upgraded
* Has the highest repair cost (100 souls per durability point)
* Part of the Crystalline Set. Pieces include Crystalline Helm, **Crystalline Armor**, Crystalline Gauntlets and Crystalline Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* The Duke's Archives. Outside in the area with Crystal Golems.
