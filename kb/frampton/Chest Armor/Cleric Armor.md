**Cleric Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Part of the Cleric Set, this substantial armor is worn by the warrior clerics of the Way of White, known for their steadfastness in battle. The armor's size and weight make the wearer appear even larger. **Cleric Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Armor worn by the warrior clerics of the Way of White, who are famous for being unyielding in battle. It is rather substantial in size and weight, making its wearer look even bigger than he actually is.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Cleric Set. Pieces include Cleric Helm, Cleric Armor, Cleric Gauntlets and Cleric Leggings.
