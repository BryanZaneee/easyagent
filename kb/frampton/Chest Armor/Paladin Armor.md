**Paladin Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls. Reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second. This is the armor of Leeroy, the Paladin of the Catacombs. It is part of the Paladin Set. **Paladin Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Armor of Leeroy, Paladin of the Catacombs. Long ago, the Way of the White produced its first Undead, a paladin in golden armor. With the legendary treasures Grant and Sanctus, Leeroy set out for Lordran, Land of the Gods, in the first Undead mission of the Way of White.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
* Reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second.
* Part of the Paladin Set. Pieces include Paladin Helm, Paladin Armor, Paladin Gauntlets and Paladin Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Tomb of the Giants. Gravelord Nito's chamber after defeating black phantom Paladin Leeroy
