**Hollow Warrior Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Armor worn by Hollow warriors, crafted from thick leather that has become tattered with age. It is part of the Hollow Warrior Set.**Hollow Warrior Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Armor worn by Hollow warriors. It is made of thick leather that is tattered with age."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Hollow Warrior Set. Pieces include Hollow Warrior Helm, Hollow Warrior Armor and Hollow Warrior Waistcloth.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Dropped by Armored Zombie
