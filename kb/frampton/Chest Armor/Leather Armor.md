**Leather Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Made from soft leather, this is a common piece of protective gear. It is part of the Hunter Set. **Leather Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Made of soft leather. Very common type of protective gear."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Hunter Set. Pieces include Pharis's Hat, Leather Armor, Leather Gloves and Leather Boots.

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Hunter (Starting Class)
* Darkroot Basin. Near the entrance on a cliff.
