**Guardian Armor** is a Chest Armor in **Dark Souls.** Part of the Guardian Set. This armor is worn by the stone knights who guard the forest sanctuary. These knights are golems animated by magic, and their enchanted armor is oppressively heavy, reducing stamina regeneration by 2 per second. **Guardian Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Armor of the stone knights, guardians of the forest sanctuary. The stone knights are golems animated by magic, and their enchanted armor is oppressively heavy."

### Description

* Cannot be upgraded
* Reduces Stamina regeneration by 2 per second.
* Part of the Guardian Set. Pieces include Guardian Helm, Guardian Armor, Guardian Gauntlets and Guardian Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Royal Wood. On one of the corpses on the ground. (AotA)
