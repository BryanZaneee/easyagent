**Black Cleric Robe** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Part of the Black Set, this robe is worn by pardoners who serve Velka, the Goddess of Sin. The black color of the robe is uniform, and it is said to be imbued with Velka's mystical power, granting resistance against all forms of magic. **Black Cleric Robe** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Robe worn by pardoners serving Velka, the Goddess of Sin. The pardoners' attire is uniformly black in color and said to be imbued with Velka's mystical power, which provides resistance against all manner of magic."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Black Set. Mask of Velka, Black Cleric Robe, Black Manchette and Black Tights.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Painted World of Ariamis, behind the locked annex door.
