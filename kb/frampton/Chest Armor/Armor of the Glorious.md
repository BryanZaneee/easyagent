**Armor of the Glorious** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Part of the Adventurer's Set, this armor also reduces stamina regeneration by 1 per second. It is an unusual piece from the ancient land of Zena, the homeland of the curious merchant Domhnall. The numerous medals adorning it are thought to represent glory. **Armor of the Glorious** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Strange armor from the ancient land of Zena, birthplace of the curious dealer Domhnall. The many medals are believed to symbolize glory."

### Information

* Cannot be upgraded
* Reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second.
* Part of the Adventurer's Set. Parts include Helm of the Wise, Armor of the Glorious, Gauntlets of the Vanquisher and Boots of the Explorer.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Domhnall of Zena for 15,000 souls.
