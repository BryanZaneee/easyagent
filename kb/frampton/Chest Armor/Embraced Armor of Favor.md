**Embraced Armor of Favor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Armor of Lautrec the Embraced, symbolizing the goddess Fina's love. The goddess's arms seem to embrace the wearer, wrapping around the armor. It is part of the Favor Set. **Embraced Armor of Favor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Armor of Lautrec the Embraced, representing the goddess Fina's love. The goddess's arms wrap around it, as if to embrace the wearer."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Favor Set. Pieces include Helm of Favor, Embraced Armor of Favor, Gauntlets of Favor and Leggings of Favor.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Found in Anor Londo, on second floor of Ornstein and Smough boss area, after invading and defeating Lautrec.
