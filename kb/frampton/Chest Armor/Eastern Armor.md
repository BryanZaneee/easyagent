**Eastern Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Part of the Eastern Set, this armor reduces stamina regeneration by 1 per second. Crafted in an Eastern land, it is a finely made piece that offers excellent defense, particularly against slashing attacks, which are common in Eastern battles. **Eastern Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "A distinctive armor made in an Eastern land. Exquisitely crafted, this armor offers excellent defense, particularly versus slash attacks, which are one of the main threats in battles in the East."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second.
* Part of the Eastern Set. Pieces include Eastern Helm, Eastern Armor, Eastern Gauntlets and Eastern Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Darkroot Garden, past the door locked with Crest of Artorias.
  + Hug the wall on the right until you reach a cliff with armor set.
  + Video Location Timestamp 12:53
