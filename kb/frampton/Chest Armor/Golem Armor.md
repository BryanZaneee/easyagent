**Golem Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Part of the Iron Golem Set. This armor is made from the body of the Iron Golem, the guardian of the ancient Sen's Fortress and the slayer of heroes who sought to reach Anor Londo. Without its core, the armor is merely a massive hunk of iron that can be equipped as solid protective gear, but its immense weight hinders stamina recovery. **Golem Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Body of the Iron Golem, guardian of the ancient Sen's Fortress, slayer of heroes who ventured forth to Anor Londo. Without its core, it is a mere hunk of iron, and can be equipped as solid protective gear, but its immense weight hinders stamina recovery.

### Information

* Cannot be upgraded
* Part of the Iron Golem Set. Pieces include Golem Helm, Golem Armor, Golem Gauntlets, and Golem Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Domhnall of Zena after defeating Iron Golem
