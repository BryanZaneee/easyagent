**Wanderer Coat** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Coat of an aimless traveler. Crafted from sturdy leather and quilting, it offers some protection, which is necessary for those who roam the land, where battles are inevitable. It is part of the Wanderer Set. **Wanderer Coat** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Coat of an aimless traveler. Made from sturdy leather and quilting. Battle comes with the territory when one wanders the land, and this coat provides a certain degree of protection.."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Wanderer Set. Pieces include Wanderer Hood, Wanderer Coat, Wanderer Manchette and Wanderer Boots.
