**Smough's Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Part of Smough's Set. This armor offers extremely high defense and can be worn by humans, though not without great difficulty due to its weight and size. **Smough's Armor**is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Armor of Smough, the Executioner, protector of the cathedral at the forsaken city of Anor Londo. It offers extremely high defense and can be worn by humans, but not without great difficulty.

### Information

* Cannot be upgraded
* Part of the Smough's Set. Pieces include Smough's Helm, Smough's Armor, Smough's Gauntlets and Smough's Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Domhnall of Zena. You need to defeat Smough second in order to make him sell this armor.
