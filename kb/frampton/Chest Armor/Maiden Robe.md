**Maiden Robe** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** A white robe worn by traveling maidens as part of their formal attire, regardless of their rank. It is soft and finely made but lacks sufficient defense, making it unsuitable for battle. It is part of Maiden Set. **Maiden Robe**is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "White robe worn by traveling maidens. It is part of their formal attire, regardless of rank. It is soft and well-made, but does not offer much in the way of defense, making it ill- suited to use in battle."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Maiden Set. Pieces include Maiden Hood, Maiden Robe, Maiden Gloves and Maiden Skirt.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Duke's Archives. In one of the cells.
