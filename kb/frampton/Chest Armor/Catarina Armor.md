**Catarina Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second. This distinctively shaped armor is worn by the Knights of Catarina. Outside of Catarina, the armor's onion-like appearance is often ridiculed, much to the knights' dismay. However, its masterfully curved design makes it highly effective for parrying. It is part of the Catarina Set. **Catarina Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Distinctively shaped armor worn by the Knights of Catarina. Outside of Catarina, it is often ridiculed for their onion-like shape, infuriating the proud knights, but the masterfully forged curved design makes it very effective for parrying.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
* Reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second.
* Part of the Catarina Set. Pieces include Catarina Helm, Catarina Armor, Catarina Gauntlets and Catarina Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Crestfallen Merchant for 10,000 souls
