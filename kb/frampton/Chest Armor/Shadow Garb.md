**Shadow Garb** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Black cloth garb worn by spooks from an Eastern land. This attire is designed to allow freedom of movement for their unique martial arts style. While it sacrifices some defense for greater mobility, it offers resistance to bleeding and poison, likely due to the nature of their clandestine activities. It is part of Shadow Set. **Shadow Garb** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

>  "Black cloth garb worn by spooks from an Eastern land. Designed so as not to hinder their unique form of martial arts. White it sacrifices defense for the sake of greater mobility, it does offer resistance to bleeding and poison among other things, perhaps due to the nature of espionage."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Shadow Set. Pieces include Shadow Mask, Shadow Garb, Shadow Gauntlets and Shadow Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Found in Blighttown past first group of Infested Ghouls
