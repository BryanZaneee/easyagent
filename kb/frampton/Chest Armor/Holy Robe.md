**Holy Robe** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** A robe worn by men of the cloth, designed with plain but durable fabric that repels rain and keeps the wearer warm. Those who question church teachings often embark on a spiritual journey for self-purification, as part of the Way of White's traditions. It is part of the Holy Set. **Holy Robe**is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Robe donned by men of the cloth. Though plain to the eye, its hardy fabric repels rain and keeps the body warm. Those dissatisfied with church teachings must test their faith by going on a spiritual journey. Such is the ritual self-purification of the Way of White."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Holy Set. Pieces include Priest's Hat, Holy Robe, Travelling Gloves and Holy Trousers.

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Cleric (Starting Class)
* The Catacombs: on a cliff in the bonewheel skeleton room. To access the cliff, jump down from the second rotating bridge above.
