**Armor of Artorias** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Armor worn by Artorias the Abysswalker, one of Gwyn's four knights. The armor’s tattered azure-blue cape, once a symbol of pride and glory, now bears the mark of the corrosive Dark of the Abyss. It is part of Artorias' Set. **Armor of Artorias** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Armor of Artorias the Abysswalker, one of Gwyn's four knights. The death of the armor's owner can be surmised from the corrosive Dark of the Abyss, and the tattered azure-blue cape, once a symbol of pride and glory."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite.
* Artorias is related to The Abyss Watchers of DS3.
* Part of the Artorias' Set. Pieces include Helm of Artorias, Armor of Artorias, Armor of Artorias and Leggings of Artorias.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Domhnall of Zena for 20,000 souls after defeating Artorias The Abysswalker
