**Knight Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Armor worn by lower-rank knights. While the metal used is thin, the grooved texture enhances its protective capabilities. It is part of the Knight Set. **Knight Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Armor equipment of a lower a rank knight. Despite the thin metal used, the grooved texture gives them added protection."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Knight Set. Pieces include Knight Helm, Knight Armor, Knight Gauntlets and Knight Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Knight (Starting Class)
* Darkroot Basin: shallow water near the Hydra
