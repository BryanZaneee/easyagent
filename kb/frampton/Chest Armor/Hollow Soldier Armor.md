**Hollow Soldier Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Armor worn by Hollow soldiers. Despite its age and worn appearance, its iron construction still provides considerable sturdiness. It is part of the Hollow Soldier Set. **Hollow Soldier Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Armor worn by Hollow soldiers. It may be old and battered, but its iron construction makes it quite sturdy."

### Hollow Soldier ArmorInformation

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Hollow Soldier Set. Pieces include Hollow Soldier Helm, Hollow Soldier Armor and Hollow Soldier Waistcloth,

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Drops from Undead Soldier (1% Chance - Undead Burg, Undead Parish, Northern Undead Asylum (Revisit), Firelink Shrine)
