**Giant Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Part of the Giant Armor Set. This armor is worn by the giant sentinels of Anor Londo. Crafted from ancient brass, it offers substantial protection against non-physical attacks. However, its enormous size and weight make it extremely heavy for humans and significantly hinder stamina recovery. **Giant Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Armor of the giant sentinels of Anor Londo. Made from ancient brass, it also offers protection versus non-physical attacks, but due to its giant size, it is extremely heavy for humans, and impedes stamina recovery.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Giant Armor Set. Pieces include Giant Helm, Giant Armor, Giant Gauntlets and Giant Leggings.
