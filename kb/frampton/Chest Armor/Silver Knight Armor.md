**Silver Knight Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Part of the Silver Knight Set, this armor is worn by the Silver Knights who protect Anor Londo. **Silver Knight Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Armor of the Silver Knights who protect Anor Londo. When Lord Gwyn departed to link the Fire, his knights split into two groups. The Silver Knights remained in the forsaken capital in the service of their goddess.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Silver Knight Set. Pieces include Silver Knight Helm, Silver Knight Armor, Silver Knight Gauntlets and Silver Knight Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Anor Londo. In a chest guarded by two Silver Knights
