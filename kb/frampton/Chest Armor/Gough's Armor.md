**Gough's Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.**Reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second. This armor was worn by Hawkeye Gough, one of Gwyn's Four Knights, since his days as a dragonslayer. It is part of the Gough's Set. Gough's Armor is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Armor of Hawkeye Gough, one of Gwyn's Four Knights. Armor donned by Gough since his days as a dragonslayer. The medallion, bequeathed by the Lord Himself, and the dragon bone pauldrons are symbols of the highest honors.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
* Reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second.
* Part of the Gough's Set. Pieces include Gough's Helm, Gough's Chest, Gough's Gloves and Gough's Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Dropped by Hawkeye Gough
