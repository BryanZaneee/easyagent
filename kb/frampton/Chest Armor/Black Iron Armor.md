**Black Iron Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Part of the Black Iron Set. This armor belongs to Black Iron Tarkus, a knight renowned for his immense strength. Forged from a unique black iron, it offers exceptional defense, particularly against fire. However, its immense weight makes it difficult to wield for anyone other than Tarkus himself. **Black Iron Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Armor of Black Iron Tarkus, a knight known for his great strength. Built of a special black iron and providing strong defense, notably against fire, but so terrible heavy to be unwieldy to all but Tarkus himself."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Black Iron Set. Pieces include Black Iron Helm, Black Iron Armor, Black Iron Gauntlets and Black Iron Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Anor Londo. To the left of the giant painting leading to the Painted World of Ariamis
