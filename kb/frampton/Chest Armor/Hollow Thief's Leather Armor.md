Hollow Thief's Leather Armor is a Chest Armor in **Dark Souls.**It is part of Hollow Thief Set.

### Description

*"Leather armor worn by Hollow thieves. It stifles noise and helps its wear hide in the shadows."*

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Drops from Undead Assassin also known as Hollow Thief (1% chance -  Lower Undead Burg)

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
