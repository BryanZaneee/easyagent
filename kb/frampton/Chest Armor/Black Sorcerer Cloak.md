**Black Sorcerer Cloak** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** A cloak worn by secret sorcerers of the Vinheim Dragon School. These sorcerers specialize in sound-based spells and maintain utmost secrecy, never revealing themselves. It is part of Black Sorcerer Set. **Black Sorcerer Cloak** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Cloak worn by the secret sorcerers at Vinheim Dragon School. They secretly work with sound-based spells, and never reveal themselves."

### Description

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Black Sorcerer Set. Pieces include Black Sorcerer Hat, Black Sorcerer Cloak, Black Sorcerer Gauntlets and Black Sorcerer Boots.
