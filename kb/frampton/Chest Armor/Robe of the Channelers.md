**Robe of the Channelers** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Found in a chest near the end of The Duke's Archives, this robe is part of the Channeler Set. It is worn by the Channelers, sorcerers who serve Seath the Scaleless. As the heaviest protective gear for sorcerers, it is also imbued with magic. **Robe of the Channelers** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Robe of the Channelers, sorcerers that serve Seath the Scaleless. Even after the onset of Seath's madness, the "snatchers" as they were often called, ventured to far lands to find suitable human specimens. The heaviest of protective gear for sorcerers, and imbued with magic."

### Information

* Cannot be reinforced.
* Part of the Channeler Set. Pieces include Six-Eyed Helm of the Channelers, Robe of the Channelers, Gauntlets of the Channelers and Waistcloth of the Channelers.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Found in a chest near the end of The Duke's Archives.
