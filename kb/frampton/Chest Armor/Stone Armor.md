**Stone Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Part of the Stone Knight Set. This moss-covered armor belongs to the Stone Knight, the defender of the Darkroot Garden. The Stone Knight is a creation of ancient magic, and while this armor is also imbued with such magic, it is incredibly heavy. **Stone Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Moss-covered armor of the Stone Knight, defender of the Darkroot Garden. The Stone Knight is a product of ancient magic, and although this armor is also imbued with magic, it is incredibly heavy."

### Information

* Cannot be upgraded
* Part of the Stone Knight Set. Pieces include Stone Helm, Stone Armor, Stone Gauntlets and Stone Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Darkroot Garden, on the cliff behind a stone structure where Alvina is.
