**Balder Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Part of the Balder Set, this armor was worn by the knights of the ancient kingdom of Balder. It is constructed from thick iron plates, providing substantial protection. **Balder Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Armor worn by the knights of the ancient kingdom of Balder. It is made from thick iron plates. Balder was the homeland of the Knight King Rendal, but it came to ruin after a great many Undead were spawned."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Balder Set, Pieces include Balder Helm, Balder Armor, Balder Gauntlets and Balder Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Crestfallen Merchant for 8,000 souls
* Drops from random Balder Knight (1% chance - Sen's Fortress and Undead Parish)
