**Chain Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Part of the Chain Set, this chainmail armor is made from thin interlinking rings of steel. It is widely used across the human world due to its ease of production, moderate weight, and decent defense. While knights may prefer more ornate armor, warriors on the battlefield prioritize anything that ensures survival. **Chain Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Chainmail armor of thin interlinking rings of steel. It is common throughout the human world since it can be easily produced, is not too heavy, and offers good defense. Knights may favor flashy armor, but for warriors on the battlefield, anything is fine as long as it keeps them alive."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Chain Set. Pieces include Chain Helm, Chain Armor, Leather Gauntlets, Chain Leggings.

### How to get / Where to Find

* Sold by Undead Merchant (Male) for 800 souls
* Drops from Chained Prisoner (20% chance - Oolacile Township)

### Notes & Tips

* The poise this chest piece gives is excellent relative to how light it is. There are very few light chest pieces in the game that also give poise, and the other options to choose from both weigh more than the Chain Armor and give less poise than it. Because of this, the Chain Armor is amazing for substantially increasing your defenses while offering good poise in an armor slot that otherwise only has heavy options to pick from.
