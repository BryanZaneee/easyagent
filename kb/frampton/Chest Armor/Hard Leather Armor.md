**Hard Leather Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Armor made of thick leather, offering a good level of defense. It is a very common type of protective gear and is part of the Warrior Set. **Hard Leather Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Armor made of thick leather. Very common type of protective gear, they provide a sound level of defense."

### Dark Soul Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Warrior Set. Pieces include Standard Helm, Hard Leather Armor, Hard Leather Gauntlets and Hard Leather Boots.

### Where to Find \ Location in Dark Soul

* Warrior (Starting Class)
* Depths: in the room where you fight Gaping Dragon
