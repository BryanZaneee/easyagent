**Brigand Armor** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.** Armor worn by brigands, notorious for raiding mountain villages and ambushing travelers. This rugged armor is part of the Bandit Set. **Brigand Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Worn by the brigands who raid mountain hamlets and attack travelers. Worn by the brigands who raid mountain hamlets and attack travelers"

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Bandit Set. Pieces include Brigand Hood, Brigand Armor, Brigand Gauntlets and Brigand Trousers.

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Bandit (Starting Class)
* Valley of Drakes
