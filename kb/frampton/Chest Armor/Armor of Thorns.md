**Armor of Thorns** is a Chest Armor in Dark Souls**.**Part of the Thorns Set, this armor was worn by Kirk, the notorious Darkwraith and knight of Thorns. The armor's surface is covered in a dense layer of thorns. **Armor of Thorns** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Armor of Kirk, knight of Thorns and notorious member of the Darkwraiths. A dense patch of thorns grows from its surface. It is a fitting item for the murderous Kirk, for by simply wearing it and rolling, one can damage enemies."

### Dark SoulsArmor of Thorns Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Thorns Set. Pieces include Helm of Thorns, Armor of Thorns, Gauntlets of Thorns and Leggings of Thorns.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Same place where The Fair Lady is. To spawn the armor set you need to defeat Kirk, Knight of Thorns in 3 places: Depths, Demon Ruins, Lost Izalith
