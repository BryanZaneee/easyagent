**Item Discovery** is a Stat in Dark Souls**.** The **Item Discovery** rate affects the chance of rare items dropping when defeating enemies. It does not impact the number of souls gained.

### Information

The item discovery rate determines the possibility of rare item drops when killing enemies. Item discovery **does not** influence the amount of souls absorbed.

**Base value is 100**, but it can be raised to the **maximum of 410** via different ways :

* Equipping either the Covetous Gold Serpent Ring or Symbol of Avarice will raise item discovery by 200. They will not stack.
* Humanity raises item discovery by certain amounts up to 110 at 10 'soft' humanity. See the table below.

Hollow or human form does not affect the drop rate of items.
