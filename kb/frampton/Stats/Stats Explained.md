### Requirements

The requirements needed to effectively wield the weapon.
 **Strength**

* Wielding a weapon without meeting the required strength will leave you unable to swing it properly.
* 2-handing a weapon will give a 50% boost to strength, so only 2/3 of the strength is required.
* Bows are 2-handed by default, so there's no need to go above 2/3 to properly wield it.

**Dexterity**

* Wielding a weapon without meeting the required dexterity will make it bounce off enemies.

**Intelligence**
 **Faith**

### Other

**Durability**

* The weapon's HP, when the durability hits 0, the effectiveness of its attacks become weakened to the point of almost useless. When an items durability is low, a message will come up saying "Weapon At Risk!" at this point the weapon does not perform at it's best.

**Weight**

* How much the item weighs when equipped.

**Attack Type**

* Defines what kind of swing set the weapon has. Regular(R), Thrust(T), Slash(Sl), Strike(St)
