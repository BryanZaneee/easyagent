**Health in Dark Souls**, also referred to as **Life**, **Hit Points** or **HP**, is a game mechanic that controls if the player is alive. It's represented by a red bar on top of the hud.

### Amount

* The Vitality stat governs the amount of Health the character has.
* The maximum Health of 1900 HP is achieved at 99 Vitality (hard cap).
* The amount of HP gained per level in Vitality fluctuates heavily, but the amount gained reduces when above 30 Vitality (soft cap) and then steadily declines.
* Health can be increased by wearing the Ring of Favor and Protection (+20%), the Tiny Being's Ring (+5%) and the Mask of the Mother (+10%).
* Health can be **decreased** by wearing the Dusk Crown Ring (-50%) or being cursed (-50% for the duration of the curse).

### Depletion of health

Health generally gets depleted by taking physical or elemental damage. It can also be lowered by poison, toxic or bleeding. There are also enviormental means of losing health such as falling, standing in fires, traps or touching lava.

### Recovery

There is no passive regeneration of health. It can only be regenerated by player actions, such as

* Drinking Estus (between 5 and 20 uses and +300 and +700 HP, fills up at bonfires)
* Using one time consumables:
  + Divine Blessing (full restore)
  + Humanity (full restore)
  + Twin Humanities (full restore)
  + Fire Keeper Soul (full restore, use **not recommended** before ng+)
  + Elizabeth's Mushroom (+100 HP per second for 30 seconds)
* Resting at a Bonfire (full restore)
  + using a Homward Bone or the mircle Homeward has the same effect
* Casting a healing mircale:
  + Heal
  + Great Heal Excerpt
  + Great Heal
  + Soothing Sunlight
  + Replenishment
  + Bountiful Sunlight
* Equipping an item with passive Health regeneration:
  + Ring of the Evil Eye (Ring; 30 HP for each fallen enemy)
  + Sanctus (Shield; 2 HP every 2 seconds)
  + Butcher Knife (Axe; 5 HP per successful hit)
  + Server (Curved Greatsword; 7 HP per successful hit)
  + Smough's Hammer (Great Hammer; 10 HP per successful hit)

### Health Trivia and Tips

* When using items to go above 1900 HP (the maximum at 99 Vitality without items) the health bar will **not show the HP**. The HP are however still present and count fully against damage, they just don't get displayed.
* One block of the Health Bar is equal to roughly 135.7HP, and each division of each block is about 27.1HP.
