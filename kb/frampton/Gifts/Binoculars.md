**Binoculars** is a Tool in **Dark Souls**. Tools are unique, reusable equipment pieces designed to assist players in multiple gameplay situations, ranging simple interactions to helping during difficult boss fights.

> Binoculars made of Brass. Use to peer at distant scenery.
> This advanced device was built by a famous craftsman of Astora. Its utility is singular, but its applications many.
> The value of these specs depends greatly on the imagination of their owner

### Usage

* Used to see distant threats, scenery, and other objects.
* This is a key item that can be used more than once.
* Zoom in and out by pressing up and down on the d-pad.

### Location

* In the Graveyard by Firelink Shrine. Keep right, along the mountain wall, located on a cliff edge just after 3 skeletons and 1 giant skeleton encounter.
* Only one of this item can be carried at a time, so place it in the Bottomless Box before picking up another.
* Video Location Timestamp 2:16

### Notes & Tips

* Equip the Hawk Ring to see even further.
* Binoculars can also be used to aim a crossbow from outside of lock-on range.
* Particularly useful when trying to aim spells or, of course, when in Princess Gwynevere's bed chamber.
* **Glitch/Fun:** Using the binoculars while talking to a NPC merchant, will result in a third person view of your character with binoculars.
  Use them just before the NPC finishes their last spoken sentence, so the binoculars are equipped while the merchant menu is shown.
