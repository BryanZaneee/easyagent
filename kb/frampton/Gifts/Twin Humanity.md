**Twin Humanities** is a Consumble in **Dark Souls**.

### Twin Humanities Usage

* Increases Humanity by 2 when consumed.
* Restores a large amount of health when consumed. (more than 2600 HP)
* Can be Traded to Snuggly for a Rare Ring of Sacrifice.
* Can be sold to Kingseeker Frampt for 2,000 souls.

### Twin Humanities Location

* Can be taken as a Gift upon character creation.
* Sold by: Patches the Hyena for 20,000 (1 only?).
* Atonement gift from Patches if you choose not to forgive him after the incident in the Tomb of Giants.
* Treasure: Lower Undead Burg, guarded by zombies with torches.
* Treasure: In a chest behind a fake wall, in tunnel between Blighttown and The Great Hollow.
* Treasure: Sniper tower above bonfire, in the lavabed by Lost Izalith.
* Dropped by Oswald of Carim when killed.
* Dropped by Humanity Spirits in the Chasm of the Abyss.
* Dropped by some Bosses.
* Dropped by some Vagrants.
