# Rust Iron Ring

**Rusted Iron Ring** is a Ring in **Dark Souls and Dark Souls Remastered**

### Item Description

"*This iron ring was used to shackle the guilty. It is terribly rusted, and faintly stained with blood.*
*Those who find this strange ring to their liking will be pleased to find it easier to gain footing on poor ground such as swamps.*"

### Where to Find \ Location

* The Rusted Iron Ring can be found on the crumbled stairway, just past the door that is opened with the Undead Asylum F2 West Key in the upper level of the Undead Asylum.
* ??

### General Info

* Allows full movement regardless of environment. The ring is useful for going through Blighttown, as increased movement through the swamp decreases the likelihood of contracting Poison. It will come in handy again during the fights with the Hydras, as well as traversing the pits of Lost Izalith. It is also helpful in Sen's Fortress, for fighting the Titanite Demons in the pit under the first bridge. Its effect does stack with the Dark Wood Grain Ring, allowing the player to "Ninja Flip" in swamp or other mobility-restricting terrain.
