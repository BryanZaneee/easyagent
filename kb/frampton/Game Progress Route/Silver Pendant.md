**Silver Pendant** is a Tool in **Dark Souls**. Tools are unique, reusable equipment pieces designed to assist players in multiple gameplay situations, ranging simple interactions to helping during difficult boss fights.

> "One of the ancient treasures of Anor Londo. Presented to Artorias for facing the Abyss.
> Effectively deflects the Dark of the Abyss, especially in its magic forms."

### Usage

* Deflects Dark Magic for a short duration when used.
* This item is reusable.
* Deflection period is significantly longer than the parrying window for melee attacks, but does take a moment to activate.
* Actually *deflects* the magic, rather than provides protection against.

### Location

* Found in a chest, inside a hidden room in Oolacile Township. Use a light-source ( Cast Light spell, Skull Lantern and Sunlight Maggot confirmed) to reveal an illusory wall to access the room.
* Video Location Timestamp 3:18
