**Leather Gauntlets** are Gauntlets in Dark Souls. Part of the Chain Set, are gauntlets made of soft leather, making them one of the more common types of protective gear. **Leather Gauntlets** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's arms to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Gauntlets made of soft leather. One of the more common kinds of protective gear."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Chain Set. Pieces include Chain Helm, Chain Armor, Leather Gauntlets, Chain Leggings.

### How to get / Where to Find

* Sold by Undead Merchant (Male) for 500 souls
* Drops from Chained Prisoner (20% chance - Oolacile Township)
