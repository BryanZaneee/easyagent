**Catarina Gauntlets** is a Gauntlets in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Catarina Set.

### Description

*"Distinctively shaped gauntlets worn by the Knights of Catarina. Outside of Catarina, they are often ridiculed for their onion-like shape, infuriating the proud knights, but the masterfully forged curved design makes it very effective for parrying."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Crestfallen Merchant for 7,000 souls

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
* Reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second.
* Part of the Catarina Set. Pieces include Catarina Helm, Catarina Armor, Catarina Gauntlets and Catarina Leggings.
