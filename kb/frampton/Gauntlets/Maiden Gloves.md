**Maiden Gloves** are Gauntlets in Dark Souls. While soft and well-made, they offer little defense and are ill-suited for battle. Part of the Maiden Set. **Maiden Gloves** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's arms to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "White gloves worn by traveling maidens. It is part of their formal attire, regardless of rank. It is soft and well-made, but does not offer much in the way of defense, making it ill- suited to use in battle."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Maiden Set. Pieces include Maiden Hood, Maiden Robe, Maiden Gloves and Maiden Skirt.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Duke's Archives. In one of the cells.
