**Brass Gauntlets** is a Gauntlets in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Brass Set.

### Description

*"Gauntlets of the Darkmoon Knightess, Fire Keeper of Anor Londo. After becoming Undead, she visited the Dark Sun Gwyndolin at the Mausoleum of the Spiral Depths, became a Blade of Darkmoon, and assumed the flame-keeping duty. She received these gauntlets, which hide her hideous form and help her hunt the guilty."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Darkmoon Tomb, after defeating Dark Sun Gwyndolin

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Brass Set. Pieces include Brass Helm, Brass Armor , Brass Gauntlets and Brass Leggings.
