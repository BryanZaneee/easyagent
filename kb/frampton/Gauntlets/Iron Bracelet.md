**Iron Bracelet** is a Gauntlets in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Iron Set.

### Description

*"Bracelets of Solaire of Astora, Knight of Sunlight. Of high quality, but lacking any particular powers. Solaire's incredible prowess must have come from rigorous training alone, for his equipment exhibits no special traits."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Drops from Solaire of Astora

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Iron Set. Pieces include Iron Helm, Armor of the Sun, Iron Bracelet and Iron Leggings.
