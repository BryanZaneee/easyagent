**Hard Leather Gauntlets** are Gauntlets in Dark Souls. Made of thick leather, these gauntlets offer a sound level of defense. They are a common protective gear and part of the Warrior Set. **Hard Leather Gauntlets** are an armor piece worn over or around the player's arms to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Gauntlets made of thick leather. Very common type of protective gear, they provide a sound level of defense.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Warrior Set. Pieces include Standard Helm, Hard Leather Armor, Hard Leather Gauntlets and Hard Leather Boots.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Warrior (Starting Class)
* Depths: in the room where you fight Gaping Dragon
