**Gauntlets of Favor** is a Gauntlets in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Favor Set.

### Description

*"Gauntlets of Lautrec the Embraced, representing the goddess Fina's love. During his solitude, he forsook everything, for he believed in the goddess's love for him."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Found in Anor Londo, on second floor of Ornstein and Smough boss area, after invading and defeating Lautrec.

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Favor Set. Pieces include Helm of Favor, Embraced Armor of Favor, Gauntlets of Favor and Leggings of Favor.
