**Silver Knight Gauntlets** is a Gauntlets in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Silver Knight Set.

### Description

*"Gauntlets of the Silver Knights who protect Anor Londo. When Lord Gwyn departed to link the Fire, his knights split into two groups. The Silver Knights remained in the forsaken capital in the service of their goddess."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Anor Londo. In a chest guarded by two Silver Knights

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Silver Knight Set. Pieces include Silver Knight Helm, Silver Knight Armor, Silver Knight Gauntlets and Silver Knight Leggings.
