**Witch Gloves** are Gauntlets in Dark Souls. Part of the Witch Set, belong to the rogue witch Beatrice. Unlike most magic users who studied at the Dragon School, Beatrice was an exception. She dared to venture into the Abyss but tragically did not survive to recount her ordeal. **Witch Gloves** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's arms to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Gloves of the rogue witch Beatrice. Almost all magic users that employ catalysts studied at Dragon School, but Beatrice is one of the few exceptions. She braved the Abyss but did not live to tell of her ordeal.

### Description

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Witch Set. Pieces include Witch Hat, Witch Cloak, Witch Gloves and Witch Skirt.

### Where to Find \ Location

* At Valley of Drakes area along with Beatrice's Catalyst. Go where the cave entrance to Blighttown is; the corpse is located at a ledge on at the opposite side of the cave entrance.
* Available after defeating The Four Kings
