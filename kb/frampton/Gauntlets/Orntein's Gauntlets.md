**Orntein's Gauntlets** is a Gauntlets in **Dark Souls**.

### Description

*"Gauntlets of the dragonslayer Ornstein, who guards the cathedral in the forsaken city of Anor Londo. Ornstein is believed to be the captain of the Four Knights. His golden lion gauntlets are imbued with the power of lightning and should provide good protection against it."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Domhnall of Zena
* You need to defeat Dragon Slayer Ornstein second.
