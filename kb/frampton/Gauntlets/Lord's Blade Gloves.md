**Lord's Blade Gloves** is a Gauntlets in **Dark Souls**. It is part of  Lord's Blades Set.

### Description

*"Gloves worn by the Lord's Blade Ciaran, one of Gwyn's four knights. Gloves common to all of the Lord's Blades. These deadly women shift nimbly between layers of darkness, etching streaks of gold into the night air, and planting visions of terror into the minds of their targets."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Killing Lord's Blade Ciaran, she appears in the same area where you fight Artorias The Abysswalker

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Lord's Blade Set. Pieces include Porcelain Mask, Lord's Blade Robe, Lord's Blade Gloves and Lord's Blade Waistcloth.
