**Gauntlets of the Vanquisher** is a Gauntlets in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Adventurer's Set.

### Description

*"Strange gauntlets from the ancient land of Zena, birthplace of the curious dealer Domhnall. They gold bracelets symbolize a vanquisher."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Domhnall of Zena for 10,000 souls.

### Notes

* Cannot be upgraded
* Reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second.
* Part of the Adventurer's Set. Parts include Helm of the Wise, Armor of the Glorious, Gauntlets of the Vanquisher and Boots of the Explorer.
