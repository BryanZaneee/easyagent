**Crimson Gloves** are Gauntlets in Dark Souls. Part of the Crimson Set, are the gloves of the sorcerers who flooded New Londo to seal away the Darkwraiths and the Four Kings. The Sealers, once known as healers, wore these bright crimson gloves as a symbol of their role. **Crimson Gloves** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's arms to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Gloves of the sorcerers who flooded New Londo to seal away the Dark wraiths, and the Four Kings who descended into Dark. The Sealers were once known as healers, and the bright crimson was a symbol of that."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Crimson Set. Pieces include Mask of the Sealer, Crimson Robe, Crimson Gloves and Crimson Waistcloth.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Blighttown, near the entrance from Valley of Drakes
