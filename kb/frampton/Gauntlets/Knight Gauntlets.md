**Knight Gauntlets** are Gauntlets in Dark Souls. Gauntlets worn by lower-rank knights. Despite their thin metal, the grooved texture provides added protection. Part of the Knight Set. Knight Gauntlets is an armor piece worn over or around the player's arms to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Gauntlets equipment of a lower a rank knight. Despite the thin metal used, the grooved texture gives them added protection.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Knight Set. Pieces include Knight Helm, Knight Armor, Knight Gauntlets and Knight Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Knight (Starting Class)
* Darkroot Basin: shallow water near the Hydra
