**Chester's Gloves** are Gauntlets in Dark Souls. Part of the Chester's Set, are leather gloves worn by Marvellous Chester, a mysterious figure lost in time. While these gloves have no distinguishing features, they are of extremely high quality. **Chester's Gloves** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's arms to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Leather gloves worn by Marvellous Chester, a man of mystery lost in the past. These gloves have no distinguishing features, but are of extremely high quality.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Chester is suspected to hail from the Bloodborne universe, because of his clothing and mentions of time travel.
* Part of the Chester's Set. Pieces include Snickering Top Hat, Chester's Long Coat, Chester's Gloves, and Chester's Trousers.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Killing Marvellous Chester in human form
