**Giant Gauntlets** is a Gauntlets in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Giant Armor Set.

### Description

*"Gauntlets of the giant sentinels of Anor Londo. Made from ancient brass, they also offer protection versus non-physical attacks, but due to their giant size, they are extremely heavy for humans, and impede stamina recovery."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Giant Blacksmith

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Giant Armor Set. Pieces include Giant Helm, Giant Armor, Giant Gauntlets and Giant Leggings.
