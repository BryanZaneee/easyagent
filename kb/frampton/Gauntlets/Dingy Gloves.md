**Dingy Gloves** are Gauntlets in Dark Souls. Part of the Dingy Set, are believed to have once been the white gloves of a maiden, but they have darkened over time due to smoke and ashes from bonfires. **Dingy Gloves** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's arms to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Worn by the Fire Keeper at Firelink Shrine. They are thought to have once been the white gloves of a maiden, but smoke and ashes from bonfires darkened them over the years.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Dingy Set. Parts include Dingy Hood, Dingy Robe, Dingy Gloves and Blood-Stained Skirt.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Part of a Lautrec questline. In Anastacia cell.
