**Gauntlets of the Channelers** are Gauntlets in Dark Souls. These gauntlets are the heaviest protective gear for sorcerers and are imbued with magic. The Channelers were often referred to as "snatchers" due to their role in seeking out human specimens. Part of the Channeler Set. **Gauntlets of the Channelers** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's arms to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped

> Gauntlets of the Channelers, sorcerers that serve Seath the Scaleless. Even after the onset of Seath's madness, the "snatchers" as they were often called, ventured to far lands to find suitable human specimens. The heaviest of protective gear for sorcerers, and imbued with magic.

### Information

* Cannot be upgraded
* Part of the Channeler Set. Pieces include Six-Eyed Helm of the Channelers, Robe of the Channelers, Gauntlets of the Channelers and Waistcloth of the Channelers.

### Where to Find \ Location

* In a chest off in one of the side rooms of the Duke's Archives, near the stairs leading down to the courtyard that leads to the Crystal Cave.
