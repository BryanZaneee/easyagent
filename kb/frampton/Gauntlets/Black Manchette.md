**Black Manchette** are Gauntlets in Dark Souls. Part of the Black Set, is worn by pardoners serving Velka, the Goddess of Sin. These uniformly black manchettes are imbued with Velka's mystical power, providing resistance against all forms of magic. Black Manchette is an armor piece worn over or around the player's arms to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Manchette worn by pardoners serving Velka, the Goddess of Sin. The pardoners' attire is uniformly black in color and said to be imbued with Velka's mystical power, which provides resistance against all manner of magic

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Black Set. Mask of Velka, Black Cleric Robe, Black Manchette and Black Tights.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Painted World of Ariamis, behind the locked annex door.
