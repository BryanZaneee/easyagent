**Xanthous Gloves** are Gauntlets in Dark Souls. Part of the Xanthous Set, are mysterious items once worn by Xanthous King Jeremiah, the legendary exile. Despite their tattered appearance, their bright yellow color is still so vibrant that it stings the eyes. **Xanthous Gloves** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's arms to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Mysterious items once worn by the Xanthous King Jeremiah, the legendary exile. No one knows where it came from. They may be tattered, but its bright yellow color still stings the eyes.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Xanthous Set. Pieces include Xanthous Crown, Xanthous Overcoat, Xanthous Gloves and Xanthous Waistcloth.

### Where to Find \ Location

* On a corpse behind Crossbreed Priscilla after defeating Dark Spirit Xanthous King, Jeremiah.
