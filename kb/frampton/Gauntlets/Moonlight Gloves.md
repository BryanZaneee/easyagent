**Moonlight Gloves** are Gauntlets in Dark Souls. Part of the Gwyndolin Moonlight Set, are gloves belonging to Dark Sun Gwyndolin, protector of the forsaken city of Anor Londo. Gwyndolin's affinity with the moon is reflected in these silk-thin gloves, which offer minimal physical defense. **Moonlight Gloves** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's arms to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Gloves of the Dark Sun Gwyndolin, protector of the forsaken city of Anor Londo. The power of the moon was strong in
> Gwyndolin, and thus he was raised as a daughter. His magic garb is silk-thin, and hardly provides any physical defense

### Information

* Cannot be upgraded
* Part of the Gwyndolin Moonlight Set. Pieces include Crown of the Dark Sun, Moonlight Robe, Moonlight Gloves and Moonlight Waistcloth.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Sold by Domhnall of Zena after killing Dark Sun Gwyndolin
