**Shadow Gauntlets** are Gauntlets in Dark Souls. Black cloth gauntlets worn by spooks from an Eastern land. They sacrifice defense for greater mobility but offer resistance to bleeding and poison. Part of the Shadow Set. **Shadow Gauntlets** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's arms to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Black cloth gauntlets worn by spooks from an Eastern land. Designed so as not to hinder their unique form of martial arts. White they sacrifice defense for the sake of greater mobility, they do offer resistance to bleeding and poison among other things, perhaps due to the nature of espionage.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Shadow Set. Pieces include Shadow Mask, Shadow Garb, Shadow Gauntlets and Shadow Leggings.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Found in Blighttown past first group of Infested Ghouls
