**Antiquated Gloves**are Gauntlets in Dark Souls. Made from ivory-colored silk with elaborate embroidery and imbued with ancient magic power. These gloves offer no physical protection as they were not meant for battle. Part of the Antiquated Set. **Antiquated Gloves** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's arms to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Gloves from the ancient fallen land of Oolacile. Their ivory-colored silk features elaborate embroidery and are imbued with ancient magic power. One cannot expect any physical protection from these, as they were not meant to be worn in battle.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Antiquated Set. Pieces include Crown of Dusk, Antiquated Dress, Antiquated Gloves and Antiquated Skirt.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Found in Darkroot Basin, after saving Dusk of Oolacile. Kill the Golden Crystal Golem in the back of the **Darkroot Basin** cave (far behind the Hydra), go back to where the corpse was in front of the Hydra, and summon her (you DO NOT have to be in human form), then return to where you found the golem. You will find the set there.

### Notes & Tips

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Part of the Antiquated Set. Pieces include Crown of Dusk, Antiquated Dress, Antiquated Gloves and Antiquated Skirt.
