**Gold-Hemmed Black Gloves** are Gauntlets in Dark Souls. Part of the Gold-Hemmed Black Set, were worn by the witch Quelana of Izalith, mother of pyromancy and Daughter of Chaos. She wore these gold-lined black gloves even before the Age of Fire, and they provide strong resistance against fire, poison, and other perils. **Gold-Hemmed Black Gloves** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's arms to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Worn by the witch Quelana of Izalith, mother of pyromancy and Daughter of Chaos. She wore these gold lined black gloves before even the Age of Fire, and they offer strong resistance versus fire, poison, and other perils.

### Information

* Cannot be upgraded
* Part of the Gold-Hemmed Black Set. Pieces include Gold-Hemmed Black Hood, Gold-Hemmed Black Cloak, Gold-Hemmed Black Gloves and Gold-Hemmed Black Skirt.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Found in Ceaseless Discharge's area of the Demon Ruins
