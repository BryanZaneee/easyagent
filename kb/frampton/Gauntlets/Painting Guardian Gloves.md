**Painting Guardian Gloves** are Gauntlets in Dark Souls. They provide substantial protection against magic. The guardians have protected the Great Painting of Ariamis for ages, with the reason for their duty lost to memory. Part of the Painting Guardian Set. **Painting Guardian Gloves** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's arms to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Gloves worn by the alabaster-clothed guardians of the paintings in Anor Londo. Offer substantial protection versus magic. They have guarded the Great Painting of Ariamis for ages, passing their duty down through generations, but the reason for doing so passed from all memory long ago.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Painting Guardians are connected to Filianore of DS3 and also to Dragons.
* Part of the Painting Guardian Set. Pieces include Painting Guardian Hood, Painting Guardian Robe, Painting Guardian Gloves and Painting Guardian Waistcloth.

### Where to Find \ Location

* Painted World of Ariamis
