**Travelling Gloves** are Gauntlets in Dark Souls. Sturdy gloves made for extended journeys. Part of Big Hat's Set. **Travelling Gloves** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's arms to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Thick, sturdy gloves made for travelers on extended journeys.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Big Hat's Set. Pieces include Big Hat, Sage Robe, Travelling Gloves and Travelling Boots.
* Part of the Holy Set. Pieces include Priest's Hat, Holy Robe, Travelling Gloves and Holy Trousers.

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Cleric (Starting Class)
* The Catacombs: on a cliff in the bonewheel skeleton room. To access the cliff, jump down from the second rotating bridge above.
