**Paladin Gauntlets** is a Gauntlets in **Dark Souls**. It is part Paladin Set.

### Description

*"Gauntlets of Leeroy, Paladin of the Catacombs. Long ago, the Way of the White produced its first Undead, a paladin in golden armor. With the legendary treasures Grant and Sanctus, Leeroy set out for Lordran, Land of the Gods, in the first Undead mission of the Way of White."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Tomb of the Giants. Gravelord Nito's chamber after defeating black phantom Paladin Leeroy

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
* Reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second.
* Part of the Paladin Set. Pieces include Paladin Helm, Paladin Armor, Paladin Gauntlets and Paladin Leggings.
