**Stone Gauntlets** is a Gauntlets in **Dark Souls**.It is part of Stone Knight Set.

### Description

*"Moss-covered gauntlets of the Stone Knight, defender of the Darkroot Garden. The Stone Knight is a product of ancient magic, and although these gauntlets are also imbued with magic, they are incredibly heavy."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Darkroot Garden, on the cliff behind a stone structure where Alvina is.

### Notes

* Cannot be upgraded
* Part of the Stone Knight Set. Pieces include Stone Helm, Stone Armor, Stone Gauntlets and Stone Leggings.
