**Leather Gloves** are Gauntlets in Dark Souls. Made of soft leather, these gloves are a common type of protective gear. They are part of the Hunter Set. Leather Gloves is an armor piece worn over or around the player's arms to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Made of soft leather. Very common type of protective gear."

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Hunter Set. Pieces include Pharis's Hat, Leather Armor, Leather Gloves and Leather Boots.

### How to Get / Where to Find

* Darkroot Basin. Near the entrance on a cliff.
