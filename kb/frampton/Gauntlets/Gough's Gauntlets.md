**Gough's Gauntlets** is a Gauntlets in **Dark Souls**. It is part of Gough's Set.

### Description

*"Gauntlets of Hawkeye Gough, one of Gwyn's Four Knights. Received as a decoration of honor. Crafted of similar material to the gauntlets worn by the Silver Knights."*

### Where to Find \ Location

* Dropped by Hawkeye Gough

### Notes

* Upgraded with: Twinkling Titanite
* Give to Frampt to receive 100 souls.
* Reduces Stamina regeneration by 1 per second.
* Part of the Gough's Set. Pieces include Gough's Helm, Gough's Chest, Gough's Gloves and Gough's Leggings.
