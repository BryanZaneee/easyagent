**Black Sorcerer Gauntlets** are Gauntlets in Dark Souls. Gauntlets worn by the secret sorcerers at Vinheim Dragon School. These sorcerers work with sound-based spells and keep their identities hidden. Part of the Black Sorcerer Set. **Black Sorcerer Gauntlets** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's arms to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Gauntlets worn by the secret sorcerers at Vinheim Dragon School. They secretly work with sound-based spells, and never reveal themselves.

### Information

* Upgraded with: Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab
* Part of the Black Sorcerer Set. Pieces include Black Sorcerer Hat, Black Sorcerer Cloak, Black Sorcerer Gauntlets and Black Sorcerer Boots.
